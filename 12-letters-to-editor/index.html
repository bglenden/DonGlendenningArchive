Don contends that PEI's education system is "really only a school system" requiring structural overhaul. The World Bank, largest public education contributor, cautions countries against confusing schooling with education. PEI does conflate them, focusing on serving youth in schools rather than all Islanders across ages, interests, and capabilities.
