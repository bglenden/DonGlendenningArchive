Don Glendenning writes a letter of nomination support for A. John Robinson for the Order of Prince Edward Island. He highlights Robinson's crucial leadership role with the Community Foundation of PEI, which has grown from $100,000 to over $10 million in endowments under Robinson's guidance. This document demonstrates Don's continued civic engagement and his role as a key opinion leader in PEI's philanthropic community.


62 Queen Elizabeth Drive

Charlottetown PEI  

C1A 3A9

April 4, 2019


Order of Prince Edward Island

Legislative Assembly

197 Richmond Street

PO Box 2000

Charlottetown, PE  C1A 7N8


Dear Sir:

A. John Robinson


I am pleased to support the nomination of A. John Robinson to the Order of Prince Edward Island. I have known John for many years and know him to be a most worthy candidate.


While I am aware of John’s status as a successful farmer, businessman, volunteer and philanthropist, it is his leadership role with the Community Foundation with which I am able to speak.  


The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island (CFPEI) is a charitable organization whose role is to encourage and assist Islanders in meeting their charitable objectives. It does this primarily through the building and administering of endowments the income from which is used to support charitable activities.  Its establishment has been a success story, and John has been a major contributor to that success.


CFPEI was established in 1993 and, when John joined the Board, had an endowment of perhaps $100,000; today that endowment is more than $10,000,000, Much of its success is attributable to John’s leadership, trust and standing in the community including the direct application of his considerable knowledge of finance and investment. John understands PEI; his insights allow him to see both a community’s needs and a community’s capacity to respond to its needs.  Those talents resulted in the growth and maturation of he Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.


The Robinson Family is a well-recognized and respected community family; giving back is a family tradition.  John and his family are concerned, generous and dedicated to the Island community; it is without any reservation whatsoever, that I support this nomination.


Sincerely




Donald  Glendenning, CM
