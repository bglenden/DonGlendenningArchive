A personal thank-you letter from Don (as Foundation Director) to John Keuper acknowledging his contribution to the Living Memorial Trust, which honors Charles Keuper's memory. Don notes the importance of the Keupers' initiative in enhancing Prince Edward Island's public spaces and commits to future correspondence regarding brochures and administrative details.
Community Foundation & Civic Life


December 16, 2006



John Keuper

Wood Islands


Dear Mrs. Keuper:

Living Memorial Trust



Thank you for your contribution to the Living Memorial Trust with the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  It is a fitting tribute to Charles’ memory.  In addition, it will continue to enhance Prince Edward Island for the benefit of future Islanders and visitors alike.  Without your initiative, it would not have happened.


I am enclosing receipts for your donation and will be in touch early in the new year on some administrative details including the development of a  brochure and other materials.


In the meantime, thanks again and have a great family Christmas. 


Warmest personal regards



Don Glendenning

Director
