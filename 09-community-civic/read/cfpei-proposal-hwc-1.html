Handle With Care

This is a request to the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island for financial support for the training of facilitators for a new initiative known as Handle with Care (HWC). HWC is a program designed to help parents and other caregivers promote the mental health of young children every day! It is useful for everyone who cares for or works with children; especially parents, grandparents and teachers, and consists of sets of interactive strategies that build on the strengths of adults in their lives. A more detailed description may be found in the attached sheet.

To prepare for its wider adoption, Education 20/20 Inc. proposes to start now with the training of facilitators, first in PEI and second for our sister Maritime Provinces. Training for facilitators will be led by two experienced trainers, Alice Taylor and Eileen Conway-Martin, in a process of 16 hours spread over two days. Participants in the program must be at least 18 years of age, have formal early childhood, teaching and/or social work education and experience. Experience dealing with children and families, personally and professionally is a great asset.

Costs per for the 2-day session are as follows:

  • Honoraria – 2 trainers $2,400

  • Materials and supplies $ 250

  • Other . $ 100

Total $2,750


If you have questions or need further information, please do not hesitate to contact me.



Sincerely

Don Glendenning
