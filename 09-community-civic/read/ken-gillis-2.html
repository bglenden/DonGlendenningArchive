A brief letter from Don (as CFPEI President) to Ken Gillis regarding IAHSP endowment establishment. Don explains that the IAHSP Board decided to establish an endowment at CFPEI with remaining funds, with grants to be made consistent with the original program objectives. The letter references pending payments to participating organizations upon completion of approved activities. This shows coordination between Don and Ken Gillis on program transition.



President 

CFPEI



Island Arts and Heritage Stabilization Program


As you know, the Island Arts and Heritage Stabilization Program is ending and finds itself with funds not used under the project. 


At the final meeting on xxx, the Board agreed that the outstanding funds be used to establish an endowment with the community Foundation with grants being made for purposes consistent with the original program. 


At the same time, as you will see from the attachment, in the case of a few participating organizations, activities have been approved but payments are pending their successful completion as authorized by the Project Director and myself.


Any funds not disbursed are to be added to the endowment.
