Community Foundation & Civic Life

Date: September 30, 2005


Subject: Description of role


Position: Volunteer – Compliance


As a community foundation, CFPEI has commitments to donors and to CCRA.  As a business, CFPEI has contractual obligations and as a good citizen wishes to operate in an open and transparent fashion.  Commitments include the following:


Agreements with major donors

Agreements for agency funds

Requirements of CCRA

Investment policies and procedures

Special project funds (IAHSP)

Granting policy (under development)


Reporting to the President and working with Directors and staff, the Compliance Coordinator is expected to:


Review documents

Prepare a checklist/schedule of compliance requirements

Recommend actions to ensure compliance

Recommend changes, if any, in agreements to facilitate procedures (Scholarships)
