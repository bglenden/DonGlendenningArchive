Draft marketing brochure distinguishing between donations and legacy giving. Explains donation concept (existing organization, one-time, used immediately) vs. legacy concept (perpetuity, organizational planning, long-term revenue stream, memorial/remembrance). Lists reasons to choose CFPEI: island-based, professional investment, managed by known people, confidentiality, safety, perpetuity, flexibility, low fees, positive investment record, 20-year service record, experienced staff. Provides sample will clauses for establishing endowments and emphasizes free consultation to help donors maximize impact.
