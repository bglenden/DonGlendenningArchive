Proposal for rural and northern development program targeting Western PEI. Objective: build/strengthen community capacity of third sector in the region. Rural and Northern Development


Background: Refer to background notes used for building communities

Objective: To build/strengthen community capacity of third sector in Western PEI.

Activities:

Engage representatives of community organizations in a process to identify regional needs and priorities.

Assess mandates and capabilities of agencies serving the area  with a view to identifying service gaps (Include a self assessment) 

Offer capacity building workshops to organizations in the area.  Although the precise list of workshops must await the assessment process, in our experience the most likely needs will be for budgeting, business plans and organizational analysis.
