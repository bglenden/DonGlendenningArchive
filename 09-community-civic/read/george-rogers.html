Personal letter from Don Glendenning (as CFPEI Director) to George Rogers in Calgary proposing the establishment of a heritage fund in memory of his mother, Irene Rogers, who made significant contributions to PEI's heritage preservation. Don articulates CFPEI's mission and how it provides a vehicle for recognizing and perpetuating charitable legacies. The letter demonstrates Don's personal touch in donor relations and his ability to articulate the Foundation's value proposition to potential donors across the country.
Community Foundation & Civic Life



Mr. George Rogers

C/o Nesbitt Burns

Bankers Hall, West Tower

4100-888 Third St. SW

Calgary, Alberta T2P 5C5


Dear Mr. Rogers:


During a recent conversation about the importance of preserving PEI’s heritage, the many contributions that your mother, Irene Rogers, made to PEI”s heritage were acknowledged.  I, too, although a newcomer to PEI some 40 years ago, am very aware of the work that she did and her appreciation of the Island’s way of life.


We believe that her contributions should be recognized in an appropriate manner; therefore, that is the purpose for my letter - would you consider establishing a heritage fund in her memory to ensure that her work is remembered and to continue the work that she loved?  If so, the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island could be of assistance to you.

 

First, let me explain that the Community Foundation of PEI is a volunteer driven, registered, non-profit, charitable organization. Our mission is to improve quality of life on PEI through the creation and building of endowment funds that will help ensure vital futures for Island communities. 


The Foundation provides a means for individuals, families, community groups, corporations, and private foundations to support philanthropic activities which invest in sustainable solutions to community needs. We connect people who care with causes that matter.


As you will see from the enclosed information, Funds can be in any amount and built with a single contribution or over time... I am enclosing some information about our activities. 


I would welcome an opportunity to explore further with you how we could recognize and honour your mother’s contribution to Prince Edward Island...  


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

Director
