Official agenda for the April 8, 2011 Cemetery Workshop held at The Best Western Hotel, Charlottetown. Document includes the Community Foundation of PEI mission statement and board roster listing Don Glendenning as a board member. The workshop featured remarks from President John Robinson, a panel discussion on cemetery operations with speakers Jack Sorensen (Tryon Community Cemetery), Tom Deblois (Sherwood Cemetery Company), and George Wright (Old Protestant Burial Ground), plus sessions on fundraising strategies led by Jodi Swan and investment strategies led by Rogers Bell. Sponsored by the PEI Funeral Directors and Embalmers Association.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island is a volunteer driven, registered, non-profit, charitable organization. Our mission is to improve the quality of life on PEI through the creation and building of endowment trust funds that will enhance life for all residents.

TheFoundation provides an established means for individuals, families, community groups, corporations, and private foundations to support philanthropic activities which invest in sustainable solutions to community needs. We connect caring people and worthy causes through endowment building.

TheFoundation strives to be aware of the diverse needs present on PEI by being attentive to existing groups and the programs they have developed, as well as the ones they hope to develop. We also look to community groups and a distinguished volunteer Board for direction.

The Foundation honours the vision and wisdom of donors and guides disbursements according to their wishes. We facilitate and simplify donor-directed giving and help establish endowments in perpetuity. We manage trust funds with diligence and transparency and adhere to conventional reporting practices.

The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island can assure all donors that their innovative contributions will make a lasting impact and help safeguard vital futures for Islanders.

Thank you to all for coming here today to convene this workshop for cemetery trustees.

The purpose of this meeting is to strengthen our partnerships with cemetery trustees, provide support for trustee development and make trustees aware of the services The Community Foundation of PEI can provide.

During the course of the meeting, we will be discussing a variety of topics dealing with the stewardship of the Island’s cemeteries and how CFPEI may be of assistance. With the expert advice of our presenters, together we can ensure that our cemeteries remain beautiful and well taken care of for many years to come.



Thank you to the PEI Funeral Directors and Embalmers Association for their generous support in funding this workshop. Without the help of Faye Doucette, John MacIsaac and the rest of the board; today would not have been possible.
