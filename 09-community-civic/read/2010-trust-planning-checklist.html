Draft template (December 17, 2009) for donor planning conversations covering objectives, interests (undesignated, memorial, religion, heritage, environment, education), beneficiary organizations, fund options, and capacity planning. Includes calculation guidance: $25,000 investment provides $1,000 annual grant in perpetuity; $500/year grant requires $12,500 investment. Educational tool designed to guide donors in structuring planned gifts to CFPEI aligned with personal charitable priorities.
