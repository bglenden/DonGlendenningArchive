CFPEI governance document (appears to be February 11, undated year) listing complete board structure. Don listed as "Past President" (62 Queen Elizabeth Drive, Charlottetown, C1A 3A9). Board includes President John Robinson, VP John Ives, Treasurer David Campbell, Secretary Margaret Ching. Director positions held by Alfred Fraser, Tilmon Gallant, Bruce Lewis, Doug MacDougall, Anne Nicholson, Elizabeth Noonan, George Wright. Shows Don's leadership transition and ongoing involvement via committees including "New Initiatives" chaired by Don, with Tilmon Gallant and Elizabeth Noonan as members. Lists volunteer advisors including Don Anderson, Donald Arsenault, Peggy Coady, Justice Norman Carruthers, and numerous others reflecting 18-year tenure built relationships.
