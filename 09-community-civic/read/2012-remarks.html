Speaking remarks delivered at July 4, 2012 US Citizens' Reception in Summerside, likely by Don Glendenning. Extends CFPEI greetings, recognizes John Robinson and other Directors, reflects on deep historical connections between PEI and United States (emigration patterns to Boston States, descendants returning). Notes that three daily trains once connected PEI to USA, many stayed and had descendants. Thanks Mayor and Summerside for hospitality and cross-border friendship. Invites continued engagement with CFPEI. Reflects Don's interest in historical connections and appreciation for cultural/family ties across borders.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

US citizens’ Reception

Summerside, PEI

July 4, 2012


Your worship, Honoured Guests,

 Ladies and Gentlemen, Friends all


Bring greetings from CFPEI

Recognize John Robinson and other Directors


While you celebrate your birthday

We celebrate the many linkages between our peoples

Islanders can tell stories of relatives heading for the Boston States

Woods industry, construction retail, manufacturing

Three times trains a day one could board a train

leaving PEI and make connections to the USA

Many stayed, married and now their descendants return


 As PEI’s community Foundation

Pleased to add our welcome

To recognize folk from earlier receptions

Thanks the many US citizens who support community activities

Recognize those who use our services

Would be happy to talk to others who are interested


Thank the Mayor and city of Summerside

Energy and hospitality are well known

A long cross boarder friendship


Enjoy the day

Enjoy the remainder of your stay on PEI

When the time comes, safe journey home.
