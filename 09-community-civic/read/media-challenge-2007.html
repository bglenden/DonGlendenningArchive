Foundation Issues Challenge to Souris Community


The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island has challenged the residents of the Souris area to increase the existing Souris Fund; the fund now stands at $17,500. The Foundation will make grants to charitable organizations in the Souris area in the amount of $1,000 each year for the next five years on condition that the endowment be increased by $5,000 per year for the same period.  If the challenge is met, the fund should warrant grants of $1,800 per year in perpetuity.


An interim committee made up of Margaret Ching, a current Director of the Foundation, Carol Fraser, Thelma Macdonald and JoAnne Chisholm has been established to advise the Foundation on matters related to the Fund.


The Souris Fund is one of 35 endowments administered by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island and was established by an anonymous donation.  Grants from the Fund  may be used to support any charitable purpose in the Souris area.
