Announcement



Education and preparedness

The challenges of young people on PEI will be the subject of a workshop on Tuesday, March 25, 2014 in Wellington, PEI.. The workshop will lead off with a review of a recent study of issues important to youth commissioned by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  Following a presentation by Steve McQuaid, Vice President of the Foundation and Katherine MacDonald, Researcher, participants will engage in a round-table discussion about the findings, especially those related to issues of “preparedness”.  The workshop, sponsored by Education 20/20, is open to the public and will be held in Centre Goeland, Wellington, PEI starting at 7:00 p.m.  Storm date March 26, 2014


For further information visit us at www.education2020.ca or phone 892-2060
