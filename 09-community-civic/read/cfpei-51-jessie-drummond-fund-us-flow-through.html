Community Foundation News

No. 51 September 6, 2006



Jessie Drummond Fund


We are pleased to announce the establishment of the Jessie Drummond Public Speaking Award Fund. The purpose of the Fund established by her son, Stuart Drummond, is to provide an award annually to recognize the contestant achieving the designation of “Best Overall First Time Speaker “at The PEI United Church Youth Public Speaking Competition.

 

Jessie Drummond was a schoolteacher who held the view that public speaking was one of the most important skills that children could develop.  The Award recognizes that view and her long and dedicated commitment to the UCW (United Church Women).

Flow-through funds from US Citizens


In response to recent questions, here is a recap on accessing the IRS charitable number (#30-0050801) through the Foundation.


  1. Donor makes contribution in either US or Canadian Funds. Cheques should be made out to CFPEI.

  2. Donor informs CFPEI of intended charitable organization at time of donation.

  3. CFPEI issues a receipt in US dollars using the rate of exchange on the day of conversion.

  4. CFPEI deposits the contribution in Canadian dollars.

  5. CFPEI deducts an administration fee (one and one-half percent of contribution with a minimum of $15.00) and issues a cheque for remainder to the charitable organization designated by the donor.
