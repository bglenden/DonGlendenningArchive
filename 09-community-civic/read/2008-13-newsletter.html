COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


SD- 2008-13

Date: February 27, 2008

Prepared by: Don Glendenning


To: Board


Subject: Electronic Newsletter


On June 4, 2004 we issued our first one-page electronic newsletter; a couple of weeks ago, we issued No. 81.  Our current distribution includes 141 people.  In addition, we issue a community reporter usually annually and around June 1.  It is time for a review:


It is time to ask ourselves a number whether or not we should continue the initiative:


What purpose does it serve?

Who should the focus be?

Who constitutes the target readership?

What should be the nature of the content?

How often should it be published?

Should it include photos?  If so, of what?

How often should it be published?

Should it carry more statistics?

If we need one, should the focus change in view of our emphasis on development?


If we decide to maintain the Newsletter, I would like to see a new Editor with a fresh point of view.
