Speaking notes from September 20, 2011 Summerside Rotary Club presentation introducing YIP program. Recognizes former directors Paul Schurman, Mary Wilcox, Dave Anderson. Details CFPEI directors including John Robinson, Tom Davies, Rod Nicholson, Stanley Macdonald, Jim Travers, Steve McQuaid, Rogers Bell, Tracey Laughlin, Andrew Daggett, Carol Fraser, Joyce Gaudet. Reports approximately $70,000 annual grants (exceeding $1,000,000 cumulatively since inception), specific scholarships and grants to regional institutions. Describes YIP youth councils as school-based clubs meeting monthly to assess local youth needs and allocate grants. Seeks Summerside Rotary sponsorship for YIP expansion to the area.
