January 2006 proposal for Organization Sustainability Centre/Program to strengthen NGO governance and management. Comprehensive program with six components: problem definition (critical emergency assistance up to $1K), organizational assessment ($2.5K for capacity evaluation), strategic planning support, volunteer recruitment/development, technology/information management, and skills development workshops. Targets arts and charitable organizations existing 3+ years, excluding educational institutions and professional organizations. Reflects CFPEI's sophisticated understanding of nonprofit sector challenges and commitment to building sustainable organizational infrastructure in PEI communities.

COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


Organization Sustainability Centre/Program

Centre for Sustainability


Background



Objectives


To assist NGOs/ Charitable/arts and cultural organizations to strengthen governance and management practices through:

Assess their strengths, weaknesses, challenges and opportunities

Increase capacity to plan and implement 

Improve the handling of information through the application of technology

Partnerships and networking

Volunteer recruitment and development

Effective communication


Clientele


NGO/Charitable Sector




Program Components


The Centre will provide practical, hands-on assistance including on-site visits.


A Problem Definition


Provides technical assistance to organizations to help define and address critical, emergency organizational issues that are having an immediate and adverse effect on the organization


Up to $ 1,000 to hire a consultant



B Organizational Assessment


Provides financial support for organizations to obtain a clear picture of their overall health and capacity as a basis for future planning


Up to $2,500

Identify and develop a cadre of local volunteer expertise to work with selected organizations



C Planning


Technical and financial assistance to organizations that have completed an organizational assessment in order to engage a consultant to prepare strategic, marketing, communications, business, fund development, governance and human resource development plans.



D Volunteer Recruitment and Development


To assist organizations to develop and implement a strategy for recruiting, developing, retaining and recognizing volunteers.


4 Information handling through the use of technology


To assist organizations to analyze information needs, to select appropriate technology to assist in its management and to gain the required skills in order to use the technology effectively..


5 Networking


To increase opportunities and skills in developing partnerships and networking.



6 Skills development


Workshops

Round tables

Technical up-dating


Delivery Components


Technical assistance


Volunteer professionals




Eligibility


Arts or charitable organizations that have been in existence for at least three years.  CCRA number?


Not eligible – schools, colleges, universities, professional organizations


Funding not available for on-going operation, board retreats, mentoring individual artists, retroactive projects, capital projects, facility planning,  projects already receiving funding under other organizational development grants
