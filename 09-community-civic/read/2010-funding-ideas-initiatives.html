Strategic brainstorm document outlining five major funding initiatives for CFPEI: charitable/NGO facilities center, 3-5 year organizational development program, endowment challenge grants, Centre for Family Philanthropy, and enhanced communications. Reflects Don's visionary leadership in identifying ways to strengthen the provincial charitable sector beyond traditional grantmaking. Shows his commitment to capacity-building for nonprofit organizations and systematic approaches to sector development.

FUNDING IDEAS

\

Introduction;


A number of ideas have bee or are floating around; it may be time for an overall view.


Ideas:

Facilities: A centre for charitable/NGO agencies to include such things as: some offices, bookable meeting rooms, administrative and secretarial services, state-of-the-art IT, 

Development:  3-5 year developmnt initiative parallel to the IAHSP which was designed to strengthen the business/ administrative side of organizations. To include such things as: Board development, IT capacity building, planning and budgeting, financial analysis, etc.  It would be a 3-5 year plan with money flowing after the first year if objectives are met.

Endowment building: Challenge grants for community funds endowment building 

Centre for Family Philanthropy -  see Calgary, fre consultation, no obligation to establish fund at CFPEI

Communications, electronic newsletter, annual conference

IT applications: technical assistance, advice selection, basic training,
