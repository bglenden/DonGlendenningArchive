Youth in Philanthropy 


A Youth in Philanthropy (YIP) program is in the making at the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  The program is intended for young people between 15 and 18 years and will help them gain awareness of needs in their communities and to gain experience in dealing with philanthropic matters.


The program will be offered through schools and youth organizations and will include, monthly meetings to study philanthropic needs the local community, visits to local organizations, soliciting and assessing proposals for funds, recommending grants and establishing an endowment.


Each group will operate independently, although with an adult advisor, and will, in the first year, be allocated $1,000 to be given to one or more groups in their local community, up to $500 as a matching grant towards an endowment and a small amount of money to offset local administrative costs.


The Foundation has already broached the idea informally with schools and youth group leaders and there is an interest. The foundation is also looking for donors and sponsors.  The program will commence at the beginning of the school year in 2011.
