COMMUNIITY FOUNDATION REPORTS SOLID INVESTMENT RETURNS


The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island wrapped up another successful year.  At the annual meeting, the President, Norman Carruthers, reported the addition of 10 new endowments in 2006 and a return on investment of over 10 percent.  The total Fair Market Value of the Consolidated Trust fund at year end was $866,866.80.


One of the major events held in 2006 was a reception on July 4 for American Summer Islanders.  The reception, which was a great success, provided an opportunity for the Foundation to thank America summer islanders for their friendship and contributions to the Island.  It also enabled people to meet one another and establish new friendships.


Other highlights during 2006 include:


The formation of the David A. Mackay fund which is our first endowment in the health field

The establishment of an endowment by an American summer Islander

The commencement of a Summer Islanders Legacy Fund

The launching of a Living Memorial Trust 

A fund for the purchase of books for Island schools, and

A rate of return on investments in the double digits.


The President Directors, Volunteers, Our Office Administrator, Committee Members, Donors and Friends for their efforts and welcomed the newest Directors, Mariie Saloumon-Dunne of Summerside and JoAnne Chisholm of Souris
