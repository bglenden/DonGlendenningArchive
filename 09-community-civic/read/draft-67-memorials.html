Draft newsletter (2007) listing 12 individuals honored through memorial contributions to CFPEI during 2006. Names include Lewis MacMillan, Debbie MacMillan-Murray, Eric Murray, and others from PEI community. Brief format emphasizes the Foundation's role in channeling remembrance into lasting charitable legacy. Reflects Glendenning's understanding of death as opportunity for community connection and perpetual giving in honor of the deceased.
