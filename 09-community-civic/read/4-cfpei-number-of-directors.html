DIRECTORS

Proposal: To increase the number of Directors of the community foundation of |Prince Edward Island  from 12 to 15. 


Background:

Since its establishment in 1993, CFPEI has operated with a Board of 12 Directors.  From time to time, the suggestion has been made that the number be increased.  

Other Foundations:

An internet search of other Canadian community foundations reveals the following numbers of Directors:

Fredericton, NB 15

Nfld 14

Fundy, NB 11

Nova Scotia,  NS 8

NS RuraL, NS 9

St. John, NB 12

Northern Carleton, NB 12

Victoria, BC 12

Sunshine Coast, BC 12

Calgary, AB 12

Winnipeg, MB 12

Kitchener-Waterloo, ON 12
