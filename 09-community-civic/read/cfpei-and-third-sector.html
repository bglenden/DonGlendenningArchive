Don's draft outline on CFPEI's role within PEI's third sector. The document articulates how the foundation contributes to quality of life, retains provincial uniqueness, attracts business, and retains population by filling service gaps. Don emphasizes CFPEI's unique position as the only organization with broad community interest, enabling long-term strategic direction and permanence beyond other organizations. He highlights the foundation's capacity to modify interventions through broadly-based directors, absorb small trusts, add value to donations, and create lasting impact. The work reflects Don's vision for institutional stability and community leadership.
