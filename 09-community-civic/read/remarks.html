Speaking notes for Don Glendenning before and after the Beyond Measure screening. Pre-screening remarks introduce Education 20/20's mission as a charitable, unaligned organization focused on education not merely schooling, offering monthly newsletters, school awards, workshops, and community convening. Don articulates personal conviction: education is the most important issue; government has narrow schooling-focused view while he advocates broader educational transformation. Notes his belief that citizens have lost control of schools and dissatisfaction is widespread. Post-screening remarks facilitate discussion based on the film and transition attendees to Haviland Club for continued dialogue. Demonstrates Don's philosophical grounding and facilitation skills.

Why I’m here:

  • Personal comments; although said about PEI could be said about most provinces
