Personal letter from Don Glendenning (Director, CFPEI) to David McKenna proposing an endowment fund with environmental focus. Shows Don's active fundraising and donor relations work: he drafts customized agreements, explains Foundation options, references recent successes (David A. MacKay Fund, 26 existing endowments totaling $700,000+), and offers to discuss further. Demonstrates Don's personal, direct approach to donor engagement and the Foundation's growth trajectory in 2006.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

Mr. David McKenna


Dear David:


Based on a very brief conversation yesterday about endowment funds at the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island, I decided to draft an agreement on the establishment of a McKenna Fund for your consideration.  This is a proforma agreement and contains our standard clauses.  Specific clauses are added, in the agreement or in an appendix if needed, to more adequately reflect the donor’s interests.


As you know, community foundations offer a wide range of possibilities; the attached draft is for an interest fund.  The draft agreement identifies environmental issues as the focus of the proposed fund, however, this can be easily changed to suit another set of donor interests.  The environment is a sufficiently broad field to include scholarships or grants to organizations working in the field.  Had we had a bit more time, we could have defined the donor interests more fully.


I am also enclosing some background information about the Foundation; I would call your attention especially to the list of funds given on page two? of the Community Reporter.    Since the Newsletter was published, our investment portfolio has grown to more than $700,000 in some 26 endowments with our most recent being the David A Mackay Fund focused on immediate response services in the case of medical emergencies.


I would be pleased, David, to discuss the matter further with you.  I am at my cottage in Earnscliffe, 651-2186, should you want to phone.  On the other hand I am in town frequently and would be happy to schedule a meeting at a convenient time.


Thanks for you interest.  Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have further questions.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

Director.
