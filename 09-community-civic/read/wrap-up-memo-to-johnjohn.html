Memo from Don to President John Robinson discussing scholarship administration improvements, referencing discussions with Gordon MacKay (UPEI), Paul Schurman, and Dr. Bonnell. Recommends CFPEI take leadership role in handling all scholarship applications, develop universal application forms, and review grant disbursement schedules. Illustrates Don's continued strategic input after presidency and focus on operational excellence and centralized administration.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

John


Carver Scholarship



As you know, we have b been reviewing our agreements and procedures, especially, procedures for handling scholarships.  As part of the process, discussions were held with Gordon MacKay, UPEI, Paul Schurman and Dr. Bonnell.  The outcome is that CFPEI can now take the leadership role in dealing with applications for all our scholarships.  The file contains the relevant correspondence especially as it relates to the Bonnell and Carver Scholarships.


I believe that the following action should now be taken:

Public information about the Caver scholars requires an address change before distribution.

I recommend that a “universal” application form be developed for all our scholarships.  One has been developed but it may need revision.

The schedule for handling grants should be reviewed; some actions are required soon.













Cc: Anne, Marla, Bob Ramsay, Don
