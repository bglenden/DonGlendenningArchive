A letter from Don Glendenning to the Eastern School District confirming establishment of the Hesta MacDonald library book endowment. Reflects Don's commitment to ensuring educational access and his role in implementing endowment policies.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

Dr. Sandy MacDonald

Eastern School District


Dear Dr. MacDonald:


I am pleased to confirm an earlier telephone conversation and that an endowment has been established at the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island for the purchase of library books for schools in the Eastern School District.


The Belfast Consolidated School has been selected for the current year with Queen Charlotte and Charlottetown Rural High School being eligible in future years on a rotating basis.


We will be in touch with the Belfast Consolidated School at an early date with the details.


We are delighted with this initiative and to continuing our service to the educational community of Prince Edward Island.


Sincerely



Don glendenning

Director
