September 16, 2009


COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND

Development - Some points for discussion on September 24


Definition: Building CFPEI endowments


Status:

  • No significant change since last report

  • Norman and Lou Ann are leading an initiative to ask Province to change legislation to allow CFPEI to be a trustee of trusts. If approved, the change will give us a significant new tool when dealing with existing trusts.

  • Next step on Cotton funds postponed pending outcome of discussions on Carver and Schurman


Target population:

  1. People we know, the friends, acquaintances, neighbors, associates and relatives of the Directors, staff, supporters, and volunteers.

  2. Successful business people living on PEI

  3. Prominent families and individuals

  4. Celebrities

  5. Directors

  6. Past directors

  7. Islanders Away

  8. Young Islanders whether living on PEI or away

  9. Current donors

  10. Existing external trusts with a high probability of becoming CFPEI endowments (Await outcome of petition for changes to Act)


Possible Development themes


  • environment

  • Reading

  • Literacy

  • Capacity Building

  • Health

  • Canadian History


Possible Special initiatives:

  • Life insurance as a mechanism for people to build fund in that the premiums are tax deductible

  • Starter funds such as $1,000 a year.


Donor instruments

  • Cash

  • Property

  • Art

  • Stocks

  • Insurance
