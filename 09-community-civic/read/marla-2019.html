A personal thank-you letter from Don to Marla, acknowledging her years of service as a volunteer and employee of the Community Foundation of PEI. The letter reflects their warm relationship and Don's appreciation for her contributions to the Foundation's success story.
Community Foundation & Civic Life



62 Queen Elizabeth Drive

Charlottetown,  PE

C1A 3A9


March 30, 2019


Dear Marla:

While I did see the recent advertisement for an accountant at CFPEI, I did not know until you phoned that the changes included your departure,  While I do not know, nor actually need to know, the details, I want to thank you and acknowledge your years of service and many contributions to the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island. 

I remember well  the day, although not the date, that I stopped at your door to see if you were interested in volunteering with CFPEI.  Fortunately for us, you were interested; the result was  long term relationship both as a volunteer and later employee – and now as a friend.  It must be rewarding, Marla, to have been an integral part of such a success story.  I thank you for your role in making it happen.

I admire your quick turn of mind, thank you for your contributions and look forward to continued friendship.  I know that you are out of Province for a few days but look forward to an early coffee when you are back.

Sincerely


Don Glendenning
