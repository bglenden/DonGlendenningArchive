Executive meeting notes addressing board restructuring to position CFPEI as the most significant player in PEI's third sector. Outlines objectives: increase endowment-building efforts, establish community initiatives committee, improve organizational effectiveness through structured board/committee meetings and orientation, define clear member expectations. Proposes committee reforms: establish recruitment committee with external chair, limit appointments to three-year terms, include community members on all committees except Executive, transition from administrative focus to policy-making focus, and enhance director participation in decision-making.


BOARD RESTRUCTURING




Goal: to position CFPEI as the most significant player in the third sector of PEI


Objectives:

Increase our efforts in building endowments/assets

Confirm role of development committee as increasing endowments only 

Add community members to the committee

Add someone with fundraising experience to the committee

Clearly define expectations of members


Increase our visibility and service through self supporting projects

Establish a community Initiatives committee

Invite broadly representative

Assign/recruit members based on willingness to work on projects Assign operational funding and project funding to Community initiatives committee



Increase our effectiveness of CFPEI as an organization

A Board

Set schedule for Board and committee meetings and adhere to it

Provide documentation for agenda items  

Keep Board fully informed

Allow Board to play a larger role in setting policies and decision making

Provide a structured orientation to all new Board and Committee members

Have a discussion about these items at the next Board meeting

Develop a detailed annual workplan

B Committees should serve as both a significant source and important training ground for new Directors:

Establish a board recruitment committee with an external chair

All appointments of Committee members are to be submitted to the Board for approval

Every committee, except the Executive committee, should have community members. 

Appointments will be for a single term of three years

Retiring committee members may be appointed to a different committee 

Terms should be acknowledged in writing.  

Eliminate audit and administration committee and place responsibility with Executive

Executive committee should take a higher profile in dealing with administrative matters.
