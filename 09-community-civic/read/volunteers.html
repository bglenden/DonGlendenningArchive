A comprehensive draft volunteer opportunities document dated May 19, 2005, listing 16 volunteer positions for CFPEI including photographer, website coordinator, IT support, comptroller, media liaison, fund development, and outreach roles targeting specific constituencies (Summer Islanders, Islanders Away, Seniors, Family reunions). Shows Don's systematic approach to building organizational capacity through volunteer engagement.

Draft TIME \@ "MMMM d, yyyy" May 19, 2005


COMMUNITY FOUNDATION F PRINCE EDSWARD ISLAND


Volunteer Opportunities



Photographer – to take photos as required.


Website coordinator – ensure that our website provides appropriate and recent information and reflects the CFPEI image


IT User Support - to maintain the hardware and software aspect of the CFPEI's computer systems including installation of new software, resolution of operating issues, debugging, system optimizing, virus control management, and recommendations re new hardware and software.

 

Comptroller - to organize and implement an integrated financial, record keeping, contact management, and investments information system.


Media liaison - to liaise with media in including the preparation of releases, provision of background information, invitations to photo ops, arranging press conferences, etc


Publications - oversee and coordinate the preparation and revision of printed materials


Proposal writing - to identify, organize, assist in the preparation of proposals for submission to potential funders


Fund development - serve as a coordinator and resource to individuals or organizations wishing to establish or enlarge an endowment fund


Donor Liaison -  Organize, maintain and monitor donor support services


Events Coordinator – provide leadership and coordination for such events as recognitions, grantmaking, and information of information.


Agency Liaison – maintain contact with actual and potential agencies interested in placing funds with CFPEI.


Workshop Organizer – to identify, organize and coordinate workshops of interest to charitable organizations and their supporters.


Summer Islanders – to inform summer Islanders of philanthropic opportunities and to encourage their support.


Islanders Away – to inform Islanders Away of philanthropic opportunities and encourage their support.


Seniors – to make seniors aware of philanthropic opportunities and encourage their support.


Publications/brochures – to plan and coordinate the preparation, revision and distribution of printed materials.


Web site coordinator – to ensure that the foundation’s website provides recent and appropriate information and reflects CFPEI’s image.


Family reunions – to contact organizers of family reunions to provide information about CFPEI and encourage philanthropic giving through CFPEI.


Estate planners – to arrange face-to-face meetings with estate planners to inform them of our services and encourage them to inform others.


Islanders’ Gathering – to promote the foundation to attendees of the annual Islanders picnic.


Speakers group – to solicit speaking opportunities for directors with Island organizations.
