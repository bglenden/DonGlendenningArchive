I, _______________ _____________(the Donor), hereby agree to transfer to the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island (the Foundation) my personal collection of cultural objects for the Foundation’s sole use or disposition, under the conditions described below:

 

  1. The Foundation will issue the Donor with charitable tax receipts.


  1. The maintenance, sale or other disposition of the collection is at the sole discretion of the Foundation, as is the use of any return arising therefrom.


  1. Costs arising from creating an inventory, transporting objects, insurance, appraisal or otherwise caring for the collection prior to its disposition, incurred directly by The Foundation shall be a direct charge against the proceeds realized from their disposition.
