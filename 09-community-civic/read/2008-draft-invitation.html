INVITATION


Dear American Neighbours,


We, on behalf of the US Citizens Day Committee of the Community Foundation of PEI, would like to invite you and your friends and family to join us for a reception in celebration of our close kinship to American friends and family.


Where: The Culinary Institute of Canada

4 Sydney Street

Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island


When: 4:00 – 6:00 PM - Wednesday, July 4, 2008


What: Greetings, Introductions, Music

Gourmet refreshments

Cash bar


The Community Foundation of PEI recognizes the valuable contribution made by all summer residents of PEI. This is an opportunity to celebrate our shared history and vision for the future of our beautiful island.


Come and bring some friends.
