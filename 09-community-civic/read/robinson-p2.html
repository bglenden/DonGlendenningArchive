This is the concluding page of a nomination letter for John Robinson for the Governor General's Award for a smart and caring Canadian. Don praises Robinson's leadership of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island, noting his role in growing foundation assets from under one to over seven million dollars. Don highlights Robinson's commitment to community foundations, his status as a respected community member, world traveler, gracious host, and good friend, positioning him as embodying the concept of a smart and caring community.




John is currently leading the Foundation efforts in the area of development; he sees the Foundation as a key component of the provinces infrastructure, a component committed to building a pool of funds to be available in perpetuity for the betterment of the Island community – and he is having  considerable success.  Under his direction, and thanks to his energy and leadership, Foundation assets grew from under one to over seven million dollars – and he has visions for continuing growth.

In addition to the above, John has an unusually high commitment to the cause of community foundations, is a fine person, a respected member of the community, a world traveler, a gracious host, a beloved father and grandfather, and a good friend.  I can think of no better person to epitomize the concept of a smart and caring community

Sincerely


Donald Glendenning C.M.

President
