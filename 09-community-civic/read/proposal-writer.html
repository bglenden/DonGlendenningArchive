Proposal for a Development Specialist position at CFPEI aimed at increasing off-island funding access for PEI nonprofit organizations. Identifies problem: smaller NGOs lack capacity and resources for proposal development and grant prospecting. Proposes components including a resource centre, trained staff for database searches, referral services, professional proposal writer, and technical support. Addresses budget considerations including staffing model and potential cost recovery options.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND



DATE: December 6, 2010


PROJECT TITLE: Development Specialist


CONCEPT: Increase funding available to third sector organizations in PEI by providing technical support in their efforts to access off-island financial support devoted to the task.  

Support to include 

access to a well-equipped resource centre,

prospecting, 

proposal preparation CFPEI develop the  capacity to prepare proposals, to provide that service to other organizations in the third sectorTo make available to the third sector specialized technical assistance in the identification of grant and project money and the preparation of proposals


BACKGROUND:


NGO’s on PEI do not receive a fair share of grants and relevant project money.  Discussions with the sector, reveal the following reasons: 


The focus of smaller organizations is on the delivery of services; proposal development while desirable is lost in the need to deliver services not many proposals get written.

Few NGO’s have the professional staff with  expertise in the field and they are not able to afford help of find volunteers with the interest and expertise

Electronic access to relevant databases is expensive and beyond the reach of most organizations for the occasional search. 


COMPONENTS:


CFPEI proposes to develop a capacity with the following elements:


A resource centre with easy access to relevant print and electronic resources.

A staff person trained in search procedures and techniques

Referral service of potential relevant leads

A writer with both training and experience in preparing and presenting proposals for project and grant funding.

Technical support in the actual preparation of supporting documents

Advice on actual proposal presentation





BUDGET NOTES:


Resource centre to include working and meeting area, furniture, IT, representative periodicals, access to relevant databases, 

Combination of full time and contract staff 

Access to quality graphics and production services

Propose some cost recovery in the form of a charge for each proposal presented or a charge for each successful proposal, or???l
