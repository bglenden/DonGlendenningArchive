Special No. 2 March 01, 2006



SCHOLARSHIPS AVAILABLE


The following scholarships are available through your Community Foundation:


ORIN CARVER SCHOLARSHIP – open to high a school graduates in the top 25% of the class and who exhibit a level of excellence and leadership in either athletics, arts and/or community service and who are accepted into a post-secondary program at UPEI or Holland College.


ARCHITECTS ASSOCIATION OF P.E.I. SCHOLARSHIP – open to a Prince Edward Island student who graduated from a PEI High School and is accepted into a recognized architectural program.


PHILLIPS SCHOLARSHIP – available to a high school graduate from the Western School District with a physical disability and is open for study in any field.


SUMMERSIDE-NATICK HOCKEY SCHOLARSHIP – available to a high school graduate who has played within the Summerside Minor Hockey Association and is open for study in any field.


RUBY AND LORNE BONNELL SCHOLARSHIP – available to a graduate of UPEI enrolling in graduate studies in the pure sciences at a Canadian university.

JOAN AULD SCHOLARSHIP – available to a high school graduate undertaking full time studies in a craft related field at a recognized institution of applied art, craft and design.

G. GORDEN LORD SCHOLARSHIP FUND– although administered by CFPEI, applications should be sent to the Grand Secretary. Grand Lodge of Prince Edward Island, P O Box 337, Charlottetown, PE  Canada, C1A 7K7 Phone:  902-894-3443
