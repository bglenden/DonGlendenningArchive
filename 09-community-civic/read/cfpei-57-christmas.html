This December 20, 2006 issue is brief, featuring a correction to the previous newsletter's photo caption and a holiday message from Don and CFPEI. The message thanks the foundation family for a successful year, acknowledges islanders' gifts of time and talent, extends best wishes for health and peace, and closes with a paraphrased Gandhi quote: "We must be the change we wish to see in the world." The brief holiday message reflects Don's thoughtful leadership voice and his connection of community philanthropy to broader principles of social change and personal responsibility.

‘Tis the Season


On behalf of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island, we offer:

  • Congratulations to the Found-ation family on a successful year,

  • Thanks to all Islanders for their gifts of time and talent for the betterment of our Island community,

  • Best wishes for good health and peace in the coming year,

  • An opportunity to enhance our Island community. To paraphrase Mahatma Gandhi:


We must be the change

we wish to see in the world
