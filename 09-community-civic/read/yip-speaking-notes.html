General speaking notes on Youth in Philanthropy program (undated, likely 2010-2011). Outlines CFPEI background (established 1993, 171 Canadian chapters), mission to build endowments and make grants allowing charitable legacy preservation. Identifies needs including finding/retaining trustees and addressing youth volunteerism gaps. YIP focuses on local communities with youth aged 15-18, typically school-based. Youth Advisory Councils self-select, meet monthly, identify youth needs, receive guidance and funds, expected to establish endowments. Benefits include community learning, leadership development, parliamentary procedure, proposal review/assessment skills. Startup costs $2,500 increasing to $3,000-$4,000 by year three per YAC.
