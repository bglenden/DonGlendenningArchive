Don's prepared opening remarks introducing the transition working group's conceptual framework. Traces issue origins to CFPEI 2013 youth survey identifying "preparedness gap" and documents subsequent conversations with students, parents, grandparents, and employers. Presents framework using metaphor of "road of life" with hurdles and helpers, positioning the problem from student perspective rather than institutional perspective. Identifies specific student predicaments: meeting institutional requirements but not program requirements, graduating without necessary credits, having good marks but not diploma, older student status. The remarks show Don's ability to translate abstract policy problems into concrete human narratives.
