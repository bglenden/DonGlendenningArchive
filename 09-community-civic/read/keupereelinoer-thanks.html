A thank-you letter from Don to Elinore Keuper for her support in establishing the Living Memorial Trust honoring her husband Charles. Don expresses appreciation for their vision clarity and reflects on his learning about living memorials through their discussions, while wishing her well on a planned Arizona visit.


December 16, 2006



Mrs. Elinore Keuper

32 Pownal St.

Charlottetown, PE C1A 3V9



Dear Mrs. Keuper:

Living Memorial Trust



Thank you for supporting the establishment of a Living Memorial Trust with the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  It is a fitting tribute to Charles’ memory.  In addition, it will continue to enhance Prince Edward Island for the benefit of future Islanders and visitors alike.


I enjoyed our discussions of the proposed fund and commend you and John for the clarity of your vision.  The process allowed me to learn more about living memorials.


I am enclosing receipts for the donations and will follow up with John early in the new year on some administrative details including the development of a  brochure and other materials.


In the meantime, enjoy your visit to Arizona and have a great family Christmas. 


Warmest personal regards



Don Glendenning

Director
