Roma Dinner – Norman and Donnie signed a contract on February 18.  The cost to us will be $40.00 which includes them pouring a glass of our “donated” wine.  Further drinks must be purchased at the bar.  The PA system will be our responsibility.  We may have to pay PST.  We will have to negotiate the availability of mussels with the chef.  Cost and tax receipts are to be determined.  Entertainment possibilities were discussed.  Final arrangements will be discussed at the next meeting.

Web site – David C. reported that Nigel Cuthbertson would charge as much as $10,000.00 to manage our website.  He also spoke with Chris Vessey.  Donnie and Marla have set up a new site which would be fine if we can make our own changes to it.  This could be possible to do for as little as $8.00/month.  David, Donnie, Don G, and Marla will discuss this issue further.

Other business – There have as yet been no candidates for the  Wilmot scholarship.
