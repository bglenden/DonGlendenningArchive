Speaking notes for the 6th Annual July 4 Reception in 2011 addressing old and new attendees. Don welcomes guests, explains CFPEI's core mission as a community foundation improving quality of life through endowments and grants. Highlights recent achievements including returning $65,000 in grants, increasing endowment, expanding digital presence (Facebook), and adding Doug Leitch to planning committee. Proposes future evolution toward broader summer islander gatherings at dates beyond July 4, offering opportunities for networking with community leaders and learning about Island life. Invites audience participation in shaping the initiative's future.

Sixth annual July 4 Reception

July 4, 2010

Welcome  - 

Great to see old friends and welcome new friends

Where are you from?

Is this your first time?

Last year, you asked us what we do.  We:

  • Are a  community Foundation
  • Improving the quality of life on PEI
  • Provide a vehicle for individuals and organization to support worthy causes of their choice
  • Build endowments
  • Flow through funds 
  • Bring people together to solve problems


To be more specific:

  • Today’s reception – No. 6
  • We sent you a letter last fall telling you about some of our activities
  • Refer to today’s program; mention Royal scholarship
  • If you are not on our email list, let us know – regular newsletters 
  • Included Jack Sorenson’s presentation on Samuel Holland
  • Updated website
  • Now on Facebook
  • Added Doug Leitch to our planning committee
  • Returned $65,000 in grants to the community
  • Increased our endowment


Thinking about the future:

  • Invite the municipalities to hold July 4 celebrations in the form of greetings, words of welcome and a cake in a number of locations
  • Change this function to a broader gathering of Summer Islanders at a date other than July 4 for the purpose of:
  • Meeting other summer Islanders
  • Meeting Island community leaders
  • Learning more about on some aspect of Island life 
  • Thank
  • Those who made contributions 
  • Those who flow through donations to other organizations
  • Those who set up funds
  • Other  -

I invite you to join the conversation and help shape the future of this venture together:

  • Your thoughts on the idea
  • Time of year
  • Nature of gathering
  • Serve on committee
