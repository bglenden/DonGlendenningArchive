A personal thank-you letter from Don Glendenning (as Past President) to JoAnn McQuaid for her contributions to the Community Foundation. Provides insight into Don's leadership style, his appreciation for collaborative relationships, and his vision for CFPEI's future.








May 1, 2005



JoAnn McQuaid

10 Shamrock Court

Charlottetown,  PE  C1A 7X4


Dear JoAnn:


Just a note to say thank you for your many contributions to the work of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.


I remember well the day that you, Henry Purdy and I had our first conversation about a secondment.   CFPEI is certainly a different organization today.  With your guidance and support, CFPEI has grown and prospered.  While we aren’t “there yet”, we’ve come a long way.  Where we are going and what we need to do to get there are much clearer than during our initial conversation..   


JoAnn, you brought a particular touch to the office.  You made it a place of welcome and, regardless of your personal priorities of the day, you took time to deal with each visitor – including me..  The reception in your honour at Younker and Kelly, across the hall, speaks volumes of your interest in and care for others.


On a personal note, I’ve enjoyed working with you, the many breakfasts, coffees and conversations; Hopefully they will continue although I know that they will be less frequent.


I am impressed by your knowledge of and commitment to the community foundation idea.  As time and opportunity allow, I know that you will continue to be an ambassador and advocate.  I hope also, that after a suitable pause, we will have you back in the fold as a regular volunteer and a Director.


With best wishes and warmest personal regards.


Sincerely




Don Glendenning

Past President
