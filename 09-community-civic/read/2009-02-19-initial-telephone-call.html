Memorandum to file documenting a telephone conversation between Don and Lt. Col. Donnie Walsh regarding venue options for the 2009 US Citizens Day reception. Records discussion of using the Armouries and notes about regimental band availability requiring wage payment, with plans to follow up with Captain DeCoste for detailed arrangements.

Date: February 19, 2009


To: File


From: Glendenning


Subject: US Reception


At the wrap up on the 2008 reception, we agreed to hold one in 2009 but one much less labour intensive.  Since then, we learned that the Culinary Institute would not be available on that day.  


John Robinson wrote a letter to LT. Col Donnie Walsh, OC, about the availability of the Armouries for the occasion.  I talked with him today at the Bank of Commerce, and he is happy to oblige subject to working out details.  As part of the conversation, he indicated that the Regimental Band would only be available provided that we paid the wages involved.


Lt. col Walsh suggested that we discuss the matter in detail with Capt Brian DeCoste (368-0104) Email:  HYPERLINK "mailto:decoste.bc@forces.gc.ca" decoste.bc@forces.gc.ca


I plan to seek an appointment with Captain DeCoste early next week.
