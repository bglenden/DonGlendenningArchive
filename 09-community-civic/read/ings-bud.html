Don's personal letter to Bud Ings regarding establishing a memorial endowment in the name of Connie (likely Connie Ings). Don recalls Connie's significant contributions to PEI, particularly her role in forming the Community Foundation, and proposes creating a permanent endowment to honor her interests in beautification and community service. The letter demonstrates Don's personal relationships with community leaders and his skill at recognizing and memorializing contributions to the Island.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

Bud Ings


It was great to see you recently if only for a few minutes.  That was my first visit to the Marina(?) and I was very impressed with the facilities and the atmosphere – and what a beautiful view over the Montague River..


During our brief conversation, we spoke briefly about Connie; her contributions to the Island were varied and many.  Among them was her contribution to the formation of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island and, as you may recall, that was a subject of many coffees and conversations.  


Our conversation also included a reference to a memorial in Connie’s name.  What I had in mind was an endowment the income from which would be used to make grants in Connie’s name in perpetuity.  As you will see from the enclosed brochures, the Foundation now administers some fifty funds, including memorials, serving a variety of interests.  I am sure that many of Connie’s family and friends would also be supportive of such an initiative.


Connie had many interests; you and your family would be in the best position to chose an area that  best reflects her interests.  I think of her  interest in beautification, the beautiful flowers around your home, but I also thing of concerns for her neighbours and her community as well.  As you will note, the Foundation already offers a number of scholarships; perhaps one in Connie’s name for a student from the Montague area would area would be in order.

.  

I am enclosing some information about th Foundation.  If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact me.  I expect to be in Montague again before the end of the month and will get in touch beforehand with a view to talking further..


Warmest personal regards


Don Glendenning

Director
