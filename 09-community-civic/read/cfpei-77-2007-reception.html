Newsletter No. 77 from December 2, 2007, documents the Foundation's awards reception held at the Arts Guild on November 14, 2007. Total grants of $20,000 were awarded to various scholarship funds and community organizations. Recipients included students receiving Lowell Phillips, Summerside-Natick Hockey, and Orin Carver scholarships. Significant grants went to the David A. MacKay Fund, various school funds, and community organizations including the Hesta MacDonald Legacy Fund and Cumberland-Rocky Point Women's Institute Legacy Fund. The event exemplifies CFPEI's community impact under Don's editorial direction, showcasing the Foundation's diverse grantmaking throughout PEI.
