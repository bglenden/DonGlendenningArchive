Letter to Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island President John Robinson proposing organizational partnership to facilitate PEI Education Group's launch and fundraising. Details plans for incorporation, fund management (operating, program, and endowment), and describes consensus among group members about need for greater public engagement in education. Demonstrates Don's collaborative leadership and strategic planning for education reform initiative.

February 19, 2012


Mr. John Robinson, President

Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island

119-121 Queen St.

Charlottetown, PE  C1A 4B3 


Dear Mr. Robinson:


PEI Education Group

. 

As a result of considerable discussion over the past year, a small group of people with an interest in education, has come together to discuss educational issues.  Members are unanimous in the view that there is a need in PEI, and across the country, for greater public engagement in education at all levels.  The group, known informally as the PEI Education Group, met recently and is beginning the process of forming an organization.  A copy of the discussion paper, still a draft, is attached.


Our plan is to proceed with incorporation and eventually to obtaining a registration number with CRA.  In the interim, we are interested in using the good offices of CFPEI to handle any financial transactions including:


  • Operating funds - cash donations to offset start-up and normal administrative expenses.  
  • Program and project funds – donations and sponsorships in support of program activities such as gathering and disseminating information, convening  public presentations and conversations, workshops, etc
  • Endowment Funds – from which income would be available to support any and all activities of the organization.  


We believe that increasing public engagement in education will have a significant and long term impact on education of Islanders, young and old, and on Island communities.  A linkage with CFPEI would, we believe, would allow an earlier start-up and would also strengthen our hand as we seek funds.  


We also have other partnership ideas but that will be the subject of separate discussions..


Thank you in advance for your consideration.  Should you require additional information do not hesitate to contact me.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

Acting Chair.
