Operational guidelines for a volunteer media relations position at CFPEI (February 2, 2007). The volunteer would handle media releases, photography, interviews, and event coverage reporting to a Volunteer Coordinator. This document illustrates Don's systematic approach to volunteer management and organizational structure at the Foundation.
Community Foundation & Civic Life

February 2, 2007


COMMUNIITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


Volunteers 


Information


This is a volunteer position reporting to Volunteer coordinator but working directly with individual Directors depending on the story.


Qualifications


Demonstrated ability to:

Communicate with a variety of people

Take photos

Write clearly 

Maintain confidentiality

Assume responsibility for own work


Duties


Prepare media releases 

Interview sources

Check facts

Cover CFPEI events

Provide a copy of all media releases for CFPEI files



Typical tasks: 

Prepare and issue release on new Funds

Cover annual meeting

Cover photo ops

Cover awards – scholarships and other awards

Cover activities such as new endowments, summer Islanders activities, annual reception, etc.
