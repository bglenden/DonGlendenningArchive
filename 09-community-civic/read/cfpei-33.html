You too can be a philanthropist


You do not have to be wealthy to leave a financial legacy – but you do have to plan for it.


The above list shows how easy it is to get started. Many of the above Funds started five or more years ago and with an initial contribution of $1,000 or less. The key is to start now. Simply open a Fund and add to it on a regular basis. CFPEI does all the rest. Simple, isn’t it.



What better Christmas gift than a Fund in someone’s name – and add to it in succeeding years. Or how about pooling family gifts into a Family Fund where all contribute and all participate in grant making? And the Fund will keep on giving.
