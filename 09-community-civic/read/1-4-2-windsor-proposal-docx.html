Windsor Proposal


Education 20/20 Inc. was federally incorporated as an independent, broad based, national, not-for-profit education organization, located in Charlottetown, PEI and is governed by a volunteer Board of Directors.  Its vision is a quality education for every Canadian and mission is to support and strengthen a culture of learning for all Canadians.  The organization operates with ten Directors, a network of some fifty people, and a mailing list of about 225. Attached you will find a brief biography of our Directors.  An application for charitable status has been filed with Canada Revenue Agency; in the meantime, The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island  accepts fund and pays invoices on our behalf.
