Speaking notes from January 26, 2012 Montague Rotary Club presentation on CFPEI's Youth in Philanthropy (YIP) initiative by Don Glendenning. Outlines CFPEI background (established 1993, improving quality of life, 171 Canadian community foundations), examples of endowments and grants, and YIP program targeting youth aged 15-18. YIP youth councils self-select, meet monthly, assess community needs especially for youth, make grant decisions, and establish endowments. Annual costs are $2,500 per YIP site with $1,000 direct community distribution and $500 endowment challenge. Presentation invites Rotarians to sponsor YIP expansion.
