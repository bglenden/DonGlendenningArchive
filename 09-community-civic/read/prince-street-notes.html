Detailed evaluation notes on Prince Street Elementary School documenting the factors that led to the school being selected for the 2015 Excellence in Education Award. Lists 30+ salient points about the school's educational approach, including student values framework (PRINCE acronym), school mascot and song, student presentations, community partnerships, inclusive practices, student recognition programs, differentiated instruction, and family engagement. Notes show comprehensive assessment of school's holistic approach to student development and community involvement. School enrolled 260 students and was led by Principal Erin Johnston.
