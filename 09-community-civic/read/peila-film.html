Dated January 18, 2016, this letter thanks Dr. Ron MacDonald and the PEI Literacy Alliance for their $100 sponsorship of the film screening "Most Likely to Succeed." Don expresses delight with both the turnout and the post-screening discussion at the theatre, as well as the facilitated discussion later at the Haviland Club. The letter treats the sponsorship as an invoice, requesting that the Literacy Alliance's cheque be made payable to the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island. This brief note exemplifies Don's graciousness in acknowledging support and his collaborative approach to educational initiatives.



January 18, 2016

Dr. Ron MacDonald, President

PEI Literacy Alliance
PO Box 20107,
Charlottetown, PE C1A 9E3



Dear Ron:


I want to thank you and the Prince Edward Island Literacy Alliance for your financial support for the presentation of the film, Most Likely to Succeed. We were delighted with the turn out and with the discussion in the theatre following the viewing. But I also want to thank you for facilitating the discussion later at the Haviland Club.


Please consider this an invoice for $100 as a sponsor; your cheque should be made payable to The Community Foundation of Prince Edward island, 119-121 Queen Street, Charlottetown, C1A 4B3


Again, thanks for your support.


Regards




Don Glendenning
