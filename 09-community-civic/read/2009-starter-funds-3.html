Strategic proposal document for Community Builders Legacy Fund initiative (circa 2008-2009). Proposes expanding CFPEI's reach to younger donors through legacy building. Details fund characteristics ($10,000 minimum, regular contributions, named/unnamed options, Canadian/US funds), multiple contribution methods, management approaches, and a comprehensive three-year budget ($6,900 total) including mailings, brochures, staff, and promotional activities. Identifies administrative challenges and proposes creating a "Society of Community/Legacy Builders."

COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


COMMUNITY BUILDING FUND


BACKGROUND


CFPEI was incorporated in 1993 and gathers and administers endowments for Islanders and Island organizations.    It is one of 160 such foundations across the country with assets in excess of $1bn all with a focus on the needs of their local communities.  CFPEI is one of the smaller community foundations with 40 funds and assets of $1m.  In retrospect, our focus has been generally people who are now seniors and who may have an interest in establishing a charitable legacy now or in a will..  Arising out of a recent planning exercise came a suggestion to mount an initiative to reach people at an earlier stage of life and people and organizations interested in a long term plan to build a charitable legacy.  This paper outlines such a proposal.


OBJECTIVE


To encourage and support individuals, families, organizations and communities to build a charitable legacy over a span of years.

  


CLIENTELE


Potential participants

Focused on individuals, families, organizations and communities

Provide support to Directors who have identified prospects

Names to be brought forward by Directors

Presentations and solicitations to be coordinated by Chair of Development Committee

Individual Directors invited to participate in presentations

On and off Islanders; US and Canadian accounts


CHARACTERISTICS OF FUNDS


Starter funds:

Endowments not flow-through

Encourage regular monthly or annual contributions

Minimum objective of $10,000

If goal not met, donations will be merged with donors fund of choice

May be named or un-named, anonymous or public, designated or undesignated

May be built in either Canadian or US funds

Contributions cannot be mreturned
