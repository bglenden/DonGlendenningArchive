A January 1, 2009 memo (dated 2009-2) proposing streamlined administrative procedures for CFPEI's Executive. Don advocates shifting administrative burden from Directors to the Executive Director, emphasizing that leadership should focus on planning rather than operational details. He recommends formal agenda development, support documents for all items, and designated responsibility assignments. This demonstrates Don's belief in organizational efficiency, proper delegation, and the importance of leadership maintaining strategic perspective rather than becoming mired in routine matters.


COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


SD- 2009- 2

Date: January 1, 2009

To: Executive

From: Don Glendenning


Subject: Revised administrative procedures


Problem: Disproportionate amount of time on administration


Background:


Review of minutes for 2007 and 2008 reveals


Conclusions:

  1. Directors spend too much time and energy on administrative matters

  2. Our resources allow us to lift some of the administrative load from Directors

  3. Executive should act on routine matters and report to Board

  4. board should deal mostly with tomorrow

  5. Key to the change is the Executive Director

  6. Need to bring the same discipline to administrative matters as we have to financial matters

  7. Much duplication and many items have recurred meeting after meeting

  8. May need to redeploy resources to respond to changes


Recommendations:

  1. Agenda be developed by Exec director in consultation with President and Secretary

  2. Support documents be prepared for each item appearing on the Executive agenda

  3. Agenda should show name of person designated to address it

  4. Make the Execute Director a gateway to the Exec for admin matters – agendas, support documents, quality control of documents, follow-up between meetings

  5. Report from Executive Director be an agenda item at each meeting

  6. Quicker response time on admin matters

  7. The Board spend more time on planning

  8. Switch the onus from directors to Executive Director


Don’s list of recurring and unfinished admin matters:


  • US Day plans

  • Summer Islanders plans

  • Policies and procedures

  • Minutes and agendas

  • Database that works

  • Shorter turnaround on office response

  • Role descriptions for volunteers

  • Database

  • More publicity
