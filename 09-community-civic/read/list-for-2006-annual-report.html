Comprehensive 2006 donor and grant recipient list for CFPEI annual report. Documents 6 scholarships awarded (including recipients like Andrea Gallant, Carolyn Doucette, Ryan Keefe); memorial contributions (Lewis MacMillan, Eric Murray, and others); and major grants across diverse causes (schools, sexual assault crisis centre, disability organizations, veterans services). Lists over 150 individual and corporate donors, including Dr. Donald and Carol Glendenning among major contributors. Reflects the Foundation's broad community engagement under Don's leadership.
