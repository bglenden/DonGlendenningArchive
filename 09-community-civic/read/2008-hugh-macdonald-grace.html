Formal letter dated July 31, 2008 from Don Glendenning (as Director of CFPEI) to Hugh Macdonald thanking him for agreeing to say grace at the First Annual Fundraising Dinner at Roma on August 20, 2008 at 6:00 PM. The letter offers one complimentary dinner ticket and expresses gratitude, requesting contact via Foundation office at 892-3440.




2008 07 31




Mr. Hugh Macdonald




Dear Mr. Macdonald,


On behalf of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward, I wish to express our appreciation to you for agreeing to say grace at our First Annual Fundraising Dinner at Roma at Three Rivers Historic Site.


As you know the dinner is on August 20, 2008 at 6:00 PM and we will be providing you with one ticket for the dinner.  


Once again thank you for joining us and agreeing to say grace.  If you have any further question, please do not hesitate to contact the Foundation’s office at 892-3440.


With warm regards,


Sincerely,



Donald Glendenning

Director
