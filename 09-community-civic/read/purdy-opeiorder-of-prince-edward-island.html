Dear Sir:

Henry Purdy


I am pleased to support the nomination of Henry Purdy to the Order of Prince Edward Island. I have known Henry for fifty years and know him to be a most worthy candidate.


Henry was a Founding member of Holland College, he was highly valued instructor, energetic and creative Director of the School of Visual Arts, a member of the first Board of Governors and trusted voice of and for colleagues..


I soon learned of Henry’s reputation in the community, his involvement and support of community activities and a regular donor of time and talent is support of his community.  


/Henry is a widely known, respected and recognized artist and, without doubt, the most creative person I know; I am honoured to support this nomination.


Sincerely




Donald  Glendenning, CM
