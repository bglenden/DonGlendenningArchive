Newsletter No. 86 from May 16, 2008, acknowledges Aliant's partnership with CFPEI, providing telephone and internet services. Keith Lambe of Aliant presented a $500 cheque to President Norman Carruthers. The newsletter's second section details CFPEI's first-ever fundraiser scheduled for August 20 at Roma at Three Rivers Historic Site in Brudenell. The event features period entertainment by Singing Strings, a multi-course meal, reception, and $90 tickets (including $50 charitable donation receipt). The newsletter demonstrates CFPEI's evolution toward diversified fundraising while maintaining its core mission of endowment building.

Community Foundation News

No. 86 May 16, 2008


Partnership with Aliant


Keith Lambe of Aliant presents cheque for $500 to Norman Carruthers, President of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island _


CFPEI partners with many organizations and agencies with mutual interests in improving the quality of life of Islanders and Island communities. In so doing, we are pleased to recognize the role of Aliant in providing us with advanced telephone and internet solutions to meet our mission – that of establishing charitable trusts to the benefit of the Community in which we both operate.
