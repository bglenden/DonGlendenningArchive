Daily Press (Hampton, Virginia) death notice and gravestone record from January 1918 for Margaret Virginia Glendenning, infant daughter of Elmer A. and Jean Glendenning, died at Langley Field age 5 days (January 13-18, 1918). Funeral conducted by Rev. Charles Friend at Hampton Presbyterian Church. Buried in St. John's Episcopal Cemetery, Hampton. Documents Don's infant sibling and family's time in Virginia.

The Daily Press Newspaper, Hampton, Virgina, for January 13-20, 1918 carried the following notice:


Death of an Infant

Mary Virginia Glendenning, the infant daughter of Mr. and Mrs. E. A. Glendenning, died in the home of her parents at Langley Field yesterday.  Funeral services will be conducted at 3 o'clock this afternoon by the Rev. Charles Friend, Pastor of the Hampton Presbyterian Church.  Burial will be made in St. John's Cemetery."  The gravestone inscription in St. John's Episcopal Cemetery, Hampton, Virginia reads:   "Margaret V. Infant of Elmer & Jean Glendenning Jan. 13,1918   Jan.18, 1918"  (353)
