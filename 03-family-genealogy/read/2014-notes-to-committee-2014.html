Family History & Genealogy

December 3, 2014


To: Amanda

Donna

LoisAnne

Glendenning Fund


Trust all is well and that the recent storm was not too unkind.


We spoke some time ago about the Glendenning Fund; it was established shortly after our 2000 Family reunion; our original target was $5,000 and, if we were lucky, perhaps $10,000.  I phoned a few minutes ago and was told that the market value of the fund today is $20.200; the actual contributions were a bit over 14,000 – the remainder resulted from returns on investment.  On top of that, we made some grants.


Enclosed is:

  • A copy of the original agreement
  • A copy of the amendment
  • Some notes on  the role of the Committee


We chose the Community Foundation to host our fund because that is what community foundations do, and because they have an excellent investment record.  I always thought that one day, there would be a community foundation in the Miramichi and the fund would be transferred to them.  Given PEI’s investment record, one shouldn’t be hasty in making a transfer.


Each year, you will be advised as to how much is available for granting purposes – usually 4% which this year would mean $800; our practice, as Lois Anne knows, has been to use less  than what is available in order to grow the Fund – but that decision is now up to you.


Please sign one copy of the amendment, (as donors) and return it to the Foundation; I’d just sign it separately rather than trying to get all three signatures on the same copy


You should deal directly with the Foundation from now on but let me know if I can help in any way


Thanks for agreeing to act and enjoy being a philanthropist


Don
