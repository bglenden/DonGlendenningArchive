APPENDIX





A Autograph Album


Among Mother’s mementoes, was a hard-covered, maroon-coloured autograph album.  The first entry was by E. A. Glendenning on September 18, 1910 in which he gave his address as Wausau, Wisconsin.  I wonder if it was a memento brought to her when he returned from Wausau but prior to their wedding. 


The next entry is on December 1, 1910, after their marriage, in which she gave her address as Bonny Eagle, Maine; then there are a number of entries in December and January just before their departure for Tennessee.  The last entry is at Little Branch on July 9, 1915; this is after Uncle Guy enlisted in Bathurst but before he was inducted in Sussex, NB.
