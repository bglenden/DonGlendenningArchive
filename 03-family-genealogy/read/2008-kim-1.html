I read your message on Irish roots web site and as I am also reseaching my gg grandfather who was John Harley and came from Courtmasherry in co Cork. He emigrated to New Brunswick in 1823 as a shipwright. His career in Canada is quite well documented as he became a master shipbuilder in Miramichi.However I am trying to find out more about his father who was William Harley born in 1770 in Cork and who was a Presbyterian school teacher in Courtmasherry. He married Ann Bidders and Died in1835. I know he had another son and daughter who were older then John and who went to New Brunswick before him but I do not know their names or if there were other children as well. I understand that the Harley family would have come over from England about the time of Cromwell. I dont know if any of this is any use to you but I am sure they must have been in some way connected!

My name is Kelsie Erskine and my grandmother Ann Harley (Johns daughter) came back to live in Ireland when she married my grand father in 1897 in Belfast.

 

My Regards Kelsie
