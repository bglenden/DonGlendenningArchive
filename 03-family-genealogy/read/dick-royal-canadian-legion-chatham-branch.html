Royal Canadian Legion Chatham Branch #3, 

PO Box 86, 

Miramichi, NB

 E1N 3A5. 


Attn: Rene Smith, Treasurer


Thanks for your Email; I am delighted to hear from you and of your interest in receiving the painting from Carolyn Haggard.

 

Actually there are two paintings:  


The first is of Gnr. Stephen Dick of Black River who was killed in action overseas during WW1.  I am enclosing a brief note about both Stephen and the artist on a separate page.  The painting is being offered to the Chatham Branch #3 of the Royal Canadian Legion by his grand niece, Carolyn Haggard who lives in Alberta.  I will get in touch with Carolyn and provide her with your mailing address.  


I know from earlier correspondence that Carolyn is pleased that the painting will have a home back in New Brunswick and especially to the Chatham Legion.


The second painting, still in progress, is by Carolyn Haggard who is an artist in her own right.  This painting is of five “Boys” from Black River, including Stephen Dick, who lost their lives overseas during WW1.  When finished, Carolyn plans to send this to you as well.


As for myself is concerned, I am a Glendenning from Black River; my brother Kenneth is a member of Royal Canadian Legion Branch #3.  Any acknowledgement or recognition should go to Carolyn Haggard; I am only too pleased to have played a role in bringing the painting back to the Miramichi.


You may find the attached copy of the attached newsletter of interest.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning
