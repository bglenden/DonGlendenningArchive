A master chronology spanning 1772–1964 documenting births, deaths, and marriages of Don's ancestors across five generations. Lists key family events including arrival of Great Grandfather John Glendinning from Scotland in 1830, settlement at Canobie and Salmon Beach, emigration to USA (Portland, Maine; Tennessee; Wisconsin), and return to Little Branch, New Brunswick. Notable: Don's birth September 8, 1929 at Little Branch; parents' deaths in 1938 (father) and 1964 (mother). Compiled by Don Glendenning in June 2005.
