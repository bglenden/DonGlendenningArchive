Email correspondence from Dorothy & Bryan (February 22, 2006) to Don Glendenning regarding MacKay family genealogy, specifically regarding Norm and Peter MacKay genealogies. Includes descendants of John MacKay (1802-1880 married Marjory Cameron 1807-1878), listing eight children including Elizabeth, Jane, Peter, Archibald, Alexander James, Allan, Thomas George, and Hugh MacKay with marriage dates and death years. Writer references Peter MacKay obituary and asks for Don's input on genealogical connections. Shows Don was actively consulting with family members on genealogical research. - **Note:** Third-party correspondence; email message preserved in genealogy files.
