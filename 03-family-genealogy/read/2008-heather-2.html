Email exchange (January 5, 2008) between Heather Wilson and Bonnie Hamilton discussing genealogy research coordination via MSN Messenger. Discussion of Irish records (Coshe Mang volumes), visits to Courtmacsherry and Cork, William Harley family research (mentions author William Harley and his artist descendant Nancy Newton), and plans for collaborative research. Demonstrates the social networks Don helped facilitate among distant cousins discovering shared ancestry.
