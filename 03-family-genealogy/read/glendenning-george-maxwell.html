An undated newspaper clipping believe to be from the Chatham Gazette, reports


"GEORGE MAXWELL GLENDENNING  "On Sunday, February 24th, at 2 the funeral of George Maxwell, infant son of Mary and John Glendenning was held from their home at Black River Bridge.

Rev. Fraser Dunbar, minister of St. Paul's Presbyterian Church  conducted the service.  The hymns sung were, When He Cometh to Make up his Jewels also Jesus Loves Me.  

Burial was in Black River Cemetery.  Pallbearers were uncles of the little one.  

Besides his parents, he leaves to mourn two sisters, Margaret and Jean and brother Frankie."
