Extended email correspondence (January 2008) between Don Glendenning and Erin Knighton establishing distant cousinship through Elizabeth J Glendenning (b. 1843, m. Amos Comeau in New Bandon, NB). Erin provides detailed family tree spanning five generations from her great-great-grandparents through herself, including ancestry through Comeau, Faulkner, Knighton, and Cook families. Don shares information about John Glendinning (b. Jan 29, 1772) and confirms Scottish Ancestry Research Society's findings. Demonstrates Don's active engagement in helping descendants understand connections to the broader Glendenning family network.
