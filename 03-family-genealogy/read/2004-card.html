Christmas card featuring a New Brunswick winter scene photograph from 1934 showing Don's parents Walter and Venetia Glendenning in front of the Glendenning residence at Little Branch, N.B. Photo sourced from collection of Venetia (Glendenning) Russell. Card expresses traditional seasonal wishes: "a joyful heart, a loving home, a peaceful world." Reflects Don's connection to family history and his childhood home in New Brunswick. Touching choice to feature his parents in yuletide greeting.
