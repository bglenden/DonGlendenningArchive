Obituary of Patricia Jane Lister, age 37, who died September 19, 1984 at Victoria General Hospital Halifax. Only daughter of K. Brydone Weeks and Lillian (Glendenning) Weeks of Black River Bridge. Wife of Paul Lister of New Maryland. Survived by husband, parents, paternal grandmother Eva Weeks, three brothers (John, Kenneth, Heath), and extended family. Funeral and burial September 23 at New Maryland United Church. Document lists attending relatives including Norman and John Glendenning families. Cousin of Don's generation.

Clipping, date and source unknown:

Died in Halifax

Word has been received of the death on September 19, 1984 of Patricia; Jane Lister of New Maryland, who died at the Victoria General Hosital, Halifax at age 37 years.  The wife of Paul Lister, she was the only daughhter of K. Brydone weeks formerly of Loggieville and Lillian (Glendenning) Weeks of Black River Bridsge.  Besides her husband and parents she is survived by her paaternal grandmother, Mrs. Eva Weeks of Loggieville; three brothers, John, saint John; Kenneth of Harvey Station; and Heath at home; several aunts, uncles, cousins, nieces and nephews.  Funeral and burial took place at New Maryland united church on sunday, September 23.

Among relatives attending the funeral or visiting the funeral home were Mr and Mrs Norman Glendenning and daughters Eileen Glendenning and LoisAnne Gregan, Mr. and Mrs John Glendenning and daughters, Mrs Ray Williston and Mrss Aubrey Gibson and Mr. Gibson; Mr and Mrs Cameron Russell, Mr and Mrs Kenneth Glendenning, also many other relatives and friends.
