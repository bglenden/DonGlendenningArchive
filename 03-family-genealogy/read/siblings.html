A genealogical plate (PLATE 3) showing Don's father Elmer Alton Glendenning (1875-1938) and his nine siblings, with birth and death years: Venetia Emily (1872-1960), John Franklin (1876-1937), Mary Ann May (1879-1955), Leonard Garfield (1881-1958), Alda Jane Maud (1883-1957), George Raymond (1885-1970), Guy Wellington (1889-1967), Margaret Violet (1893-1956), and Chipman Lowe (1897-1948).
