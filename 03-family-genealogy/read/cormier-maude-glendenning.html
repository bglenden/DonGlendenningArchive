This obituary documents Alda Maude Glendenning Cormier (1883-1956), wife of James Cormier, who died at Saint John Hospital. Maude was daughter of Walter and Elizabeth Glendenning, making her one of Don Glendenning's siblings or close relatives. The obituary lists her surviving children and siblings, including connections to Florida, New York, Ontario, and Maine. The document shows the widespread geographic dispersal of the Glendenning family across North America.

Obituary provided by Grandson, Walter Berry; paper and date not known


(Transcription for Mrs. James Cormier)

“Mrs. James Cormier died April 19, 1956 at Saint John Hospital.  She was the former Alda Maude Glendenning born in Canobie May 2, 1883.  She was the daughter of Walter and Elizabeth Glendenning.  She is survived by her husband and four daughters Mrs. George Hood (Edna) in Lewiston, Maine;  Mrs. Fred Bierley (Doris) in Long Island, NY;   Mrs. Clayton Scott (Lillian) in Bathurst Mines;  Mrs. James Berry (Meta) in Hamilton, Ontario.   Three sons Raymond in Boston, Mass.;   James in Komoka, Ontario;   Sanford at home.  She is also survived by three brothers;  Guy in Florida;  Leonard in Salmon Beach;  and Raymond in Canobie.    Two sisters; Velnetia and Margaret in Florida.

Internment was made in the New Bandon Cemetery.”
