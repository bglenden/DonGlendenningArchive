THE FOUNDATION

CFPEI was incorporated in 1993 and gathers and administers endowments for Islanders and Island organizations. It is one of 160 such foundations across the country a focus on the needs of their local communities. CFPEI is one of the smaller community foundations with 40 funds and assets of $1m. In retrospect, our focus has been generally people who are now seniors and who may have an interest in establishing a charitable legacy now or in a will.. Arising out of a recent planning exercise came a suggestion to mount an initiative to reach people at an earlier stage of life and people and organizations interested in a long term plan to build a charitable legacy; grants are made from income to charitable organizations as directed by an agreement with donors.
