Alternate cover page for "Glendenning Family Memories" volume showing four portrait photographs: Elmer Alton Glendenning (top left), Jane Elizabeth Jardine Watling (top right), Walter and Elizabeth (Peters) Glendenning (bottom left), and Jubal and Grace (MacDonald) Watling (bottom right). Credited as "From the Collection of Jane Elizabeth Jardine (Watling) Glendenning." Dated June 28, 2005 by Donald E. M. Glendenning. Serves as visual frontispiece for the genealogical project, presenting the two main ancestral couples.
