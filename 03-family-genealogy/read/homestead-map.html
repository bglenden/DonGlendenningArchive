Homestead Map - 1876


A homestead map by Roe & Colby shows the names, but not lot numbers, of people living in Black River in 1876; In the Meadows, on the south side of the River, we find D. Macbeth, J. Watling, W. MacNaughton, D. McNaughton, D. Cameron, J. Watling, and G. Archibald.  On the north side, across from the Meadows it shows J. Edge and W. Watling.


On the south side of the Little Branch, we find the Branch School, J. McDonald, J. McNaughton, G. Fowley and S. McDonald.


The McKenzie road included a McKenzie -no initial, J. Anderson, R. Robinson, J. McKenzie, R. MacKenzie, Mrs. Ward and M. McDonald.


The Glendennings were latecomers to Black River and are not included.
