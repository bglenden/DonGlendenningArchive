GLENDENNING FUND 

Advisory Committee


In the year 2000, the Glendenning Family established an endowment with the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island for the purpose of providing financial support for projects that contribute to the quality of life of residents of Black River Bridge, Northumberland County, New Brunswick. 

An Advisory committee has been struck to implement the wishes of the family as to the nature of activities and projects to be supported.  Advisory Committee members include:

  • Lois Anne (Glendenning) Gregan, Chair, 325 Hardwicke Road, Hardwicke, NB E9A 1L5
  • Donna (Russell) Goodfellow, PO Box 114, Upper Kent, NB, E0J 1Y0
  • Amanda (Taylor) Manuel, 7903 Rte. 117, Black River Bridge, NB, E1N 5K7


The Committee:

  • Shall be known as  the Glendenning Fund Advisory Committee
  • Shall be comprised of three members, preferably descendants of Elmer and Jane (Watling) Glendenning in whose memory the fund was established.
  • Will make reasonable efforts to inform and involve other descendants informed.
  • Will focus on how decisions will impact the quality of life in the community.
  • Will maintain a record of actions and recommendations
  • Will encourage contributions to the Fund
