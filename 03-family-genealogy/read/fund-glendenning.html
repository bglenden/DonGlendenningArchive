Fund overview for the Glendenning Fund (originally "Glendenning Family Fund"). Established to make grants for charitable purposes in Black River, NB (Don's birthplace). Contacts: Janet Glendenning and LoisAnne Gregan. Notes that fund will transfer to Miramichi if/when that area develops a community foundation. Grant history includes donations to Miramichi Historical Society (history prizes about Black River) and Miramichi Rural School (library books, 2005-2006). Demonstrates Don's enduring connection to his New Brunswick roots and commitment to community in his native region.

COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND

Fund Overview



Official Name: Glendenning Fund 

Originally the Glendenning Family Fund but apparently some 

people thought that it was only for the Glendenning family


Date of establishment:


Purpose: to make grants for charitable purposes in the community of Black River, NB


Contact: Janet Glendenning or LoisAnne Gregan


Notes:

Anticipated that the Fund will be transferred to the Miramichi if and when that area develops a community foundation

While overall purpose is set, actual priorities may change from time to time on recommendation of contact


Fund History:


200?- a grant of $200 to the Miramichi Historical society for first and second prizes for

a history of a person or event in Black River

2005- $200 grant to the Miramichi Rural School for the purchase of books

2006- $200 to the Miramichi Rural School for the purchase of books
