Brief genealogical note from Daniel F. Johnson's New Brunswick Newspaper Vital Statistics database. Reports death of Margaret (Harley) Glendenning on October 21, 1895 in Canobie at age 92; husband John Glendinning, Sr. died May 15, 1877. Burial at Presbyterian cemetery in New Bandon. Note about unmarked graves encountered during burial work. Purely genealogical reference.
Family History & Genealogy

John Glendinning, Sr. died May 15th 1877 in Canobie; his wife, Margaret (Harley) Glendenning died on October 21, 1895.   Daniel F. Johnson's New Brunswick Newspaper Vital Statistics reports: "Clifton (Glou. Co.) October 23 - Mrs. Margaret Glendenning of Canobie departed this life Monday, Oct. 21st, at the age of 92 years.  The interment took place Wednesday 23rd in the Presbyterian burying ground, New Bandon.  Mr. MacInnis was the officiating clergyman."; presumably her husband is buried there also. George Glendenning also told me that, it was not unusual to encounter bones from unmarked graces when digging new graves.
