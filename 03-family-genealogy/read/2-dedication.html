Dedication page for "Glendenning Family Memories" volume book by Don, dated April 3, 2005. Dedicates the work to the memory of his parents: Elmer Alton Glendenning (June 29, 1875 – May 18, 1938) and Jane Elizabeth Jardine (Watling) Glendenning (February 28, 1890 – March 24, 1964). Simple, reverent dedication expressing the purpose of the genealogical research as honoring parental memory. NOTE: Discrepancy in Elmer's birth date (shown as "1975" in transcription, clearly should be 1875 based on all other documents). Reflects Don's deep filial devotion and the emotional motivation behind his substantial research project.
