A newspaper clipping, paper and date not known, reports:

"Mrs. H. Glendenning

CANOBIE (Special) - Mrs. Hilda Glendenning, 95, of 2055 Vallee Lourdes Dr., Bathurst, formerly of Canobie, died Sunday at Foyer Notre Dame de Lourdes.

Born in Canobie, she was the daughter of  the late Edwin and Nina (Jagoe) Ellis.  The last surviving member of her immediate family, she was a member of the United Church, Clifton and the Women's Institute.  She was also a member of the Canobie Community Club.  Her husband, Raymond Glendenning, died in 1970.

She is survived by three sons, Marson of Bathurst, Guy and George both of Canobie; six daughters, Mrs. Kenneth Scott (Ada) of Clifton, Mrs Shirley Wiseman of New Hampshire, Mrs Robert Borland  (Ruby) of Florida, Mrs. Charles Sullivan (Muriel) of Toronto, Mrs Elmer Daley (Fran) of Bathurst, and Mrs Margaret Peters (Peggy) of Robertville; 56 grand children, 94 great grandchildren,  and seven great great grandchildren.  Two sons, Sterne and Gordon,  and two daughters, Mrs Everett Daley (Alice) and Mrs Patricia Scott died previously.

The body will be at Elhatton's Funeral Home, Bathurst after 2 p.m. Tuesday.  Funeral service will be held Wednesday at Central United Church, Clifton at 2 p.m.  Interment will be in the New Bandon Cemetery."
