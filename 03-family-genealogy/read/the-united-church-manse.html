Brief note about the United Church Manse in Black River, built in 1880, with a photo credited to Glendenning taken May 13, 2005. Don visited Black River in 2005 to document historical buildings related to his birthplace and family heritage.





The United Church Manse


Photo by Glendenning on May 13, 2005




Although the nearby church was built in 1836, the manse was not constructed until 1880. In 19xx a new manse was constructed nearby and the original manse became the property of the Donnelly Family.
