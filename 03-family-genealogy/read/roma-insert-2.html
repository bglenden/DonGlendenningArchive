Comprehensive promotional document for the First Annual Fundraising Dinner celebrating fifteen years of CFPEI service and over one million dollars in grants. Explains administrative costs shortfall necessitating fundraising dinner. Details $90 ticket price, reception with steamed mussels and period bread, multi-course dinner with wine inclusion, Singing Strings entertainment, and silent auction. Lists all 50+ funds under management including Glendenning Family Fund. Names board of directors and professional advisors.

Celebrating fifteen years of service

and grants in excess of a million dollars to Islanders and Island organizations

“CONNECTING PEOPLE WHO CARE

WITH CAUSES THAT MATTER”


The Foundation is a volunteer driven, non-profit, charitable organization whose mission is to improve the quality of life on P.E.I. through the creation and building of endowment based, charitable trust funds. The foundation achieves its objectives through gathering and administering endowments and make grants in support of causes of interest to donors


In 2007, grants were made to the following organizations and individuals
