THE FOUNDATION



The Community Foundation of PEI is a volunteer driven, registered, non-profit, charitable organization. Our mission is to improve quality of life on PEI through the creation and building of endowment funds that will help ensure vital futures for Island communities.


The Community Foundation of PEI provides an established means for individuals, families, community groups, corporations, and private foundations to support philanthropic activities which invest in sustainable solutions to community needs. We connect people who care with causes that matter.



The Community Foundation of PEI honours the vision and wisdom of donors and guides disbursements according to their wishes. We facilitate and simplify donor-directed giving and help establish endowments in perpetuity. We manage trust funds with diligence and transparency and adhere to conventional reporting practices.








I am impressed with the ability of the foundation to design funds that reflect both donor interests and community needs – Dr. Thomas Hall





ACHIEVEMENTS


Ongoing annual Scholarship and Granting awards

Investment portfolio reached $1,000,000

Hosted a number of events to build a network of Summer Islanders interested in  giving back to their adopted home;

Organized  capacity building and leadership workshops for the charitable sector

Completed an $800,000 project  to help arts and heritage organizations gain long term stability

Awarded $100,000 in capacity building grants to various PEI non-profits over a three year campaign

Leading agency in the design of charitable endowments


SPECIAL NOTE FOR US CITIZENS


CFPEI offers the following services to US citizens:


Receipts acceptable to the US Internal Revenue Service

Anonymity, if desired 

Flow through to charity of choice

A single donation to CFPEI can be distributed to a number of charities of your choice.


The impact of charitable contributions on a citizens income depends upon individual financial circumstances, therefore, you are encouraged to check with your financial advisor.
