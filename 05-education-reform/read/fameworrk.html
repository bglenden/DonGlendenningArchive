AN EDUCATION FRAMEWORK

Education:

  1. A service of helping people learn

  2. A sector of the economy

  3. Educated – a level of performance

  4. Benchmark – a non-technical stage or level used assigned and understood by the public.

  5. Needs educated people to provide services

  6. Levels of learning (Bloom) -Creating, Evaluating, Analyzing, Applying, understanding and rmembering

  7. Purpose usually stated in the context of a life role.

  8. it strange that individual services are not listed for education. In education, it seems, one simply goes to school .


Community:

  1. Community of 160,000 people

  2. Future of individuals and community impacted by nature and level of education

  3. Community has a major role in encouraging and supporting education

  4. PEI community prepared to help people improve their education


Individuals:

  1. Life roles: Individual, family member, citizen, consumer and producer

  2. Must learn in order to survive; must learn even more in order to thrive


Education needed:

  1. Essential skills/basic education/literacy

  2. Math

  3. Science

  4. Communication skills’

  5. Soft skills

  6. Self care

  7. Etc



  • Future of PEI depends in large measure on the nature and level of education Islanders

  • Education is a process of helping people develop their knowledge, skills, attitudes, values and other attributes

  • Each of us fills six life roles, that of an individual. Student, family member, citizen, consumer and producer.

  • The role of a Department of Education is to encourage, support and regulate education on PEI
