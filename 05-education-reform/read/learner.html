A brief framework outlining the characteristics of a learner from Don's educational philosophy perspective. The document identifies three key dimensions: prior experiences (home, school, travel, culture, lifestyle, support), prior conditions (physical, mental, emotional, and social wellness), and imagined future (family, career, community, income, lifestyle). This represents Don's holistic approach to understanding learners as whole people with complex backgrounds and aspirations.
