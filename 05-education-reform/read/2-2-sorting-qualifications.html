Brief notes defining credentials and credentialing as distinct from education. Explains that credentials are documents attesting to participation in educational programs, while credentialing is a sorting process carried out by independent bodies based on assessment. Emphasizes that credentialing is not itself a learning function but rather reflects what a person can do and how well.

QUALIFICATIONS


Credential-

Credential 

  • Document
  • Usually issued by an educational institution
  • Attesting to participation
  • Of a person in a program of studies
  • Following an assessment


Credentialing:

  • A sorting process 
  • Not an educational function
  • Different from teaching
  • Should be carried out by an independent body
  • Based on assessment
  • Not in itself a le is a statement of what a person can do and how well.
  • Certification is not part of a learning model
