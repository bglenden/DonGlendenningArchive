Become a member of the

Education Network

Education 20/20 Inc. is an independent, broad based, national, not-for-profit education organization governed by a volunteer Board of Directors and is committed to ensuring a quality education for every Canadian. Members of our Education Network span the field from a young parent to retired educators; from preparing for or improving employment to seeking advanced studies; teaching, training, curriculum development to assessment.

People become members of the Education Network because of their interest in education and also to engage with others who share their interests

2-CVA

Since its creation, the CVA has had a membership of individuals from various sectors interested in vocational education, workplace training and lifelong learning.

The CVA members are typically drawn from post-secondary and secondary educational institutions, the business community and government officials from across Canada as well as internationally.

Becoming An Associate Member

Thank you for becoming a member of the Canadian Vocational Association.

The information you provide will not be shared with others unless you give your permission to share it for professional purposes only. Your membership information will allow the CVA to determine the professional profile of members and how to develop the association to meet member’s needs. Currently, the CVA executive needs to hear from persons involved in competency-based education and training, both nationally and internationally, to reformulate our vision and rebuild our capacity to tell the vocational education story.
