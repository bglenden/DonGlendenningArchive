In this June 26, 2014 letter, Don accepts an invitation from Hon. Gerard Greenan, Chair of the Standing Committee on Education and Innovation, for Education 20/20 Inc. to present to the committee on July 8 at 10:00 a.m. in the Pope Room at the Coles Building in Charlottetown. Don notes that the 10-15 minute presentation will be led by Tom White-Hassler and Alice Taylor, two of Education 20/20's board directors. This represents a significant opportunity for Don's organization to influence provincial educational policy at the highest legislative levels and demonstrates the credibility Education 20/20 had achieved within government structures by 2014.

Hon. Gerard Greenan, Chair

Standing Committee on Education and Innovation

Prince Edward Island Legislative Assembly

PO Box 2000, Charlottetown PE



Dear Sir:

Education 20/20 Inc. is delighted to accept your invitation to meet with the Standing Committee on Education and Innovation on Tuesday, July 8 at 10:00 a.m. in the Pope Room in the Coles building in Charlottetown.


We believe that education is an important issue, and therefore, we are pleased to provide any support we can. As discussed with your Committee Clerk, our presentation will be of 10-15 minutes duration following which we will be happy to participate in a discussion or answer questions. Our presentation will be led by two of our Directors, Mr. Tom White-Hassler and Ms. Alice Taylor.

Again, thank you for the invitation and we look forward to meeting with you on July 10.

Sincerely



Don Glendenning

President
