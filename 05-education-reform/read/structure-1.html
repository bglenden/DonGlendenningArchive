Structural changes called for

About 4-11

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

There are questions as to whether the current structure of our education system organization servicesAn organization’s structure outlines services to be provided and how they will be organized and directed in order to achieve purposes, goals, roles and responsibilities.  Organizations may take many forms – functional, divisional, matrix, open boundary, virtual, learning, modular, vertical and others. Service organizations, like education, should be local or decentralized giving members freedom to respond to individual and local demands.  typical education services include Information, counselling, instruction, learning products, technology, administration and leadership


Much has been written about the need for structural change including:

  • OECD
  • WB
  • MEI


Based on available documents and conversations, the writer suggests:

  • A review an restatement of purpose
  • A functional structure for the Department
  • A position paper outlining services government will pay for
  • various accountabilities


For the record, I should point out that Education 2020 Inc. recommended a similar plan of action in a public document in April 2015.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning
