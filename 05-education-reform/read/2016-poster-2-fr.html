French-language promotional poster for the 2016 Excellence in Education Award with $2,500 prize value. Announces nominations are open to all K-12 schools and early learning centres on PEI, with nominations closing April 30, 2016 and award presentation in late May. Poster highlights technology's role in quality education and provides website, contact information, and address for Education 20/20 Inc. and the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island. Standard marketing material encouraging school participation in the award program.
