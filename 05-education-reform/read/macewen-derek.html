Don provides a professional reference letter for Derek MacEwen, who is applying for Curriculum Director in PEI's reorganized Department of Education. Don commends MacEwen as professional, knowledgeable, willing to share expertise, and principled in disagreement. Though unfamiliar with earlier teaching/principal work, Don reports positive dealings over two years and recommends MacEwen without hesitation. This letter demonstrates Don's influence in PEI's education system and his willingness to support qualified professionals advancing education reform. It also shows his collaborative relationships with education officials.




April 4, 2016




To Whom in May Concern:



Derek McEwen


I am pleased to provide a letter of reference for Derek McEwen who is applying for the position of Curriculum Director in the re-organized Department of Education.


I’ve gotten to know Derek over the last couple of years and, during this time, found him to be professional in all our dealings, knowledgeable about matters of education, willing to share his knowledge and expertise, and able to stand his ground on issues where we differ. Although not familiar with his earlier work, I understand that he has a good record as a teacher and principal in the public schools of PEI.


Suffice it to say that, based on our working relationship, I have no hesitation in providing this recommendation.


I would be pleased to discuss the matter in relation to the Director’s position with you at your convenience, and can be contacted at 902-892-5859.



Sincerely




Donald Glendenning, C.M

President
