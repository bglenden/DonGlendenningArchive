2017-02-20


$2,500 for an Island community, and soon

Education 20/20 has $2,500 to give away soon. The money will go to an Island community in support of an education project – and it will be awarded during the month of May. If you have an education project in mind but and need a few dollars to make it happen, now is your chance, but you must act soon. Your proposal must be in our hands by April 30, 2017.

Education isn’t just for schools; it’s everybody’s business. So, talk with your neighbours about something you think should be done; get busy and write your proposal – no more than three pages. Describe what you want to do and why? Who will be served? The activity may be full time or part time; your school or community centre may pitch in as well. Don’t worry that someone else will think it silly; let Education 20/20 make that decision. But if you win the award, we expect that you will actually carry out your plan. Check www.education2020.ca for details

Education 20/20 Inc. is an independent, broad-based, national, not-for-profit education organization, located in Charlottetown, PEI. The Award was established by Education 20/20 Inc., thanks to an anonymous Island family, to encourage and support doing education better. In addition to an education award, Education 20/20 maintains a website, publishes a monthly electronic newsletter and offers workshops on a variety of subjects.
