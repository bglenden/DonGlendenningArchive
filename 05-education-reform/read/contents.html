Table of contents for "The Glendenning Letters" manuscript documenting Don's published letters to editors from 1996-2014. The extensive list shows 62 published letters organized chronologically by year, covering topics including education in Atlantic Canada, labour training, school closings, bullying, funding, graduation requirements, OECD reports, teacher pay, curriculum standards, reading clinics, skills development, and education governance. The breadth of topics demonstrates decades of sustained engagement with education policy issues and public advocacy through editorial correspondence.
