Formal letter from Don Glendenning (Education 20/20) to Ella Kelly at Skills PEI requesting funding for a continuing education and workforce development needs assessment in Eastern Kings County. Includes consultant proposals, HST estimates, and methodology preferences for surveying the 2,313 residents of Souris, demonstrating Don's professional grant-writing and community development expertise.
Education Philosophy & Reform






March 9, 2018


To: Ella Kelly

Skills PEI


From: Don Glendenning

Education 20/20

Application for Funding


We are pleased to provide the additional information in support of our application for Funding:

  1. A request for proposals was sent to 4 potential consultants; two proposals were received; one consultant was interviewed and additional information provided. Attached is a proposal from MRSB together with an addendum arising from our interview

  2. You will note that the proposal mentions but does not include HST which we understand to be 15% or $3,750

  3. The cost of travel and meetings is estimated at $800

  4. As for the addendum, our preference is advertise through the social media, sample all 2,313 residents and provide a direct incentive for each; resulting cost figures are included in the addendum.


We regret the delay in providing the additional support information; if you need any further information, please do not hesitate to let me know.


We look forward to working with you on this initiative to improve education and employment opportunities for the people of Souris.
