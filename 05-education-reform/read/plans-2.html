EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Communications-2

Information: the fuel of change 


The mission of Education 20/20 Inc. is to ensure a quality of education for every Canadian; one of the pillars of change is information, but while information fuels change, information by itself it does not guarantee it. The nature and range of information about education available to the public does not reflect the significance of the subject, therefore the provision of education will be one of our major activities. Communication activities will focus on education in the broadest sense with our website as the hub.
