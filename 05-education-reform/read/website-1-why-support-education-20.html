Why Support Education 20/20?

BECAUSE:

  • Education is more than having a diploma or degree.  It is all of these and much, much more.
  • Communities Need - Good citizens, strong individuals, self-directed learners, contributors to the economy and community, wise consumers and functioning family members.
  • Education  Systems Should Be: Learner centered, flexible, transparent, user friendly, focused on teaching, collegial, with teacher freed and accountability.
  • Islanders Should Be informed, engaged, innovative and knowledgeable
  • Education 2020, Inc. Is: new, independent, national, not-for-profit, transparent, Advocating for quality education for every Canadian
