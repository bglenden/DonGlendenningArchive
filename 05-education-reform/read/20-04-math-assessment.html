Proposal 20-04 for mathematics assessment and certification service. Offers math assessment on fee-for-service basis, independent operation, open to companies (for employee testing) and individuals of any age, by application. No school credit offered, but test-takers informed of achievement level attained. On request, records forwarded to employers. Service includes comprehensive competency list development and identifiable achievement level establishment. - **Key People**: Not named - **Key Organizations**: Education 20/20 Inc. - **Tags**: PROFESSIONAL, EDUCATION, TECHNICAL - **Eulogy Relevance**: MEDIUM - Shows systematic approach to educational assessment and competency measurement
Education Philosophy & Reform

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

July 29, 2014

Proposal

Math Testing service


Background




Concept:

Offer a math assessment service to include:

  • Assess individual 


Offer a math certification service:

  • Develop a comprehensive list of math competencies  
  • Establishing identifiable levels of achievement in Math
  • Offer a testing/qualification service  



Characteristics of service:

  • Fee for service basis
  • Independent
  • Open to companies for employees 
  • Open to individuals of any age
  • By application
  • No school credit offered
  • Test takers will be informed of level attained
  • On request by test taker, a record of student level may be forwarded to employer
  • Experience to be documented
