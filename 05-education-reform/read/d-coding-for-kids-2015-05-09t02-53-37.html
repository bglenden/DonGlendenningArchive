Coding for kids


Coding is a foreign language to me, and probably the same for many readers of this lnewspaper but it is an important language for our young people to learn and learn now. According to Don glendenning, President of Education 20/20 Inc. “Coding introduces people to a different way of thinking, it is a language of the age of technology” It’s not because everyone whould or will become a programmer but it may be considered a tool fot tomorrow.


Computer coding is being used S a tool fot to revitalizelanguages, The Squamish Nation in British Columbia, Britain launched launched a new computer curriculum as part of The Year of the Code, the Fredericton Library offers sessions on coding, the Ottawa Library offers opportunities fo patrons to try out a variety of leading edge technologies. Even a few hours of instruction and hands on experience is useful;
