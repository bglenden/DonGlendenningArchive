Education in Canada is a provincial responsibility; the role of a Province is to encourage, support and regulate education services; in the case of PEI. although ducation services are not separately identified they appear to be mainly delivered through schools, college. universities and private schools. Although the system allows for independent study the writer is unsure at the moment just how that works. Like most Provinces, PEI appears to be serviced by a school system rather than an education system. Growing numbers of independent learners will likely demand changes.
