Foundational analysis defining education as a process of personal development and distinguishing it from schooling. Glendenning argues education systems remain underdeveloped and identifies key issues: clarity, structure, autonomy, accountability, standards, research access, and documentation. Includes recommended readings on pedagogical autonomy and 21st-century learning.
