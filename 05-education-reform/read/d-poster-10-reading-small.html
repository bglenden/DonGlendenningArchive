A comprehensive poster integrating reading competencies with performance criteria and learning conditions. It covers comprehension, fluency, vocabulary, interpretation of various text formats, critical thinking, independence, practical reading tasks, and reference use. Combined with the learning improvement conditions and reading performance criteria, this poster presents a complete framework for reading instruction and assessment that aligns with Education 20/20 Inc.'s competency-based approach.


Key Reading Competencies:


  • Comprehend what has been read

  • Speak fluently

  • Exhibit a wide vocabulary

  • Read and interpret drawings

  • Read and interpret manuals

  • Read and interpret symbols

  • Read and interpret instructions

  • Distinguish fact from fiction

  • Interpret writer’s intent

  • Read and interpret computer print outs

  • Read text messages

  • Read rapidly for the main points

  • Read rapidly to find specific information

  • Read signs, labels or lists.

  • Complete forms

  • Read tables, schedules and other table-like text

  • Obtain information from graphs or charts.

  • Obtain information from sketches, pictures or icons

  • Read and interpret written information

  • Use references



Reading Matters



Key Performance Criteria:


    • Range of material

    • Comprehension

    • Fluency

    • Independence

    • Speed

    • Word knowledge/Vocabulary

    • Flow

    • Phonics















Learning Improves with:


  • Good Health

  • A positive attitude

  • Relevant information

  • Counseling

  • Quality Instruction

  • A supportive culture

  • Recognition

  • Engagement

  • Feedback

  • Clear expectations

  • Having role models
