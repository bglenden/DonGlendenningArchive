Educational definitions document for Education 20/20 Inc. materials. Defines core education-related terms: education (experiences with formative effect on thinking, feeling, acting), schooling (teacher-directed learning), purpose of education (gaining lifelong skills and attitudes), role of schools (developing potential and community skills), learning, teaching, learner, and standards. Document serves as philosophical framework for organization's quality education principles. Definitions:


  • Education – any experience that has a formative effect on the way we feel, think or act

  • Schooling – learning under the direction of teachers

  • Purpose of education – to help people gain the attitudes, skills, knowledge needed for the rest of their lives

  • Role of schools – to help people develop their full potential and to ensure that our community has the skills needed to grow and prosper

  • Learning – changing the way we think, feel or act

  • Teaching – helping people change the way they think, feel or act

  • Learner – a person engaged in changing his/her attitudes, skills or knowledge

  • Standard – a readily distinguishable level of performance demonstrated through the making of a product or provision of s service
