Education Philosophy & Reform

August 3, 2024

Dear Editor:

Coming to a school near you

I scanned the education literaturerecently for anticipated changes. in education.  Came up with  a list of 29. Items; I did not rank or otherwise compare lists but found the following interesting:

  • Personal learning profiles
  • Increased learner choice
  • Greater detail in student tracking.
  • More  documentation of activities and processes
  • Availability of separate learning services, not just school

Expect these soon on the local education agenda..

Sincerely

Don Glendenning
