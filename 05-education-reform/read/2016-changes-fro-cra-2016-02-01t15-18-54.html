Document detailing proposed changes and updates to Education 20/20 Inc. submission to CRA for 2016.

Changes to purposes:

  • Advance education by providing:
  • Leadership development programs and workshops to individuals who plan, administer and develop educational programs;
  • Supplemental educational support services such as study centres and educational programs for parents and children;
  • Awards to schools and early learning centres in Canada to encourage academic excellence; and
  • To undertake activities ancillary and incidental to the attainment of the above charitable purposes.


Changes to dissolution section:

Upon the dissolution of the corporation and after payments of all debts and liabilities, its remaining properties shall be distributed  or disposed of to qualified donees described on subsection 149.1(1) of the Income Tax Act (Canada)
