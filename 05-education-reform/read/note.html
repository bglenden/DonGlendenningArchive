Draft column critiquing PEI Department of Education's narrow focus on "delivering curriculum" versus developing people. Don contrasts PEI's approach with other provinces like Alberta and New Brunswick, which emphasize lifelong learning attributes and success skills. The piece questions whether the Department's narrow mandate constrains teachers' ability to innovate and argues for aligning educational administration with holistic human development goals. This reflects Don's advocacy for systemic educational reform during his later career.
