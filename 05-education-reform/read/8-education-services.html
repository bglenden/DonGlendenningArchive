Dear Editor:

Essential Education Services

 

Educators are in the service business; they develop, organize and deliver products and services designed to help people learn; typical education services include:

  • Governance and leadership 
  • Funding 
  • Instruction 
  • Human Resources 
  • Products and technology 
  • Information and counselling, and
  • Quality assurance


Readers may wonder where schools fit; I think of schools as purpose- built facilities where students receive instruction from teachers.  Schools are only one of many places where instruction takes place – home and work are good examples; schools are great when dealing with large numbers, group projects or specialized equipment.  but “home schooling” is also an effective way to help people learn.


Based on the above, perhaps we should start asking questions about the quality and quantity of services, not just schools

 

Sincerely


Don Glendenning

A long-time student of education.
