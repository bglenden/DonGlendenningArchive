A framework document outlining operating guidelines for school system transformation. System-level requirements include supporting self-directed learning, adopting learning manager roles, separating teaching from certification, implementing performance-based assessment, organizing around competencies rather than disciplines, requiring teacher recent/relevant experience, and removing time as a course descriptor. Individual school, teacher, and learner guidelines follow but are incompletely detailed. The document represents Don's systematic approach to educational reform, establishing concrete structural and operational changes needed to shift from traditional discipline-based systems to competency and outcome-focused education.
