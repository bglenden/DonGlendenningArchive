Dear Editor:

Changes in public education

Recently I reviewed a number of lists of likely changes in public education: here’s a short list of my findings:

  • Learning guides rather than lesson plans
  • Learner profiles rather than grades
  • Rating scales based on Bloom/s taxonomy
  • Greater school autonomy
  • Better access for teachers to education research
  • Increasing numbers of independent learners
  • Increasing collaboration.

These would make interesting agenda items. 

Sincerely

Don Glendenning

D-23
