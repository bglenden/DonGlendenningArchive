Minutes from Third Annual Meeting of Education 20/20 Inc., June 3, 2015, with Alice Taylor, Maureen Kerr, Katherine McQuaid, and Don Glendenning present. Key decisions include expanded director recruitment targeting students, parents, teachers, and employers. Governance improvements discussed: limiting director meetings to four yearly with executive handling interim business, increased social media presence, and committee structures with director leadership. Significantly, Don expressed his view that "while he plans to remain active, it is time for new leadership for the organization," agreeing to meet with Bill Whelan and Alice Taylor to discuss succession. Directors and officers for 2015 were elected with Don remaining as President.

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Third Annual Meeting

 For the Year ending December 31, 2014

June 3, 2015 at 12:00 am

Haviland Club

MINUTES: Annual meeting No. 3


In attendance: Alice Taylor

Maureen Kerr

Katherine McQuaid

Don Glendenning


  • Call to order: President welcomed members present with a special welcome to Maureen and Katherine.
  • Approval of Agenda: 
  • Minutes of previous annual meeting: Moved by Katherine and seconded by Maureen; carried
  • President’s Report : adopted
  • Financial Statement for 2014: Moved by Katherine and seconded by Alice; carried
  • Budget for 2015: Moved by Katherine and seconded by Alice; carried 
  • Future Directions: A discussion followed:
  • Additional Directors: among the comments were suggestions for the inclusion of students, additional parents with children in school, employers, and teachers who are active in the field. 
  • We probably need a core group of members, even a network; because we have members does not mean that we charge fees; not having fees negates the need to issue receipts.
  • Need for more structure; it was agreed that all committees or work teams should include a Director as a member and ideally as chair. In effect, we now have an Awards Committee of four with the President as chair. It was generally agreed that we need to be in the social media.  The President will work with Maureen and Tom to move the idea forward.  
  • There was also agreement that meetings of Directors should be limited to four per year and business between meetings be delegated to the Executive;  It was agreed also, to poll Directors as to most convenient meeting dates and times. 
  • Don expressed the view that, while he plans to remain active, it is time to for new leadership for the organization; Don and Alice agreed to meet with Bill Whelan to discuss options.
