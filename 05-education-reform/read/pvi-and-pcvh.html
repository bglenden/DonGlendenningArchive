Brief letter to editor questioning the closure of Prince County Vocational School and Provincial Vocational Institute. Don notes that employers report former graduates started businesses based on training received, and asks whether equivalent vocational training is available today and where to find it. Reflects concern about loss of practical skills training infrastructure.

Dear Editor:

Employers often ask me why we closed the Prince County Vocational School and the Provincial Vocational Institute? Many had  gone there years ago and started a business based on what they learned.. Is the equivalent training available today? And where can I find out about it

Sincerely


Don Glendenning

D-30
