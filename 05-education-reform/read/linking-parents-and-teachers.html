A brief proposal to link pre-service teachers with parents through an information-sharing project focused on reading. Objectives include providing parents with relevant information and connecting teachers-in-training with parents. Concept involves engaging small groups of teachers-in-training in projects where they provide information about reading to parents and create short videos for web distribution. Benefits/deliverables include: students meeting with parents to identify needs, reporting findings back to parents, maintaining video availability on tape/archive, and preparing media articles. Represents Don's effort to integrate teacher education with community service and parental engagement around literacy. - **Key People**: Don Glendenning - **Key Organizations**: Education 20/20 Inc., likely UPEI Teacher Education - **Tags**: PROFESSIONAL, EDUCATION - **Eulogy Relevance**: LOW - Outline only, though reflects Don's parent-teacher bridging philosophy.

Linking parents and teachers


Objective:  

  • provide parents with relevant information
  • Link teachers-in-training with parent


Concept:

Engage a small group of teachers –in-training in a project in which they provide information about reading to the parents, provide short videos on the web. 


Benefits/Deliverables:

  • Students will meet with parents to identify their needs
  • Students will report their findings to parents
  • Student videos will remain available on tape.
  • Students will prepare an article for the media
