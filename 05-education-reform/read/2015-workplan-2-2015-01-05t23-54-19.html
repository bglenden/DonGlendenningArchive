A workplan for Education 20/20 Inc. for 2015 proposing new objectives focused on professional development, strengthening education partnerships, serving lifelong learners, and advancing research and innovation. Active initiatives include newsletters, website maintenance, the Excellence in Education Award, and Learning Path, while emerging projects encompass workshops, presentations by Island educators, and exploration of offering courses.
