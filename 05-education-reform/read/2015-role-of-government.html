Dear Sir:

Time to update basic education documents   


Every Islander who is interested in education should take a look at the “Philosophy of Education” found on the website of the Department of Education and Early Childhood Development. It’s a short document and includes a statement of purpose, basic principles, goals and notes about schools in society. It’s a basic document, reflects the beliefs, values and of leaders in the system and provides a basis for decision making.
