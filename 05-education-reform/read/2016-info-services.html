Strategic document dated November 25, 2015, outlining Education 20/20's information and communications strategy. It articulates the organization's mission ("to ensure a quality of education for every Canadian") and identifies key communication vehicles including newsletters (Open Education), website, social media, surveys, and occasional papers. The document reflects Don's commitment to multilingual bilingual service and evidence-based public engagement in education.
