Editor-Learners

Learners and learning

Putting thoughts into brief bullets is part of my learning style; below are ten such bullets about learners and learning

  • Learning is a personal experience
  • Groups do not learn but individuals may learn in a group setting.
  • Most people can learn skills, knowledge and attitudes needed to live and work in a modern economy
  • Learners come to learning situations with attributes they were born with, results of learning since birth and future expectations
  • Each learner learns at his or her own pace
  • Learning results in a change of behaviour.
  • The role of a teacher is to help students change behaviour;
  • Teachers design experiences to bring about desired changes in learners.
  • Teaching is a developmental process
  • Learning patterns re set by age six
  • If learner hasn’t learne the teachr hasn’t taught.
  • New learning is demonstrable through new skills, increased knowledge or changed attitudes. 


Comments always welcome; conversations can be arranged. 


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

A long time student of education
