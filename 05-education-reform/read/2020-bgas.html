Activity report from October 1, 2020, outlining Education 20/20 Inc.'s initiatives including monthly electronic newsletters (both official languages), website posting, annual education awards ($2,500 to Island institutions), twelve occasional papers, presentations promoting educational change, and survey of Souris residents. Lists ongoing "Citizen Mathematics" donor-funded project examining math competencies in Islanders' daily lives and labor market entrance requirements. Education Philosophy & Reform



ACTIVITIES


  1. Newsletter - a one-page electronic newsletter published monthly in both official languages; our thanks to Edmond Richard for translation

  2. Web site - used mostly for posting newsletters.

  3. Education Award - Annual award in the amount of $2,500 to an Island institution for a special or innovative activity

  4. Occasional papers – twelve papers to date

  5. Presentations – occasional meetings and presentations promoting change in Island education

  6. Survey of Souris residents - to determine the nature and level of interest in continuing education.

  7. Citizen mathematics - Donor- funded project to identify math competencies used by Islanders in their every day lives, to develop a profile competencies sought for entrance into the local labour market and to develop a community- based rating scale (Ongoing)

  8. Promotional material – occasional for use with presentations
