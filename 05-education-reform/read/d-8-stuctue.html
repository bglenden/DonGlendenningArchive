Don proposes structural changes for PEI education following leadership transitions (thanking Deputy Minister Bethany MacLeod, welcoming Erin McGrath-Gaude). His "top five" priorities: (1) Terminology—distinguishing education from schooling; (2) Purpose—clarifying PEI's education purpose; (3) Roles—correcting the Department's role description from curriculum delivery to broader mandate; (4) Services—identifying education services; (5) Structure—organizing around branches/fields rather than schools. Additional recommendations include establishing accountabilities (helping change lives, not just running schools), increasing school autonomy (per OECD), documenting functions, providing benchmarks, encouraging engagement and collaboration, discontinuing student ranking (not an educational function), and maintaining an education focus. Conclusion: PEI should replace its school system with a full education system.

Dear Editor:
