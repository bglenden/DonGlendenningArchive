Partially completed course outline for "System Overview" - a five-session educational program designed to increase understanding of Prince Edward Island's education system operation. Sessions cover education fundamentals, public documents review, continuing education, curriculum, instruction, and key players. No charge to participants. Reflects Education 2020's commitment to public education and system transparency.
