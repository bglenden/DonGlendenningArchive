This foundational proposal document presents three initiatives: comprehensive analysis of math competencies with identifiable stages and rating scales; establishing competency-based math levels equivalent to high school graduation; and confirming the mathematics skill gap through research and assessment piloting.

PROPOSAL 1


Improvement process


Education 20/20 .Inc. proposes a concerted effort to improve math skills for Islanders of all ages by:


  • Undertaking a comprehensive analysis of math competencies  
  • Establish identifiable stages or levels
  • Develop a rating scale 
  • Establish a testing service to certify stage reached by individuals. 



PROPOSAL 2


High School Equivalency


Education 20/20 proposes to establish competency based math level equivalent to high school graduation by:

  • Find an existing analysis that approximates the level
  • Convening a committee to revise
  • Revise analysis
  • Pilot performance based assessment
  • Pilot TOWES Assessment
