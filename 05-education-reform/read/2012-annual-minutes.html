EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

First Annual Meeting

 Thursday, March 28, 2013, 11:15 am

Casa Mia

MINUTES: MEETING: No. 1


In attendance: Alice Taylor

Eileen Conway-Martin

Don Glendenning


  • Call to order – President welcomed other members, noted that it was the first annual meeting of the Association, and therefore an historic gathering


  • Approval of Agenda – moved by Eileen and seconded by Alice that the agenda be approved.  Carried


  • President’s Report - moved by Alice and seconded by Eileen that the agenda be approved.  Carried


  • Board Regulations: Moved by Eileen and Seconded by Alice that the bylaws be amended as follows:


A  DIRECTORS:        

  • To increase the number of Directors from three to eight
  • Increase the length of the term of office from one to three years
  • Stage the appointment dates so that one third of the Directors retire each year
  • Provide for the re-election of Directors for a second three-year term
  • Require Directors to take at least a one year break at the end of the second term before being eligible for new appointment

 

       B  MEMBERS 

  • Admit new Directors to membership, thus making our members and Directors the same people.
  • Postpone, at least for the coming year, the identification, solicitation, or admittance of new members
  • Consider all people who have attended meetings or otherwise expressed support, as being part of the Education 20/20 Network.   
  • Defer consideration of dues until the matter of membership is resolved.


        C  COMMITTEES 

  • Proceed with the establishment of committees with the proviso that each Committee Tor be tabled with board for approval
  • Membership and terms of reference be subject to Board Approval
  • Committees normally be chaired by s Director
  • Committees normally include community members


  • Financial Statement for year 2012 – the Association has an agreement with the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island under which donors may make donations to the Foundation in support of Association activities.  Expenses were paid on behalf of Education 20/20 Inc. in the amount of $707.43 


  • Budget for 2013 - moved by Alice and seconded by Eileen that the agenda be approved.  Carried

Note:  Financial statement should be amended to read $2,492.57


  • Officers re-elected for coming year – Don Glendenning, President; Eileen Conway-Martin, Vice President; Alice Taylor, secretary


  • Adjournment – there being no further business, moved by Alice and seconded by Eileen that meeting adjourn
