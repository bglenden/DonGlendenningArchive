Minutes from Education 20/20 Inc. board meeting No. 16 documenting strategic focus on information dissemination, public engagement, and incentives. EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Board Meeting

Monday, December 9 at  4:35 pm

Culinary Institute of Canada Board Room



MINUTES:  Meeting No. 16

 In attendance:  Alice Taylor, Don Glendenning, Brenda Goodine, Tanya Craig,    

Regrets: Bill Whelan  

Guests: Rick Gallant, Dave McCabe and Tim McRobert’s


Agenda

  • Call to Order
  • Approval of Agenda
  • Minutes of meeting No.14 – Deferred 
  • Business Arising

General:

  • Focus - information, engagement and incentives
  • Target – public at large with particular emphasis on parents, learners and employers

Plan for 2014:  

  • Information:
  • Open Education – monthly electronic newsletter distributed via Email (75) and available on website.
  • Website - Demo site to be circulated, meeting planned with Tom for December 19.
  • Occasional papers – to be developed
  • Communications committee - emerging 


  • Engagement:
  • Vital Signs –series of workshops (target of 5) throughout Island; Workshop No.1 scheduled for January 20 in Southport Room, Stratford Town Hall 7:00 pm, Monday, January 20; Steve McQuaid to make initial presentation on results of survey; Education 20/20 to facilitate round table discussions on “preparedness gaps” (see page 5 of report) and what, if anything Education 20/20 can/should do; details to be worked out and will be the subject of a follow-up paper.
  • Nothing further planned although some ideas were put forth


  • Incentives:
  • Education 20/20 Inc. Award –for a school or family of schools that best demonstrates engagement in everyday activities; sponsor, who wishes to remain anonymous, has deposited $2,500 in our account at CFPEI; propose to announce award on January 20, 2014, close nominations on March 1, 2014 and make award in early May. Committee chaired by Eileen Conway-Martin and includes Brenda Goodine, Don Glendenning, Ex Officio and (awaiting other confirmations). 
  • Other opportunities identified but no action planned  at the moment. 


  • Next meeting – at the call of the chair
  • Meeting adjourned at 6:30 pm
