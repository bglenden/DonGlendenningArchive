Congratulatory letter to Dr. Alice Collins, Chairperson of the K-12 Education Task Force in Newfoundland and Labrador. Don congratulates her on the appointment and emphasizes that education is the defining issue of the day. He identifies himself as a retired educator still active in the field and describes Education 20/20 Inc. as a recently established organization devoted to influencing education's future. Don offers to share Education 20/20's brochure, "Call for Action" document, and website information (currently being updated), and offers to add Collins to their mailing list if she provides an email address. He extends assistance if needed for her task force's work.



December 4, 2016


Dr. Alice Collins, Chairperson

K-12 Education Task Force

Department of Education and Early Childhood Development

P.O. Box 8700

St. John's, NL A1B 4J6


Dear Dr. Collins:

Congratulation to you and your committee on your recent appointment. I view education as the issue of the day and I wish you well in your deliberations. Our individual and collective futures will, in a large measure, be determined by the educational services we provide our citizens.


I am a retired educator but still interested and active in the field of education. Recently, we established a new education organization, Education 20/20 Inc., with a view to influencing the future of the field. I’m enclosing a brochure but also enclosing a Call for Action that we published just before our last election. We have a website (currently in the process of updating) on which we post item of interest and; if you provide an Email address, I’ll include you on our mailing list.


Again, I wish you well. If you have questions or feel that we can offer any assistance, do not hesitate to let us know.


Best wishes on you new assignment.


Sincerely,


Don Glendenning

President
