Workshop invitation for "A Framework for Learning" event held October 21, 2013, at the Quality Inn in Charlottetown, PEI. Don Glendenning is listed as opening and closing speaker. The program features presentations on teaching and learning from Alice Taylor, Brenda Goodine, and Eileen Conway-Martin. Event sponsored by Education 20/20 Inc., a PEI-based education advocacy organization, highlighting Don's continued involvement in educational initiatives and learning frameworks in his post-retirement years.
