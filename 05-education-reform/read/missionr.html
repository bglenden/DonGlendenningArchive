Dear Editor:

Education mission not clear

First a word of welcome to our new Minister of Education. It’s an important post; the Minister’s decisions will shape the future of Islanders and the society in which they live.

Department documents describe the Department’s role as one of “developing and delivering curriculum” to young people. That is a very narrow role.  My discussions with Islanders reveal a different expectation, that of helping Islanders of any age develop knowledge, skills, attitudes  and other attributes needed to thrive in future years.

Sincerely

Don Glendenning

Editor-D-6
