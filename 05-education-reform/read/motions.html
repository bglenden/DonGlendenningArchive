Email notification from Bill Whelan regarding continuation of Education 20/20 Inc.'s 2016 Annual General Meeting held July 20 in Charlottetown. Five members were present but lacked quorum, so two motions were submitted for electronic vote by deadline July 26, 2016: approval of 2016 budget and retention/modification of current officers. Message requests members vote FOR, AGAINST, or ABSTAIN on each motion and respond to all. Don Glendenning's role as President continues through this governance procedure.

Dear Education 2020 Members,

A continuation of the 2016 Annual General Meeting was held on Wednesday, July 20th in Charlottetown. Five members of the organization were present which does not represent a quorum. A decision was made to bring two motions forward and have them voted on by Email Poll.

MOTION 1:

Moved by B. Whelan, and seconded by T. McRoberts


"That the 2016 Education 2020 Annual Budget (circulated and attached) be approved."


MOTION 2:

Moved by A. Taylor and seconded by K. McQuaid

"That the current officers of Education 2020 (below) remain in place for the coming year and, that the office of Secretary-Treasurer be split, with Alice Taylor remaining as Secretary and Tanya Craig assuming the office of Treasurer."

Don Glendenning, President

Bill Whelan, Vice President

Alice Taylor, Secretary

Tanya Craig, Treasurer


Approved


I ask that you vote (FOR, AGAINST, ABSTAIN) on each of the motions and respond to all. The Email Poll will remain open until Tuesday, July 26, 2016 at 4:00 pm.

If you have any questions, please get in touch.

Regards,

Bill Whelan
