Souris Project

Summary of Activities


Work included preparation of survey insruments and interview guides, promotion of study through print and socil media, and the posting of notices in places frequented by residents.

Surveys were distributed to 2,313 households, an on-line ersion created and and the survey period was extended.

212 responses were received, 189 were considered valid at the 1.7 confidence level.  Stakeholder consultations included business owners, employers, education service providers, community organizations and government representatives.


A summary of findings was prepared and presented to Education 20/20 and a public presentation of findings is scheduled for November 21





Signature: Date: November 21, 2018

Don Glendenning

President

Education 20/20 Inc.
