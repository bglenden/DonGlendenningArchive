2013-2014 work plan for Education 20/20 Inc. detailing active and emerging projects including workshops on learning frameworks, public engagement initiatives, website development, and fundraising. Identifies Don Glendenning as co-facilitator for learner-focused workshops, reflecting his direct involvement in organizational programming and community education.


EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Sept 25, 2013

Work Plan – 2013-2014


Active:

  1. Engagement Award for school or family of schools that best demonstrates engagement in every day activities; committee in place, guidelines drafted; award to be presented in April, 2014; agreement by sponsor; awaiting actual funds


  1. Dialogue with Deputy Minister about role of Education 20/20 Inc.



  1. Open Education, an electronic newsletter now being published, to be posted on website when it becomes operational; seeking an Editor.



  1. Learners and Learning – first in a series of public workshops led by Eileen Conway-Martin, Alice Taylor and Don Glendenning in Charlottetown on Monday, October 21; other dates and locations to be announced.



  1. Website (Phase One) – a website to host contact information, Open Education, Canada Helps and announcements expected to be available by October 1(?); to be further developed as a hub to organization’s activities.



  1. Webinar – learners and Learning


  1. Funds – seek project and operating funds


Emerging:

  1. Learners and Learning – test at an off-Island location

  2. Helping Learners Learn – second in a series of learner based workshops

  3. Reading –ongoing exploratory discussions

  4. MOOCs – workshop and demonstration of on-line courses.

  5. Speakers list -

  6. Expand volunteer base –evolving in support of actual and proposed activities

  7. Information – expand nature and range of information available to parents and learners

  8. Surveys – explore web based software for gathering and disseminating views of stakeholders.
