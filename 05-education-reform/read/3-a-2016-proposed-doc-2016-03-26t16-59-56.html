Detailed proposal for 2016 focusing on key skills development including math, communication, science, coding, and self-management. Proposes navigator services, learner support systems, and capacity-building initiatives for Education 20/20.


Potential/Proposed program

Planning

April, 2016

  1. Key skills

  • Preparedness Gap – it appears that some graduates do not meet requirements to enter postsecondary education or secure employment; need further information as to missing skills and numbers involved; propose to (a) make the issue public, (b) make a presentation to government, (c) seek information to better define the gap – both numbers and content) (c) gather delve into duplication financial implications

  • Graduation equivalent- prepare a position paper on what, in our view, constitutes key skills for high school graduation (math, communication, science, self-management skills and coding)

  • Achievement levels – develop a set of standardized subject achievement levels for Island schools

  • Independent assessment – establish an independent assessment service to certify levels of achievement.
