Education for the road of life


Education 20/20 Inc. invites you to join the conversation about Education and the road of life; The session will be held at the Inspire Centre on Summerside Thursday, March 1, 2018 and will open with a short presentation by Dr. Don Glendenning followed by questions and discussions. The session will be held from 12:00 – 1:00 pm in Room ; participants are invited to bring a sandwich and coffee will be provided.


In Glendenning’s view, every Islander is on the road of life and education is a support system designed to help us learn the things needed to cope, enjoy and even thrive during the journey – new attitudes, skills and knowledge. Complications arise because of individual differences and stages of development.


Governments provide funding for education for two reasons, one is to help its citizens develop personal capacities but government also funds education to make sure that our community has the skilled people to be the community where people want to live. In case of inclement weather, check the Inspire website for up=to-date information.


Education 20/20 of, which Glendenning is President, is an independent educational organization focused on ensuring a quality education for every Canadian.
