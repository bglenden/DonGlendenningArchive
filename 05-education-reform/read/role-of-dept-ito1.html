Education encompasses all learning—at home, school, church, play, work—developing knowledge, skills, attitudes, and values. Don critiques PEI's narrow departmental self-description: "developing and delivering curriculum" to young Islanders. March 9. 2025

Dear Editor:

Role of Department of Education

We should talk about education; some people, including educators, confuse it with schooling. The World Bank (       ) cautioned developing countries about confusing the two.  Education is about all you learn - at home, at school, at church, at play, at work - knowledge, skills and attitudes. 

The Oregon state Department of public Instruction identifies six life roles – individual, family member, student, worker and citizen education contributes to one’s success in all.
