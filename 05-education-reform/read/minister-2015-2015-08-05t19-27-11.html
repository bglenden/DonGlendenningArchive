Meeting agenda and notes from a meeting between Don Glendenning (President of Education 2020), Bill Whelan (Vice-President), and PEI's Minister of Education Hon. Hal Perry and Deputy Minister Susan Willis. The agenda covered Education 2020's vision, mission, activities, and educational philosophy including discussions on learning pathways and prior learning assessment. Key issues raised included challenges with obtaining credit through the Department and preparedness gaps.
