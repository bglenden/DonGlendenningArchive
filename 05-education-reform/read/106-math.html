July 4, 2019 letter proposing a math skills mapping initiative for everyday Islander living. Don identifies three reasons for the focus: importance for individuals/community, frequency of math concerns in public conversation, and disconnect between standardized testing results and workforce/further study outcomes. Advocates for citizen-based math mapping, restructured rating scales, and common analysis across math instruction on PEI.
