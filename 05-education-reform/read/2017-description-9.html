EXCELLENCE IN EDUCATION AWARD (2018)

$2,500


BACKGROUND

Education 20/20 Inc. is pleased to offer an Excellence in Education Award in the amount of $2,500 for the purpose of supporting and encouraging a quality education for Prince Edward Islanders,


The Award is intended to encourage collaboration as a strategy for improving education in PEI public schools Collaboration may be thought of as two or more people working together to accomplish an objective or complete a piece of work: it is a well- known contributor to both teacher performance and student achievement, and is a key element in driving school improvement. Collaboration comes in many forms, between teachers working in the same school, among all teachers in a small school, between schools serving the same age group, between school and another at a different level, collaboration with a college or university, or between a school with an individual or other organization. Experience with collaboration shows that as experience with collaboration grows, the actual practice changes.


SUBMISSIONS

Submissions should address the underlying issue, the objective and the educational principles on which submission is based. Please describe the proposed activity, number of people involved and the time period over which the activity will take place. Also, whether a one-time activity or one to be continued.


APPLICANTS

Applicants should note that:

  • Applications are limited to Prince Edward Island

  • Costs of the proposal. if any, are to be borne by the group making the proposal

  • Education reserves the right not to make an award

  • Final decisions will rest with Education 20/20 Inc.

  • Participants may be invited to present their proposals publicly

  • Submissions shall include but not be limited to a written format

  • Proposals shall arise from the group and not be the result of a contract with professionals

  • Presentations shall be limited to 3 pages



Competition closes: April 30, 2018
