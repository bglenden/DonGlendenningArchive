GLENWAY ESTATEs ASSOCIATION

STATEMENT OF POLICIES


ROLE OF ASSOCIATION – to represent the interests of its members


BOARD OF DIRECTORS/EXECUTIVE  (President, Treasurer. Secretary, Past President) 

To carry out the wishes of the Association

  • Inform the members of issues needing attention
  • Present a plan for the coming year
  • Execute wishes of the Association
  • Report on actions taken

POLICIES 

  • Maintain reasonable direct access  to east and west beaches for the benefit of owners and guests
  • Earmark  funds for the major projectsmaintenance of the entrance gates (A gift from Bob Cullen, one of the first owners in Glenway.)
  • Maintenance of   road to be shared among cottage owners\
  • Other?

NOTE:

  • At Part 2 of our annual meeting, members amended the earlier motion thereby raising fees from $100 to $150 in order to create a fund to repair gate
  • Thanks to members who have already paid dues; at the moment, we have no outstanding bills but neither do we have money to cover planned items; 
  • Was in touch with Delta Surveys about replacing pin where east line meets water; original pin seems to have been lost through erosion
  • Visited Glenway again today and, again stopped at East corner. Given that the shoreline make a right angle turn, am wondering if it wouldn’t make reduce congestion  to relocate  Glenway access stairs closer to the Lang Lot than the McInnis lot. 
  • Brenda is in transit back to Ottawa but will provide minutes of AGM in due course
