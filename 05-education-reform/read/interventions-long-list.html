An extensive working document outlining systemic interventions and reforms for PEI's education system organized into major categories: changers (information, engagement, innovation, recognition, professional development, research), desired system characteristics, enabling changes, and structural recommendations. Don proposes restructuring delivery systems with education advisors, early childhood centers, and special needs clinics; establishing an independent qualification authority; overhauling governance; increasing public engagement; updating curriculum models; encouraging innovation; and implementing market mechanisms. The document reflects his comprehensive vision for transforming education from a school-centered, bureaucratic system to a learner-centered, flexible, transparent system.
