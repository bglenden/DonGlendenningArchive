During the past few weeks, I have been travelling, reading local and national newspapers, scanning the internet sand reflecting on earlier discussions about education.  Here are some conclusions:

  • There is widespread view that better education is a key to our future both as individuals and as a country.
  • The education system is unlikely to have the means or the will needed to institute required changes; outside interventions are required.
  • The Premier is the key decision- maker to initiate required changes
  • We need action not further study
  • Key changes for improving education include:
  • Increasing choice
  • Clearly defined standards
  • Designate schools as the key change agents and provide them with increased authority, freedom to act, accountability and supporting resources
  • Improve early childhood education but Increase resources for early childhood education
