This educational brochure defines key concepts including education, schooling, learning, teaching, and standards, establishing Education 20/20's foundational philosophy. It emphasizes education as experiences with formative effects on how people think, feel, and act, providing the conceptual framework for the organization's initiatives and advocacy work.



Definitions:


  • Education – any experience that has a formative effect on the way we feel, think or act

  • Schooling – learning under the direction of teachers

  • Purpose of education – to help people gain the attitudes, skills, knowledge needed for the rest of their life

  • Role of schools – to help people develop their full potential and to ensure that our community has the skills needed to grow and prosper

  • Learning – changing the way we think, feel or act

  • Teaching – helping people change the way they think, feel or act

  • Learner – a person engagd in changing his/her attitudes, skills or knowledge

  • Standard – a readily distinguishable level of performance demonstrated through the making of a product or provision of s service
