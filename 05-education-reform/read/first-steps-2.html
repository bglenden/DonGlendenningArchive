First steps

I was asked recently about the first thing I would change in public education. The answer is simple; I would develop and post a definition of “education”. It is likely that a definition exists in Department literature, however, my search did not reveal it. Alternatively the Department of Education could simply be renamed “Department of Public Instruction

I am also told, however, that the Department actually replaced the term “education” with “school” during a revision of the Act thus leaving us with a school system rather than an education system. ”.

A taxonomy of key education terms would be helpful.

Sincerely

Don Glendenning
