Restructuring called for

Blog-D-1

In early 2015, the newly formed Education 2020 Inc.issued a “call for action” to improve public education; specific suggestions included a clear and compelling vidsion, a learner centered delivery model, development of a learning culture, delegation, professional accountability and the encouragement of innovation. While we still support  those changes, we are now of the view that the structure of education, and  not the people stands in the way of improvement.


  • The World Bank tells us that Ministries of Education confuse going to school with getting an education; we think that is the case also on PEI.  We need to change the conversation to learners, programs, services and quality instead of buses, storm days, technology and grades.
