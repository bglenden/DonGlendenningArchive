Initial concept notes for an educational forum floated in July, which received positive response. Purpose defined as keeping education debate alive, bringing together interested people, strengthening stakeholder partnerships, featuring Island leaders, exploring parent views, and identifying expectations. Format options included panel, forum, round table, expert presentations, and discussions. Thematic options ranged from Education 20/20's 7-point Call for Action to future education systems and best practices. Organization involved independent chair/facilitators and partner organizations. Proposed phased approach: (1) Convene Presidents and Executive Directors, (2) Workshop for organizational members, (3) Community organizations workshop, (4) Public conference.

In July, I floated the idea of an educational forum; response was positive so I’d like to move the idea forward:

 

  • Purpose:  Keep the debate alive; bring together interested people; strengthen partnership with stakeholders; feature Island educational leaders; explore parent  views ; identify expectations 
  • Format: panel; forum; round table; experts;  presentation; discussion, series of questions; 
  • Theme: 20/20  7-point Call  for Action; Future of education; future of schools, best practices; an education system for the 21st century 
  • Organization: Independent chair/facilitators; sponsors, Partner with other organizations; 
  • Location: single event, several locations 
  • Timing:Winter or spring; day, half day, Saturday; evening 
  • Phases: 1 Convene Presidents and Executive directors to explore interests (2) Workshop for members of various organizations (3) Workshop for community organizations (4) Public conference
