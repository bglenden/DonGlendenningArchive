Detailed evaluation of three school nominations for Education 20/20's inaugural School Award in 2015. Glendenning served as ex officio member of Awards Committee. Document analyzes three finalists: Spring Park School, Queen Charlotte Band Program, and Prince Street Elementary School. Spring Park highlighted for innovative practices (literacy, numeracy fairs, 21st century learning, clubs, playground equipment). Queen Charlotte noted for long-established band program with free concerts and community involvement. Prince Street praised for inclusive practices, student recognition, community partnerships, and creative programming.
