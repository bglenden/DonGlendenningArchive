Typical Education Services


Education is a service, or perhaps more accurately, a set of service intended help people learn. Typical services include Information, counselling, instruction, feedback, learning guides, learning products, technology, funding, and human resources.  Surprisingly, services are seldom mentioned in the education literature


Department literature lumps services together under “schools”. Since learners can learn most anywhere, the writer is of the view that services should be available to home schooled, part time and independent learners as well. It is one of the characteristics that differentiates an education system   from a school system


Don Glendenning
