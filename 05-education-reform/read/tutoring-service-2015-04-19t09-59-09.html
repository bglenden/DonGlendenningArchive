This proposal outlines establishing a trial tutoring service at two locations focused on basic skills in both official languages. The service would operate on a fee basis, require organizations to have charitable status, include minimum 40-hour commitments, and involve Education 20/20 oversight of facilities, tutors, and resource materials.
