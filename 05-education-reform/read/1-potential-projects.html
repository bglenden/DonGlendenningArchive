Potential Projects-1

  • Achievement levels – Need for a field-based (math) set of standards open to individuals based on learning not age or time spent

  • Qualification authority – independent qualification authority to assess and award levels of achievement to people who need ir want them

  1. Challenge for credit – a campaign to encourage Island schools to reward students who study independently or finish school work in advance of peers
