HELPING ANOTHER LEARN


Teaching –

  • helping another change behaviour
  • arranges situations to help others learn
  • planned and deliberate efforts to help others learn


Teacher

  • One who helps another learn


Domains:   

  • Attitudes – act in a way that is different from before
  • Skills – able to perform existing skills better or to perform new skills 
  • Knowledge – has additional or new knowledge


Characteristics

  • If learner hasn’t learned, teacher hasn’t taught
  • Only learner can learn
  • Lerarner may learn in a group setting
  • Groups do not learn



How do you know when you’ve taught ?

  • Definable
  • Demonstrable 
  • Observable
  • Measurable

Measure the change in behaviour



What to teach 

  • Must know
  • Should know
  • Nice to know


Typical tasks of Teachers:

  • Review and assess performance, 
  • Create instructional materials and activities, 
  • Make presentations lead discussions, 
  • Encourage counsel, coach, diagnose, rate performance, 
  • Set an example
  • Create projects, assign homework, organize activities
  • Create learning plans
