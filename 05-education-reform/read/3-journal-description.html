Journalism Award


Background

It is widely recognized that education is a key factor in determining each citizen’s quality of life; in the same way, the collective education of our citizens will determine to a significant degree the quality of the community in which we live. One of the keys to developing and maintaining a quality education system is providing stakeholders with current and independent information. In this regard, Education 20/20 Inc. is of the view that the public is underserved generally with information generally and investigative reporting about the sector.


Purpose: To encourage journalists to investigate and report on matters that increase public understanding of the education sector.

Criteria 
The criteria for judging entries are:

  • Accuracy,

  • Originality,

  • Effectiveness in explaining issues to the public,

  • Iinformational value, and

  • Insight.
