This is a follow up to our application for registration as a charitable organization with Canada Revenue Agency:

Original application dated - 

Your response dated – May 30, 2013

Withdrawal of application, July 16, 2013 

 

Given the delay, your comments and our recent experiences, we reviewed and revised the objectives as found in our incorporation documents to better reflect our interests and plans. The review and rewriting have benefitted the organization; based on our experience since our incorporation and future plans.  We appreciate that you may have additional about our objectives and have taken no action to make changes to our incorporation documents.   


  • Enclosed is a new application; with regards to Question 11a, we are listing reporting activities as per the proposed revised set of objectives which we feel more accurately reflects the nature of the organization.
  • During the past year, the education climate has changed in PEI with more public discussion, additional media coverage, and with issues and concerns being taken up by recognized community agencies like Chambers of Commerce.  While Education 20/20 Inc. does not claim credit, it certainly has been a player in keeping the subject in public view. Detailed descriptiom of our activities are provided in the application
  • In recognition of our expertise, Education 20/20 Inc. has been invited to make a presentation to the PEI Legislative Committee on Learning and Technology – letter attached; we have also been given a spot on the agenda of the English Language School Board for their September meeting.
  • An announcement of  our series of occasional papers will be included in out next newsletter
