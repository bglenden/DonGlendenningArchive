Amended version of the math skills competency list identifying specific mathematical abilities needed for everyday citizen life. Compiled by the citizen panel with facilitator Meg Savory and recorder Marla Gidney. Lists 125+ competencies organized into categories: whole numbers, measurement, fractions/decimals/percentages, spatial reasoning, patterns/relationships, statistics, and financial literacy. Shows Don's innovative approach to curriculum development grounded in practical citizen needs rather than academic structure.

hart of Competencies – Citizen’s Math



Completed in Charlottetown Prince Edward Island October 7 and 8 2019


Purpose: To identify the math skills that are required in order to complete the tasks that involve math and that a citizen of P.E.I. performs in day-to-day activities.


Methodology: This list was compiled by a group of citizens. The group drew the math skills from a chart of the tasks they complete in their daily life.
