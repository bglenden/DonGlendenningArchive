August 13, 2013

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.


Strategic Plan


Vision: An organization that supports Canadians in their efforts to improve the quality of education in their respective communities.


Mission: To improve the quality of education of every Canadian by providing a vehicle for all interested people to pool their respective efforts.


Mandate: To influence the evolution of our education system by providing information, encouragement and support for learners in their quest to improve their personal education.


History: Education 20/20 Inc. was incorporated nationally on July 18, 2012, is headquartered in Charlottetown, PEI and has applied to Revenue Canada for charitable status. During the past year it has explored educational issues, consulted widely with many Islanders both individually and in small groups, is in the process of establishing an identity including a logo and website, launched a newsletter, and organized events


Current situation: Education 20/20 Inc. needs a written plan of action that includes a clear focus, an action plan that encourages change, a cadre of volunteers and efficient administrative support.


Beliefs: An educational system that:

  • Supports most learners through to graduation from high school

  • Focuses on education rather than schooling

  • Provides clear accountabilities

  • Engages all stakeholders

  • Uses criterion referenced assessment

  • Is outcomes based

  • Responds to learner interests and needs

  • Encourages innovation

  • Encourages lifelong learning

  • Encourages independent learning

  • Exhibits flexibility

Education 20/20 Focus:

  • Learners rather than teachers

  • Learning rather than teaching

  • Users rather than providers (learners, parents, public, employers)


Priorities:

  • Information for parents, learners and public

  • Engagement

  • Independent/lifelong learning

  • Networking

  • Organization: Governance, Funding, activities in support of change
