Don Glendenning's letter awarding $1,000 from the Numeracy Award to UPEI's Outreach Committee, reflecting Education 20/20's commitment to numeracy fluency. Demonstrates Don's philanthropic engagement with higher education during the pandemic.
Education Philosophy & Reform




April 7, 2020


Shannon Fitzpatrick,

Associate Dean

School of Mathematics and Computational Sciences

UPEI


Dear Shannon:


Congratulations. The Outreach Committee has been selected for a contribution from the Numeracy Award in the amount od $1,000. A cheque in this amount will be issued to the University of Prince Edward Island by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island


We are of the view that fluency with numbers is vital for every Islander and, therefore, we also support efforts to help those in the field to remain aware of latest thinking, action, trends, events and materials surrounding us. As someone interested tomorrow’s education, I look forward to the results of your project.


While we would like to meet and make a presentation, restrictions due to the pandemic make that impossible. The donor of the award wishes to remain anonymous, however, should you wish to say acknowledge it, send it to me and I will pass it along.


Again, our congratulations; I wish you every success and hearing more about the project.


Sincerely



Don Glendenning

Education 2020 Inc.
