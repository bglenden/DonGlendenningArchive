Some time ago, I drafted a letter to the Editor pointing out the need for public discussion on matters of education. I did this based on my personal interest and experience in education and more importantly, issues that arose in my many conversations with Islanders about education matters. While I still believe we need conversation, the real need is to switch from a school system to an education sytem.  While I write this about PEI, other provinces should also consider the same change.
