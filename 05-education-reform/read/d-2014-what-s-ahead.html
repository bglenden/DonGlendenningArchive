A January 2014 draft letter to the Guardian reflecting on education trends and predictions for 2014 PEI. Don observes rising public concern about basic skills gaps, notes increased media coverage of education issues (citing three of six Globe and Mail opinion pieces in December), and predicts growing pressure on schools from online learning, demands for transparency, and rising teacher activism in reform movements.
