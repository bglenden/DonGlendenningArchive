Proposal

July 28, 2014


Information project 


Background: 

Survey parents as to information needs, where the get their info, format, media, etc about their children’s education

  • Develop an “information sheet” for each topic 
  • Make available directly and to the media free of charge for insertion in local paper
  • Focused on parent as the key person
  • Organize sessions to discuss, internalize



Background:

  • Education
  • Importance
  • Change
  • Para about change
  • Key role played by information
  • Information itself is not enough
  • Needs to be internalized


  • Paucity of information about education
  • Common complaint among parents
  • Public and parents not well served
  • No regular columns
  • Parents would like to know
  • See my notes on information and on change


Each Item

  • 600- 1,000 words
  • Both languages
  • Posted on website
  • Newspaper style


Steps:

  • Steering committee
  • Prepare, test and revise questionnaire
  • Conduct survey and tabulate results
  • Select desired topics
  • Prepare script
  • Publish in both official languages
  • Use as a basis gfor local discussions


Potential topics|

  • Report cards
  • OBE
  • Math skills
  • Numeracy skills




Delivery

  • Format
  • Print or electronic
  • If a webinar, what time of day


Specific examples:

  • Report cards
  • Regular  column
