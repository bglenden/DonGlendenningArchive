Dated May 1, 2018, this letter from Don Glendenning to Education 20/20 supporters thanks them for encouraging their education improvement efforts on Prince Edward Island. He outlines ongoing work in mathematics instruction, training surveys in Souris, and general advocacy for education quality. Requests donations ($10-20) to continue operations, reflecting his post-retirement leadership of the advocacy organization.

May 1, 2018

Dear Reader:


First a sincere “thank you” to the many Islander who encouraged and supported our efforts to improve the quality of education in Prince Edward Island. The dialogue has begun; now the real work begins.


In addition to our Newsletter and public presentations, we have been working to improve instruction in mathematics across the Island, we will be launching a survey of training needs in Souris, and generally raising our voices in support of improving the quality of our education system.


We need a bit of support to continue our work

and wonder if you can help

A donation of $10,00 from each reader would cover or operating costs for a whole year;

A donation of $20.00 would allow is to do some new things.

To make a donation:

  • Click here to reach our website, www.education2020.ca, click “Make a Donation” and follow directions

  • Mail your donation to the address shown below

  • Drop your donation at the office of the Community Foundation. 121 Queen St., Charlottetown C1A 4B3


Donors of $10.00 or more will be issued a charitable receipt.


Thank you SO much


Don Glendenning



Education 20/20 Inc,

101 Kent St., PO Box 624/rue Kent,

Charlottetown, C1A 1M0
