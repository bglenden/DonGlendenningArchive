A Qualification Framework

One-pager

As you know, our group has been exploring issues in education generally but especially I PEI

Our call for action issued in April identified an independent qualification authority as a step on our journey to improvement. This letter addresses that mattering more detail




Tasks

  1. Prepare analyses

  2. Establish levels and profiles

  3. Estsblish assessment criteria

  4. Assess individuals

  5. Report results


Would not:

  1. Organize competitions

  2. Rank students


Benefits:

  • Ease the assessment burden on the school system

  • Improve consistency in reporting school achievement

  • Could be helpful in comparing foreign credentials

  • Ease mobility process

  • Improve communications between and among stakeholders

  • Encourage self- study

  • Grade inflation

  • Levels that have the same meaning – widespread recognition

Canada

  1. CICIC
