Don examines the distinction between schooling and education, emphasizing that individual learning is fundamental and that education's value depends on preparing people for daily life. He critiques the Department of Education's focus on knowledge delivery rather than holistic education preparation, and notes that learning outcomes lack sufficient specificity.

SCHOOL OR EDUCATION – 

15 Doc 5


  • Learning is an individual matter
  • All need to learn to survive
  • All need to learn more to thrive
  • Nature and level of education is a major determining factor in our individual and collective  futures
  • Goal of Province is to help each Islander and the Island community reach a level that enables them to handle daily life.
  • Department of Education focused on knowledge delivered to young people rather than education – purpose, structure; activities
  • Outcomes not sufficiently specific
