Don outlines a competency-based math curriculum pilot with competency-based principles, dynamic rating scales, and self-assessment. He describes learner enrollment and assessment procedures. This document reflects his innovative thinking about curriculum design and his emphasis on learner-centered approaches beyond traditional grades. Enrol learners:
