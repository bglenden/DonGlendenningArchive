Dear Ms. Baksh:

Education 20/20 Inc. – file 3050233


This is a follow up to our application for registration as a charitable organization with Canada Revenue Agency; our original application was submitted on January 8, 2013 and, following receipt of your letter, was withdrawn on July 16, 2013.


Based on the comments in your letter and our experience in the intervening months, we have restated our purposes and regrouped our activities; it is our understanding that, once these are accepted by Revenue Canada, our incorporation documents will have to be resubmitted to Industry Canada for amendment.


A completed application and package of supporting documentation is enclosed.

If you need additional information please let me know.



Sincerely,



Don Glendenning C.M

President
