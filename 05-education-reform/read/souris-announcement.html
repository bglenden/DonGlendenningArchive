Public announcement for the May 1, 2014 Souris workshop on "Education and the Workplace" organized by Education 20/20 Inc. The announcement highlighted the workshop's focus on youth preparedness based on community foundation research findings. Steve McQuaid and Katherine MacDonald were announced as presenters. Don Glendenning is listed as the contact person (902-892-2060), confirming his leadership role in organizing the Souris event and overall workshop series.

Announcement



Education and the Workplace

Education and the Workplace will be the subject of a workshop on May 1, 2014 at the Access Centre, Souris, PEI starting at 7:00 pm.. The workshop, sponsored by Education 20/20 Inc., will lead off with a review of a recent study of issues important to youth commissioned by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  Following a presentation by Steve McQuaid, Vice President of the Foundation and Katherine MacDonald, Researcher, participants will engage in a round-table discussion about the findings, especially those related to issues of “preparedness for work”.  


For further information visit us at www.education2020.ca or phone 892-2060
