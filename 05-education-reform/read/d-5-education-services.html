Educators are in the service business; they develop, organize, deliver and apply products and services designed to help people learn; typical education services include:

  • Governance and leadership 
  • Funding 
  • Instruction 
  • Human Resources 
  • Products and technology 
  • Information and counselling, and
  • Quality assurance
