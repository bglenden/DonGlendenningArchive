INCREASING EDUCATION VALUE

of public schooling

No. 10 Doc 5


The World Bank is the largest financial supporter of education in developing countries. In a recent report the Bank cautioned developing countries about treating education and “going to school” as though they were one and the same. Based on a review of the literature and my own experience, below are suggestions for increasing the education value of school experiences.

  • Require learners to self-rate

  • Provide Learning guide foreach outcome/competency

  • Provide context for learning activities

  • Post assessment criteria

  • Engage students in discussion of topics being studied

  • Provide teachers with Inservice training

  • Increase school autonomy

  • Add elements of choice

  • Encourage team projects

  • Ensure clear purpose

  • Specify accountabilities

  • Focus on education not schools

  • Have learners develop individual profiles

  • More responsibility to learners for own learning

  • Add more detail to student report cards
