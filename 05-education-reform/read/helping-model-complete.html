A brief framework document outlining a learning model with key phases and supporting functions. The model progresses through stages of welcoming learners, providing information and encouragement, assessing skills, providing feedback and awards, and concluding with credentialing and qualification. The framework emphasizes demonstration, modeling, coaching, assistance, and the distinction between awarding credentials and qualifications.
