Early draft version of charitable application for Education 20/20 Inc. outlining the organization's revised objectives and five core activity areas aligned with advancing education in Canada. Details the shift from original incorporation purposes to more focused activities emphasizing information provision, community engagement, research support, excellence recognition, and direct instruction delivery.
