Minutes from Education 20/20 Inc.'s Second Annual Meeting held June 2, 2014 at Haviland Club with Alice Taylor, Brenda Goodine, Don Glendenning, and Bill Whelan present. Notable points include approval of the previous year's minutes moved by Don and seconded by Bill. Financial discussion reveals the organization had no bank account; expenses were paid privately or through the Community Foundation of PEI. The $216.60 reimbursement to Glendenning covered postal box costs. Seven directors were elected for the coming year.
