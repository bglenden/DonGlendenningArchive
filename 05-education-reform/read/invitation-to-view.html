Public invitation promoting the Beyond Measure screening, targeting viewers from the general audience with the same core message about educational transformation. Details the October 16, 2016 event at City Cinema with $5 admission, repeating that islanders deserve exposure to alternative educational philosophies. Includes Education 20/20 directors list with Glendenning as President. Emphasizes the post-screening discussion as integral to community learning, inviting continued civic engagement.
