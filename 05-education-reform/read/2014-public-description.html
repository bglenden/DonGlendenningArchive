EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Excellence in Education

Awards Program – 2014-15


Education, as compared to other sectors, lacks appropriate economic incentives for either the student, teacher, institution to provide a better product or service and do so ahead of schedule. Having said that, however, there is increasing support for the view that incentives and awards help to improve student results; The National Parent Teacher Association in the USA, makes awards to schools that demonstrate a high level of engagement because, they point out “when families, schools, and communities work together,

    • Student achievement improves,

    • Teacher morale rises,

    • Communication increases”


Proposal

Education 20/20 Inc. shares the view on the importance of engagement and the value of recognition, and has instituted an awards program as a mechanism to call attention by the public to important characteristics of effective schools and to provide recognize schools for their efforts to implement them. For the purpose of the award, the following five areas of engagement will be considered:

  • student to student engagement,

  • student to teacher engagement,

  • student to learning materials,

  • student to community partnerships, and

  • parental involvement in schools.

Criteria

The focus of the award is on engagement in the daily operation of the school and only those items over which the school has substantial control such as:


  • communication with family, culture, community

  • frequency of community based events

  • Community involvement in curriculum development and planning



Nomination process

decisions will be made solely on the basis of information presented.
