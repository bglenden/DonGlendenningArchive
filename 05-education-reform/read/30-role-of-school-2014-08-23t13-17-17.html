Newsletter article examining the fundamental question of what role schools should play in educating youth. The piece presents multiple choice options for school functions (guide, provide, support, teach, help, prepare, foster, develop) combined with intended outcomes for students. Draws on dialogue from a Stratford workshop to explore the gap between what schools do and what communities expect them to accomplish.

Why schools?


What’s the role of a school? That was the first question asked at a workshop in Stratford last January. It is a simple question, a direct question, an important question. I suspect that it springs from a gap between what schools do and what the community wants them to do.
