What do teachers do?


Education 20/20 has had a couple of sessions focused on helping the kind of help that learners find useful; for this Newaletter, I thought that it would be interesting to focus in what teachers do to help learners learn. Here’e a recount of my search:

  • First I googled PEI

  • I looked for a position description for a public school teacher. Couldn’t find one

  • Since we operate on aan outcomes based model, I decided to lcheck out copies of teacher education outcomes; no luck in that search either

learners learAlthough we pay teachers based on their years of study, in return for their salaries we expect them to do something.









Typical tasks of Teachers:

  • coach, instruct,

  • prepare instructional materials,

  • present information, lead discussions,

  • counsel, diagnose,

  • rate performance,

  • develop learning activities,

  • review assignments

  • Motivate

  • Encourage

  • Assess

  • Organize setting an example, providing information, asking questions, setting time lines, assigning projects or home work, organizing field trips and assessing performance.
