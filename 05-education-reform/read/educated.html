Definitional document providing 16 descriptive terms and characteristics of what it means to be "educated." Encompasses both traditional measures (schooled, skilled, lettered) and broader developmental aspects (cultured, polished, informed). Reflects Don's comprehensive view that education produces multidimensional human development, not just academic credentials. Includes practical capability components (able to perform daily routines) alongside intellectual achievement.
