School Engagement Award


When families, schools, and communities work together, positive results happen: 1) Student achievement improves; 2) Teacher morale rises; 3) Communication increases among learners, teachers, parents and the community at large. For this reason, Education 20/20, a non-profit, national organization dedicated to a learner focussed approach and community engagement, is instituting an awards program to call attention to these important characteristics of effective schools.


Proposal

For the purpose of the award, the following five areas of engagement will be considered:

  • student to student engagement,

  • student to teacher engagement,

  • student to learning materials,

  • student to community partnerships, and

  • parent involvement in schools.

Criteria

The focus of the award is on engagement in the daily operation of the school and only those items over which the school has substantial control such as:

  • Level of communications with community, parents, students

  • Frequency of school events

  • Community based student activities

  • Parent involvement in curriculum development

  • Parent involvement in planning

  • Staff participation in community organizations
