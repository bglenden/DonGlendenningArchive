SOURIS STUDY


Education 20/20 is undertaking a survey of needs for continuing education in the Souris area. The study will include a review of current offerings and capacity, interests and needs for further training as viewed by residents, employers and the community at large. The final report will include a strategy for the future. The study is being funded by the Department of Workforce and Advanced Learning through the Canada-PEI labour Market Agreement


DYNAMIC RATING SCALES

Diagnosis and assessment are important components of instruction; it is interesting, therefore, to see an upswing in the use of dynamic rating scales. Defined as a “fluid evaluation process that changes with development and learning, versus a static model that identified knowledge previously learned”. They are also known as “holistic” rating scales.


Dynamic rating scales have the potential to add significantly to the teaching-learning process; they project a better focus on progress than do static ones.


I’ve encountered their use a couple of times recently on PEI. If you are a user, please send me a note.
