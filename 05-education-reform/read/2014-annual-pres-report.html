EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

President’s Report

For the Year ending December 31, 2014


I am pleased to present the following report of Education 20/20 Inc. for the year ending December 31, 2014.



  1. Newsletter: Open Education, monthly, one-page electronic newsletter now in both official languages

  2. Website: www.education2020.ca

  3. Blog: Education 20/20 Blogs

  4. Workshops:

  • Preparedness: a series of workshops in Stratford, Summerside, Montague, Souris, O’Leary and Wellington; sessions opened with presentation by Steve McQuaid and Katherine MacDonald presenting results of a recent survey of Island Youth undertaken by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island; report available on website.

  • Learning Path: a panel of students identified the nature of support for learning they find helpful; further work planned.

  1. Occasional Paper: Preparedness: a series of workshops in Stratford, Summerside, Montague, Souris, O’Leary and Wellington; sessions opened with presentation by Steve McQuaid and Katherine MacDonald presenting results of a recent survey of Island Youth undertaken by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island; report available on website.

  2. Learning Path: a panel of students identified the nature of support for learning they find helpful; further work planned/.

  3. Award: Engagement: $2,500 to Kinkora Regional High School for demonstrating a high level of engagement in its day to day activities

  4. Presentation: English Language School Board: at Board meeting in Summerside on September 23, 2014 recommending expansion of opportunities for students to receive credit for prior learning, exemptions, challenge exams, challenge process

  5. Presentation: Standing Committee on Education: on September 30, 2014 recommending a framework for making decisions on educational matters

  6. Reception: at Haviland Club on Wednesday evening, December 10, 2014 with 30 people in attendance



Respectfully submitted



Don Glendenning, President
