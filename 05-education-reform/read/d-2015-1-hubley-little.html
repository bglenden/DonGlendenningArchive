Date: January 23, 2015


Improving Student outcomes


Steps underway, and still to come, were the subject of a recent dialogue with Dr. Tammy Hubley-Little of the English Language School Board (ELSB). Dr. Hubley-Little’s opening remarks focused on support provided by ELSB for students at any grade who were encountering difficulty in keeping up with their studies, with steps being taken to diagnose learning difficulties at earlier stages, and providing equity and opportunity for all students. An animated discussion followed.


The dialogue was organized by Education 20/20 Inc. as part of its mandate to provide opportunities for educators and the public to gather information and exchange views n matters of education. Readers interested in being part of upcoming sessions or have particular topics of interest, should contact the organization at pei@education2020.ca.
