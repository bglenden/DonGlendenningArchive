A brief personal letter dated May 1, 2014, from Don to Emily Shaw congratulating her on acceptance to the Academie de Paris for art study. Don acknowledges the significance of her initiative but informs her that Education 20/20 Inc. does not have a scholarship program and suggests she consult her school's guidance counselor. This letter demonstrates Don's awareness of individual student achievements and his willingness to encourage educational pursuits, while being clear about organizational limitations.

First, my congratulations on your acceptance into the Academie de Paris for study in art; it will be a great experience and I commend you for taking this initiative.

Good luck in your search.
