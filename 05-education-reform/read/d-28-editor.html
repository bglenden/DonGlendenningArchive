Dear Editor:

Education and schooling

 I am intrigued by a comment by the World Bank that “going to school’ and “getting an education” do not have the same meaning. 

My search of the ducation literature suggests yjat schooling can be upgraded by:

  • Engaging students in self evaluation
  • Allowing choice when assigning projects
  • Allow students to choose own projects
  • Have students submit learning plans


Interesting !
