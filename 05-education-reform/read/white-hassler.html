A personal letter dated July 12, 2016, from Don to Tom (and Jane) expressing gratitude for their community contributions through church, music, and education engagement. Don thanks them for supporting Education 20/20 Inc., praising Tom's breadth of experience, insights, availability, ability to clarify concepts, and collaborative spirit. Don expresses appreciation for their joint ventures including ministerial meetings, school visits, online delivery discussions, employment-education gap exploration, donor thanks, official meetings, and award presentations. He acknowledges missing their partnership and extends warm personal regards.



July12, 2016




Dear Tom:


Thanks for your Email; sorry we didn’t connect but know that the move would be demanding of both your time and energy. I trust that your belongings arrived without incident and assume that you and Jane are busy unpacking and wondering where to put stuff.


First let me thank you and Jane for your contributions to our community; you were such good citizens in the way that you engaged in your church, the music community and education, to name but a few. The fact that things did not work out as hoped is PEI’s loss.


Thank you also for your contribution to education generally and to Education 20/20 Inc. in particular. You brought such a breadth of experience and insight to our discussions, were available and always willing to help; seemed to have an appropriate phrase or word to summarize or explain a notion and generally brought clarity to any conversation.


I`ll miss our joint ventures, whether to meet a Minister, visit a school, provide insight into on-line delivery, explore the gap between education and employment, thank a donor, meet with officials or present an award. And thank you for your personal and professional friendship.


My best to you and Jane wherever life`s journey takes you.


Warmest regards


Don
