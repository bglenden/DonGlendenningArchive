Official congratulatory letter from Don Glendenning, President of Education 20/20 Inc., to Ms. Kelly Gillis, Vice Principal of Prince Street Elementary School, dated June 4, 2015. Don announces that Prince Street Elementary has been selected for the 2015 Excellence in Education Award. He commends the school's educational practices, including student engagement, inclusive environment, innovative programs (mascot, school song, reading nooks, anti-bullying initiatives), community collaboration, and staff efforts to enhance student spaces. Letter emphasizes the school's comprehensive approach to student development and community partnership.
Education Philosophy & Reform

June 4, 2015


Ms. Kelly Gillis, Vice Principal

Prince Street Elementary School

60 Upper Prince Street

Charlottetown, PE, C1A 4S3


Dear Ms. Gillis:


Excellence in Education Award


On behalf of Education 20/20 Inc. I am pleased inform you that Prince Street elementary School has been selected for this year’s Excellence in Education Award.


A positive school environment contributes to student growth and development . Your nomination describes a range of sound, even innovative and unique, educational practices that collectively, paint a picture of engagement at many levels – a school proud of its heritage, inclusive and respectful of its members and seriously engaged in and collaborating with the community it serves. We were impressed with many activities including the conscious effort to honour students for accomplishment beyond typical academic studies, case management meetings, your school mascot and song, steps taken by staff to brighten student spaces, cooperation with external individuals and organizations, reading nooks, bully free zones, and the list goes on.


We were also impressed with the rich detail of your documentation the wide range of engagement activities; your nomination certainly presents a picture of a school and community working together for the benefit of students.


Again, I thank you for the nomination, and congratulate you, your colleagues and the Prince Street Elementary School for your educational leadership. I will be in touch again shortly about the actual presentation.


Sincerely




Don Glendenning

President

Education 20/20 Inc.


cc: Erin Johnston, Principal
