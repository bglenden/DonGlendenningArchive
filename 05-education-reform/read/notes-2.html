EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Communications-2

Information: the fuel of change 

Background:


The mission of Education 20/20 Inc. is to improve the quality of education for every Canadian; one of the pillars of change is information, but while information fuels change by itself it does not guarantee it. The nature and range of information about education available to the public does not reflect the significance of the subject, therefore the provision of education will be one of our major activities. Communication activities will focus on education, not just schooling, with our website as the hub.


Purposes:

  • To inform 
  • Spark discussion
  • Report
  • Thank and recognize 
  • Stimulate to action 


Guidelines:

  • Include information, evidence and informed opinion
  • Provide a medium for others to express opinions
  • Target readership – public , 
  • Encourage others to write
  • Undertake surveys
  • Aimed at the public
