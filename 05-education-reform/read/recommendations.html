A comprehensive policy recommendations document outlining educational system reform priorities including essential skills development (literacy, numeracy, IT, science), delivery model changes, governance restructuring, and public engagement strategies. The document reflects Don's sophisticated understanding of systemic educational change, advocating for competency-based approaches, municipal delivery of education, and independent qualification systems.
