An introductory masthead for Don's "Education 20/20" publication, explaining his motivation for engaging with education. Don identifies education as his lifelong passion and the most important issue facing society—more significant than oil reserves or global warming. He argues the kind of education provided determines the nation's future. Don criticizes the current system as impervious to change, overly bureaucratic, hierarchical, and based on outdated industrial structures. He notes people both inside and outside education are calling for reform, arguing the climate for change has never been better. However, systems are incapable of self-change and require external intervention—the purpose of his remarks.
