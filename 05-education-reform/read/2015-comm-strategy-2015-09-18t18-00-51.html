EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Information/communication strategy


Mission: to ensure a quality of education for every Canadian

One of the pillars of change:

Purposes of 20/20 information services:

  • To inform

  • Spark discussion

  • Report

  • Thank and recognize

  • Stimulate to action

  • Encourage people to write as a professional development activity


Elements:

  1. Newsletter: Open Education, a monthly, electronic but printable, bilingual newsletter.

  2. Website:

  3. Social media: Blog and Twitter under development

  4. Surveys: of parents, students, employers, students, teachers,

  5. Other: occasional papers and posters


Focus:

  • Seek contributions

  • Primarily for public not professionals

  • Gather and disseminate local information

  • Undertake surveys

  • Writing for the public not the professionals

  • including but not limited to students

  • evidence and informed opinion

  • Focused on best practices and desired system change...

  • Will publish in both official languages to the extent that resources allow



As a professional development activitiy

Issues:

  1. Establish guidelines

  2. Services to be offered

  3. Do we accept sponsors and advertisements

  4. Priorities

  5. Oversight

  6. Staff and volunteers,

  7. Budget,

  8. Audience,

  9. Target groups and what they want/need

  10. Level

  11. Kind of info: curriculum, standards,

  12. Other options
