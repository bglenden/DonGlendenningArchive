COMMENT about Education in PEI:

• The writer has encountered seeming acceptance of school attendance and education as being one and the same.

• Educator’s views of education appear to be narrowing and reflecting a view of education as a product rather than a service.

• The absence of definitions may signify significant differences of opinion within the field as to Education’s meaning and mission.

• Public discussion appears to focus on schools rather than education.

• Graduation requirements reflect traditional subjects rather than 21st century concepts

• The school system seems to ignore the importance of context

• Purpose of education offerings are not clear.
