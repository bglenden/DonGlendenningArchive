DRAFTED BUT NOT SENT

NOVEMBER 9, 2014

Dear Owen:


First I bring greetings.  I trust that all is well with you and your family, that you are still on the Island, and that you enjoyed our beautiful summer.

Because you are on our mailing list, you will know be up to date on our activities.  We have a website, a monthly newsletter and organized some workshops and made an award to the Kinkora Regional High School.

We are now beginning to make plans for 2015 and, among other things, want to update our Board and add new members.  Our records show your last meeting you attended was the planning meeting in June, 201, and the last correspondence received was on June 26, 2013

Because you have not attended any meetings nor responded to any messages during the past year, I am assuming that you are either not interested nor available to continue as a Director.  Consequently, unless I hear from you on or before December 31, 2014, I will table a motion at our first Directors meeting in 2015 removing you're name from our list of Directors.

Should you  be available and interested, I would be delighted to have coffee at a mutually convenient time and place. Should you wish to return to the Board at some future date, please let me know.

I am assuming that you are receiving Education 20/20 materials because no Emails have bounced back, however, I am also sending a copy of this to your last known address, Interim Chair, Veterans Appeal Board, Charlottetown , PE C1A 8M9

Sincerely


Don Glendenning,
President
