January 14, 2017

Maureen Kerr


Dear Maureen:


It is with regret that I acknowledge your letter of resignation as a Director of Education 20/20 Inc. I recall our first meeting, your concern about and your interest in youngsters experiencing robotics, coding and other applications of technology,


I thank you for coming on board, for your contributions to Education 20/20 Inc. and your personal support. I also commend and thank you for your many activities in support of the betterment of our community. Our community is richer because of your presence.


As for education, it remains challenge. I believe that we know how to make the educational experience for our children much better but lack the will to do so.


Warmest wishes in all your endeavours.


Regards




Don Glendenning

President
