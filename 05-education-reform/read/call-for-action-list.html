A concise bulleted action list presenting Education 20/20's core policy recommendations for educational transformation. The seven-point call addresses: developing a clear educational vision for Islanders, creating learner-centered service models, assuming government leadership in learning culture, delegating instruction provision to multiple providers, establishing independent qualification bodies, implementing professional accountability models, and developing innovation strategies. This distilled format appears designed for easy dissemination and communication. The list is presented twice identically, suggesting preparation for multiple uses (publication, speaking, distribution). This represents the executive summary of Education 20/20's advocacy agenda.

A Call for Action on Education


  1. Develop a “clear and compelling” vision of education for all Islanders.

  2. Develop a learner centered service model that provides support in all domains to learners as, when and where needed.

  3. Assume a leadership role in creating a learning culture and providing a quality education for every Islander.

  4. Delegate the role of providing instruction.

  5. Establish a qualification body for the purpose certifying individual levels of achievement

  6. Establish a professional model of service delivery where professionals are directly accountable to clients for the nature, extent and quality of service provided.

  7. Develop a strategy to encourage and support innovation.
