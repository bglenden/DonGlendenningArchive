Cross Country Checkup

Canadians were again presented with a litany of complaints and concerns about our education systems  on Cross Country Check-up; it’s a problem of quality.  I don’t blame our government, past or present, for causing education problems but government does have a responsibility to deal with them – and government must take th first step.

In April of 2015, Education 20/20 Inc. released the following Call for Action to improve education on PEI  

  • Develop a “clear and compelling” vision of education for all Islanders. 
  • Develop a learner centered service model that provides support in all domains to learners .
  • Assume a leadership role in creating a learning culture and providing a quality education for every Islander.  
  • Delegate the role of providing instruction. 
  • Establish a qualification body for the purpose certifying individual levels of achievement to persons wanting or needing such documentation. 
  • Establish a professional model of service delivery where professionals are directly account-able to clients for the nature, extent and quality of service provided.  
  • Put in place a strategy to encourage and support innovation.
