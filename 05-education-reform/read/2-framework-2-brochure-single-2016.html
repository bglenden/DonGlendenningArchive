An education framework

The road of life


One approach to decisionmaking about education is to consider one’s education as preparation for the next stage on the road of life. This framework has three components:


First we have a learner who brings into each new learning situation certain physical and mental conditions with which they were born, the attitudes, skills or knowledge learned to date, and of course the learner’s plans and aspirations.


Then we have demands of the road itself, the nature and weight of the roles we play, the impediments and potholes we encounter,


And finally comes a range of educational services available to us such as reliable information, adequate and timely instruction, trustworthy feedback, self study resources, professional counselling, practive and assessment.
