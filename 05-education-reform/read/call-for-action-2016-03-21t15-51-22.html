Education 20/20 Inc. advocacy brief calling for systemic education reform in PEI, including learner-centered services, autonomous school operations, independent qualification authority, professional teaching models, and support for innovation. Represents Don's core education philosophy emphasizing flexibility, accountability, and responsiveness to learner needs rather than traditional time-based credentials and centralized control.
Education Philosophy & Reform



PEI Education needs:


  • A “clear and compelling” vision of education for all Islanders.

  • A learner centered service that provides support to learners as, when and where needed.

  • A learning culture focused on increasing opportunities for all.

  • Greater autonomy and accountability for schools in their day-to-day operation.

  • An independent qualification authority.

  • Professional teaching model.

  • Support for innovation.
