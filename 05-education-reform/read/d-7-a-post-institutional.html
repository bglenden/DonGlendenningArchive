Brief conceptual paper on emerging post-institutional education paradigm. Outlines shift from traditional institutional models to broader ecosystem including family educators, school focus on social-emotional health, separate qualification authority, and community education centres. References Kevin Carey's "The End of College" and Walter Gordon Symposium discussion. Explores how education is transitioning beyond institutional boundaries.
