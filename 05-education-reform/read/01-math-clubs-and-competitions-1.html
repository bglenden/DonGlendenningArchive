PEI Math Club


Proposal:

  • Organize math clubs in PEI

Purpose:

  • To raise the level of interest in mathematics in Prince Edward Islanders

  • Raise the level of skill among Islanders in the field of mathematics

  • Encourage students to enter math competitions


Why:

  • PISA results

  • No mathematics society on PEI

  • Few Island students participate in math competitions

  • Math can be fun


Concept

  • Initiate math club in Charlottetown with a view to expanding the service across PEI.


Descriptors:

  • Boys and girls

  • Initiate with a single club

  • Initiate at Junior High school level

  • Meet off school property


Steps:

  • Convene a small group of people to explore the idea

  • If there is interest, prepare a brief description

  • Issue invitation to interested people to discuss idea
