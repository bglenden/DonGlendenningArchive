Draft Board Regulation outlining the Communications Committee's role and responsibilities for Education 20/20. Establishes committee composition (minimum 5 members, 3 from community), specifies duties (develop communication plan, oversee website and publications, manage privacy policy), and governance structures. The document reflects Don's commitment to formal organizational governance and clear delegation of responsibilities.


EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

DRAFT

Board Regulation No.


Category: Committees Effective date:

Subject: Communications

___________________________________________________________________________________

The Communications Committee plays a lead role on behalf of the Board of Directors in keeping the network and the public informed about education and the work of the Association.


Specific duties:

Supporting the work of Education 20/20, the committee shall:

  • Develop, coordinate, and implement a communication plan

  • Oversee the website

  • Oversee the quality of all publications

  • Prepare and monitor the privacy policy

  • Develop strategies for communicating with various interest groups

  • Ensure a public profile of the Association within the community at large

  • Undertake such other duties as may be assigned


Committee Membership:

The committee shall ordinarily be composed of a minimum of 5 members with three appointed from the community at large. The President is an ex officio member; Directors who are not members of the committee , may attend committee meetings as non-voting members. Committee membership be increased with Board approval. Members serve without remuneration.


Chair:

The committee Chair shall ordinarily be a Director and appointed by the board of Directors; the committee may select a vice chair if deemed necessary.


Term of Office

Committee members serve for three years


Meetings

The Committee meets at the call of the chair


Voting

Motions arising at any Committee meeting shall be decided by majority vote


Reporting

The Chair reports to the Board of directors; minutes are distributed to committee members and may be distributed to Directors
