Letter dated August 20, 2018, to the Charlottetown Guardian. Don questions whether PEI's education system is "lost," citing lack of clear mandates, guiding principles, and coherent structure. He proposes action to review and broaden departmental mandate, redefine the Department's leadership role, and implement clear learning models. Signed as "A long-time student of education." Reflects his broader critique of PEI's education system and advocacy for systemic change.


August 20, 2018


Editor,

Charlottetown, PEI

D-
