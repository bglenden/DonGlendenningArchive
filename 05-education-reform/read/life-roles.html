Brief educational framework document identifying six life roles developed by Oregon educational system. Roles identified: Individual, Family Member, Citizen, Learner, Consumer, and Producer. Represents holistic approach to education emphasizing development across multiple life contexts beyond traditional academic learning. Short conceptual framework used in pedagogical planning.
