Strategic planning notes aligning Education 20/20 Inc.'s activities with proposed Canadian nonprofit legislation objectives. The document maps the organization's programs (newsletters, websites, workshops, surveys, awards, presentations) to five core mission areas focused on public education, research, stakeholder cooperation, and professional development support.

Based on Objectives proposed for new Non Profit Act:


1 Inform and educate the public and policy makers on the impact of policies on citizens and communities:

  • Publish a monthly electronic newsletter 
  • Maintain a website (www.education2020.ca) 
  • Publish occasional papers 
  • Make awards to a schools, learning centres, other organizations and people to recognize leadership or high levels of performance in support of education  


2 Monitor the education system to ensure that actions proposed and taken reflect research findings and best practices:

  • Directors will meet with and make presentations to governing bodies including PEI Government, English and French School Boards, Chambers of Commerce and others as seem appropriate from time to time
  • Undertake surveys of stakeholders (learners, teachers, employers, public, etc.)  to keep in touch with issues and interest to stakeholders
  • Engage the public through the social media


3 Engage parents and communities in activities that improve access of learners to a quality education

  • Organize workshops on a range of issues such  as Essential skills, Learning framework, Challenge for Credit, Charter schools, Handle with Care, application of technology, report cards, 
  • Establish a qualification authority


4   Support activities that reflects the interests, needs and activities of learners

  • Challenge for credit
  • Clubs – math, robotics, environment, arts, etc.
  • Organize an electronic forum for the public at large on schooling issues
  • Host discussions to build relationships and support quality programming


5 Encourage and support educators in their on-going professional development

  • Engage educators in writing papers as a way to improve their professional development
  • Convene meetings and discussions where educators can exchange information and views
  • Organize sessions for teachers to learn of  innovative ideas and best practices   
  • Monitor and disseminate information about current, valid research


6   Encourage organizational growth and sustainability

  • Seek registration as a charitable organization
  • Review and update  matters of governance
  • Seek funds in support of projects
  • Seek sponsors in support of events and activities
