FUTURE EDUCATION-1

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Education is complex; learners have different interests, capabilities, experiences and needs; every Islander is said to fill six life roles that of a citizen, family member, individual, student, consumer and producer; education could benefit from a review of the definition itself

NATIONAL ARTS ADMINISTRATION

  1. Performance or outcome based

  2. A successful system uses assessment strategies as strong and rich as the outcomes

  3. Successful system rewards schools for success , helps schools in trouble and penalize schools for persistent or dramatic failure

  4. A successful system gives school-based staff a major role in instructional decisions

  5. A successful system emphasize staff development

  6. A successful system provides high quality prekindergarten programs , at least for every disadvantaged child

  7. A successful system provides health and other social services sufficient to reduce significant barriers to learning

  8. A successful system use technology to raise student and teacher productivity and expand access to learning overcoming challenges –
