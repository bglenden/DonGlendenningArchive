Some key

Reading competencies


  • Comprehend what has been read

  • Speak fluently

  • Exhibit a wide vocabulary

  • Read and interpret drawings

  • Read and interpret manuals

  • Read and interpret symbols

  • Read and interpret instructions

  • Distinguish fact from fiction

  • Interpret authors intent

  • Read and interpret computer print outs

  • Read text messages

  • Read rapidly for the main points

  • Read rapidly to find specific information

  • Read signs, labels or lists.

  • Complete forms

  • Read tables, schedules and other table-like text

  • Obtain specific information from graphs or charts.

  • Obtain information from sketches, pictures or icons

  • Read and interpret written information

  • Utilize dictionaries

  • Utilize Thesaurus

  • Utilize style manuals
