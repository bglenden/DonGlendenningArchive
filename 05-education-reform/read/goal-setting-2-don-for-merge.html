Goal-setting document for Education 20/20 Inc. dated May 9, 2013, outlining organizational goals in six categories: obtaining charitable status, differentiation from other groups, dissemination of information, communication and engagement, advocacy, and research guidance. Don Glendenning likely authored this strategic planning document reflecting his leadership approach focused on structured advocacy and public engagement on educational matters.
Education Philosophy & Reform

May 09, 2013

Program


EDUCATION 20/20 INC.


Suggested Goals


ORGANIZATIONAL GOALS

Goal 1:  To obtain a charitable number from CRA

Goal 2:  Differentiate Education 20/20 from other education advocacy groups


DISSEMINATION OF INFORMATION 

Goal 3: To provide an electronic forum for the public at large on matters pertaining

to education


COMMUNICATION AND ENGAGEMENT

Goal 4: Assist the Canadian Education Association to organize “a series of

  public advocacy campaigns designed to stir up a sense of urgency for change that

draws the public into authentic conversations with policy-makers, educators, 

students and parents.”

Goal 5: Based on findings of current workshop series, organize a series of workshops 

to engage educators in discussions about education and schooling

ADVOCACY

Goal 6:  Make a presentation to cabinet on our views about education

Goal 7:  Presentation to English School Board


TRENDS, ISSUES, AND CURRENT RESEARCH

Goal 8:  Ensure that we are being guided by current, valid research


INCENTIVES AND AWARDS

Goal 9: Offer an additional incentive award

OTHER:

  • Strengthen Organization
  • Qualification authority
  • Think tank
  • Legacy project
  • Best practices
  • Early childhood
  • Possible projects
  • Math
  • Education for the workplace
