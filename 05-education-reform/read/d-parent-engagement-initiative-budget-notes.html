Budget notes for Education 20/20 Inc.'s Public Engagement Initiative, outlining Phase One activities and costs. Activities include engagement awards ($2,500 per award), electronic newsletters with volunteer coordinators, occasional papers (electronic, no charge), videos/webinars (8-video learning series), media releases, social media activities, public presentations, speaker lists, and surveys. Support structures include website development, project leadership and coordination, communications committee, volunteer recruitment, and grant/sponsorship acquisition. Reflects a multimedia, volunteer-leveraging approach to educational outreach with estimated core budget around $11,900 for project director (6 hrs/week), website, administrative support, and surveys.
