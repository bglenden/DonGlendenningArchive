A November 2013 document presenting the foundational philosophy and governance model for the "Better Writing" organization. Don articulates his belief that reading and writing are foundational competencies for individual and community development. The organization operates through an unaligned volunteer board with expertise in education but without institutional affiliations, ensuring transparency, public service orientation, and evidence-based decision-making. This document reveals Don's values around independence, stakeholder cooperation, and systemic improvement through public engagement.
Education Philosophy & Reform



November 03, 2013


Writing


Better Writing is founded on the belief that education is important, our future as individuals and as a community will be shaped, in a large measure and by the nature and level of education we provide to our citizens. In improving education, two key competencies stand out, reading and writing - and each complements the other. Better Writing is an unaligned public organization whose purpose is to inform, educate, provoke discussion and lobby for change when the evidence warrant it


Better Writing is governed by a volunteer Board of Directors who have demonstrated an interest in and knowledge of education but who are not, currently occupying positions in the field. Directors, collectively, are representative of the constituency being served but are not representing any particular organizations or institutional point of view. In relation to CEF, directors will operate an organization that is public, user friendly, unaligned, transparent and evidence based.


Better Writing may organize and carry out any and all activities that promote and contribute to gathering, disseminating information and encouraging the improvement of reading including:

  • Providing volunteers

  • Advocating

  • Undertaking and sponsoring research and studies

  • Production of educational materials

  • Organizing conferences, seminars and workshops

  • Organizing discussion groups

  • Fostering understanding and cooperation among all interested parties
