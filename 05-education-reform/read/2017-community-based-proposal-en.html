Call for proposals for Souris Community Education Fund from Education 20/20 Inc., seeking community-led educational initiatives addressing local needs. Emphasizes preference for projects rooted in existing community resources, best practices, and equitable approaches. Demonstrates Don's commitment to community-based learning and local capacity building.

BACKGROUND

Education 20/20 Inc. is pleased to offer support for community led education services that encourage a quality education for Prince Edward Islanders.

educational services initiated by a community organization

project may be led by individuals and organizations other than schools and learning centres.

Community groups are invited to present their ideas on the use and organization of resources in their community to meet a specific educational need of community members. The call for proposals is open to any group, either already existing or coming together for the purpose of making a submission.


PREFERENCE WILL BE GIVEN TO PROJECTS:

  • Be community led

  • Address an actual community education issue

  • Be integrated into the education services of the community

  • Reflect and build on existing community resources

  • Be equitable

  • Include activities that would actually be initiated if resources allowed

  • Be based on best practices, leading edge thinking and research in the field of education


DOCUMENTATION:

  • Shall include but not be limited to a written format

  • Shall arise from the group and not be the result of a contract with professionals

  • Limited to 5 pages


PROPOSALS

Documentation should address the following:

  • Description of community

  • Description of an educational need

  • Educational principles on which submission is based.

  • Proposed activity

  • Likely future of the initiative
