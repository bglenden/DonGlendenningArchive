Why do I need an education?

No. 03 About-A-1

We are all travellers on the road of life; we are plopped there unceremonousyly at time of birth and stay on as long as we live; for some the stay is like the blink of an eye but, for increasing numbers, it is three score and ten with a few extra.


Each traveller is unique, unique in at least three ways, (1) the conditions we were born such as our physical, mental, emotional and social well being; (2) what we’ve learned since birth, to walk, to talk to run, to recognize people to love, to fear and all the others; (3) and then we have our aspirations, our imagined futures, a home and family, a job, travel and friends.



The road is not easy to describe; while for many and in many ways it is the same for all, it many other ways it is an individual road that you travel relatively alone among the many millions others. The road itswlf appears in constant motion caused by the dynamics of all the motion on it
