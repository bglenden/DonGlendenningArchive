Education 20/20 Inc. fundraising proposal outlining the need for education improvement beyond schooling, proposing a public campaign with speakers and tiered donor support (Friend, Founder, Network tiers). Articulates Don's vision for mobilizing community investment in education reform.

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Development

Background:

  • Education is important for our futures
  • Greater than schooling
  • Education is a government operated enterprise
  • an enterprise that has no competitor, 
  • where the provider decides what is to be taught,
  • how much time will be allowed to both learn and to teach, 
  • methods to be used; 
  • standards to be met
  • a provider with little interest in the views of clients, research findings or best practices.
  • Needs help



Purpose: to raise funds for the improvement of education in PEI
