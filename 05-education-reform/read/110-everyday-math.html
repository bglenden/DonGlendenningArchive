Dear Sir: 

Every day Math


This letter responds to a number of comments and questions about the Education 20/20 math map mentioned in a letter to the Editor of Saturday, August 3, 2019


Whereas traditional curriculum development usually begins with  detailed analysis of the field of study; Education 20/20 proposes instead to identify math skills actually used by citizens  in their every life. We are simply approaching math from a different perspective, a perspective that should make content more relevant, interesting and directly applicable. This approach is consistent with the addition of numeracy to  high school offerings in Australia and the UK and appears to add much needed practice of basic math skills.  


The completed map  math may be useful to teachers, home schoolers, credentialing and certification bodies, parents, employers, and of course, learners themselves, Whether or not this form of analysis will be used I schools will depend on those who teach.

 

Sincerely, 



Don Glendenning

A long-time student of education
