Support document prepared by Don Glendenning outlining organizational priorities for Education 20/20 Inc.'s board composition and structure. Addresses desired board diversity (age, geography, profession, language, gender, culture), policy development, committee structure, and activities including workshops, conferences, and community dialogues.

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Annual Meeting No. 3

Support Document: 1


Subject:   Organizational matters Prepared by: D Glendenning 

      Date: February 15,  2013

A Composition of Board of Directors

  • Age range
  • Island wide
  • Educators, parents, teachers, students,
  • Representative of Island population
  • Employers, 
  • Not politically active,
  • Willing to play an active role (chair a committee?)
  • Reasonable balance- language, gender, culture, newcomers
  • Strengths  and needs


B Policies

C Organization:

  • Committees
  • Frequency of meetings
  • Expectations


D Activities

  • Workshops
  • Speakers
  • Conference
  • Dialogues with educators
  • Charlottetown or throughout Island
