Fourth findings summary reiterating research priorities and systemic failures: research access and teaching relevance, school autonomy evidence (OECD, PISA, MEI), education/schooling distinction failure, departmental goal clarification needs, pervasive lack of clarity, fear-based silos, structure misaligned with education service delivery, bureaucratic rather than field-driven direction, politicization effects, missing self-correction mechanisms, financial incentive misalignment, professional organization deficiencies, and documentation gaps.
