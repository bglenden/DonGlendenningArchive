Education 20/20 proposal (July 2014) for establishing independent math certification and testing services for individuals and employees of all age, including partnerships with existing assessment tools like TOWES. Reflects Don's emphasis on competency-based assessment independent of school credit systems.
Education Philosophy & Reform

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

July 13, 2014

Proposal

Testing service


Proposal 1: Certification service


Offer a math certification service:

  • Develop a comprehensive list of math competencies  
  • Establishing identifiable levels of achievement in Math
  • Offer a testing/qualification service  

Characteristics of service:

  • Fee for service basis
  • Independent
  • Open to companies for employees 
  • Open to individuals of any age
  • By application
  • No school credit offered
  • Student will be informed of level attained
  • On request by student, a record of student level may be forwarded to employer
  • Experience to be documented
  • Credit, if any, will be at the discretion of the school, college or university

Proposal 2: Testing Service

Offer a math testing service to employees

  • Become an agent for TOWES

Project ideas:

  • Volunteer tutoring service, Outside normal regular school hour, 15-25 year olds, employed or unembloyed
  • Fee for service basis
  • Work with employees as part of hiring process
  • Partner with professional associations
  • Committee
  • Focus on citizenship skills/citizen’s math
  • Testing service
  • Survey of available resources for people wanting help 
  • Workshop(s) on math
  • Identify competencies and levels
  • Math clubs
  • Prov math association
  • Incentives for students making high scores on Towes
