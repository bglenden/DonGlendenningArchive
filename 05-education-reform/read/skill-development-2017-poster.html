Promotional poster for a facilitated discussion on skill development for Prince Edward Island held April 5, 2017 from 7:00-8:30 pm at the Southport Room, Stratford Town Hall. The event was organized by Education 20/20 Inc. to gather Islander perspectives on provincial budget speech initiatives, including advanced manufacturing, agri-food, clean technology, digital industries, health biosciences, and clean resources. The poster highlights support for vulnerable youth skills development and integrated post-secondary learning opportunities. Views expressed at the event were to be forwarded to both PEI and Canadian governments. The contact information indicates Don's leadership role with Education 20/20 Inc., headquartered in Charlottetown.

The recent budget speech includes new dollars for innovation and skill development including:

  • Focus Areas: advanced manufacturing, Agri-food, clean technology, digital industries, health bio-sciences and clean resources

  • Skills development for vulnerable youth

  • Integrated learning opportunities for postsecondary and graduate students

  • A new organization to address skill gaps

  • Program evaluation
