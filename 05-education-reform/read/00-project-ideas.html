PROJECT IDEAS


For which we may seek funds:


Issue: Focused action group that selects an education issue, explores it in depth and from a variety of angles with a view to bringing about change.  Possible issues include: public information about education, basic skills, career training, early childhood

Surveys – become an independent  source of information in taking the pulse of people in relation to their education system

Independent Assessment Service - based on the view that teaching and determining levels should not reside in the same person/institution

Greenfield Project – sponsor an award to teams of people to develop the “best” school system for a hypothetical community

Learning Café- a space set aside for people who want to study and feel the need for occasional assistance – tutoring but not formal instruction

Student Advocate

Learning circle – formal or informal gathering of like-minded people who want to meet, exchange information and views on matters of education – it may or may not result in joint action

Education development worker:  a community education facilitator to work with the community in resolving issues pertaining to the education of its citizens

Host – organize events and activities that bring educators together to engender discussion on educational matters 

Non-credit Courses – coding, robotics, weekend courses, evening classes for any and all who are interested including children, employed people, people in prison, tourists, summer camps, religion, summer school

Hooks – public campaign(s) on subjects that are important but also high profile to attract attention to our cause – challenge for credit, home helper service, summer schools, call for proposals for study centres, 

Pathways to education

Off Island courses

For profit development company 

Self- instructional material/webinar

Learning support materials for basic skills

Subject matter conference – math

Retreat
