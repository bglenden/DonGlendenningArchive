A brief but provocative letter proposing that schools should focus on making it easier for students to enter rather than imposing barriers. This reflects Don's student-centered philosophy and belief that education should be inclusive and accessible. The brevity and directness of the piece suggest a carefully crafted editorial comment designed to prompt reflection on student access and school responsiveness to learner needs.

January 25, 2014


Editor, Charlottetown Guardian

Charlottetown, PEI


Dear Sir:

Easier to get in 


If I were designing a school. I think that I’d make it easier to get in



Sincerely


Don Glendenning

A long time student of education
