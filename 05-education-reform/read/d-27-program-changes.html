A brief letter proposing program-level interventions in education including: updating belief statements about education, establishing independent qualification bodies separate from schools, standardized curriculum development templates using competencies and rating scales, and introducing "learning managers" as student advisers. Don positions these changes as reducing friction between education system and community.
