President's report for the year ending December 31, 2018, reflecting significant project expansion. Education 20/20 secured funding from SkilsPEI to conduct a comprehensive survey of continuing education needs and opportunities in Souris, contracting with MRSB for the research. Glendenning's conclusions emphasized that continuing education represents an important but underrecognized component of the educational landscape in Island communities. The organization continued publishing "Open Education" newsletter (monthly bilingual publication) with translation support. No education award postings were accepted that year. The report identifies mathematics skills development as an ongoing research interest. Glendenning documents the organization's evolution toward substantive research projects addressing real community education gaps while maintaining advocacy and networking activities.
