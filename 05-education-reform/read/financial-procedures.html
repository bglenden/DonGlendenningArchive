Formalized accounting procedures policy for Education 20/20 Inc. establishing internal controls and operational responsibilities. Key policies include: cheques require President and Treasurer signatures; bank statements email to both officials; Tanya authorized for Revenue Canada dealings. Revenue procedures: Don collects mail, makes copies, forwards donations to Treasurer; Tanya deposits and maintains records. Expenditure procedures: Don reviews invoices, authorizes payment, forwards to Treasurer; Tanya prepares cheques for Don and Treasurer signature. Other mail handled by Don with copying and filing at Tanya's discretion. This reveals Don's dual role as President and co-signer, with clear separation of duties and financial governance protocols.


Education 20/20 Inc.


Accounting Procedures-1

Policy:

  1. Cheques require two signatures - President and Treasurer

  2. Ask Credit Union to Email statements to both President and treasurer

  3. Tanya to be authorized to deal with Revenue Canada


Revenue:

  1. Don to pick up mail

  2. Don make copies for his files

  3. Forwards donations to Treasurer

  4. Tanya deposits cheques

  5. Maintains bank deposit book


Expenditures

  1. Don to pick up mail

  2. Don make copies of invoices for his files

  3. Don authorizes for payment and forwards to Treasurer

  4. Tanya prepares cheques for signature

  5. Don will co-sign

  6. Tanya mail cheques


Other:

  1. Don to pick up mail

  2. Don to copy as he sees fit

  3. Pass to Tanya for action

  4. Tanya consults as needed

  5. Tanya maintain official files
