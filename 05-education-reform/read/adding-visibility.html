Dear Editor:

Making Learning Visible

I came across this phrase earlier today. although new to me, it seems like a welcome addition to the education improvement process. 

One way to increase visibility, is to switch from lesson plans to learning maps. Doing so would change the focus from what teachers teach to what learners learn. Some sample maps were colouful and cewative while others were simply lists. All tracked leanrse progress by topic, outcomes or competency. 

This approach provides each learner with:

  • A map of the planned learning journey
  • A place to record experiences encountered
  • A focus for discussion
  • Information for the interested parent 

The map with ratings become a record of achievement and a launch pad for further study.

Sincerely

Don Glendenning
