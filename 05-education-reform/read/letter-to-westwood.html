Don Glendenning's letter awarding $1,500 to Westwood Elementary School from the Numeracy Award for early-stage math equipment. Illustrates Don's belief in supporting numeracy development at foundational education levels.




April 7, 2020


Pamela Kennedy, Teacher

Westwood Elementary School

Cornwall, PEI

UPEI


Dear Pamela:


Congratulations. Westwood Elementary School has been selected for a contribution from the Numeracy Award in the amount od $1,500.


We are of the view that fluency with numbers is important to every Islander and are pleased to support the purchase of equipment to help make that happen in the early stages of learning. A cheque in this amount will be issued to Westwood Elementary School by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island


While we would like to meet and make a presentation, restrictions due to the pandemic make that impossible. The donor of the award wishes to remain anonymous, however, should you wish to say acknowledge it, send it to me and I will pass it along. Some day, when the pandemic is behind us, I hope to drop out and see them in use.


Again, our congratulations; I wish you every success in your chosen profession.


Sincerely



Don Glendenning

Education 2020 Inc.
