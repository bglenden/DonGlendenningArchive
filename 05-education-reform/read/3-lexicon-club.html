LEXICON CLUB


If you are reading this newsletter, you are likely eligible for membership in the Lexicon Club; in fact, you may already be a member. I live on PEI in Canada’s smallest Province, and know that there are thousands here who are eligible for membership. I’ve met and enjoyed conversations with many of them.  Many, however, would not qualify for membership and are destined to trudge the learning path alone


The LEXICON CLUB is for people who communicate clearly about education, people who differentiate between schooling and education, who know the difference between  learner and  student or instruction  and education, and even between credential and qualification. They probably are good at crossword puzzles and DACUM analysis, but that is not a requirement for membership.


The Club is fully incorporated into my thinking, applies s legal on all pats of my farm north of Finnegan’s Brook.  The LEXICON Club supports initiatives to improve the quality of education any where in Canada.  Donations to Education 20/20,  a charitable organizations will issue receipts for $10.00 or more in support of its work, has d has arranged with   Education 20/20 Inc. to accept donations and carry out activities in support of education In PEI.  All donors of $10.00 or more will be added to our mailing list

SOURIS STUDY

Education 20/20 is undertaking a survey of needs for continuing education in the Souris area of PEI.  The study will include a review of current offerings and capacity, interests and needs for further training as viewed by residents, employers and the community at large.  The final report will include a strategy for the future.  The study is being funded by the Department of Workforce and Advanced Learning through the Canada-PEI labour Market Agreements."


A CENTURY OF FEDERAL SUPPORT
