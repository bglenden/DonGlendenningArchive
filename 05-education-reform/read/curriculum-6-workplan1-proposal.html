Project proposal outlining implementation steps for universal curriculum platform focusing on mathematics competencies. Six-task workplan includes: conducting DACUM-style analysis of math competencies with 10 representative Islanders and experienced facilitator (2-day session); developing or adopting dynamic rating scale; creating holistic rating scale with local panel and community/employer input; identifying citizen skill profiles; developing competency descriptions with rationale and performance expectations (led by experienced math teachers); and developing learning guides (not included in initial project scope). Deliverable is single-sheet learner-friendly competency map adaptable to IT systems. Project originated from Education 20/20 Inc.
