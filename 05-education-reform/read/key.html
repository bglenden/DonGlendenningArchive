Planning document for a five-session "Issues in Education" series limited to 20 participants, led by Don Glendenning (892-5859) and a co-leader. Sessions cover educational overview/basics, curriculum development (with Derek McEwen, Director of English Curriculum), and three participant-determined topics. Encourages participants to bring iPads/smartphones, planned for 2015, offered in multiple communities by demand. Reflects Don's philosophy of participant-centered learning and flexible program design.
