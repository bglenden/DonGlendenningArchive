Self-funding activities


From time to time the matter of direct delivery of services has come up, we should talk about it to determine the size and nature of the need/interest and is we can or should do something about it. Some examples:


Coding

The importance of knowing a computer language is not in question; how we meet the growing demand is still unclear.  There is a growing interest and need for instruction for all ages. Maureen Kerr ‘s experience illustrates the issue; she has tapped into a niche, expanded all ages need for an organization to handle the administrative side.


Handle With Care

Alice and I have had some conversations about both the need for and funding possibilities for this program.   


Self- development courses 

I’m struck by an apparent lack of part time education other than credit courses; perhaps there is more available than I realize.


Continuing professional development

Seems to me that we have a cadre of educators on PEI whose skills could be tapped in terms of educational reform


Such a service could be available for people of any age; it could be a continuing education model like Senior’s College; there is supplemental education for the young; then there are all the employed people.  There are no end of possibilities – even that of certification.
