Education 2020

Numeracy Award

2020


PROPOSER 1: Pamela Kennedy

French Immersion Kindergarten classes 

Westwood Primary, Cornwall, PEI


Request: Financial assistance for purchase of math teaching aids or 5 homeroom classes with a total of 73 students at a cost of $2,000.

The School lacks  concrete manipulatives for teaching and learning number sense in French Immersion Kindergarten. Given the play-based, hands-on approach these classes require more manipulatives for numeracy. We are seeking to acquire large format Rekenreks for our 5 classes.  There are red and white beads used to count and add; Rekenreks are abacus style teaching aids in various sizes. A quick Google search will provide an abundance of examples.

https://youtu.be/JBEKOb7tWEY

These materials will facilitate the teaching and learning of counting to 10 and simple addition.  As we explore number sense in Kindergarten it is vital to learn the abstract concepts and to represent them with concrete materials.  Prices range from $15-500 per Rekenrek depending on size.

Request: $400 dollars per class for a total of $2,000.
