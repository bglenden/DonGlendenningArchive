This concise letter outlines key structural reforms Don believes necessary for improving PEI education: redefining government's role to policy, evaluation, and funding (not daily operations); granting schools operational autonomy; adopting criterion-referenced rather than norm-referenced assessment; shifting from content delivery to developmental learner support; and clarifying decision-making authority. The piece serves as a policy summary of themes developed more extensively in other letters, establishing the foundational framework underlying his broader reform agenda.
Education Philosophy & Reform
