All on the education payroll


No Minister has more control over the operation of a sector than the Minister of Education.  He not only determines what we will learn and when, where and by whom it will be delivered, but he has all the people who deliver education on his payroll.  Think for a moment; how many educational services can you name that are not provided directly by government employees with a direct reporting line to the minister; if the Minister’s employees don’t provide the service, you probably won’t get it.
