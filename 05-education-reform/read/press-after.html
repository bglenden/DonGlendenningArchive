Workshop on Learning


The learner and learning was the focus of discussion at a workshop sponsored by education 20/20 in Charlottetown Monday evening; all educational planning starts from there. Because of this, participants were asked to tell about their most memorable learning experiences and why they still remembered them. This was designed to put a focus on learning, the core of the education process.


Workshop identified four elements to be taken into account: learner, learning process, needs as well as the ways others can help. Presenters Alice Taylor, PEI partner of Handle with Care Program, Eileen Conway-Martin, Communications Director, Native council of PEI, and Brenda Goodine, Learning Manager, Early Childhood Education, Holland College led participants through presentations and discussions of learner differences, the learning process, and learning support activities.


Education 20/20 Inc. is a broad based, national, not-for-profit education organization, located in Charlottetown, PEI and is governed by a volunteer Board of Directors. The focus of the organization is to ensure is a quality education for every Canadianand will focus on the areas of information, encouragement and and incentives.
