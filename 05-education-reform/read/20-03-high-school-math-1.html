Proposal 20-03

High School Requirement


Education 20/20 proposes to establish competency based math level equivalent to high school graduation by:

  • Find an existing analysis that approximates the level
  • Convening a committee to revise
  • Revise analysis
  • Pilot performance based assessment
  • Pilot TOWES Assessment
