Forward-looking outline titled "Characteristics of Education Tomorrow" dated November 2013. Details desired characteristics across three levels: Education system (diverse, affordable, evidence-based, learner-centered, flexible, transparent, community-based, results-oriented), School system (includes learner choice, school autonomy, publicly funded), and Teachers (professional autonomy, accountability, independence).
