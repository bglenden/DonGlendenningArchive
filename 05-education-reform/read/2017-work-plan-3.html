This November 2017 document outlines proposed initiatives for Education 20/20 Inc. addressing identified gaps in education: information, counselling, independent grading, coordination, communication, leadership, and accountability. Specific initiatives include continuing education programs, information and counselling services, drop-in education centres, qualification authority development, instructional materials, education pathways, navigator programs, and workshops promoting an ideal school system. Additional priorities include roundtables, discussions, newsletters, assessment services, and operational projects like managing the Souris Garden Club and exploring charitable sector developments. The comprehensive list reflects Don's ambitious vision for systemic educational improvement on Prince Edward Island.
