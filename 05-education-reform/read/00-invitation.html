An invitation to write


You are invited to share your views on education through a series of occasional papers directed to issues of interest and relevance to the general public. Papers may be on any of topic and are intended to provide information, raise question, express an opinion, generate ideas and spark new thinking.


Education 20/20 is initiating the series at this time because:

  • Education is an important issue

  • Information is the fuel of change.

  • Education is under-represented issue in the public media.


Putting pen to paper is a learning activity, and a creative one at that; writing helps the writer to clarify thinking, challenge assumptions, make sense of things, improve your vocabulary , and step outside the hurly burly and think about alternatives.



Call for Papers


  • 1,000 to 2,500 words

  • Ideas, activities, innovations

  • Best practices

  • Will be posted on our website

  • Either official language

  • Topics of interest to the public

  • Provide information,

  • Spark new thinking
