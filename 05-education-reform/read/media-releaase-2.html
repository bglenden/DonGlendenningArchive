For immediate release



A new direction for education award


Collaboration will be the theme for the 2018 Education Award offered by Education 20/20; it’s intended to encourage teachers to work together to improve education of their students; “Collaboration is a key element in school improvement” says Don Glendenning, President of Education 20/20. The award, in the amount of $2,500, is available to schools throughout the Island.


The purpose of the award varies from year to year. The first two awards recognized engagement, the third recognized the application of technology throughout a school and last one recognized the wonders of mathematics, a program to demystify mathematics. Collaboration may occur within a school when two teachers work together, between schools or a school and a college or university, or even between a school and an individual or agency.


Education 20/20 is a charitable organization devoted to improving the Quality of education for all Islanders; detailed information is available at www.education2020/ca
