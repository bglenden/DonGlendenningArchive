Comprehensive framework for systemic interventions in PEI education across four levels: Education System (terminology, purpose, principles, accountability), School System (organizational structure, management, autonomy), Classroom (culture, teaching models, assessment), and Teaching/Learning Model (competencies, curriculum mapping, self-assessment).
