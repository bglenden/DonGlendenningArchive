Education 20/20 proposes a series of short educational videos (3-4 minutes each) exploring fundamental learning processes. Topics include "What happens when we learn," "The learning process," "What learners bring," "Helping learners learn," measurement of change, and reporting/feedback mechanisms. Don would lead the initial series development, with a steering committee providing oversight and external contractors handling production and evaluation. Videos target parents but would serve teachers-in-training, new teachers, workshop leaders, and presentation professionals. This reflects Don's belief that learning processes deserve attention as central to educational improvement.

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Learning Series

Background:

Learning is at the heart of gaining, or helping others gain, an education and yet, it appears to get little attention in the education process whether that be curriculum development, teacher development, assessment, grouping of students for instructional purposes.  And certainly, a better understanding of learning would be helpful to parents.



Education 20/20 Inc. proposes to address this shortage through organization of workshops on the subject but we also believe that there is a place for a series of 3-4 minute videos each providing insight into some aspects of the learning process.  The content is of the series is universal, applies to learning in any and all fields and at any level.   Content Videos will be geared for parents but should be useful to teachers in training, new teachers, workshop leaders, etc.  They also should be useful for people who make presentations on the subject.  On completion, videos will be available on Education 20/20 website.

Titles:

Based on our findings to date; subjects for which videos will be prepared are as follows:

  • What happens when we learn
  • The learning process
  • What learners bring
  • Helping learners learn
  • A learning flowchart
  • Measuring change
  • Reporting/feedback
  • Helpers

Process:

  • Prepare script
  • Pilot one video
  • Test with target audience
  • Adjust process
  • Prepare series
  • Undertake a test of all titles


Budget notes:

  • Initial series will be led by don Glendenning
  • Establish a steering committee
  • Contract the actual production to a third party
  • Contract evaluation to a third party
