Brief commentary on communication problems in PEI education during pandemic-challenged times. Don identifies three communication issues: conflating "schooling" and "education" as identical; lack of shared vision about education roles; confusion between credentials and qualifications. He notes seniors ask where one "got their education" when seeking schooling information, and employers misunderstand the distinction between credentials and qualifications. Don argues these terminological confusions raise questions about the Department's leadership and vision clarity. This document shows his concern with precise language and conceptual clarity in education discourse.
