July 6, 2013:



Dear Henry:

Just a note to show you our letterhead and to thank you for your contribution; I really liked the extra life in the original design but settled for a single colour.

I was looking for a parking space yesterday to visit the Royal Bank; thought that I would have a chance to say hello to you and Gertie inside. Went completely around an extended block and returned just as you and Gertie were leaving; thanks for vacating a spot – and one with surplus time on the meter.

Your visit to the bank was also comforting because I knew that you would be making deposits; I, on the other hand, had to withdraw funds and was worried that the bank might be running low.

This, Henry, is all by the way of again thanking you for your friendship and support for yea these many years.

Have a great summer.

Warmest regards



Don
