Don, as President of Education 20/20, invites stakeholders to 2018 annual meeting to discuss youth learning support strategies. References Occasional Paper No. 4 and proposes facilitated discussion on WHAT, WHERE, WHEN, WHO, and WHY of educational support delivery.
Education Philosophy & Reform


Gov/annua/2018


Dear …

INVITATION.

Some time ago, Education 20/20 Inc convened a youth panel to identify the kinds of support they found most helpful when learning; a report of the session was reported in Occasional Paper No. 4 and is attached to this letter. As part of our upcoming annual meeting, we propose to discuss their suggestions and the various ways that the education system colld respond.


Our annual meeting will be held on June 32 at 3:30 pm in the grand room at the Haviland Club. 2 Haviland Street, Charlottetown. The brief business meeting will convene at 3:3o followed by The W-5 of Learning support – an open discussion of the WHAT, WHERE, WHEN, WHO AND WHY of educational report . This session will be led by an external facilitator and open to the public.


We would welcome you to either or both of these sessions; please come, participate in the discussion and professional conversation to follow.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

President
