Dated March 12, 2013, this comprehensive letter argues that current school models are unsustainable due to cost escalation (rising 90% in five years), quality decline, inflexibility, technological limitations, and public dissatisfaction. Don applies business model frameworks (value, resources, processes, revenue) to education, identifying that while PEI has strengthened resources and revenue, values and delivery processes remain outdated. He contends schools must shift from content delivery to learner development and explores whether current institutional structures can support needed transformation. The letter exemplifies his analytical rigor and belief that education requires systemic innovation.
