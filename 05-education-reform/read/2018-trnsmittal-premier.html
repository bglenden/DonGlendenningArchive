Don's letter to Premier Wade MacLaughlin transmits the MRSB report with similar messaging about educational service fragmentation. By sending to the Premier, Don elevates the issue to the highest provincial level, emphasizing that coordinating education services for small communities requires leadership at the top political level.



December 19, 2018




Hon. Wade MacLaughlin, Premier

PO Box 2000

Charlottetown, PEI


Premier:


As you know, Education 20.20 Inc. contracted recently with MRSB to undertake a study the need for continuing education in the Souris area. Attached is a copy of the final report.


The report raises a number of issues and possibilities; I must say, however, that I was struck by the fragmentation of services in the area; no one, it seems, except the Premier, has responsibility for education services for the Souris community.


With all good wishes for the New Year,


Sincerely




Don Glendenning


President
