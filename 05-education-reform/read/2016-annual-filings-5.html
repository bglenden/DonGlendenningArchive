Dear Sir:


Corporation No. 822339-4

Education 20/20 Inc. was incorporated on July 18, 2012; we do not have a bank account, although we organized some activities in the name of the organization, costs were covered by Directors or friends.


Recently we received designation by Revenue Canada as a charitable organization, 80082 7081 RR0001, and plan to become active in the field including setting up a bank account, accepting donations and paying invoices. In a phone call to Revenue Canada on Thursday, March 24, 2016, I learned that returns for 2012-2015 should be filed.


Attached are completed forms. As I explained on the opening paragraph, all are nil reports because we did not receive or disburse money during those years.


If you need further information, please let me know.


Sincerely



Don Glendenning

President
