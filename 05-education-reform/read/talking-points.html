A DISCUSSION GUIDE



EDUCATION

  1. Learning is a process of gaining /changing knowledge, skills, attitudes and other attributes

  2. Education is a process of helping/supporting the learning process

  3. Our future as citizens and a community is heavily impacted by the nature and level of education of our citizens

  4. The role of a Department of Education s to encourage, support and regulate education services

LEARNER

  1. Only the learner can learn

  2. Future greatly shaped by nature and level of education

  3. Each learner learns at own rate

  4. Typical life roles: individual, citizen, student, family member, consumer and producer

COMMUNITY

  1. 190.000 citizens

  2. Future greatly shaped by nature and level of education of citizens

EDUCATION SERVICES – not ientifiied

  1. Information

  3. Instruction

  4. Assessment

  5. Progress Reports
