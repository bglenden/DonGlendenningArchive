Instructing and teaching


I sometimes wonder if teachers really teach or if they only instruct; my dictionary didn’t really help. Teaching was defined as causing or helping someone to learn; instruction was described as providing someone with knowledge or furnishing someone with information in a systematic or methodical way. The terms teacher, instructor, lecturer, professor, teaching assistant may also be employment or pay classifications.


On way of framing the difference is related to the freedom of the teacher
