Personal letter to Eileen Conway-Martin accepting her resignation as Director of Education 20/20 Inc. Don expresses understanding while noting the timing was difficult. He warmly acknowledges her significant contributions including experience, insights, communication skills, facilitation abilities, and leadership in the school engagement award process. The letter reveals Don's appreciation for collaborative relationships and his ability to recognize team members' unique strengths. His offer to keep the door open reflects his commitment to retaining connections with valued colleagues.
Education Philosophy & Reform



October 23, 2013



Ms. Eileen Conway-Martin

34 Beechwood Avenue, PO Box 191

O’Leary, PE C0B 1V0



Dear Eileen:

This will acknowledge your letter of October 16 and your decision to resign as a Director of Education 20/20 Inc. I am sorry to see you leave just at a time when we are about to move into the public domain, however, I respect your decision and accept your resignation.

I thank you for your help in shaping Education 20/20 Inc. Your experience, your insights, your facility with words helped keep us focused; your facilitation skills kept us engaged, and your contributions of time, energy and ideas motivated all of us.

Engagement is fundamental to the change process, and I thank you both for your leadership to date and willingness to continue to lead our School engagement Award process. Thank you

I enjoyed working with you, getting to know you and working on shared interests; and thanks for your friendship. We’ll keep the door open and hope someday that you will rejoin the team.



Warmest regards



Don Glendenning
