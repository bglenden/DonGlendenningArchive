Support document detailing governance proposals for the First Annual Meeting. Sections address: (A) Directors - proposing expansion from 3 to 8 Directors with 3-year staggered terms and re-election provisions; (B) Members - admitting Directors to membership status while postponing external recruitment, recognizing the broader "Education 20/20 Network" of meeting attendees; (C) Committees - establishing board-approved committee structure normally chaired by Directors. These structural proposals demonstrate thoughtful organizational design, emphasizing term limits, succession planning, and community integration.

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Annual Meeting

For the Year ending December 31, 2012

Date and time:     March 28, 2013 at 1:15 am

Meeting No.:     1

Support Document 1-2

Subject:                     Governance


A  DIRECTORS:

        Background

  • Article 3 of the incorporation provides for 1 to 15 Directors
  • Article 40 of the Bylaws states that, subject to the articles, the Board of directors may, by resolution, make, amend, or repeal any bylaws that regulate the activities or affairs of the Corporation.
  • Currently the board consists of three Directors
  • Bylaw 28  states that “Directors shall be elected to hold office for a term expiring not later than the close of the next annual meeting following the election

Proposal: 

  • To increase the number of Directors from three to eight
  • Increase the length of the term of office from one to three years
  • Stage the appointment dates so that one third of the Directors retire each year
  • Provide for the re-election of Directors for a second three-year term
  • Require Directors to take at least a one year break at the end of the second appointment before being eligible for new appointment 

B  MEMBERS

Background:

  • Article 6 of the incorporation provides for the Corporation to “establish one class of members”.
  • Article 40 of the Bylaws states that, subject to the Articles, the Board of Directors may, by resolution, make, amend, or repeal any bylaws that regulate the activities or affairs of the Corporation.
  • Currently the board consists of three members

Proposal 

  • Admit new Directors to membership, thus making our members and Directors the same people.
  • Postpone, at least for the coming year, the identification, solicitation, or admittance of new members
  • Consider all people who have attended meetings or otherwise expressed support, as being part of the Education 20/20 Network.   
  • Defer consideration of dues until the matter of membership is resolved.


C  COMMITTEES

Background:

  • Under Article 33 of the Bylaws ‘The Board may from time to time appoint any committee or other advisory body, as it deems necessary or appropriate for such purposes and, subject to the Act, with such powers as the board shall see fit. 


Proposal: 

  • Membership and terms of reference be subject to Board Approval
  • Committees normally be chaired by s Director
  • Committees normally include community members
