CALCULATE GROSS AND NET PAY

Competency description

Mathematics Code 2-3

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

RATIONALE

The gig economy is real. People work for a variety of employers, others are self employed and still others do a c0mbination of both. As a result, a person’s income may come from a variety of sources, varying amounts and irregular periods. Paying and reporting becomes a bit of a nightmare; calculations must be accurate.


ELABORATIONS

Upon development of this skill, you will be able to:

  1. Calculate employee wages

  2. Calculate employee salaries

  3. Calculate income from contracts

  4. Calculate income from commissions

  5. Calculate pay from piecework


TYPICAL ACTIVITIES

The following learning are representative of activities are intended to assist you in the development of the above-mentioned skill. They are representative of the work you are expected to do this guide.

  1. Aggregate pay slips

  2. Interpret company policies

  3. Report errors

  4. Record transaction


PERFORMANCE ASSESSMENT

You can use performance assessments to assess what you can already do in the way of assessment and also as items showing progress. To demonstrate your performance you will have:

  1. Familiarized yourself with relevant tax law

  2. Gathered information from pay records

  3. Used pay slips as a basis
