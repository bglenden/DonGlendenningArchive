Education and instruction

Welcome to Don’s Posts.  I’ve had some instruction on how to post my blogs and am hoping to become a regular. I also plan to keep my posts short, and focused mostly on education.

My definition of education is a broad one; education refers to “experiences that have a formative effect on the way a person thinks, feels and acts” – whenever, wherever and however they came about.  Schooling, or instruction, is more limited and is usually equated with receiving instruction under the direction of teachers in schools.   I must admit that I am puzzled by the fact that so many educators use the terms interchangeably; some friends, more cynical than I. believe that educators use the terms interchangeably just to confuse us. 

I welcome your comments and hope you’ll return for Blog No. 2 when I plan to share my newest significant educational insight.
