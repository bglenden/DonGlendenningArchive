Welcome letter from Don Glendenning, President of Education 20/20 Inc., to Gordon MacDonald and Sharon Cameron inviting them to join as directors. Expresses appreciation for work on the "transition initiative" and welcomes new board members. Demonstrates Don's leadership style and his engagement with community board development.
Education Philosophy & Reform


Gordon MacDonald

Sharon Cameron


Dear Gordon:


I was delighted with our recent conversation and your interest in joining us as a Director of Education 20/20 Inc.  


Formalizing your appointment will take place at our annual meeting  on Wednesday, June 28 at 4:30 pm in the upstairs Board Room at the Haviland Club; we hope you can join us as we report on activities for 2016 and make plans for 2017.


I’ve enjoyed working with you on the “transition initiative” and, although much remains to be done,  I consider it a bit of a break through in our efforts to assist you people in transitioning to another phase of development. 

Welcome and warmest regards


Don Glendenning

President
