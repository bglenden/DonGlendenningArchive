Two letters from Don Glendenning as Education 20/20 Inc. President. First letter congratulates Kinkora Regional High School on their nominated parent-led engagement initiative that demonstrated school-community collaboration. Second template expresses appreciation to non-winning nominees, acknowledging the breadth of nominations across all educational levels (early childhood through high school) and the diverse engagement activities presented. The letters exemplify Don's commitment to inclusive recognition, appreciation for all participants, and his understanding that award programs strengthen community engagement and educational culture.

Kinkora Regional High School


On behalf of Education 20.20 Inc, I want to thank you for your nomination Kinkora Regional High School for the Engagement Award.


The eleven nominations painted a picture of engagement activities in Island schools.  Nominations came from across the Island and representing all levels, early childhood centres, elementary schools and high schools.  Some nominations covered a wide range of engagement activities while others were quite focused; some were generated by members of the school or centre while others came from parents and the public.


We were impressed with your nomination, that the proposal was parent-led and provided such a wide range of engagement activities; it also presented a picture of a school and community working in harmony for the benefit of students.


Again, I thank you for the nomination; I will be in touch again shortly about the actual presentation of the cheque.


Regards


Don Glendenning

President

Education 20/20 Inc.   





Others

On behalf of Education 20.20 Inc, I want to thank you for your nomination for the Engagement Award.

There were eleven nominations in all and together they painted a picture of a wide range of engagement activities in Island schools.  Nominations came from across the Island and representing all levels, early childhood centres, elementary schools and high schools.  Some nominations covered a wide range of engagement activities while others were quite focused; some were generated by members of the school or centre itself while others came from parents and the public.


I want to thank you for your efforts and for documenting the positive activities put in place to encourage and support learning. Unfortunately, however, your nomination was not selected for the award.  We expect to offer a similar award next year and look forward to your further participation.


Regards


Don Glendenning

President

Education 20/20 Inc.
