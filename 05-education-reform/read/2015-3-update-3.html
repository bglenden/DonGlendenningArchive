Activity Update-3

April 15, 2015

_____________________________________________________________________________________


I know that all are busy, some have been travelling, and hope all are surviving the winter weather; the following notes are intended to keep you up-to-date.

Completed:

  1. Virtual tour of PEI education: convened on March 3 as planned; only a half dozen participants so session converted to an open discussion.

  2. Discussion on Education- Paused in our planning process to share views on education; as a result, we were able to develop aS Call for Action, which has now bee made public.

  3. Conversations – invitational series of presentations by Island educators; launched on January 21 from four to 6:00 at Haviland Club with Dr. Tamara Hubley-as guest speaker.

  4. DACUM/HC workshop- at invitation of Peter Rukavina, I made a presentation to PEIHSF and some Directors about DACUM model developed in Nova Scotia and used at Holland College.

  5. Workplace Learning PEI – arranged for a presentation by Lori Johnston and team at Workplace Learning PEI on training, assessment and support services provided to employers on PEI.

  6. Information – published Newsletter No. 27; recirculated Occasional Paper No. 1, Preparedness, and released Occasional Paper No. 2, the Eight Year Study; thanks to Tom we now have a blog site

  7. PLAR and Challenge for Credit – have asked Minister for opportunity for Bill and I to make presentation; request has not been acknowledged yet

  8. CRA – mailed application seeking charitable status

  9. School Award: Nomination papers distributed throughout the school system thanks to Imelda Arsenault, a number of hits on our website but we do not know the number of nominations copied, nominations close on April 25; our award committee includes Donald DesRoches, Peter Russell, Dave MacCabe and Don Glendenning, Ex officio.

  10. Learning path: Draft paper ready for distribution

  11. Call for Action: given the announcement by the PEIHSF of a leaders debate, in consultation with VP and Sec-Treasurer, decided to release the Call for Action.

  12. Workplace Learning PEI: A presentation by Lori Johnston, Director, and Mark Douglas of their work with both individuals and companies in PEI, may well be one of the best examples an education model serving the needs of individuals.

  13. Travel: I will be out of Province on personal travel from April 21 to May 5.
