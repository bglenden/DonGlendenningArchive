Minutes from reconvened meeting of Education 20/20 Inc. held June 28, 2016 at Haviland Club due to lack of quorum at initial June 15 meeting. Two motions were conducted via electronic email poll: approval of 2016 budget (moved by B. Whelan, seconded by T. McRoberts) carried unanimously; and retention of current officers with modification splitting the Secretary-Treasurer position, with Alice Taylor as Secretary and Tanya Craig as Treasurer, carried with one abstention. Don Glendenning remained President, Bill Whelan Vice President.
Education Philosophy & Reform

12. The meeting reconvened at the Haviland Club on June 28, 2016:

Agenda:

  • 2016 budget
  • 2016 officers

Because the meeting lacked a quorum, members agreed to poll Directors electronically; on date, the following two motions were put to Directors via Email:

MOTION 1:

Moved by B. Whelan, and seconded by T. McRoberts


"That the 2016 Education 2020 Annual Budget (circulated and attached) be approved."

Carried unanamously

MOTION 2:

Moved by A. Taylor and seconded by K. McQuaid

"That the current officers of Education 2020 (below) remain in place for the coming year and, that the office of Secretary-Treasurer be split, with Alice Taylor remaining as Secretary and Tanya Craig assuming the office of Treasurer."

Don Glendenning, President

Bill Whelan, Vice President

Alice Taylor, Secretary

Tanya Craig, Treasurer


Carried with one abstention

There being no other business, the fourth annual meeting adjourned
