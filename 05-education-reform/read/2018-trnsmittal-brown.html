This formal transmittal letter from Don Glendenning, as President of Education 20/20 Inc., sends the MRSB final report to Hon. Jordan Brown, Minister of Education, Early Learning and Culture. Don notes his concern about fragmentation of educational services in Souris and identifies that without leadership, no single entity (except the Premier) has clear responsibility for coordinating community education services.
Education Philosophy & Reform



December 19, 2018




Hon. Jordan Brown, Minister

Education, Early Learning and Culture

PO Box 2000

Charlottetown, PEI


Minister:


As you know, Education 20.20 Inc. contracted recently with MRSB to undertake a study the need for continuing education in the Souris area. Attached is a copy of the final report.


The report raises a number of issues and possibilities; I must say, however, that I was struck by the fragmentation of services in the area; no one, it seems, except the Premier, has responsibility for education services for the Souris community.


With all good wishes for the New Year,


Sincerely




Don Glendenning


President
