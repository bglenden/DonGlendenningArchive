This proposal document presents the concept of a Community Education Service for Souris/Eastern Kings. It describes Education 20/20's proposed education service agency to encourage and support local educational services. Governance structure specifies three-person local board with community representation (not provider representatives), plus advisory committee of education leaders. The document identifies potential host organizations, describes ideal manager characteristics (self-starter, mobile, IT knowledgeable, bilingual preferred, contract position), and notes community benefits including increased training, local capacity development, and strengthened social capital.


Community Education Service-1

Souris/Eastern Kings, PEI

3-Souris;/2019/Service

Background:

Souris is an urban centre in eastern Prince Edward Island; it has a population in 2016 of 1,053; with additional 2745 people living in the immediate surrounding area. Together they make a community one of 3,798 people. The economy of the area is dominated by seasonal industries of fishing, aquaculture, agriculture and tourism. A recent study of the area revealed an interest and needs for additional continuing education services in the area.In response, Education20/20 proposes an education service agency to encourage and support educational services for the Souris area.


Working with the local community, The Centre will:

  • Encourage and support education services for residents of the area

  • Seek out, organize courses, facilitate and promote training

  • Promote and advertise

  • Organize and host educational activities of interest to the community

  • Represent the community’s interests in matters of education

  • Liaise with other providers of educational services


Community Board

  • Advisory committee of local education leaders
