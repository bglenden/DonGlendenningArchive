Agenda for the January 6, 2016 second stakeholder meeting held at Stratford Town Hall, continuing the Preparedness-2 initiative. This meeting narrowed focus to numeracy/mathematics after determining the initial scope was too broad. The agenda emphasizes a "planning framework" for identifying key mathematical skills, introducing participants' roles, and determining next steps. The document reflects adaptive project management—the group pivoted from examining all preparedness dimensions to concentrating on mathematics as the foundational competency. This demonstrates Don's pragmatic approach to large reform initiatives, working within realistic constraints to achieve measurable progress.
