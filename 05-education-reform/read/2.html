Education Philosophy & Reform

Dear Editor:

Definition of Education

PEI needs a definition of “education”.  The Education Act of a number of years ago included a definition but I learned recently that “education”  had been replaced recently with ”school”.  Now, I have no objection to a definition of “school” but, in my books, education and schooling are not the same and using them interchangeably is confusing. Furthermore. while we refer to our education system, it appears that what we now have is really a school system.

Given the importance and complexity of education, I urge the Department to develop an education taxonomy soon and circulate it widely throughout the education community.

Sincerely

Don Glendenning
