Promotional invitation emphasizing Education 20/20's educational vision: removing content from center stage, moving beyond standardized tests, emphasizing relational skills and project-based learning with student/teacher autonomy. The film features High Tech High's approach to preparing students for the innovation age. The invitation notes priority for mixed groups including students, teachers, principals, and parents—reflecting educational research on transformative learning. This document shows Don's ability to distill complex educational philosophy into compelling, accessible messaging for diverse audiences.


Imagine education where:

  • Content is taken off centre stage

  • Success is not measured by standardized tests

  • Relational skills are emphasized

  • Project based learning is key

  • students and teachers are given and trust

  • students and teachers are allowed to find their own approach to learning”
