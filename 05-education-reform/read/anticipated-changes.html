Don summarizes anticipated changes in education from reviewing eight literature lists (ranging 5-10 points each). His synthesis identifies six key anticipated changes: personalized learning, competencies (shift from traditional knowledge transmission), increased school autonomy, schools as learning hubs (community centers), more relevant content, and greater student and teacher agency. He suggests it would be valuable to develop a PEI-specific list of anticipated changes to guide local education reform.

Dear Editor:

Anticipated changes

I scanned the literature recently to  check anticipated changes in education; found eight lists ranging from 5 to 1 0 points each. Couldn’t compare directly but below is my summary of most anticipated changes.

  • Personalized learning
  • Competencies
  • Increased school autonomy
  • Schools as learning hubs
  • More relevant content
  • Greater student and teacher agency.

It would b interesting to make a PEI list.

Sincerely

Don Glendenning
