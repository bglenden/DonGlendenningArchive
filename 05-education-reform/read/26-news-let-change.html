Reform/change


Each change of government brings an opportunity for improvement in education; new governments cannot be blamed for the system they inherit but they must take ownership for what happens while they are in charge.  The only changes we hear about are the ones that involve large sums of money. It appears that our government is focused on efficiency rather than effectiveness.



By now, I hoped to see and hear rumours of change, even small ones, such as:

  • Increased autonomy for the day to day operation at one of our schools.
  • Appointment of the leader for the change process.

  • A replacement for our 19 year old educational philosophy
