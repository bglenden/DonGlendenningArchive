FIRST STEPS-4


Department:

  1. Separate oversight and operations

  2. Publish a taxonomy of key terms

  3. Retrieve, review and publish definition of education

  4. Review and publish role of Department

  5. Identify education services

  6. Adopt competencies as a base for Instruction and tracking

  7. Develop curriculum maps

  8. Develop learning guide for each outcome

  9. Develop profiles

  10. Institute self rating
