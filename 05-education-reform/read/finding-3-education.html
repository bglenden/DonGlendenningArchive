Third findings document (April 3, 2024) consolidating conclusions from research, conversations, and experience. Explicitly states PEI has a school system rather than education system. Documents that the Department of Education lacks a definition of education, confuses educational branches with levels, and has inappropriately narrowed its role to curriculum delivery. Identifies knowledge and definitional gaps, recommends external standards body, calls for public dialogue on education purpose, emphasizes need for transparency, collaboration, and documentation. Suggests restructuring government involvement to promotion and regulation rather than operation.
