Guidelines for Occasional Papers

Background:


Information is the fuel of change and, while the availability of relevant information does not guarantee change, it certainly supports it. Education 20/20 Inc. is of the view that providing the public with relevant information, informs, influences and may move to action addition to being useful in its own right, will support the public in its quest for greater participation in the education process. Proposal

To gather and disseminate information geared to the public at large especially those with children still in the education system. Education first proposes to do this through a series of occasional papers focused on topics gleaned from our conversations with educators and the public alike. The primary focus will be on topics that, in the view of Education 20/20 Inc. contribute to a quality education for every Canadian.

Guidelines

  • Each issue is a stand-alone issue

  • Limited to two sides of a standard page

  • Format as attached

  • Guest authors will be invited

  • Preference for Island authors
