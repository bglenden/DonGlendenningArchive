Comprehensive listing of all schools and learning centers nominated for the 2014 and 2015 Education Engagement Awards under Don Glendenning's Education 20/20 organization. The 2014 nominations represent diverse Island institutions at all educational levels, including early childhood centers, elementary schools, and high schools from Tignish to St. Peter's Bay. Includes contact information and nominator details, providing administrative record of the award program's reach across Prince Edward Island's educational landscape.
