EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

DRAFT

Board Regulation No.  


Category: Committees

Subject: Fund Development

Effective Date:

General

The Fund Development Committee plays a lead role on behalf of the Board of Directors in securing funds in support of Education 20/20mactivities/


Specific duties:

 The committee shall:

  • Identify, research and establish donor prospects
  • Manage and cultivate opportunities
  • Develop and implement strategies to raise awareness
  • Develop and maintain a donor recognition program
  • Identify and develop fund raising materials
  • Recommend policies for fund development
  • Provide leadership and direction 
  • Provide regular reports to the Board of Directors

Committee Membership:

The committee shall ordinarily be composed of a minimum of 5 members with three coming  from the community at large. The President is an ex officio member ; Directors who are not members of the committee , may attend committee meetings as non-voting members.  Committee membership be increased with Board approval.  Members serve without remuneration.


Chair:

The committee Chair shall ordinarily be a Director and appointed by the board of Directors; the committee may select a vice chair if deemed necessary.


Term of Office

Committee members serve for three years


Meetings

The Committee meets at the call of the chair


Voting

Motions arising at any Committee meeting shall be decided by majority vote


Reporting

The Chair reports to the Board of directors; minutes are distributed to committee members and may be distributed to directors
