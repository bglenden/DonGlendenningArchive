A comprehensive proposal (October 2014 and March 2014 versions) for establishing and maintaining the Open Education newsletter as a sustainable publication for Education 20/20 Inc. The document outlines the newsletter's purpose to inform the public on educational matters, details target audiences (directors, media, elected representatives, and ~200 subscribers), and proposes solutions for bilingual publication, funding, distribution, and editorial governance. The proposal addresses critical issues including circulation growth, sponsor arrangements with CFPEI, and readership surveys. The newsletter was envisioned as monthly in frequency with single-page, two-column bilingual format, demonstrating Don's strategic thinking about educational communication.

October 20, 2014

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Open Education

Background

Our newsletter, Open Education, is designed to inform the public on matters of education and is now assuming a regular monthly schedule and format; it is published in electronic format each issue has a quote of the day, an opinion piece and a column for announcements, links, items in print, etc.  The current mailing list consists of directors;  media, elected reps and about 140 “friends” for a total of just under 200.  It is also posted permanently on our website.


Proposal: 

I suggest that we review the issue of newsletter, decide whether or not we want to maintain it and, if so, both routinize it and put it on a self-sustaining basis including.  Issues include:

  • Bilingual
  • Format
  • Steering committee
  • Editor
  • Increase circulation
  • Image
  • Funding (sponsor, advertising, appeal)
  • Arrangements with CFPEI
  • Survey of readership
  • Increasing readership
