Don's formal workshop proposal for Education 20/20 Inc., designed to establish a list of pressing issues in education through community consultation. Demonstrates Don's commitment to evidence-based educational advocacy and inclusive stakeholder engagement methodology.

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Support Document


Subject: Issues Workshop Proposal Prepared by: Don Glendenning

Number: SD: 6.2 Date: January xxx 2013


_____________________________________________________________________________________


Objective: to establish a list, perhaps only a preliminary one, of pressing issues in education.


Background: While we each have our views as to the most pressing issues in education today,

there is a need to either confirm or amend our list – or even build a new list from

scratch involving community input.


Date and Time: February 19, 2013 from 4-6:00 pm.


Location: Room 125 at Royalty Centre, Enman Crescent


Participants: Between 40-50 people by invitation from previous participants; need flipchart paper

and felt pens?


Format: After initial presentation, groups will break out into groups of 6-10 for discussion

purposes. Steve will facilitate the group.


Agenda:

  • Brief overview of education 20/20 - Don

  • Group discussion

  • Reporting

  • Discussion of plans for 2013 with invitation to participate


Refreshments: Coffee and bottled water available


Sponsor: Community foundation of Prince Edward Island and R. Birt and Associates
