A comprehensive activity report documenting Education 20/20 Inc.'s achievements from incorporation in July 2012 through December 2014, including workshops on early childhood education and learning, the launch of the Open Education newsletter, the Excellence in Education Award (given to Kinkora Regional High School in 2014), and various presentations and consultations. The report shows Don's leadership in building the organization's mission to advance education in Canada.
