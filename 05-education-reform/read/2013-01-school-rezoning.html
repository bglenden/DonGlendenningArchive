Dear Sir:

School Rezoning


After reading an item in this morning’s Guardian on school rezoning, I decided to do a quick scan of the internet to familiarize myself with the issue.  Most of the “zoning” items I found referred to issues of regulating traffic near schools.


A second search, this time on “school choice” produced a number items of interest including the fact that this is there is a National Centre for School Choice located in Peabody College at Vanderbilt University.  The second point, and an interesting coincidence, is that January xx to  is being celebrated as national School Choice Week.


In Alberta, I am told that students ay choose any school.  I believe that should be our starting position and then, we search for restrictions, if any.



Feel free to share these views and your views, whether or not they are the same, with the new school board, and with me at  HYPERLINK "mailto:standards2020@gmail.com" standards2020@gmail.com 

Sincerely



Don Glendenning
