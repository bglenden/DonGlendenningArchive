Public announcement for October 2013 workshop on learning hosted by Education 20/20 Inc. at Quality Inn in Charlottetown, chaired by Dr. Don Glendenning, featuring presentations and roundtable discussions on frameworks for educational decision-making.
Education Philosophy & Reform

Workshop on Learning

A framework for making decisions about learning will be the subject of a workshop on October 21.  The workshop, sponsored by Education 20/20, will be held at the Quality Inn in Charlottetown starting at 7:00 p.m. and will include a presentation by Alice Taylor, PEI partner of Handle with Care Program, Eileen Conway-Martin, Communications Director, Native council of PEI, and Brenda Goodine, Learning Manager, Early Childhood Education, Holland College.  Dr. Don Glendenning, President Emeritus of Holland College will chair the session.  


The session will include both formal presentations and round table discussions, and participants will be encouraged to formulate their own elevator speech on the subject.  The public is invited to attend.  For further information phone 892-2060
