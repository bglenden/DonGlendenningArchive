This planning table outlines proposed public information sessions for parents and the general public, indicating Education 20/20's commitment to community engagement and public education. Suggested topics include emerging occupations (with interest level of 1), helping with math (level 4), and educating grandchildren (level 4), with identified presenters from education institutions and the private sector. The document reflects Don's understanding that education improvement requires public understanding and participation. The selection of panelists including UPEI faculty and Holland College staff indicates his collaborative approach to drawing expertise from PEI's educational institutions.
