Course outline for "Education System Overview" study group - five 2-hour sessions examining PEI's education system. Includes virtual tour of current educational philosophy, delivery system overview, professional support services, continuing education opportunities, and informal networking with educational leaders. Designed for participants with interest in education and willingness to engage in discussion. Reflects Education 2020's philosophy of community-based learning and transparency.

Course Outline

Prog/course


TITLE: Education System overview

OBJECTIVE: To increase our understanding of the education system on Prince Edward Islands

PRE-REQUISITES: An interest in education, a willingness to participate in information gathering and discussion

LEADER(S):

HOURS: Five 2 hour sessions

PLACE, DATE AND TIME: TBA

SPECIAL PROVISIONS: Access to internet an ability to print occasional papers

____________________________________________________________________________________


DESCRIPTION

  • Virtual tour- a self study session during which we familiarize our selves with the current philosophy of education, roles of government and school boards, organization of Department and School Boards, role of principals and teachers, alignment, professional role of PEIIF

  • Delivery system – overview of support made available to students on their learning path including Instruction, including policy setting, funding, providing information, monitoring, quality control from a students perspective

  • Professional support – support and roadblocks made available to invite senior personnel from Department and school boards for an overview of professional support servives made available to students, parents and teachers

  • Continuing education – Overview of what’s available to adult Islanders

  • Meet the players – an informal session to meet and chat informally with a variety of educational leaders including zeudents, teachers et.
