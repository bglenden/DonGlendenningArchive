Assessment rubric for evaluating written work using six-level scale. Level 1 (lowest): disorganized, unfocused, many errors, limited vocabulary. Level 2: generally organized, limited vocabulary, significant errors. Level 3: organized with acceptable structure and grammar. Level 4: organized with few errors in grammar and sentence structure. Level 5: well organized, precise vocabulary, good sentence structure, some creativity. Level 6 (highest): creatively written while maintaining acceptable standards.
