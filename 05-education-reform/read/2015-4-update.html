Activity Update-4

June 1, 2015

_____________________________________________________________________________________


Completed:

  1. New Directors: We are pleased to welcome two new Directors, Maureen Kerr, a social media and technology trainer, and Katherine McQuaid, Director of Training, at the Academy of Learning.

  2. Resignation: Eileen Conway-Martin has tendered her resignation; Eileen has been with us since the beginning, has recently retired, lives in O’Leary and wearied of winter travel

  3. Letter of congratulations sent to all MLA’s along with Call for Action and brochures

  4. Received our first out-of-Province Email, other than from Brenda: it was an enquiry about Colonel Gray ending the policy of granting exemptions.

  5. PLAR and Challenge for Credit – we asked Minister of Education for an opportunity for Bill and I to make presentation on P:AR; request has not been acknowledged.

  6. CRA – Application acknowledged; additional technical data provided; was told that the evaluation would take 5-6 months

  7. School Award: as I recall, there were 20 hits on this item on website; 3 nominations received – Queen charlotte Band Program, Prince Street Elementary School and Spring Park School; information being reviewed by Awards Committee: Donald DesRoches, Peter Russell, Dave MacCabe and Don Glendenning, Ex officio.

  8. Learning path: About 25 people attended meeting on May 14; Path being revised based on comments and will be published as an occasional paper.

  9. Occasional papers: I’ve spoken to s few people about occasional papers but have not circulated a formal notice yet.

  10. Workshops: none scheduled yet for 2015-16
