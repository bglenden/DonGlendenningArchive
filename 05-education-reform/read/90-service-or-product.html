Don explores whether education should be conceptualized as a product or service, drawing on his own experience becoming a teacher and his observations of education systems. He argues that schools often treat education as a factory producing standardized products, but suggests that character development differentiates learners. The piece reflects his lifelong commitment to reimagining education delivery. Published February 20, 2020 in Open Education No. 86.
