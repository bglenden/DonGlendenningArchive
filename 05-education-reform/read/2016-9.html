This year-end 2016 report shows Don managing complex stakeholder relationships in the Souris Garden Club project. He presented a four-part proposal to private donors for a summer program including a 12-week program for 20 students, indoor green space development at Souris Regional School, promotion of garden clubs elsewhere on PEI, and a Camp Bean competition. Don notes that while donors responded positively without committing funds, Education 20/20's direct involvement would create bureaucratic burden rather than value. This reveals his strategic thinking about organizational scope and the appropriate role of Education 20/20 in community initiatives. He also reports meeting with Sally Coles at the Guardian about media coverage and remaining focused on Canada 150 Fund applications.

Activity Update- 2016-9

December 31. 2016

Activities:

  1. Distributed Newsletter No. 47

  2. Talked with both Tom and Jane White-Hassler recently and both are well.

  3. Presented proposal to a private donor for financial support for a summer program by the Souris Garden Club; Proposal in four parts(!) A continuation of the basic 12-week program for 20 students, (2) indoor green space at the Souris Regional school, (3) promotion of garden clubs for children elsewhere in PEI, (4) Camp Bean competition for schools; we had a positive response from the prospective donors but no commitment of funds yet. I had anticipated more of a role for Education 20/20 but it became obvious during development that 20/20 involvement would simply increase the bureaucracy and not really add to the project.

  4. Met with Sally Coles at the Guardian about activities of Education 20/20

  5. No response yet on our proposals to the Canada 150 Matching Grant fund; CFPEI is now accepting another round of applications. Would welcome ideas and partnerships,

  6. The second EdCan Workshop sponsored by the Canadian Education Association is scheduled in Halifax on Tuesday, May 9.

  7. Delighted to see the item on our Education Award in the Charlottetown Guardian in late November.

  8. My thanks to all for your support during 2016 and Best Wishes for 2017





Reminder: Next regular meeting of Directors will be on Wednesday, January 11

at the Haviland Club commencing at 4:30 pm
