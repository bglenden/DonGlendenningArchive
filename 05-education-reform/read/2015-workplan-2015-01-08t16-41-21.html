Workplan -2015

January 2015

____________________________________________________________________________________

Existing Objectives

  1. To promote education and educational opportunity for every Canadian

  2. To exercise leadership in the improvement in all facets of education

  3. Foster cooperation between and among all stakeholders

  4. Undertake and support research and innovation

  5. Improve public understanding of the education system

Proposed Objectives

  1. Advance education by organizing professional development and leadership for people who plan, administer and deliver educational programs

  2. Advance education through strengthening linkages between education partners

  3. Advance education by catering to needs of life long learners

  4. Advance education by supporting research and innovation, and promoting best practices.
