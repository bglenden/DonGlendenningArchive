Education Philosophy & Reform

Date: February 8, 2016

To: Education 20/20 Board of Directors

From: Tim McRoberts

Regarding: Continuation of Planning Sessions 



On February 2, 2016, the Education 20/20 Board of Directors was invited to the Haviland Club in Charlottetown, PE to continue a planning session facilitated by Tom White-Hassler.  The purpose of the session was to help Board members articulate a vision for Education 20/20 by exploring larger, thematic, guiding goals and related, measurable outcomes.  In addition to the facilitator, attendees included Dr. Don Glendenning, Alice Taylor, and Tim McRoberts.  After a series of activities intended to help the group freely consider and craft goal and outcome statements, participants voted upon the statements as to their level of importance.  It should be noted that, in numerous cases, a statement may have received 0 votes because it was closely matched another statement.  The following summary is provided after the goal statements and outcomes were organized: 

Goal #1: Contribute to Improving the Quality of Education for Every Canadian.

The following outcomes emerged as to how Goal #1 could be realized:

    • Acknowledge different ways of knowing (2 votes)
    • Break down barriers (1 vote)
    • Run demonstrations (1 vote)
    • Identify best-practices (1 vote)
    • Support social-emotional learning (0 votes)
    • Convene discussion with formalized stakeholders (0 votes)

Goal #2: To Participate in the Unfolding of Learning Communities throughout Canada

The following outcomes emerged as to how Goal #2 could be realized:

    • Engage parents and other citizens (3 votes)
    • Help the community make their views known (3 votes)
    • Acknowledge and celebrate the achievements of others (3 votes)
    • Lobby decision makers (3 votes)
    • Find champions (3 votes)
    • Plan and facilitate conferences and workshops (3 votes)
    • Provide instruction (0 votes)
    • Increase the flow of communication (1 vote)
    • Grow the presence of Education 20/20 (0 votes)
    • Increase accountability (0 votes)
    • Get more people talking about education (0 votes)

The session concluded with agreement that a summary of the findings would be shared with all Board members.  It is expected that further discussion will take place in regards to the goals and outcomes which emerged during the session.

Respectfully submitted,

Tim.
