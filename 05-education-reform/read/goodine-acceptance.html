This February 19, 2016 letter is Don's gracious acceptance of Brenda Goodine's resignation as a Director of Education 20/20 Inc. Goodine, who has relocated to Alberta, resigns to allow local representation on the board. Don thanks her for her foundational support during the organization's formation and expresses appreciation that the organization's growth and impending charitable status represent validation of her early confidence. He acknowledges the unique nature of Education 20/20 in the education sector and notes the challenge of engaging teachers as active partners. The letter demonstrates Don's diplomatic relationship management and his understanding of organizational transitions.
Education Philosophy & Reform




February 19, 2016

Ms. Brenda Goodine

405 -10903 105 Avenue

Edmonton Alberta, T5K 2N6


Dear Brenda:


This will acknowledge your letter of February 19, 2016 and your resignation as a Director of Education 20/20 Inc.


First I want to thank you for your encouragement and support during our founding years.

Ours is a rather unique organization, especially in the field of education, so it was, and still is important to have the support of leaders in the field, and I am pleased to say that our support is growing.


The nature of education is such that it will likely take a while for teachers engage with us as active partners in the education process. The impending acceptance as a charitable organization is an important step in that direction, and will enable us to be more active, specific and public in our initiatives. What we do next is very much on our agenda at the moment


It was great to hear from you and to know that all is well.


Again, thanks for our contributions.


Warmest regards




Don Glendenning

President
