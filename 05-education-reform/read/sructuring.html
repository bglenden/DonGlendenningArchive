Letter showing government departments typically organize by function/service (Agriculture by crops/livestock/policy; Health by care/children/drugs; Transportation by drivers/roads). Observes PEI Education appears organized around clients served rather than services offered. Advocates restructuring around essential skills, life roles, or service functions to refocus on education service provision.


Restructuring called for-1

D-22-3

My scan of Government Departments shows them to be organized around major fields or services. The Department Agriculture is organized around Land, Crops, Livestock, Policy and Supports; Health is organized around Care, Children, Coved, Drugs, I-Health, Health Services and Hospitals, etcand Transportationy is organized around Drivers, Roads and infrastructure, Highway Safety, MV Registration and Service.

The same approach would see the Department of Education organized around essential skills, arts, science, mathematics, literature, technology, etc.. Or better still, around life roles such as self management, citizenship, producer and consumer. Instead, the Department such as seems to be organized around clients served. Instead, the Department sees to beorganize around school matters. Cerification, Internatonal studies, human resouces, student services and the PSB


Restructuring around branches or fields of education would put the focus on the education service being provided rather than the building in which the services are offered.


Sincerely

Don Glendenning
