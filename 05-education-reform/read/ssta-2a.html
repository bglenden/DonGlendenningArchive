Letter from Don Glendenning as Education 20/20 President inviting PEI Federation of Parents and Home and School Associations to dialogue about education (November 2013). Expresses interest in understanding parent support needs and building community-wide education improvement efforts.

November 1, 2013

Ms. Anne Marie Rioux, President

PEI Federation of Parents

Home and School Association


Dear. Ms. Rioux:


On behalf of Education 20/20 Inc., I am pleased invite you, your Directors and Executive Director to join us on October 26, 2013 for a discussion about education.


As background, Education 20/20 Inc. is a new and independent organization in Prince Edward Island with an interest ensuring a quality education for every Canadians. We believe education to be important to our individual and collective futures. It is also our belief that providing a quality education requires the combined efforts of the whole community. Enclosed is a copy of our Vision statement, our most recent newsletter and a list of current Directors.


Home and School Associations have a long history both in PEI and across the country of working for the improvement of education. We would be interested, therefore, meeting both to gather information and share views about future needs.


While we are open to discussions on any and all aspects of education, we would be especially interested in discussing the role of parents in the education process, and even to identifying kinds of support that would be most helpful to them.


We look forward to meeting you on November 26 at 4:30 pm in Room 125 at Royalty Centre, 40 Enman Crescent, Charlottetown.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

President
