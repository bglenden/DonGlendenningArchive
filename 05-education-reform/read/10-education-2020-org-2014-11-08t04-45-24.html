20/20 Education


20/20 Education is a member -driven organization of people who recognize the importance of education in the lives of Canadians. Members are of the view that our individual and collective levels of education will determine, to a significant degree, the quality of our individual lives and the future of Canada on the world stage. Consequently, members have come together in support of a better education for Canadians everywhere regardless of current level of education, age, current level of formal schooling, employment status, interests, and whether the pursuit of education is for pleasure or for profit. Learner driven, accountable, accessible


Supporters of 20/20 Education believe that, while Canada has made laudable strides in the making a quality education available to its citizens, gaps exist, quality is uneven, the system is inflexible and services need to be improved.


Focused on the needs of Canadians as they fill and function in their various life roles


While 20/20 Education will work with any and all educators and educational organizations, it is designed to be forward looking, un-aligned, member driven, independent, and transparent.


Without limiting its future, 20/20 Education will be proactive in:

  • Acting as a clearing house

  • Support the efforts of others working in the field

  • Raising public awareness of the importance of educational needs

  • Encourage early detection of reading difficulties

  • Stimulating and promoting research

  • Lobbying for improvement

  • Increasing opportunities for public participation

  • Influence public policy

  • Raising funds in support of educational improvement

  • Encouraging public discussion among stakeholders

  • Advocating

  • Convening and supporting community based action groups

Typical Activities
