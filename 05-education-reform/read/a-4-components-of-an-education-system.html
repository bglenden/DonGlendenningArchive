Structured overview of education system components organized around an integrated model. Defines education as experiences with formative effects and education systems as community services meeting educational needs. Identifies key components: information systems (guidelines, technologies, records, HR), providers (instruction, coaching, tutoring, assessment), community agencies (libraries, bookstores, facilities, technology access), feedback mechanisms (research, quality control, awards), funding structures, industry partnerships, and leadership/governance. Demonstrates systems-thinking approach to education service delivery with interdependent components. - **Key People**: None named - **Key Organizations**: Education 20/20 Inc. - **Tags**: PROFESSIONAL, EDUCATION, POLICY - **Eulogy Relevance**: MEDIUM - Illustrates Don's systematic approach to education reform and his view of education as an integrated community system rather than school-centered function.
