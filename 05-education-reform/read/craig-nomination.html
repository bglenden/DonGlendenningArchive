Nomination letter for Tanya Craig to serve on the Learning Partners Advisory Council. Don provides detailed biographical information about Craig, highlighting her education background, youth program experience, community involvement through the Home and School Association, and current role as a director of Education 20/20 Inc. He emphasizes her deep experience with youth, communication skills, facilitation ability, and current relevance through active parenting. This letter demonstrates Don's judgment in identifying and supporting emerging education leaders and his commitment to community participation in education governance.



December 29, 2015

Learning Partners Advisory Council

PO Box 2000

Charlottetown, PEI

C1A 7N8


Dear Sir:


I am pleased to nominate Tanya Craig as a member of the newly formed Learning Partners Advisory Council.

Ms Tanya Craig

41 Rodgerson Crescent, Stratford, PE C1B 2G7

Tel: 902-314-4030 Email:tjcraig@eastlink.ca


Tanya was born in Charlottetown, attended West Kent Elementary School, Queen Charlotte Junior High School, and Colonel Gray Senior High School and graduated from Acadia University with an Honours degree in Sociology.


After university, Tanya’s returned to Prince Edward Island and where she worked with young adults, people who, because of intellectual disabilities, lived in group homes where they were provided with a variety of support services. For a number of years she was Youth Coordinator for the town of Stratford and was responsible for youth programming and a community youth Centre for youth ages 10 to 18 years. One of her projects was the highly-regarded “Youth Can Do Project”. Tanya is currently Program Coordinator for Arts and Culture for the Town of Stratford; as part of that role, plays a leading role in incorporating newcomers into to the community.


Tanya is the mother of four, all of whom attended public schools, has direct and current experience working with the major cohort served by the education system. She was an active member of the Home and School Association and active in her community; she is also a Tanya is knowledgeable about the public school system and about youth, is good communicator and an experienced facilitator; she is also a Director of Education 2020 Inc. Tanya’s deep and long term experience with youth is great background for the work of the Council.


I would be happy to provide more complete biographical information, if needed.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning, C.M.

President
