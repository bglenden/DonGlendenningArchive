Teaching:


Teachers are change agents; they help others change, to gain new attitudes, skills or knowledge. Their role is to stimulate learning – to create a set of circumstances that will result in another person changing behaviour.


Teachers support another’s learning through:

  • Modelling

  • Providing timely information

  • Instructing

  • Demonstrating

  • Providing feedback

  • Setting an example

  • Creating instructional materials and organizing,

  • Making presentations and leading discussions,

  • Reviewing and assessing performance,

  • Encouraging, counselling, coaching, diagnosing

  • Creating projects, assigning homework, organize activities
