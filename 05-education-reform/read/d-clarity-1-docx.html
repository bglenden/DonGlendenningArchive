Dear Editor:

About Education

Doc5/Hand

Education is complex.   Learners have different interests, capabilities, experiences and needs; They also fill a number of life roles, that of an Individual, family member, citizen, student, consumer and producer; education is needed or may help with any and all.

Education is also an umbrella word; a person’s education includes all the experiences that have changed a life and is demonstrated by our actions; Our education depends, to a large degree, on our experiences at home, in our community, at school, through travel, interactions with others, personal exploration and roles we play in life. A good education has been described as one that provides a person with the knowledge, skills. attitudes and other attributes needed to deal with circumstances that confront us in our- everyday lives; but there are other definitions as well 

 An education system consists of programs and services put in place to support learners and learning; typical tools of change include information, instruction; diagnosis, counselling, quality control, products and technology. 

Schools are an important component of any education system; they focus on instruction.  Attendance of the young is required by law. Like a hospital or spa, schools are purpose-built facilities that house the people and services needed to support education. Having a well-equipped school is important but it is the experiences within that school that impacts one’s education.  

It is said that education should help us develop the attitudes. skills, knowledge and other attributes that will allow us to thrive in later life. To borrow a phrase from a sister Province, each child should leave school “having mastered the tools to (continue to) learn”.

Sincerely


Don Glendenning

D-22
