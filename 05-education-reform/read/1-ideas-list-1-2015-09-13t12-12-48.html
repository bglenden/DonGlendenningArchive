This planning document outlines potential projects for Education 20/20 including math curriculum analysis, standards establishment, challenge-for-credit programs, and Handle With Care training initiatives. The document also addresses organizational development priorities such as funding, charitable status, and committee structures, plus miscellaneous initiatives involving surveys, stakeholder meetings, and MOOCs. It represents Don's comprehensive planning approach to education reform.


September 13, 2015

Potential Projects-1

  1. Math analysis – DACUM style analysis of a subject (Mathematics) taught at all school levels as a basis for curriculum development and establishment of levels of student achievement

  2. Standards - Quality theme: education, schooling, Report cards, Courage, Challenge for credit/exemptions

  3. Challenge for credit – a campaign to encourage Island schools to reward students who study independently or finish school work in advance of peers

  4. Handle With Care – training for actual and potential caregivers of young children that carries high school credit

  5. Educational round - table for people interested in broadening or deepening their knowledge and understanding of education in society.
