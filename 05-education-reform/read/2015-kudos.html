July 31, 2015


Editor,

Charlottetown Guardian

Charlottetown, PEI


Dear Sir: 

Kudos for leadership

Kudos to the Summerside Rotary Club for leadership in establishing a Summerside Learning Centre; also to Murphys’s Community Centre for the Stratford After-School Program.  Both are worthwhile additions to the Island’s educational services.  

The Summerside initiative, at least as described in the media, recognizes that education is more than schooling, that education is a key component in developing  and maintaining a vibrant community; and also that communities have an important role to play in providing education services.  The “care” function of schools became really evident during last winter’s many storms and I commend Murphy’s for moving to fill the gap.

Thanks for your leadership.

Sincerely,

Don Glendenning

A long-time student of education
