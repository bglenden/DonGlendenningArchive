This procedural document establishes evaluation criteria for Education 20/20 projects, emphasizing clear focus, measurable objectives, community support beyond proponents, philosophical consistency, identified competencies with performance levels, solid documentation, stakeholder role clarity, realistic timelines (3-5 years), evaluation components, partnership inclusion, minimal bureaucracy, clear decision-making, and replicability. The document also presents project scenario frameworks based on geography (local to Atlantic Canada), setting (school, library, home, workplace), life roles (consumer, producer, citizen, family, individual), and identified gaps. This framework reflects Don's systematic approach to educational program development and his commitment to rigorous evaluation methodologies.
