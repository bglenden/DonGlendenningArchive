About Education - 7


EDUCATION:

  1. Education - experiences that alter what a person knows, feels, values and is able to do.

  2. Education system - a set of services put in place by a community to support the education of its citizens

  3. School – a facility designed, equipped and staffed to perform education services


BELIEFS LEARNERS:

  1. Only a learner can learn

  2. Student – a learner on a school or teacher list



EDUCATION SERVICES:

Improving one’s education may involve a range of support services; surprisingly, the literature does not reveal services available, nor how to access them.except to enrol in a school.


Thanks to the Business Council for the following list:

  1. Information – recent, accurate and independent information needed in the making of decisions about one’s own education or the education of others

  2. Counselling – including diagnosis and assessment

  3. A delivery organization with vision and responsibility to put services in place and monitor their effectiveness.

  4. Instruction - help that may include presentations, demonstrations, practice, conversation, questioning and more.

  5. Quality Assurance – to assure students their learning experiences meet community standards.

  6. Learning products – books diagrams, models, photos brochures, manuals, maps, manuals, etc.

  7. Technology – to provide access to educational resources, often at a distance.

  8. Funding – Funding to a level and in a manner that is equitable for all Islanders.

  9. Human resources – teachers, administrators, counsellors, advisors and technical specialists as needed.

  10. Data – report student learning experiences including student progress
