LEARNING PRODUCTS


One of the functions of an education system is that of ensuring the availability of education products – boyh supports for learners and supports for the people who help them. them. eachdeveloping and makis the lessons learned from Kovid 19 is the importance of education products, especially products used directly in support of learning. That’s the equivalent od a pharmacy in the health care system. Most products would be available over the counter; some may require a teacher’s authorization. Education outlets would be open to the public; mini outlets may even be opened on campus or in schools


There are a number of operational possibilities Students could have an account or membership; the centre could provide space for viewing.

Or listening. Many years ago, I recall pulling into a parking spot in xxx, BC. When I got out of the car, I noticed that I was directly in front of a “Sears” outlet. On stepping inside, I found a mini learning centre, 2-3 accounting students working on a project. It was operated by North Island Community College, I was told that a local accountant was their instructor and was available to students a set number of hours each week.


Seems to me that such a model would make Kovid planning much simpler.










Don Glendenning, Editor
