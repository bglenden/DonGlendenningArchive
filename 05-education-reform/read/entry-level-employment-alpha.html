Entry Level Employment

Employability skills have been identified by the Conference Board of Canada as “the skills you need to enter, stay in and progress in the world of work - whether you work on your own or as part of a team”. Employability skills fall into three categories, fundamental skills, personal management skills and teamwork skills.

In an attempt to address the gap between people seeking work and available jobs, Education 20/20 is conducting a number of surveys of both employers and individuals. Information gathered will be reported publicly but will not identify any individual or the organization to which they belong.

Below is a list of employability skills, 56 in all. Please go through the list checking their importance when hiring new employees. Ignore any duplication and skills that do not apply to your company.
