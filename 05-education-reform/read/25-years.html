THE FUTURE OF LEARNING IN 25 YEARS

  • Assessment will be performance based
  • Content will reflect real world skills
  • Learners will develop personal profiles
  • System will provide learning maps
  • Learning will include field experience
  • E-learning will  be an entegral component of all programs
  • Learner progress will be tracked at the competency level
  • Increase in development of social capital
  • Curriculum development will feature learning guides rather than lesson plans
  • Learners will have guies, managers nd advisors
  • Learner interest will drive studies
  • Community partnerships
  • Easier/better access to  research
  • Separation of oversight and operations
  • Increased school autonomy
  • Education systems rather than school
  • Development of independent services
  • Learning will  be personalized
