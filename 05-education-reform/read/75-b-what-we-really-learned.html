Open Education

No. 75 -3 April 17 2019


WHAT WE ARE LEARNING IN SOURIS


We reported earlier on results of a formal survey in Souris of needs and opportunities for training leading to employment. A copy of the report by MRSB: Assessment of Continuing Education and Workforce Development Needs in the Souris Area of PEI is available at www.Education2020.ca).


Based on the report and ensuing discussions, we can concluded that Souris:

  • Residents would like additional training opportunities offered in their area

  • Has no voice in decision-making about local educational matters

  • Is without an education plan for the area

  • Faces gaps in available services – information, counselling, interest courses, credit courses, personal development opportunities

  • Would like a better mix of hands-on and practical training

  • Lacks an obvious feedback loop about both training quality and quantity offed in the community
