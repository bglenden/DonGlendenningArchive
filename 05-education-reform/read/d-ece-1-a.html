This document records Don's exploratory notes and brainstorming for early childhood education (ECE) initiatives under Education 20/20 Inc. The notes document his consultation process including meetings with Steve McQuaid and research into funding opportunities (McCain Foundation, federal government). Project ideas span diverse approaches: parent email advice, baby-sitter activities, programs for prisoners with young children, weekend camps, infant education workshops, parent-child reading clubs, parent needs surveys, and webinars. The document demonstrates Don's systematic approach to problem-identification and solution-generation, reflecting his commitment to foundational education access.
