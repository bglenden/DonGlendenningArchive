Education Philosophy & Reform

September 23, 2024

Dear Editor:

Definition needed

Definitions are critical to the clarity of communication; they help us to think through and communicate more clearly; they also help us to avoid misunderstanding. 

I was surprised, therefore, to learn recently that the Department of Education no longer has a definition of education. Apparently, the word “education” was replaced with “school” during an amendment of the Education Act.  This may explain the narrowing of educational services

Both education and school are perfectly good words but they have different meanings. A taxonomy of key terms is called for and should be widely circulated.

Sincerely

Don Glendenning.
