Comparative analysis document examining definitions of "education" and "school" from PEI's Education Act across different time periods (1988, 2024). Documents the evolution and consistency of legislative definitions, noting that "education" itself is not defined in the Act while "school" is described as a structured learning environment through which education programs are offered by an education authority. This gap in defining the core concept is significant to Don's larger critique.
