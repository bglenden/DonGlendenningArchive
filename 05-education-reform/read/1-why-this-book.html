Why this binder?



It is important, I believe, to have a simple framework for making decisions. the notes that follow describe mine. Each of us should develop our own; the notes that follow are a brief description of mine. I present it, not for others to follow, but as an example of what I have found helpful. Why is a framework important?


A framework helps us understand the complexity of learning; identifies the components of a learning strategy, increases the chances of making relevant decisions and making and making them more consistently. It also provides common language and perspectives when collaborating with colleagues in the field.
