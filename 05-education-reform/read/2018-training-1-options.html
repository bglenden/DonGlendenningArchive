Comprehensive planning document for continuing education options in Souris outlining research-based approaches to workforce training in small rural communities. Includes modular programming in business, technology, literacy, IT, trades, and robotics. Demonstrates Don's systematic approach to community education needs assessment and program design following the Souris Study.

Prior to the study:

Education 20/20 held a couple of informal idea generation sessions with Souris residents as to their training interests and needs; the items below arise from those discussions:


  1. Invite equipment manufacturers and dealers should be encouraged to provide product demonstrations in Souris Area to which students and the public would be invited

  2. Community showcase”, a convening of local educational resources and residents., especially young residents. The proposed showcase would be for a half day and include such resources, tables or booths as: know your weeds, next career steps (HC, UPEI, Academy of Learning, Skills PEI, Career Development Services, apprenticeship), local (technology; drones, agriculture, fishing, marine), Demonstration of On line courses, Handle with Care,

  3. Tutoring services and/or drop- in centre for essential skills

  4. Community information and counselling service

  5. Occasional displays and presentations of local advanced technology

  6. Technology awareness tour to create awareness of career opportunities resulting therefrom; Holland College Marine Centre in Summerside, Aerospace in tic.

  7. A business of your own

  8. J- courses – short and job specific Job instruction, Job Readiness, Job Safety, Job Supervision, essential skills, Residential worksite site safety, how to orient staff

  9. Personal interest courses: Farm safety, parenting, self-management, welding, iPad, car maintenance, home maintenance, basic instruction, gardening, drone operation, arts and crafts,

  10. Organize a program of continuing education activities: employability skills, skill development, personal interest and well-being, arts and crafts
