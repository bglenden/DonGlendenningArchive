Journalism Award


Background:

Education is important and needs to change; information is a change agent; the changes  that are needed are unlikely to come from within the system; the award may help a student, may provide some new insights and information, may even spark a journalist to write more articles in the future.


Purpose: To encourage journalists to investigate and report on stories may increase public discussion or influence public policy
