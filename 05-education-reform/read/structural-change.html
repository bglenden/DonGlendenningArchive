Education Philosophy & Reform

October 20, 2014

Editor, Charlottetown Guardian

Charlottetown, PEI


Dear Sir:

A clear vision


The recent presentation by the Greater Charlottetown Area Chamber of Commerce to the Standing Committee on Education identified “a clear vision” as the first need; I concur,  so I decided to scan Department publications for a document that, in my view, met their suggestion.


Readers interested in the subject are referred to the Education Handbook for School Administrators, 2014-2015,  pages 20 and 21, where they will  find a statement of purpose, basic principles(5) and goals (16).  The purpose of the Prince Edward Island public education system is given as

” 

to provide for the development of children

 so that each child may take a meaningful place in society.


All Islanders interested in education should read these to pages; they provide great place to start a discussion about education on PEI


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

A long time student of education
