Education Philosophy & Reform


EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Board Meeting

Tuesday, January 8, 2013 at 4:00 pm

Location:  Stratford City Hall

MINUTES:  Meeting No. 5


Present: Alice Taylor

Eileen Conway-Martin

Don Glendenning


  • Minutes of meeting No. 4, December 11, 2012; Moved by Alice; Seconded by Eileen; Approved


  • School Award – After a lengthy and detailed discussion of documents on criteria and procedures (Thanks to Alice and Eileen), Moved by Eileen and seconded by Alice that we proceed to make an award to a school that exhibits a high level of engagement in its day to day activities, dispense with the workshop on criteria proposed for January 30, and adopt in principle the guidelines prepared by Eileen.  Moved by Eileen; Seconded by Alice Approved.    Eileen agreed to be our lead on the School award initiative.


  • CRA application for charitable status was discussed, draft responses to Questions 11(A) and 17, the budget, and were reviewed.  President was authorized to proceed with application.  Moved by Alice; seconded by Eileen.  Approved  


  • Draft of communication strategy was discussed and some minor changes proposed including timelines.  Don to arrange discussion with Maureen, Bernadette and Eileen to discuss a proposed newsletter.


  • Don to meet with Maureen Kerr and Bill Whelan about a test of engagement software.


  • Agreed to schedule a workshop on pressing issues in education in late February, hopefully, with Steve McQuaid as facilitator.  Session to begin with an overview of Education 20/20 activities to date, activities planned and short list of  pressing issues as gleaned from our consultations.  Participation by invitation, limited to 40- 50  people. 


  • Administrative Matters:
  • Moved by Alice and seconded by Eileen that we open a bank account at Metro Credit Union.  Carried
  • Moved by Alice and Seconded by Eileen that the President be designated chief signing officer and that cheques on the account require a second signature, that of another Director.
  • Moved by Eileen and seconded by Alice that we arrange for telephone service.


  • Next Meeting at the call of the chair


  • Meeting adjourned at 6:15 pm
