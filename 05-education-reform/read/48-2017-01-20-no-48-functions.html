SCHOOL AND EDUCATION FUNCTIONS

Given the current level of discussion about education, I thought it might be interesting to review the functions of both an education system and a school system; I did not find as much as I hoped, and sometimes the material confused functions and components. My first thought was to select another subject for this newsletter, however, given the current discussions about education, I decided to publish what I have, and correct and update it in a future newsletter.


Education functions:

  • Leadership

  • Information

  • Counselling

  • Instruction

  • Human resources

  • Education products

  • Technology

  • Governance

  • Feedback


School functions:

  • Assessment

  • Accountability

  • Standards

  • Professional development

  • School autonomy

  • Engagement

  • Technology

  • Safety

  • Discipline


Even though I share the lists, I must confess that I have questions about some of the items. For instance, should information and counselling be listed under education rather than schools? Should standards and professional development be part of a school or an education system? We would like to hear your views on the subject.
