Event poster promoting a public discussion on improving quality in public education. Held March 15, 2018, 4:30-5:30 pm at Southport Room, Stratford Town Hall. Dr. Don Glendenning, President of Education 20/20 Inc., led the discussion examining recommendations from OECD, UNICEF, UNESCO, MEI, and the Glaze Report. The poster includes Education 20/20 contact information and emphasizes bringing international best practices to local PEI education discourse.
