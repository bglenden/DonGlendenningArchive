Board strategic planning document for Education 20/20 Inc. identifying organizational goals for 2014-2015. Lists Don Glendenning as President and retired educator among the directors. Goals include obtaining CRA charitable status, creating electronic forums, partnering with the Canadian Education Association on advocacy campaigns, organizing workshops, and presenting policy recommendations to government. Demonstrates Don's vision for advancing Canadian education policy.
