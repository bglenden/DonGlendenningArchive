This document outlines strategic steps for improving education systems, organized into three categories: conceptual clarity, system creation/modernization, and operations. It emphasizes publishing key definitions to differentiate education from schooling, promoting research dissemination, and clarifying system purpose. The implementation section addresses establishing operating principles, identifying services, restructuring based on those services, and clarifying roles. It calls for providing in-service training, salary incentives for advanced degrees, and addressing the "fear factor" in education systems. For schools, it discusses teaching/learning models, competencies, holistic rating, self-assessment, and ranking frameworks for accountability.
