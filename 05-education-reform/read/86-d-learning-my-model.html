Don proposes provincial government role as encourager, supporter, and regulator of education serving both individual Islander development and community building. Reflects his systems-thinking approach balancing individual and community needs.

The role of a provincial government is to encourage, support and regulate educational services. In providing services, government has two clients in mind, individual islanders themselves and the community as a place we want to live.


Individuals want to further develop knowledge, skills, attitudes and other attributes needed to thrive in their chosen community; but community itself needs people to plan, run, manage, maintain and build the community itself – hence the community as a separate client.
