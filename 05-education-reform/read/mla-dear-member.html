Don Glendenning's cover letter accompanying Education 2020 Inc's 'Education Primer' to all PEI MLAs, positioning education as vital to Islander prosperity. Demonstrates Don's strategic approach to policy advocacy and government engagement.
Education Philosophy & Reform



Dear Member:

An Education Primer


On behalf of Education 2020 Inc, I am pleased to provide you with a copy of our recent publication, an Education Primer – a set of notes about education and the education process. You may have received an earlier copy of the content but not in booklet form.


Education is important to every Islander and to the Island community; it potentially engages every Islander. It is our hope that the Primer will help you understand its workings and its importance – and even spark other possibilities; hopefully it may even help in dealing with education matters that come before you in the Legislature.


Should you have questions, ideas, comments or concerns, or simply want to talk about education matters, do not hesitate to contact us.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning

pei@education2020.ca
