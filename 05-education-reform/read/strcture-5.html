STRUCTURE-5

May 2, 20024

An organization’s structure outlines how activities are to be directed to achieve goals, roles and responsibilities.


Typical branches of education:

  • Arts
  • Science
  • Math
  • Language


For each branch, typical education services include:

  • Information 
  • Counselling
  • instruction, 
  • assessment, 
  • products and technology, 
  • human resources,
  • administration and finance.
  • Governance  


While organizations may take many forms, service organizations, like education, are usual local or decentralized giving representatives freedom to respond to individual and local demands finance. it is likely that main services will be represented at senior levels of the organization.  


Principle for service organizations

  • Structure reflects  function or services
  • Key functions or services are represented at senior management table
  • Mission and assignments are clear
