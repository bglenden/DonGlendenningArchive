Minutes from Education 20/20 Inc. Board Meeting No. 26 held September 16, 2015 at Haviland Club. Education Philosophy & Reform


EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Board Meeting

Wednesday, September 16, 2015 at 7:00pm

Haviland Club, 2 Haviland Street, Charlottetown


MINUTES:  Meeting No. 26

In attendance: Donald DesRoche, Maureen Kerr, Katherine McQuaid, Bill Whelan and Don Glendenning.

Regrets: Alice Taylor,  Tim McRoberts, Tanya Craig, Tom White-Hassler and Brenda Goodine, 


  • Call to Order
  • Approval of Agenda
  • Minutes of meetings No.25 – Maureen/Katherine; approved
  • President’s report – Discussion of Activity Report No. 7; Bill and Don consider the meeting with Minister and Deputy to have been positive; discussion ranged over many topics; 20/20 raised issues of updating philosophy, value of challenge for credit opportunities for students and idea of an independent qualification authority.
  • Business Arising
  • Information/communications strategy –  information is central to our service; 20/20 needs rebranding perhaps even a new name and logo;  IT central to expanding and coordinating service, probably key to attracting parents; website should be more dynamic, web format (Word Press) has a number of built in features that increase flexibility for a growing organization;  needs to be engaging, flexible, eye catching, responsive
  • Workshops/presentations – agreed we should continue; also suggested that we consider a role of convening/round tables.
  • View was expressed that our wide range of interests sometims makes it difficult to explain the role of Education 20/20 to other people; perhaps need to focus on a few areas.
  • A concensus appears to be forming that the time is right for Education 20/20 to pursue a more active role in informing the public and convening groups of people around issues of interest or concern; 


  • Other business:
  • September 23, 2015, 7-9:00 pm - Reception for Directors at Glendenning Cottage, 147 Glenway McInnis Point, Earnscliffe,
  • December 10, 4-6:00 pm - Christmas reception, Wednesday, at Haviland Club
  • Next meeting -  November 18, 2015, at 7:00 pm at Haviland Club
  • Adjournment
