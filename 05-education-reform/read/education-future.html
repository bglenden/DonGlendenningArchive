A Mission and vision/foundations/Pillars

Every organization needs to review its basic beliefs from time to time; education is no exception. The beliefs themselves may not change but the very act of review is important for new entrants to the field, and the process may result in confirmation, amendment as well as interpretation. As a focus for the review, let me remind the reader that the questions are focused on education as it applies to each and every Islander:


  1. What constitutes an education?

  2. Why should each person be educated?
