Promotional poster for Education 20/20 Inc.'s October 22, 2014 workshop "From a Learner's Perspective" at Stratford Town Hall, inviting participants to chart the learning process course. The workshop features a youth panel sharing support needs, participant-led path design at roundtables, and student panelists wrapping up the evening. The event exemplifies Education 20/20 Inc.'s collaborative approach to educational research and community engagement.



“From a Learner’s Perspective”


An invitation for participants to chart the course of a learner

working his/her way through the learning process


Date: October 22, 2014

Time: 7:00- 9:00 p.m.

Location: Southport Room, Stratford Town Hall



The Workshop will be led by the 20/20 Team


  • Youth panel…tell us the kind of help you need

  • Participants…design a path to provide it

  • Developing the path…panelists wrap up the evening
