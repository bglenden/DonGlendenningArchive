Education Philosophy & Reform

May 23, 2025

Dear Editor:

Role Change Recommended,

The Department of Education gives its role as one of “developing and delivering curriculum ” to children.  I find that very narrow; I think of the role as one of “creating experiences that cause others to learn”– regardless of age.

The role should be reviewed, changed if needed, and circulated widely throughout the system.

Sincerely

Don Glendenning
