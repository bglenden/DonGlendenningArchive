Expanded version of T2050 Question 11a notes with refined language and additional details. Maps activities to five incorporation objectives with more specific language about expected/future activities: (1) Promote education - planning to solicit and publish "one-pagers" on future-oriented education topics, organize electronic public forums on schooling matters; (2) Exercise leadership - presentations to government bodies, school boards, continued public workshops and awards program, establishment of qualification authority; (3) Foster cooperation - hosting discussions with educational organizations (Home and School, Women's Institute, Construction Association, Professional Engineers Association); (4) Support research - partnering with University, Holland College, professional associations, employers on math needs identification; (5) Improve public understanding - monthly newsletter in both languages to 140-person growing mailing list, website maintenance, six-community education and preparedness workshop series, surveys, social media engagement, education think tank, parent engagement initiatives, research dissemination monitoring, organizational collaboration. Shows ambitious vision for education system improvement. - **Key People**: None named - **Key Organizations**: Education 20/20 Inc., Holland College, University of Prince Edward Island, PEI Home and School Federation, PEI Women's Institute, PEI Construction Association, PEI Association of Professional Engineers - **Tags**: PROFESSIONAL, EDUCATION, COMMUNITY, FOUNDATION - **Eulogy Relevance**: MEDIUM - Demonstrates scope and ambition of Don's educational reform agenda for PEI.
