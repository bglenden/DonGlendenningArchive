April 10, 2016

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Information services


The mission of Education 20/20 Inc. is to ensure a quality education for every Canadian; central to accomplishing its mission are the twin pillars of information and engagement; information being the fuel and engagement the cauldron of change. The nature and range of information about education available to the public does not reflect the significance of the subject; we propose to initiate some changes. Communication activities will focus on education, not just schooling, and the needs of learners and the learning process with our website as the hub. While the availability of relevant information does not guarantee change, it certainly supports it. Education 20/20 is of the view that providing the public with independent and relevant information, in addition to being useful in its own right, will support the public in its quest for greater participation in the education process.
