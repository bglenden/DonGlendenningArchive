Education Philosophy & Reform

Education and Preparedness

The challenges of young people on PEI will be the subject of a workshop on January 20, 2014. The workshop will lead off with a review of a recent study of issues important to youth commissioned by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  Following a presentation by Steve McQuaid, Vice President of the Foundation and Katherine MacDonald, Researcher, participants will engage in a round-table discussion about the findings, especially those related to issues of “preparedness”.  The workshop, sponsored by Education 20/20, is open to the public and will be held in the Southport Room, Stratford Town Hall, starting at 7:00 p.m.  Storm date January 22, 2014


For further information visit us at  www.caneducation2020.com or phone 892-2060
