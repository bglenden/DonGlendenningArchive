Math Award



Background



Canadian Mathematical Society (cms.math.ca)

  • Goal: promote and advance the discovery, learning and application of mathematics

  • Activities include: math competitions, math camps, prizes and awards, scholarships, math at work, outreach, science fairs, kabol and teachers resources.Funding: membership, plus donations from individuals, corporations and Governments

  • Organizes and helps to fund a variety of competitions including the Canadian Open Mathematics Challenge; some students who do well on the COMC may be invited to compete in the Canadian Mathematical Olympiad




Canadian Open Mathematics Challenge

  • Open to any student with an interest in mathematics




Proposed Award:

  • PEI student with highest mark in the Canadian Open mathematics Challenge and To the school in which the student is currently enrolled

  • Award to include actual funds
