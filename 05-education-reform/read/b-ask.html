Workforce Study

Souris, PEI


Education 20/20 Inc. is seeking $1,500 in support of an education project in Souris


Souris

Souris is a year-round port in Eastern PEI with a population of 1,053 (Statistics Canada, 2016 Census), a decline of 10% from the 2011 Census. Compared to PEI as a whole, Souris has a smaller proportion of residents under age 15 (13.8% vs. 15.9%) and age 15 to 64 (57.6% vs. 64.7%) and a higher proportion of residents age 65 and older (28.6% vs. 19.4%). Souris also had a much lower median total household income ($42,368) compared to PEI as a whole ($61,163). Wealth generating activities are focused around tourism, farming, marine services, fishing and related food processing, and the normal trades and services of any small community. Compared to the PEI population generally, Souris had a higher percentage of residents with high school education only and the same for any postsecondary educational certification as of the 2011 Census.


Education

Educational services in Souris are provided by a variety of people, institutions and organizations including Eastern Kings Early Learning Academy, Souris Regional School (K-12), Ecole La Belle Cloche (Francophone K-12) Holland College, Harmony Training Projects, Harbourview Training Centre, and an office of Career Development Services.


Needs Study

Education 20/20 Inc. has met a number of times in Souris in and about to explore the nature and extent of current education services. Arising from our discussions is an initiative to undertake a survey of the Souris area to identify current training capacity, determine the interests Souris residents in continuing education, Interview some key informants, review training strategies and make recommendations. The results of the study will be made public
