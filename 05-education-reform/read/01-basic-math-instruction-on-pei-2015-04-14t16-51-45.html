Basic Math Instruction on PEI


PEI Goals of mathematics education are to prepare students to (PEI Education Website MAT613A)

  • use mathematics confidently to solve problems;

  • communicate and reason mathematically;

  • appreciate and value mathematics;

  • make connections between mathematics and its applications;

  • commit themselves to lifelong learning;

  • become mathematically literate adults, using mathematics to contribute to society.


  • School transcripts do not describe skills in sufficient detail for employers to understand skill levels of new employees.

  • We have not been able to locate a comprehensive list of math outcomes or competencies.

  • No evidence of a quality control procedures

  • Each grade level appears to have separate outcomes; no evidence of spiralling
