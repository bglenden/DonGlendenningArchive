SOME DEFINITIONS

DEFINITIONS:

  • Education - PEI definition not found; am told that “education” was replaced by “school” during recent revision of Act

  • Education-one’s education is the result of experiences that have had a measurable effect on the state or level of development of a person’s thoughts, actions and feelings

  • Education System- a set of services put in place by a community to encourage and support the development of its people

  • Educated means the degree to which person can deal satisfactorily with issues that arise in their everyday lives.

  • Learner – a person trying to bring about change in his/her life – trying to change an acritude, gain new knowledge, or add skills.

  • Life roles – adopted from Oregon education belief that each person fills six life roles – individual, family member, student, citizen, consumer and producer. Education can support any and all

  • School- a resource rich facility designed to support the education process

  • School system- schools in a district reporting to a single senior executive member

  • School – (PEI Act 2024) – a structured learning environment though which an Education program is offered to a student by an education authority

  • Teaching - arranging experiences for the purpose of helping others learn.
