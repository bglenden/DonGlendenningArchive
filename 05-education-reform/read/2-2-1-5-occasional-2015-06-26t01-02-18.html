Proposal:

he idea behind this proposal is to establish an independent body with authority to assess and certify a person’s level of performance on a task or set of tasks such as found in an occupation or field. A qualification authority would reduce if not eliminate the confusion faced by teachers between teaching and sorting, focus certification more clearly on performance that matters, support lifelong learning and self-directed study, clearly focus school evaluation on helping learners learn, and reduce major for distractions for teachers caused by operating the sorting process. It would, in effect, have two clients, the organizations desiring an assessment service and individuals wanting to confirm thelevel and suitability of their qualifications. A qualification authority would take its cues from the destination of the learner, focus on what a client can do and how well, examine each skills carried and assess their suitability also identify gaps for the individual would not simply administer . A qualification authority could also play a useful role in helping people identify gaps in their preparation for a given field of work.

  1. Be an independent organization with its own governing structure

  2. Operate on a fee for service basis

  3. Accept clients on direct application or by referral

  4. Issue certificates based on demonstrated performance

  5. On request, provide applicants with information on gaps to be filled to meet certification requirements.

  6. Make extensive use of advisory/working panels.



Benefits/Advantages

It is anticipated that the availability of AQUA will:

  1. Remove barriers to certification encountered in the current approach

  2. Focus certification more clearly on performance

  3. Encourage more self study

  4. Allow educational providers to focus on the teaching function

  5. Allow a certification body to focus on certification without regard to teaching.

  6. Removes length of learning time as a component of certification

  7. Provide an avenue to certification for self directed learners
