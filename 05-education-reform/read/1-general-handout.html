Education 20/20 Inc. general handout and organizational information document. Presents the organization's mission ("A quality education for every Canadian"), vision, mandate, and activities (awards, newsletters, website, research, consultation, workshops). Lists board of directors including Don Glendenning as Treasurer at the time this version was prepared (indicating his leadership changed from President in other documents to Treasurer in this iteration). Emphasizes that quality education requires learners to be healthy, in safe/respectful environments, with relevant content linked to personal/community objectives, focused support, timely/independent information, and professional/autonomous/accountable teachers. References UNICEF standards.




Education 20/20 Inc.


Learners on the road of life

A quality education is one where: *

  • Learners are healthy

  • Environments are safe and respectful

  • Content is relevant.

  • Outcomes are linked to personal and community objectives

  • Support is focused on learners and learning

  • Information and advice are timely and independent

  • Teachers are professional, autonomous and accountable

Education 20/20 Inc.


Education 20/20 Inc. is an independent, broad based, national, not-for-profit education organization, located in Charlottetown, PEI and is governed by a volunteer Board of Directors.


Vision: A quality education for every Canadian.


Mission: To support and strengthen a culture of learning for all Canadians.


Mandate: To contribute to the education of our citizens by providing information, encouragement and support for a quality education for all citizens.
