Subtitled "Our Education Journey," this document presents the framework for educators and support professionals. It emphasizes that "education is intended to help us on the next leg of the journey" and the presentation "provides a framework for people whose responsibility it is to help others along the way." It identifies extensive barriers including physical, emotional, personal, financial, age, language, access, education qualifications, responsibilities, childcare, elder care, work, household, social/cultural factors, peer pressure, family background, discrimination, and poverty. Includes attribution crediting Education 20/20 Inc., the Oregon concept, and noting Don Glendenning as Educational Adviser with artist Louise Carota.
