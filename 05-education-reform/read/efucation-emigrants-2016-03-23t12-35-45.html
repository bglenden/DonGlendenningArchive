Educational emigrants 

March 23, 2016


_____________________________________________________________________________________



Definition: a person in transition from one level of education to another, from education to 

Employment or currently employed but seeking new employment opportunities.


Characteristics: Currently in institutions, seeking entry to institutions, seeking new destinations, 

Wish to relocate, caught on shoals, and encountered submerged rocks and rocky shores, economic, social 

Barriers encountered: lack of documentation; formal skill levels, 

Problems encountered: cultural, levels of schooling, mobility, red tape, documentation, lack skills that the community needs, restless


Problem: They often fall between the cracks, Need help:

Proposal: Smooth the migration problem

Benefits: economic, social, 


Step 1: invitational conference of migrants, migrants and stakeholders, or?
