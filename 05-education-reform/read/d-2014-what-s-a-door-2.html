A January 2014 Guardian letter where Don uses architectural terminology (inspired by his drafting instructor) to explore fundamental questions: What is a school? What is its purpose? He argues schools provide two services—childcare and education—and questions whether combining them is effective. The draft explores competing definitions of school purposes and the ambiguity in what a high school diploma represents.
