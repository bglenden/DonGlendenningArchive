Extensive findings and conclusions document (February 3, 2024) providing comprehensive system critique with detailed categorization. Documents widespread public concern about education quality, particularly regarding basic communication and math skills. Identifies structural rather than resource problems as urgent. Notes the system's inability to self-correct without external intervention but also recognizes broad support for change. Lists specific systemic characteristics (monopoly, institution-centered, obscured accountability, time-based, lacking innovation encouragement, insufficient collegiality) and crucial gaps (early childhood education, independent learner support, prior learning certification, entrepreneurship development). Concludes with recommendations for restructuring.
