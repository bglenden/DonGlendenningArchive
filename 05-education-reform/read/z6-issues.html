A detailed analysis of systemic issues in education covering mission and vision gaps, schooling delivery problems, and expectations of schools from multiple stakeholders. Don critiques the age-based versus needs-based system paradigm, advocates for professional autonomy, clear accountability structures, and decentralized decision-making with principals as educational leaders. This document exemplifies his comprehensive critique of educational systems and his vision for reform.
