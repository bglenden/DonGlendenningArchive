This document presents a strategic framework for educational change in PEI consisting of nine sequential steps: clarify terminology and purpose, identify clientele, promote functions and services, establish an education system (vs. school system), increase school autonomy, clarify accountabilities, institute tracking systems, restructure to reflect functions, and establish functional advisory committees and education hubs. This represents Don's systematic roadmap for comprehensive educational reform in the province.
