Formal recommendations (2018-5) summarizing the working group's conclusions. Recommends PEI undertake mathematical skills analysis for typical citizens and work-entry competencies, establish math profiles for different contexts, create an independent evaluation body for achievement levels, adopt community-aligned rating criteria, and develop a dynamic mathematics rating scale. These recommendations represent the distilled wisdom from months of stakeholder discussions and position an independent external assessment body as central to solving transition problems.
Education Philosophy & Reform

RECOMMENDATIONS:

2018-5

That PEI:

  • Undertake an analysis of math skills used by a typical citizen including competencies associated with work entry.
  • Strike math profiles as for (1) math skills for citizens and (2) work entr
  • Establish an independent body to determine official levels of achievement and a procedure for assessing levels achieved by individual students.
  • Adopt rating criteria that reflect those used in the community
  • Develop a dynamic math rating scale
