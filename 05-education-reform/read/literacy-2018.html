No. Abt- 1


It is widely recognized that boosting our education levels would boost the Island economy. The matter of preparedness for the workplace was identified as an issue by The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island (CFPEI) in its survey of Island youth in late 2013. According to the Conference Board of Canada, education drives success yet some 40 percent of our adult workforce lacks education skills needed for a modern economy. Responsibility for literacy training in PEI is shared; the Department of Education, Early Learning and Culture serves young people still in school and the Department of Workforce and Advanced Learning. serves out of school youth and adults.
