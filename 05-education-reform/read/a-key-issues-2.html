The literature speaks of the need for educational reform; there are a number of issues; here my first three:

  1. Clarity

  • Education and Lifelong education both in title

  • Role of “developing and delivering curriculum” does not reflect the task

  • Taxonomy not in evidence

  • Purpose not clear

  • Focus on schooling rather than education

  1. Structure

  • Proposed Role of Department to encourage, support and regulate ;

  • Current role does not reflect task

  • Services not identified

  • Functions/Services not represented in upper echelons of structure

  • Services for independent learners

  1. Accountabiity

  • Not clear

  • Not practiced

  1. Workforce

  • Fear factor

  • Inservice training

  • Silos

  • Collaboration

  • Autonomy
