2015 Original and revised Objectives


Objectives in Articles of Incorporation:

  • To promote education and educational opportunity for every Canadian
  • To exercise leadership in the improvement in all facets of education
  • To foster cooperation between and among all stakeholders
  • Undertake and support research and innovation
  • improve public understanding of the education system


From submission of March, 2015


  • To Advance education by organizing leadership development activities for people who plan, administer and deliver education programs.
  • Advance education through increasing the amount and flow  of information and ideas between and among education partners 
  • Advance education by providing supplemental educational support services
  • Advance education by supporting research and innovation, and by promoting best practices in the field of education and training. 


Changes proposed by Revenue Canada, January 20, 2016

  • Advance education by providing:
  • Leadership development programs and workshops to individuals who plan, administer and develop educational programs;
  • Supplemental educational support services such as study centres and educational programs for parents and children;
  • Awards to schools and early learning centres in Canada to encourage academic excellence; and


  • To undertake activities ancillary and incidental to the attainment of the above charitable purposes.
