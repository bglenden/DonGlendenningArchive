Triple-fold brochure presenting consistent educational philosophy. Features eight educational definitions repeated across multiple panels, establishing shared terminology for Education 20/20 Inc. work. Core definition: education as experiences with formative effect on how we think, feel, and act. Includes contact information for Education 20/20 Inc. in Charlottetown, PEI (101 Kent St., PO Box 624), emphasizing consistent messaging across promotional materials.

Definitions:


  • Education – any experience that has a formative effect on the way we feel, think or act

  • Schooling – learning under the direction of teachers

  • Purpose of education – to help people gain the attitudes, skills and knowledge needed for the rest of their lives

  • Role of schools – to help people develop the skills of good citizenship and to ensure that our communities have the skills needed to grow and prosper

  • Learning – changing the way we think, feel or act

  • Teaching – helping people change the way they think, feel or act

  • Learner – a person engaged in changing his/her attitudes, skills or knowledge

  • Standard – a readily distinguishable level of performance demonstrated through the making of a product or provision of a service
