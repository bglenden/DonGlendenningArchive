Analysis of fear as institutional dysfunction in education systems. Documents how contemporary students view college instrumentally, and identifies faculty responsibilities to demonstrate credibility, clarify expectations, and promote self-efficacy. Critiques PEI's Toronto District School Board (TDSB) culture of fear trickling from administration to principals to teachers. Questions punitive grading systems and advocates for progress reporting instead. Dated July 2, 2023, representing Don's observation of systemic anxiety undermining educational effectiveness.
