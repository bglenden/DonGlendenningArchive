This document presents Don's discussion questions on school standards for Meeting 34, revealing his sophisticated thinking about educational assessment and structure. Topics addressed include: confusion about grade-level outcomes versus universal outcomes; the basis for grade-level differentiation (number of skills demonstrated, achievement levels, clustering extent); and critical questioning of why uniform criteria shouldn't apply across all educational levels including community and employment contexts. Most provocatively, Don challenges the role of teachers in sorting/stratifying students, suggesting this function creates teacher-student conflicts and should be reserved for regulatory bodies rather than classroom practitioners.
