Opening remarks document for Education 20/20 Inc., a national education advocacy organization with headquarters in PEI. Outlines the organization's belief that education is broader than schooling and emphasizes the need for public engagement in supporting quality education. Defines the organization's focus areas including awards, information dissemination, surveys, and community engagement. This represents the conceptual foundation for Don's education advocacy work.

Welcome


Education 20/20

  • Believe education to be important
  • Education more than schooling
  • Role for the public in providing a quality education

  • A quality education for every Canadian

  • Nationally incorporated; head office in PEI

  • Awards
