Education Philosophy & Reform

October 9, 2015

Handwritten note to Roger Birt, 


Dear Roger:


I trust that all is well and that you and Elinor were able to enjoy some of our fantastic September weather. I am now back from the cottage, our summer visitors have departed and I’m settling into our normal routines. Have a couple of things on my mind:

  • We did not have a chance this summer for our usual conversation about education; while I can’t and shouldn’t take credit for what’s happening, I understand that  the operation of the Department is under review.
  • As we plan our winter activities, we look forward to your usual financial support.  If it is more convenient, just drop it in the mail to the Community Foundation – and we’ll talk later. 
  • It was also my intention to convene another coffee, this time hopefully with both John Robinson and Regis Duffy. John is out of Province at the moment so I’ll be back in touch when he returns. 


Warm regards


Don
