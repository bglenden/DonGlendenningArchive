AB Learn-5 June 1, 2017


Actual Learning Process

As a result of our prior learning, especially in the early years, we end up with a complex mix of attitudes, skills, and knowledge. But the futures we want may require a different mix of attitudes, skills and knowledge than the ones we now have. That’s called getting or improving one’s education, that’s why we continue to learn. Every new experience is a learning experience, and all the experiences put together, or at least all the experiences that changed us, constitutes our education. That makes both learning and education a process of changing behaviour, of changing:

  • Attitudes,

  • Skills, and

  • Knowledge


While learning needs and goals may be the same, we reach those goals in our own unique way.

Support needed

As we said, learning is a very personal matter and is highly influenced by our previous experiences. Support also needs to be customized. Our pace, style and even ability to learn may be influenced by our state of physical, emotional and social wellness. But the tools of educational change for learners include some combination of:

• instruction,

• opportunity,

• role models

• encouragement

• information,

• advice

• Recognition


• Recognition

The responsibility for a person’s education rests first with family and later with the individual himself or herself, but it is such an important issue and so complex that most homes and most individuals need help. Governments help, especially with education for the young, for two reasons, (1) to encourage and support citizens to develop their personal capabilities and (2) to ensure that our communities have the people needed to sustain themselves
