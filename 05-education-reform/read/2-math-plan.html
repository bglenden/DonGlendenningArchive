Outline of Don's competency-based mathematics instruction plan emphasizing learner self-rating, universal rating scales, community-based criteria, individual profiles, and student-held competency records. The approach replaces traditional grading with descriptive competency ratings that serve as transcripts, reflecting his broader philosophy of education focused on demonstrated learning rather than institutional assessment.
