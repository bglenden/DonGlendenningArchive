Continuing Education


In a sense, the second thing you learn, and everything thereafter, is continuing education although continuing education usually refere to education taken to top up and fill gaps in educational experiences of our youth. One could call it lifelong education but the educational experiences of your youth are part of that as well.


Reasons to continue ones education include:

  • Keep up with changes in your job

  • Increasing opportunities for promotion

  • Transition to a new line of work

  • Personal growth

  • Gain prestige or social standing

  • Love of learning


Continuing education opportunities may be in the form of self learning opportunities, organized by ones employer or organization, by education and training institutions, by professional and business, by the private sector and NGO’s. full time, part time, on line, residential
