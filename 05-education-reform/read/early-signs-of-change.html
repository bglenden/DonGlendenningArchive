A field report document from Education 20/20 Inc. conducting a survey of early signs of educational change in PEI, based on Don Glendenning's letter to the Charlottetown Guardian (July 10, 2015). The survey tracks five specific changes: appointment of community members to curriculum committees, expansion of exemptions and credit-by-challenge opportunities, release of updated philosophy of education for all Islanders, stakeholder collaboration on basic skills, and new measures of autonomy and accountability for school boards. The document includes blank fields for respondents to record date, time, and notes on observing these changes—representing Don's effort to document and track educational reform movements on the island.
