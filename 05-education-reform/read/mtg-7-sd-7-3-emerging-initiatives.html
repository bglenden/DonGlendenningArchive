Strategic document outlining Education 20/20 Inc.'s emerging initiatives with focus on "learner and learning" rather than traditional institutional approaches. Includes plans for reading improvement, engagement awards, public workshops including "Leaders Series" featuring UPEI and Holland College presidents, newsletters, and consultative governance. Reflects Don's educational philosophy emphasizing learner-centered approaches.


Programming

March 24, 2013

EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Emerging Initiatives


  1. Proposed Focus for activities

  • Learner and learning rather than teachers, teaching and schools

  • Gathering and disseminating information,

  • Strengthening our network,

  1. Reading improvement

Continue work with committee, to expand membership, encourage sharing of information, and developing materials for learners and parents.


  1. Awards

Planning completed for an Engagement Award for a school or family of schools that for demonstrates significant engagement in its day-to-day activities.


  1. Workshops and presentations

  • Public Workshops – workshops on subjects of public interest such as outcomes based education, reading, writing, high cost of postsecondary studies, CAMET, career education, etc.

  • Leaders Series – proposed for fall of 2013; presentations by President of UPEI, President of Holland College, Deputy Minister, President of College d’Acadie, Superintendent of English School Board, and superintendent of French School Board.

  • Regional Workshop - offer at least one workshop in neighbouring province


  1. Information

  • Newsletter

  • Occasional papers

  • Social media

  • Media releases


  1. Administration

  • Governance – new directors, structuring committees and clarifying bylaws

  • Funding – need a plan to raise both sponsorships and operating funds


  1. Consultations – continuing wide ranging discussions and conversations
