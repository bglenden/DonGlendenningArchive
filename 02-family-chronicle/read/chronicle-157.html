Editor’s Notes:

 

Obviously I believe the data to be accurate but am aware how easy it is to misinterpret data.

Three children died young and are buried in Syleham; in the case of Emily and John, later children were given the same names.

John Watling married to a Carruthers and living in Napan was mentioned in Aunt Janet Godfrey’s tapes but I failed to note the reference until I listened again recently to the tape.

Three successive Jubals, all quite prolific, adds to the confusion.

If my conclusions about PEI are accurate, there could be offspring born on PEI but so far am unable to make a family connection.

I would be delighted to receive additional information or corrections to any and all of the above.
