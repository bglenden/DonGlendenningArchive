Issue 149 · Jan 13, 2008
Publishes a 1954-1955 Miramichi Rural High School yearbook with Grade 12-10 graduate and student lists, including names like Jean Gregan, Lois Williston, Claude Kingston, Doreen MacRae, Teddy Fowlie, and many others. Grades 9, 8, and 7 also documented. Don notes finding the yearbook and photos during personal archive review. Demonstrates his direct involvement with the school and contemporary student cohorts; provides genealogical reference for local families of that era. The yearbook is a historical artifact capturing MRHS at a particular moment and confirming Don's institutional presence during that period.
