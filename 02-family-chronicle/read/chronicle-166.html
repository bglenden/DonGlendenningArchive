Black River Agricultural Society #103


Readers will remember that my father, Elmer Glendenning, and later my brother, Walter Glendenning, were Secretary Treasurers of Black River Agricultural Society, #103 for about 35 years.


The following list of members of the Black River Agricultural Society comes from a record book maintained during the period 1942-48 by Walter Glendenning.  I believe that the Society only sold to members (membership was $1.00).
