Educational presentation on the concept that education encompasses formative experiences affecting how people think, feel, and act. Provides key definitions distinguishing between education, school, schooling, learning, teaching, and standards. Associated with Education 20/20 Inc. in Charlottetown, PEI.

Definitions:


  • Education – any experience that has a formative effect on the way we feel, think or act

  • School – an institution that provides instruction

  • Schooling – learning under the direction of teachers

  • Purpose of education – to help people gain the attitudes, skills and knowledge needed for enable them to thrive in their chosen community.

  • Learning – changing the way we think, feel or act

  • Teaching – helping people change the way they think, feel or act

  • Learner – a person engaged in changing his/her attitudes, skills or knowledge

  • Standard – a readily distinguishable level of performance demonstrated through the making of a product or provision of a service
