Visual presentation mapping instructional components onto learners as citizens on the road of life. Shows instruction system elements (presentation, diagnosis, counselling, guided practice, information, demonstration, benchmarks, curriculum) applied to learners with varying genetic makeup, prior learning, and aspirations. Emphasizes preparation for six life roles: individual, learner, family member, consumer, producer, citizen. Demonstrates Don's holistic view of instruction serving complete human development across multiple life contexts.
