A structured curriculum framework for English language and communication arts using the "anentafel" methodology. The document maps ultimate system outcomes to course exit outcomes and specific skill-building competencies across six-to-twelve grades. It includes 16 general competencies and 108 specific skills organized across domains like reading interpretation, technology-mediated communication, and multimedia presentation.
