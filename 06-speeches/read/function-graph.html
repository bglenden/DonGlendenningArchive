A visual educational functions diagram depicting the intersection of educational services with diverse island citizens. The framework presents Education Functions organized by Counselling (information, counselling, assessment, interests), Governance (board, commission, leadership, quality control), Instruction (presentation, demonstration, practice, assessment, analysis), Diagnosis (health, previous education, proficiency), Funding (tuition, grant, fund institution), Quality Assurance (credential, inspection), and Learning Products (textbooks, references, self-instructional material, assistive technologies, internet, software, MOOCs). This represents a comprehensive graphic framework for educational service delivery.
