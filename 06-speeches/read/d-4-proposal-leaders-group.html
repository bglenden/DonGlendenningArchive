A November 2012 proposal to establish an institute recognizing and developing young educator leaders exhibiting innovation qualities. Selection criteria include Canadian citizenship, current education work, ten years experience, strong communication skills, demonstrated innovation, leadership roles, and employer support. The program features twice-yearly meetings: an initial four-day intensive with dialogue, visits, lectures, and projects; and a follow-up meeting in PEI for dialogue, lectures, project work, and public presentation. Open nominations with supporting letters ensure diverse participation. Budget includes travel, accommodation, meals, and local visits. This initiative reflects Don's belief that leadership for change comes from practicing educators and his commitment to creating platforms for practitioner voices to be heard and developed.

November 27, 2012

Leaders Group


Working title:   Canadian Education Leaders Institute


Purpose: To recognize, encourage and develop young educators  who exhibit qualities of leadership and innovation. 

Background:


Number of Participants: 

  • Year one: One per Province for two year period 
  • Year two: 12 students in second year and  12 students in first year
  • Year three


Selection Process: Open nomination by an accompanied by five letters of support


Qualifications:

  • Canadian citizen
  • Living in Canada
  • Currently working in education


Selection Criteria:

  • Ten years of work experience in education
  • High level of communication skills
  • Innovation in work role
  • Leadership roles
  • Support by employer


Program Components:

  • Lectures
  • Dialogue
  • Visits
  • Project that can be completed from home base
  • Public presentation
  • Meeting with members of public


Program schedule:


  • Twice yearly
  • Initial meeting a four day meeting consisting of dialogue, visits, lecture and project work
  • Second meeting in PEI for dialogue, lectures, project work and public presentation 
  • Local guest lecturer






Budget notes


Meeting1

  • Travel – 15 people@ $2,000
  • Accommodation and meals – 15 x $350 
  • Local visits
  • Hosting of local educators



Meeting 2

  • Travel – 15 people@ $2,000
  • Accommodation and meals – 15 x $350 
  • Local visits
  • International guest lecturer
  • Public presentation


Administration

  • Program coordinator
  • Communication Costs
  • Project administration
