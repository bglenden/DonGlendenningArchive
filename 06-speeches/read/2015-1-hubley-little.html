Earlier draft invitation from Don Glendenning to a presentation by Dr. Tammy Hubley-Little, Leader of Curriculum Delivery for the English Language School Board, on "Education." Event scheduled for January 21, 2015, at Haviland Club as part of Education 20/20's series fostering dialogue among Island educators. Showcases Don's effort to engage educational leaders in discussing contemporary issues.


On behalf of Education 20/20 Inc.

I am pleased to invite you to a presentation by

Dr. Tammy  Hubley-Little.

Leader of Curriculum Delivery for the English Language School Board

on the topic of


“Education”


The event is informal and will provide opportunities to meet and talk

to others who share an interest in education. 

The session, the first of a series of conversations with Island educators,

will be held at the


Haviland Club from 4-6:00 pm

Wednesday, January 21, 2015


Dr. Hubley-Little has taught pre-school, secondary and post secondary education in the private and public sectors in England, Norway and Canada;

she holds a Master's degree in Counseling, Master of Arts in Education,

and a Doctorate from Durham University in England.


I look forward to seeing you on January 14.

Don Glendenning, President


Space is limited so an early RSVP is requested

Email: pei@education2020.ca







Education 20/20 inc. is an independent, broad based, national, not-for-profit education organization located in Charlottetown; its mission is to support a quality education for every Canadian.
