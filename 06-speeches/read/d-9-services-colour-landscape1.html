Visual presentation paper titled "Education Services" detailing typical educational services provided by provinces to citizens. Lists seven main service categories: Information/Counselling (information, counselling, assessment, diagnosis, referral, advocacy, promotion), Instruction (presentation, demonstration, practice, diagnosis, materials, liaison, coaching), Quality Assurance (credential, qualification, proficiency levels), Human Resources (teachers, administrators, advisors, teaching assistants, specialists, counsellors), Learning Products (texts, references, self-instructional materials, assistive technologies, internet, software), Funding (capital, operating, transportation), and Governance/Leadership. Presents services in visual diagram format organized by function.

Education Services

Occasional paper No. 9 December 22, 2019


Background

Definitions

  • Education: any experience that has a formative effect on the way a person thinks, feels or acts

  • Education System: services put in place by a community to encourage and support the education of its citizens

  • Education services: the performance of a task for the benefit of others



A search of the literature reveals the following typical educational services provided by a Province for its citizens::

  • Information, counselling and advice

  • Funding

  • Instruction - A couple of years ago, Education 20/20 invited a panel of young people to talk about the support they needed or found helpful when engaged in the learning process. ; chief among their suggestions were the need for information, help in analyzing their experiences, feedback, instruction, self-evaluation, coaching, practice, observations, presentations, counselling and demonstrations.See No. for learning map

  • Leadership and Governance

  • Human resources

  • Learning products and technology.

  • Quality assurance
