Detailed speaking notes (September 22, 2010) for Trinity Men's Group presentation in Summerside. Positions CFPEI among 170 Canadian community foundations as transforming charitable giving by shifting agency from organizations to donors. Provides extensive local examples: Mel Campbell youth fund, hockey fund, women's institutes, art matching funds, MacKay emergency response fund, Robinson memorial fund. Explains four CFPEI services with specific examples, addresses differences from traditional charities (foundation status, grant-making model, flexibility). Demonstrates Don's sophisticated understanding of community psychology and engagement strategies.
