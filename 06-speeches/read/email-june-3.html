Executive

Email to Whelan, Read, Glendenning

June 3, 2018

My understanding is that I should work very closely with the Executive as to the handling of future items; in saying that, we agreed on an Executive meeting two weeks before each Directors meeting.  While I agree with that, I find that we have a number of items about which decisions must be taken.  Here are six. I expect there may be others:

1. Award; Jeniene (Awards Committee) and I visited Bluefield and presented the Award; distributed earlier

2. Time to review the Education Award as to focus, confirm availability and committee including chair.  Discussions with  committee and recent recipients reveal support for asking a grant to do something rather than recognizing something already accomplished. 

3. “Hosting Projects”: we’v now hosted two projects and have been asked about a third,  Many organizations charge a fee for tis kind of service; the issue was raised some time ago and should be resolved.

4. I was asked to amend some financial documents at the annual meeting; I’ve done one but need some clarification on the other.

5. Our agreement with MRSB names me as the chief contact; I’ve spoken with Bill, Tess and Katherine; Katherine is willing to assume the role.  It should be confirmed

6. Given that the President now is also Secretary, should we now consider  invitingone or two others to the Executive – Chair of Award Committee and Chair of Souris project committee????? 

Regards

Don
