COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


Board Minutes


Meeting No: Meeting #108

Date and time: 10:00 a.m., November 21, 2007

Location: Board Room, Century 21, St. Peters Road


In attendance: Norman Carruthers, David Campbell, Myron MacKay, 

Tom Davies, Joyce Gaudet, Don Glendenning, Marie Salamoun-Dunne

Heather MacDonald, John Ives, LouAnn Thomson

Regrets: John Robinson, David Anderson


Call to Order and Welcome – Norman issued a special welcome to our newest Director, LouAnn Thomson who lives in Brudenell

Approval of Agenda

Approval of Minutes of meeting #107 (distributed earlier)


Business Arising

Office space – Don M presented a sketch of the proposed office layout, Directors agreed to implement the plan.

Treasurer’s Report – financial reports were presented, discussed and accepted; John R to follow up with IAHSP to ensure close out before year end; David C agreed to update signing authorizations...

Cadogan Fund – still under discussion

Confidentiality form – Norman presented revised form and Directors present signed and returned; Marla to request signatures from remaining Directors.


Committee reports

Granting – Tom reported a successful reception/presentation, thanked John Ives for his sponsorship; the following changes are suggested for nest year: recipients and donors sit close to podium, hold reception at an earlier date – perhaps September, make grants at reception, allow recipients to speak, choose another location, preference for a current Director or Committee member to Emcee.  Don to follow up with Gordon MacKay about selection process for next year. 

Investment – David C reported on communication with Investment committee and with PH&N; Board agreed to hold the course on investments; considering a review in 2008

Nominating – On behalf of John R, Don moved and Heather seconded motion to appoint LouAnn as Director.

Information/Publicity – Norman will send year-end letter to selected donors; Don M presented list of financial advisors to whom presentations should be made

Other reports - none


New Business


Plans for 2008 – Don presented a support document and Directors were asked for

         views.  There was consensus that the reception be held earlier

         in the year.


President’s Report – Norman reported most favourably on workshop he attended

 in Saint John and urged other directors to consider future

 workshops when opportunity arises.


Next meeting – December 12 followed by no-host Christmas luncheon at 12:30 at Charlottetown Hotel.  Please advise Marla of your plans


Adjournment
