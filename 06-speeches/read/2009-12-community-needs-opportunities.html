An undated strategic document identifying community needs that CFPEI could address, including literacy training, capacity building for smaller organizations, educational improvement initiatives, and youth philanthropy programs. Don recognizes fragmentation in service delivery and advocates for CFPEI to serve as a "third force" supporting improvement in critical areas. He highlights the third sector's importance in social and economic development and positions CFPEI as uniquely qualified to lead organizational capacity building, improve sector communication, and support information technology implementation.
