This is an internal planning document for a workshop titled "A Quality School" designed to identify characteristics of quality schools using UNICEF criteria. The workshop was planned for the Southport Room at Stratford Town Hall with capacity for 20-30 participants organized into small facilitated groups. The document includes logistical details such as date/time to be announced, facilities requirements (flipcharts, recorders, water), and planning checklist items covering volunteers, promotion, door prizes, press releases, and photography. The format calls for a presentation followed by facilitated discussion at tables with handout materials distributed pre-, during, and post-conference. This represents Don's involvement in organizing educational quality improvement discussions in Prince Edward Island communities.
