COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


SD-2007-2

Date: August 21, 2007

Prepared by: Don Glendenning


Subject; Discussions with Mark Robertson


I had coffee yesterday with Mark Robertson to talk about our mutual interests in CFPEI; in particular, 


I identified the following as areas of need:  Editor of community Reporter, media releases, preparation of brochures, community information sessions, Editor of the electronic newsletter, presentations to professional advisors, etc. and wondered if any areas were of interest.


He mentioned that he had accompanied John to a presentation last spring and was impressed with John’s presentation.  He also mentioned that, I think, he had met with John and Myron about presentations to professional advisors and he had gathered a few names but not made presentations.  He also said that he would be willing to be a member of a Development committee but not a chair.  Hhe awaits a specific request from us.


I suggest the following:


That CFPEI identify community presentations yet this year in such places as Wellington, Kensington and Cardigan as one of our information priorities and that Mark be asked to work with appropriate directors to make it happen.

That we designate director(s) to work with Mark in making presentations to the professional advisors he has identified.

early in the new year, we review the matter and make other plans  based on our experience.
