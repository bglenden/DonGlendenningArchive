Updated 2018 version of the Road of Life presentation framework with refined language and structure. Maintains core educational beliefs while sharpening key concepts: education is urgent, quality requires collective effort, all people can learn individually yet in group settings. Framework components remain consistent—learners with diverse backgrounds, six-lane road metaphor, barriers and support systems. Updates population reference from 140,000 to 150,000 Islanders. Includes identical skills taxonomy (must know: literacy/numeracy; should know: IT/science; nice to know: history/geography) and support resources (information, instruction, practice, counseling, feedback). Credits the same production team and acknowledges Oregon School System inspiration from early 1990s.
