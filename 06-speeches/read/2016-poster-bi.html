Bilingual (English-French) promotional poster for the 2016 Excellence in Education Award highlighting technology's application to quality education. Poster announces open nominations for K-12 schools and early learning centres, closing April 30, 2016, with award presentation in late May. Includes $2,500 award amount and contact information for both organizations. This bilingual format reflects PEI's commitment to accessibility and Don's inclusive approach to program promotion.
