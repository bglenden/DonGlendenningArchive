This is the first presentation framework notes documenting the core educational philosophy. It establishes that education is the most urgent issue of the day, crucial to individual futures and community development. The document introduces the metaphor of life as a journey with travelers playing six roles: individual, family member, citizen, student, consumer, and producer. It describes each traveler's unique characteristics (innate conditions, learning experiences, and aspirations) and identifies barriers on life's road. The framework outlines how education issues require information, instruction, practice, counseling, feedback, and self-study resources, with support coming from family, self, community, schools, friends, and others.
