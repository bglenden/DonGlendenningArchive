Titled "Our Education Journey," this version provides comprehensive slide notes with significant white space for visual presentation. It reintroduces the framework with refined language: "Our Personal Knapsacks" (instead of characteristics), "The Road of Life Has a Number of Lanes" (six life roles), and "Barriers on the Road of Life" (comprehensive list including cultural, personal, workplace, poverty, friends, time, emotional, social, and basic skills barriers). Educational support comes through information, instruction, practice, counseling, feedback, and self-study resources from a "Learning Village" of family, self, community, schools, friends, and others.






















OUR EDUCATION JOURNEY
















THE NEXT FEW MINUTES PROVIDE AND INTRODUCTION AND FRAMEWORK FOR THINKING ABOUT EDUCATION

IT IS AN ISSUE OF IMPORTANCE TO ALL OF US REGARDLESS OF WHERE WE ARE ON LIFE’S JOURNEY.

EDUCATION IS THE MOST URGENT ISSUE OF THE DAY BECAUSE

OUR INDIVIDUAL FUTURES, AND THE FUTURE OF OUR COMMUNITY, DEPENDS TO A LARGE DEGREE, ON THE QUALITY OF EDUCATION PROVIDED FOR YOU, FOR ME AND ALL OUR CITIZENS

ENSURING QUALITY REQUIRES THE EFFORTS OF MANY AND A SHARED VISION BY ALL

THIS PRESENTATION PROVIDES A FRAMEWORK FOR CONVERTING THOSE VISIONS TO REALITY
