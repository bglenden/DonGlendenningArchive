Dated July 8, 2015, this survey instrument contains five draft questions designed to gauge public opinion on PEI high school policy issues. Questions address provincial exam reinstatement, value for education dollar, exemptions from final exams, minimum basic skills requirements for graduation, and prioritization of high school roles (basic skills, citizenship, employment preparation, further study, childcare). Each question includes audience segmentation (parents, educators, employers, grandparents, students). The document represents exploratory research methodology for understanding stakeholder perspectives on education system design and student achievement standards.

1-3-5

July 8, 2015

Possible Survey Questions


Question 1


Do you think that it is time for PEI to reinstate “Provincial Exams” for high school graduates?

  • Parent with a child in school

  • Student
