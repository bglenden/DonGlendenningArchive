Planning sheet for the Montague workshop scheduled for Tuesday, February 18, 2014 (storm date February 20) at the Montague Rural Action Centre. The workshop, titled "Education and Preparedness," aimed to inform the public about youth issues in PEI and discuss preparedness gaps for further education and employment. The format included opening remarks, a presentation by Steve McQuaid (VP, CFPEI) and researcher Katherine MacDonald, roundtable discussions, and wrap-up. Logistics covered venue capacity (25 people), coffee service, door prizes, handouts, and media coverage. Facilitators and volunteer roles were left to be assigned.
