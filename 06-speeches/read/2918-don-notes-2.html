SOURIS PROJECT

My takeaway from presentation

Souris 2018-2

Needs  

  • More/better information about offerings
  • A local promoter, organizer coordinator of education services
  • Local learning managers, especially because much instruction will not be full time.
  • Increased emphasis on hands-on, application, practical education (Need to investigate)
  • Training support needed for all domains:  attitudes, skills and knowledge
  • Encourage local involvement and oversight

 

Possible projects: 

  • Establish instruction space equipped with 10 computers and a local learning manager; 
  • Offer supervised on-line training, student’s choice, in 2-hour blocks on Monday, Wednesday and Friday
  • Investigate practical education; my search of the internet doesn’t define practical, investigation needed as to what “practical” means in Souris; develop a description, a course or program and run a pilot project,
  • Support Souris High School in becoming a full-service or community school
  • Entice postsecondary institutions to offer modules of regular programs in Souris Area; construction, computers, supervision
  • Approach local agricultural, fisheries, marine and food processing about sponsoring “tech awareness” days for the community but especially for the young people
  • Increase short, part-time night classes some/all of which could be self- supporting.
  • Itinerant education counsellor
  • Educ center that schedules providers on a regular basis
  • Local education society; perhaps aa wing f Education 20/20 to host speakers, convene discussions, raise knowledge
  • Local drop-in education centre focused on basic skills
  • A research project focused on helping small communities develop ways to meet their education needs
