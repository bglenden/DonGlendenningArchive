Notes for Don Glendenning's presentation to Minister of Education Hal Perry and deputy minister Susan Willis regarding Education 20/20's vision and mission. Topics include quality education philosophy, the Learning Path Model concept based on student needs, Prior Learning Assessment Recognition (PLAR), and a preparedness gap between school credentials and workforce qualifications.
