Detailed presentation on Competency-Based Education (CBE) using Holland College DACUM model. Traces historical context from postwar Canada through Canada NewStart Corporations to Holland College 1969 founding. Explains DACUM (Developing A Curriculum) concept and competency identification through committee-based brainstorming. Discusses oil burner mechanic example, rating scales with benchmarks, program planning grids, learning guides, resource centers. Covers learning management emphasizing instructor characteristics and training. Includes recognition/credential systems and general comments on field-based analysis and transparent processes.
