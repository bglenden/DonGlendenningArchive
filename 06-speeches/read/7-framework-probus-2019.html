Speaking notes for a March 14, 2019 presentation to PROBUS (Professional Business Service organization) at Blanchard Hall. Opening remarks reference "50 years yesterday" (since Don's arrival in PEI in 1969). Discusses current educational turmoil including curriculum development challenges, provincial variations in math approaches, school autonomy questions, and accountability frameworks. Identifies significant gaps in education delivery: night classes, continuing education, part-time options, hands-on learning, geographical access, and social capital building. Proposes systematic framework needs: overarching belief statements, functional definitions, service specifications, and accountability mechanisms. Presents the Road of Life framework as solution, emphasizing its application to complex policy decisions and its representation of educational reality across all citizen levels.
