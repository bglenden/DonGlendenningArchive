Framework presentation notes articulating Don's core educational philosophy that education is the most urgent issue facing society. Develops the 'Road of Life' and 'Traveller' conceptual vectors, emphasizing that quality of life journey depends on educational quality, drawing from the Oregon 1990 life roles concept.

POWERPOINT

An Education Framework


EDUCATION IS THE MOST URGENT ISSUE OF THE DAY

OUR INDIVIDUAL FUTURES, AND THAT OF OUR COMMUNITY, DEPENDS TO A LARGE DEGREE, ON THE QUALITY OF EDUCATION PROVIDED FOR OUR CITIZENS

ENSURING QUALITY REQUIRES THE EFFORTS OF MANY AND A SHARED VISION BY ALL

THIS PRESENTATION PROVIDES A FRAMEWORK FOR THOSE DECISIONS


THE FRAMEWORK HAS TWO VECTORS

THE ROAD OF LIFE

AND THE TRAVELLER

THE QUALITY OF THE JOURNEY IS LARGELY DETERMINED BY OUR EDUCATION


THE IDEA OF LIFE ROLES AND EDUCATION CAME OUT OF OREGON  ABOUT 1990

THERE ARE SIX ROLES THAT MOST OF US PLAY

THAT OF AN INDIVIDUAL, FAMILY MEMBER, CITIZEN, STUDENT, CONSUMER AND PRODUCER 

SOMETIMES WE PLAY SEVERAL AT THE SAME TIME
