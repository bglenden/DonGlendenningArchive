Speaking notes for March 27, 2019 presentation to Charlottetown Rotary Club at Charlottetown Hotel. Opening remarks again reference "50 years since my arrival in PEI," expressing concern about the coming generation and acknowledging distance from public education during his tenure. States he is "not a natural critic" despite concerns. Presents three core components of education: Learners (people engaged in learning), Teachers (people providing help), Education System (community services supporting citizen education). Defines citizen population as 152,000 with differences in innate conditions, prior learning, and future roles requiring new skills. Outlines government's educational support including information services, counseling, economic support, practical and hands-on training, continuing education, and skill upgrading options.
