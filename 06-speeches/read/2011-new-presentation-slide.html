Outline suggestions for presentation slides addressing individual and group benefits from CFPEI. Lists 21 key value propositions including: privacy, low fees, single giving gateway, flexibility, transparency, perpetuity, convenience, participation in decisions, anonymity options, professional management, national network participation, and local leadership. Represents refined messaging about differentiating CFPEI benefits and appealing to diverse donor motivations from legacy preservation to immediate impact.
