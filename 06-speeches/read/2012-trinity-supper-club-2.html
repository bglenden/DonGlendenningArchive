Speaking notes prepared for a presentation to Trinity United Church Supper Club addressing key issues in education. Don discusses education as the most important issue of modern times, argues that schools have hijacked the education agenda, and raises concerns about public engagement in educational policy. Proposes that education must be broader than schooling, that credentials receive too much emphasis, and that external interventions and informed public engagement are necessary for meaningful change.
