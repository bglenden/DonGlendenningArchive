January 2011 speaking points for Holland College's 'Learning. For Life.' capital campaign with Don as Honorary Chair. Brief remarks emphasizing the college's importance to island and wider community, with gratitude to campaign leadership and donors.








Learning. For Life. Capital Campaign – “Celebrating Success”

January 28, 2011


Speaking Points



Order

  1. Dr. Brian McMillan – will speak first, will welcome Don Glendenning to the podium, and will introduce the campaign. 3 minutes

  2. Dr. Glendenning – shares the stage with Brian, after opening remarks. 2 minutes

  3. Robert Irving and Ray Murphy – will share the stage, introduce the cabinet, show the video, and introduce the Premier. 5 minutes speaking and introductions / 4 minute video

  4. Premier Robert Ghiz. 2 minutes

  5. Dr. McMillan – to close. 4 minutes


Total: 20 minutes





Dr. Donald Glendenning, first president of Holland College, and Campaign Honorary Chair





























  • President Brian, Honoured Guests, Friends All



  • Consumed and nurtured a significant part of my life



  • Honoured and delighted by invitation to join the campaign team

  • Obvious that Holland College fills an important role, not only in our Island community but in the wider community as well.

  • support from those on and off Island has been nothing short of outstanding.

  • Many of the people and organizations who have generously stepped up to support the college are here today.

  • As Founding President of Holland College and Honorary Chair of Learning. For Life., I thank you, personally, for your outstanding leadership.

  • (Lead Applause)



  • Great pleasure to introduce to the Co-Chairs of the Learning. For Life. Campaign, Mr. Robert Irving and Mr. Ray Murphy.



  • Please join me in providing a warm welcome.
