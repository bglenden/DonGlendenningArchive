A detailed enumeration of six primary functions that education systems must perform: Information/Counselling/Advice (including assessment, referral, advocacy, diagnosis), Financing/Funding (grants, tuition, fees), Instruction (presentation, demonstration, coaching, assessment, recordkeeping), Governance/Leadership (planning, research, coordination, monitoring, quality control), Human Resources (teachers, administrators, specialists), and Products/Technology (textbooks, technologies, devices, assessment tools, Internet access). Provides functional framework for evaluating education system completeness and effectiveness, dated 2018.
