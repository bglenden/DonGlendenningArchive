This version expands on the framework with introductory context, emphasizing that the framework applies to all people regardless of life stage. It refines the presentation of unique characteristics and barriers, including more detailed landscape metaphors (hills, hollows, rough spots, rocky stretches, flooded areas). The document introduces the concept of detours and emphasizes that barriers may arise from personal conditions, interpersonal issues, and societal factors (poverty, discrimination). Educational support includes information, instruction, practice, counseling, feedback, and self-study resources from a learning village of family, self, community, schools, friends, and others.

POWERPOINT






1



THE NEXT FEW MINUTES PROVIDE AND INTRODUCTION AND FRAMEWORK FOR THINKING ABOUT EDUCATION

IT IS AN ISSUE OF IMPORTANCE TO ALL OF US REGARDLESS OF WHERE WE ARE ON LIFE’S JOURNEY.

EDUCATION IS THE MOST URGENT ISSUE OF THE DAY BECAUSE 

OUR INDIVIDUAL FUTURES, AND THE FUTURE OF OUR COMMUNITY, DEPENDS TO A LARGE DEGREE, ON THE QUALITY OF EDUCATION PROVIDED FOR YOU, FOR ME AND ALL OUR CITIZENS

ENSURING QUALITY REQUIRES THE EFFORTS OF MANY AND A SHARED VISION BY ALL

THIS PRESENTATION PROVIDES A FRAMEWORK FOR CONVERTING THOSE VISIONS TO REALITY












































WE ARE ALL ON THE ROAD OF LIFE

THINK OF YOUR EDUCATION AS PREPARATION FOR THE NEXT LEG OF THE JOURNEY

AND YIU ARE PREPARING WHILE YOU ARE ACTUALLY WHILE ON THE ROAD, YOU PLAY MANY ROLES; IN FACT, THERE ARE SIX

AND YOU PLAY MORE THAN ONE AT A TIME

THAT OF AN INDIVIDUAL, FAMILY MEMBER, CITIZEN, STUDENT, CONSUMER AND PRODUCER 

SOMETIMES WE PLAY SEVERAL AT THE SAME TIME

THE LINKAGE BETWEEN LIFE ROLES AND EDUCATION IS NOT NEW

IT CAME OUT OF A REVIEW OF OREGON SCHOOLS IN THE SARLY 1990’S

THE QUALITY OF OUR JOURNEY IS LARGELY DETERMINED BY OUR INDIVIDUAL EDUCATION
