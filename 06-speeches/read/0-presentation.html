INTRODUCTION

Some preliminary notes.


KEY DEFINITIONS

  • Educate - Help people change the way they think, feel or act

  • Education-a sector of the economy concerned with helping people learn

  • Purpose of education system – to help people change the way they way they think, feel or act

  • Teacher- a person, usually paid, who organizes and creates experiences for other people as a way of helping them change the way they think, feel or act.

  • Student – a person on a teacher’s list

  • Education system – services put in place by a community To support learning by its resudents

  • School – a facility, usually purpose built, where students and teachers meet

  • Education system – services put in place by a community in support of furthering the education of its citizens

  • Good Education – stage or level of educational development that allows a person to handle the demands of daily life
