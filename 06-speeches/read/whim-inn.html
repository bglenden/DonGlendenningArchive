Meeting notes from March 19, 2007 Whim Inn gathering with Tom Hall, Tony Glencross, Bud Ings, Henry Compton, and Martin MacLeod planning a province-wide agricultural equipment collectors' gathering. The proposed April 28, 2007 event at the Farm Centre featured presentations from county representatives, a keynote by PEI Museum & Heritage Foundation Director Keenleyside, a virtual museum presentation by Barry King, and open discussion. Don notes were responsible for confirming attendance; he suggests expanding the thin agenda to either shorten the day or add more programming content.
