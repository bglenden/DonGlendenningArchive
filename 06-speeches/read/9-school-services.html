School Services


Functions and services can be confusing

  • Function -

  • Service -


Education is a service; a service is further defined as:

  • an act of help or assistance

  • an organized system of labour and material aids to supply the needs of the public

  • The supply, installation or maintenance of goods carried out by a dealer

  • The state of availability for use by the public

  • Functions performed by a person or organization
