Detailed program for the Living Memorial Trust official launch and inaugural planting ceremony at Trails PEI, Wood Islands. Lists speakers including Don Glendenning (DG) for welcome and Living Memorial Trusts explanation, Norman Carruthers (CFPEI President), remarks from Wood Island Development Corporation and Island Trails leadership, remembrances from Keuper family members, and the inaugural planting by Mrs. Keuper and Don. Includes refreshments hosted by Plough the Waves Centre volunteers.
