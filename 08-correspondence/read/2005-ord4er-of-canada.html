October 2005 letter from Don Glendenning (C.M.) to Rideau Hall supporting Wade MacLaughlin's nomination for Order of Canada. Signed as C.M. (Companion of the Order of Canada), confirming Don's own Order of Canada status and his respect for MacLaughlin's university leadership contributions.






October 24, 2005



62 Queen Elizabeth Drive

Charlottetown, PE C1A 3A9




Ms Jacqueline Barton

Researcher and Regional coordinator

Orders Section

Rideau Hall

1 Rideau Street

Ottawa, Canada K1A )A1


Dear Ms Barton:


I am delighted to add my support to the name of Wade MacLaughlin for membership in the Order of Canada.


I have known the MacLaughlin family for many years and they were and are well established and respected on Prince Edward Island.  I got to know Wade first as Dean of Law at the University of New Brunswick and later when he became President of the University of Prince Edward Island.  


The appointment of Wade MacLaughlin as President of UPEI was well received in the Island community.  He exudes warmth, is both articulate and commanding, and has a significant media presence..


Under his Presidency, the University has certainly strengthened its community liaison, has expanded its offerings and has greatly added to specialized facilities and research.  The Senior’s College, although not initiated by Wade, has expanded significantly and is certainly is a highly recognized and valued services to the community.


His appointment to the Order of Canada would not be surprising and would be well received.  I certainly support it.


Sincerely




Don Glendenning  C.M.
