FINDINGS AND CONCLUSIONS – 3


Based on my reading and conversations, PEI;

  • Has a school system rather than an education system
  • Has a Department of Education without a definition of education
  • Has both education and lifelong learning in its title thereby suggesting that lifelong learning is not  part of education
  • Sees it’s role as one of delivering curriculum rather than helping others learn
  • Seems to confuse education with going to school
  • Seems to confuse branches of education with levels of education
  • Should identify and promote a full  range of education services
  • Is structured to run schools rather than encourage and support education
  • Government role is to promote, encourage and regulate but should not run  increased conversation about educat
  • Would benefit from increased transparency in education matters
  • Would benefit from a more collaborative model
