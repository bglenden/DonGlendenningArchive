Meeting minutes from the US Citizens Day Committee attended by Dr. Glendenning, Garth Staples, Anne Nicholson, and Don MacDonald. Holland College & Institutional Legacy

U.S. Citizens Day Committee Meeting

April 7, 2009


Present: Dr. Glendenning, Garth Staples, Anne Nicholson, Don MacDonald


Dr. Glendenning welcomed all for their attendance.  In light of last year’s event, the large attendance was not anticipated and consideration of having a celebration consisting of a cake and perhaps some munchies (chips, crackers and cheese) versus a meal was discussed and there is general agreement.  


The Culinary Institute is not available for July 4th this year


Queen Charlotte Armouries - A letter has been sent to the Queen Charlotte Armouries (Walsh/DeCoste) and they are open to staging the event.  If music is requested, there would be a charge applied.


Discussion: The suggestion that the cadets prepare and serve hot dogs and/or hamburgers, is problematic if liquor is being served.  If desired to have such a service, maybe volunteers can be arranged to do the same.  Beer and Wine can be made available.  One tremendous feature at the Armouries is the Military Museum that may be of interest to our American friends and Islanders as well who may be in attendance.  The availability of the Regimental Band is not yet confirmed.  


Action: Garth to contact Officer McKearney to see if they are available.


Confederation Landing Park – It is unlikely that beer and wine could be served.  This location is a hold.


HMCS Queen Charlotte – CFPEI held a function there in 2008 and while a very nice facility, the facility charge is considerable.  


Discussion: If a ship is in the harbour, this may be of interest.


Action: Donnie to contact his son to see if a ship will be in Charlottetown on the 4th of July.


Officers Club (Haviland Club) – The first celebration was held at this location and went well with a few hick-ups.  Garth believes that Col. Jack Stewart is the contact.


Action: Donnie to make arrangements to view the location and contact the above recipients.


Adjourned
