About Education

A Universal Curriculum Platform

About-Curric-2


OBJECTIVE: to develop a platform for presenting competencies in support of competency-based education.


DEFINITIONS

  • Curriculum – experiences planned in support of learning

  • Platform – a base on which applications, processes or technologies may be developed or mounted

  • Universal – applicable in all fields

  • Competency – a statement of observable behaviour resulting from the application of knowledge to the solution of a problem

  • Multi-level -applicable to all grades


PLATFORM COMPONENTS:

  1. Analysis

An identification of competencies practised in an occupation or field

  1. Description of each competency

  • Rational – why important

  • Elaboration – what it means/involves

  • Typical tasks/activities that include this skill

  • Performance criteria – criteria used to measure

  • Performance levels.

  1. Profile(s)

A profile or pattern of competencies considered essential to typical life roles

  1. Rating scale

Numerical scale with descriptions incorporating criteria and showing progression not just status

  1. Benchmarks

A typical and widely recognized level of performance on a task or in a role comparable to the one being taught.




PRINCIPLES/BELIEFS/CHARACTRTISTICS:

  • Widely available to all stakeholders

  • Written in the language of learners

  • Engages practitioners

  • Follow the learner


DELIVERABLEs: User friendly map of every-day numeracy skills.


BENEFITS: Serves as a common and versatile platform or base for users and providers of instructional services - curriculum development, course purchases, student career planning, interagency discussion and negotiations, student tracking, tutoring, student record keeping, hiring, setting entrance requirements. Industry surveys, gap analysis and certification, engages stakeholders, etc.


When used as the basis of instruction, offers support to learners and the learning process, allow teachers to become managers of learning rather than instructors, support independent study, eases transition and Increases understanding of the skill
