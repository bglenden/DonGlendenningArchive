Concept outline for proposed annual "Education Thinkers Conference" to convene educators discussing consequential educational issues. Don envisions three-day gatherings including public presentations, elected chair, discussion papers, and reports. Proposes Holland College as host and September/October timing with endowment at Community Foundation of PEI. Emphasizes futuristic, thought-provoking, provocative themes. Sketches administrative structure with design committee and annual organizing committee. Document remains conceptual with limited operational detail.
