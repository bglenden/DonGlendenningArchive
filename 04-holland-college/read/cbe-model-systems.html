Detailed systems model for competency-based education with six major phases and component details: (1) Assess Needs—review trends, examine jurisdictions, consult stakeholders, determine service levels, identify gaps; (2) Establish Standards—identify competencies, performance criteria, evaluation frameworks, benchmarks; (3) Plan Resources—establish policies, learning models, identify staff/facility/instructional requirements, arrange funding; (4) Implement Program—promote services, register learners, assess learning, counsel learners, monitor progress; (5) Certify Achievement—assess achievement, award certificates, institute warranty; (6) Manage System. This comprehensive framework demonstrates sophisticated understanding of CBE program development suitable for college-level implementation.
