Updated planning checklist dated May 31, 2007 for 2nd Annual US Citizens Day Reception, showing significant planning progress. Assigns specific responsibilities to committee members: Don Glendenning to send letters to committee members, Bruce Garrity managing refreshments and entertainment coordination, Anne Nicholson handling promotion and media relations, volunteers including George Mackay for flowers.
