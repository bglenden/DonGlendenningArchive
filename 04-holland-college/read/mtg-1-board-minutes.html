Executive board minutes for Education 20/20 Inc. (formerly Education First Canada Inc.) showing Don Glendenning's leadership role. Documents organizational initiatives including name change, awards program development, and public panel on early childhood education featuring Don in opening remarks. Reflects his continued professional engagement and educational advocacy after retirement from Holland College.

Education First Canada Inc

Executive Meeting, November 08, 2012

Canadian Red Cross Board Room

Time: 9;30 to 11:00 am


Minutes


Present: Alice Taylor

Don Glendenning

Eileen Conway-Martin –(Absent but consulted immediately before and after)

Business:

  • Function on December 4 to be held at Haviland House, 2 Haviland St., Charlottetown on Tuesday, December 4 at 7:00 pm.  (Revised program attached).  Don to arrange for cheese tray; anticipate 25 people including participants in past meetings, DM Education. other representatives from Department of Education, Dean of Education, and others. (Eileen, there may be others that you want to invite).
  • Motion:  Moved by Alice, seconded by Eileen (by phone before the meeting) that “The Board approve the change of name from Education First Canada Inc. to Education 20/20 Inc.”  Carried. 
  • Following a discussion based on notes distributed earlier, it was agreed that we should proceed with the development of an awards program.  
  • Board to hold a preliminary discussion to identify criteria
  • Secure funding
  • Convene a small advisory group to flesh out details.
  • Strike a committee, the chair of which will be a member of the Board.
  • Don to draft a simple statement, including a mission, for public use to be discussed at next meeting.
  • Don reported that Education 20/20 has been approved by Industry Canada and that he will consult with our lawyer to expedite the signing.
  • Don reported that he had a meeting scheduled with Dean Turnbull at UPEI to, among other things, explore a possible project involving students of the Faculty of Education.
  • Agreed to meet again on Tuesday, November 13 subject to Eileen’s availability





Agenda 


NOTES: 

  • I came to computer to send agenda when I found your notes; this note willbecome our new agenda
  • Sorry that I cannot reschedule either but I have another commitment on Friday. 
  • Suggest that Alice and I meet on Thursday morning at 9:30 at Cancer Society board room as planned especially to flesh out plans for December 4 – SD attached
  • Our “advisors” strongly recommended that we move forward on a school award. Attached is a copy of notes made from clippings, etc. in my files. It is not a proposal or plan but I thought it would provide useful background information. I’d like to have at least a preliminary discussion especially around the matter of how we handle initiatives. – SD attached
  • I am assuming that we will strike a committee/team to develop and steer each major initiative. If so, we need to discuss committee representation meaning public, parent, educator, student, etc. We do not have many respurces so we need to keep the process simple.
  • There were some other items but I’ve pulled them from the agenda for the moment. 
  • We also should discuss a meeting schedule, at least from not until Christmas.

Sorry we cannot accommodate you plans, Eileen; please share any information, views and ideas about any and all of the above; I’ll try to take more formal notes. At our last meeting, Eileen, you mentioned serving on an “awards committee”. Any chance that you’d have terms of reference?

Thanks and regards

Don
