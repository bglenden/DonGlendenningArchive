Comprehensive minutes from Education 20/20 Inc.'s Fourth Annual Meeting held June 15, 2016 at Haviland Club, with Dr. Glendenning presiding. Present were Amber Jadis, Maureen Kerr, Anne Miller, and Alice Taylor. Dr. Glendenning welcomed new members and distributed the agenda. President's report highlighted comfort with newsletter authorship, ongoing "Bridging the Gap" initiative, awards to Prince Street School, and new award direction recognizing educational opportunities in communities. Discussion covered expanding membership, leveraging social media, partnering with Canadian Parents for French for translation services, updating website with Holland College computer science students. Board composition and organizational effectiveness were discussed, with suggestions to establish learning partnerships. Meeting was prorogued and reconvened June 28 for budget and officer elections.
