Don's June 2011 commencement address at Holland College sharing his teaching origin story (Kouchibouguac, 1947) and career in vocational/technical education. Discusses the exciting postwar period in Canadian technical training and his foundational insights into teaching and learning theory. Emphasizes education's importance to individual and community futures.
