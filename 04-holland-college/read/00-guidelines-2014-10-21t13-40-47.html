Occasional paper No. 01 February 10, 2014


Education 20/20 believes that:

  • Education is the most important issue of the day,

  • Our futures as individuals and as a country will be determined to large degree by the nature and extent of the education we offer our citizens.

  • There is a shortage of information geared for the public , especially for parents of children now in school

Occasional papers are being published by Education 20/20 in order to fill a major gap in our education system, that of the scarcity of independent information about education generally available to the reading public, especially to parents with offspring now in school.

  • There is widespread support for change by the public and educators alike.

Occasional papers are directed to the general public, covers a variety of topics in public education at all levels but especially in the K-12 period. The newsletter is intended for a general audience, is a personal contribution to the cause of education, and will include views and opinions of the publisher and others.
