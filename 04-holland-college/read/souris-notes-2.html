Souris Notes-2

Aug 3/2017

NOTES: I decided to recast and reformat my notes as a running record of our activities


  1. Purpose – to explore possible small projects that would contribute to education of people in the Souris area,

  2. Findings:

  • Area lacks local education office to provide information and counselling.

  • for people not in public school system

  • Participants expressed interest in activities that encourages awareness of local technology (fisheries, marine and agriculture); short and night classes, general information and counselling service for out of school youth, career counselling for people still in school, and continuing education

  • While it may be possible to secure volunteers for initial projects, there may be costs involved for repeat projects


  1. Technology awareness:

  • Purposes: (1) create awareness of career opportunities and to (2) increase awareness of nature and extent of technology now in use on PEI;

  • Organize displays and demonstrations of local advanced applications of technology;

  • Tour Holland College Marine Centre and Vector Aerospace to expand knowledge of current technology

  • Consider adding a training component to tours that would engage young people in planning the tour, handling arrangements, and documenting both the tour and the technology.

  • Organize follow-up sessions following each tour,

  • Other tours were suggested in the Souris area and elsewhere such as a tour of a fishing vessel, aa Coastguard, large dairy operation, blueberry operation, etc.

  • Tours to be preceded by a session for participants to prepare for the tour
