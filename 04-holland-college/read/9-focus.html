Don compares PEI's education department (focused on "delivering curriculum") with other provinces' broader mandates focused on developing lifelong learners and productive citizens. He argues for organizational structure based on subject fields (mathematics, arts, languages, science) rather than grade levels, and criticizes the interchangeability of "school" and "education" as contributing to system confusion. This critical analysis reflects his concerns from Holland College presidency.

Role of PEI Department of Education
