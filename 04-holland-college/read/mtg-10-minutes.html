EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Board Meeting

Tuesday, June 4,  2013 at 12:00 noon 

Room 2L, Royalty Centre

MINUTES:  Meeting No. 10


Present: Alice Taylor, Eileen Conway-Martin, Don Glendenning, :  Bernadette Allen, Bill Whelan, Owen Parkhouse and Norman Finlayson



Agenda

  • Call to Order
  • Welcome new Directors – President welcomed the following new Directors:  Bernadette Allen, Bill Whelan, Owen Parkhouse and Norman Finlayson
  • Minutes of meeting No.9 –moved by Bernadette, seconded by Norman; approved
  • Business Arising

A lively discussion of items on the agenda followed; comments were wide ranging and future oriented

 included the following:

  • Education 20/20 can be an engine for change 
  • We must think outside the box
  • Our financial and human resources are limited, we depend on volunteers
  • We can multiply our impact by forming coalitions and partnerships  
  • Our focus is on education not just schooling
  • We need to select initiatives that make an impact.
  • Agreed that the next step is to develop a strategic plan; to invite Steve McQuaid to facilitate, to meet either Saturday, June 15 or 22 at 8:00 am for a three hour session subject to Steve’s availability; Don to approach Steve and confirm arrangements. 
  • Meeting adjourned at 1:00 pm
