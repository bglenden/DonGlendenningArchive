Don's thank-you letter (February 2006) to Dr. Brian MacMillan, Holland College President, for the honor of Glendenning Hall naming. Expresses gratitude to board and college community, notes pleasure at informal ceremony and family attendance, and appreciates professional organization.
Holland College & Institutional Legacy


    62 Queen Elizabeth Drive

Charlottetown, PE C1A 3A9


                February 22, 2006




Dr. Brian MacMillan, President

Holland College

140 Weymouth St

Charlottetown, PE

C1A 4Z1



Dear Dr. MacMillan:


Thank you for your generous words at the ceremony giving the name, Glendenning Hall, to the new Holland College residence.  It was an honour, indeed and somewhat overwhelming.  Also, please extend my thanks and appreciation to the Board of Governors and the larger college community for this magnanimous gesture.  


I liked the informality of the ceremony and was pleased to see so many familiar faces.  I was also pleased that my two remaining brothers and their wives could attend.  It provided another opportunity for our dwindling family to get together. 


The event was professionally organized thanks to Maria, Sherri  and their people.  Everyone went the extra mile.    


Again, thank you for your generosity.  When I recover and am back from the sunny south, it will be my turn to buy coffee.



Sincerely




Don Glendenning
