Learning activities section of the Time Management Competency guide, providing 11 optional activities for skill development. Activities progress from self-assessment (estimate time, keep time logs) through exploration (identify time management techniques and time wasters) to application and reflection (practice techniques, create schedules, interview efficient people). Demonstrates Holland College's learner-centered approach allowing flexible, individualized learning pathways while maintaining competency standards.

Apply Time Management Techniques 

The following learning activities are intended to assist you in the development
of the above mentioned skill. 

They are suggested activities only. You may wish to undertake alternative
learning activities. If so, you should consult the Resource Person listed at the
end of this Learning Guide. 

Estimate the time required to complete this skill. 

Estimate the time required to complete other tasks you are 

undertaking.

Establish a time schedule for the coming week.

Estimate impact of wasted time on cost of a service used.
