AWARD FOR JOURNALISM

Journalists are invited to write about education. Students enrolled in the journalism program at Holland College and UPEI are invited to submit copies of their published articles for consideration. The award, valued at $400, is sponsored by Education 2020 Inc., an education group active in Prince Edward Island.


Education is an important component of preparing for and securing our individual and community futures. Institutions are under pressure to change, to do more and sometimes to do it differently or even with less money. Students are wondering if their ‘education debt” is worth it, institutions are rearranging how they fund different programs and it is likely that pre-COVID-19 delivery patterns will be permanently changed.


Further information about the award is available through Journalism Programs at Holland College and UPEI. Students are invited to submit a series of three stories, reports or postings; articles will be judged on the basis of accuracy, originality, effectiveness in explaining issues to the public, informational value and insight. Submission date is November 15, 2021.
