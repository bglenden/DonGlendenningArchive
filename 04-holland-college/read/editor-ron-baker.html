An editor's letter from Don expressing sympathy for Dr. Ronald J. Baker, founding president of UPEI, who passed away. Don notes their parallel arrivals on PEI and their collaboration on institutional boards, highlighting Baker's experience from founding Simon Fraser University and BC's regional colleges system.
Holland College & Institutional Legacy

Editor

Charlottetown Guardian


Dear sir:

I am sorry to hear of  the recent passing of Dr. Ronald J. Baker, Founding President of the University of Prince Edward Island.   

We arrived on PEI within months of each other. he was a  member of the Board of Governors of Holland College during its founding years and I sat on the University Board.  Ron brought a unique experience to our Board in that he had been intimately involved with the founding of Simon Fraser University as well as the system of Regional Colleges in British Columbia.  Needless to say, his contribution to our discussions were valued.

My deepest sympathy to his family 


Don Glendenning, 

President Emeritus

Holland College
