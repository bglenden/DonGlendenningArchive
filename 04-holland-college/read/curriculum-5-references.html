Curated bibliography of contemporary educational research and initiatives supporting competency-based and mastery-based learning approaches. References span 2016-2017 innovations in New Hampshire performance-based assessments, New York mastery-based classrooms eliminating grades and failure, competency-based education models, and student satisfaction research. Includes references to provincial initiatives in Newfoundland and Labrador, Alberta curriculum overhaul, Ontario mathematics score concerns, and MPHEC (Maritime Provinces Higher Education Commission) competency frameworks. Emphasizes emerging trends moving away from standardized testing toward performance-based assessment and competency demonstration, aligning with Don's educational philosophy.
