Letter of recommendation by Donald Glendenning, C.M, PhD, LLD (UPEI), President Emeritus of Holland College, supporting Dr. Tess Miller's application for full professorship at University of Prince Edward Island. Praises Dr. Miller's dedication, work with students, expertise in mathematics, and contributions to Education 20/20 Inc. board, where she participated in continuing education surveys in Souris area. Written by Don in his capacity as respected educator and institutional leader.
Holland College & Institutional Legacy

Departmental and University Review Committees.

University of Prince Edward Island

550 University Avenue

Charlottetown, PE C1A  4P3

Dr. Tess Miller

Tess

I am pleased to provide a letter of support for Dr. Tess Miller in her application for full-professorship at the University of Prince Edward Island.  I’ve known Dr Miller for a number of years and am impressed with the energy, dedication and insights which she brings to any conversation.  On a number of occasions, I was able to observe her working with students; she knew her students well, showed care and concern, and remained ever aware of opportunities to reinforce their learning.


Dr. Miller is known in the community for her work in mathematics; she strives for currency and relevancy in her field, and is ever alert to needs and opportunities for change.     


Dr. Miller joined the Board of Education 20/20 Inc during my term as President; as a Director, she was regular in her attendance, had read relevant documents in advance of the meeting and participated fully in discussions.  Dr. Miller brings many insights to matters under discussion and willingly played roles in various projects including overseeing a continuing education survey in the Souris area. 


Based on my knowledge of Dr. Miller and the University of Prince Edward Island, I have no hesitation in recommending her for advancement and I am confident that she will continue to be a leader in her field and bring honour to her University.


Sincerely



Donald Glendenning, C.M, PhD, LLD (UPEI)

President Emeritus (Holland College)
