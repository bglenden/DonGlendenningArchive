This document contains Don Glendenning's talking points for a Glenway Estates Association discussion regarding property access rights for the McInnis family. Don raises technical concerns about the proposed agreement, including questions about jurisdiction, liability, and unresolved details. His systematic approach demonstrates his methodical leadership style and attention to legal and governance details in community matters.

July 5, 2008


Initial Statement

on Request for Access to Glenway Drive


Opening

Important question, one of the most important we will have

I am quite prepared to deal with it but lack adequate information

I also have concerns about the process and the optics but these remarks only deal with technicalities of the access issue.

Why are we being dragged into a family squabble; McInnis have the resources to resolve the issue within their own resources?

Issues

Access by Linda

Verbal agreement with McInnis

Who are dealing with? Gerald? Linda? Pius/ McInnis family?

The manner in which the issue will be resolved


Insufficient or unclear information:

What did Gerald ask for?  I’d like to see a copy

Why McInnis family can’t or won’t provide access to a family member? (Are we getting involved in a family dispute?)

Why is granting access good for the Association 

MOU contains hearsay about an alleged permission

Why present a draft agreement that you yourself say may not be enforceable? 

What consideration have you given to the needs/interests of Glenway members - dust problem at Lot No. 1, erosion at corner near lot No.1, awkward and perhaps dangerous corner near Lot No. 1

Options are incomplete – do the clauses in option A apply to options B and C?

Factual error in Option C – it does not take more land out of production

How large a lot does Gerald intend to give?

Location of access – regulations and safety concerns

Size of access opening

Location of lot

Are there any regulatory requirements to be met? 

Costs to Glenway, if any, for changes to road, surveying? Legal documents?

Liability for a non-member of Glenway  to whom we give legal right to use


Closing comment

With the above information, I will be glad to deal with the issue

Too important an issue to be dealt with along with road repairs, financial statements and a request for a grant.

Move that the Executive be asked to gather more detailed information and convene a special members meeting to deal with the  access issue and this issue only.
