Meeting minutes from February 3, 2017, with Don Glendenning, Bill Whelan, Tanya Craig, and Alice Taylor. The executive reviewed and approved governance regulations with minor editorial changes to present to the full Board. The group agreed to publicly recognize Regis Duffy for support of Education 20/20's work and the Excellence in Education Award. They designated the executive committee as the finance committee pending further notice. Katherine McQuaid was asked to chair the nominating committee. A contract with Holland College student Crystal to develop the organization's website for $1,000 was approved, with budget provisions for domain registration and hosting.


EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Executive Meeting

Monday, February 3, 2017 at 12:00 noon

Battery Room, Stratford Town Hall, Stratford, PEI


Minutes: Meeting No. 2


  1. In attendance: Alice Taylor, Bill Whelan, Tanya Craig, and Don Glendenning

  2. Call to order:

  3. Regulations: Members reviewed drafts of the following regulations: Membership, Directors, Duties of a Board, Officers, Establishing committees, (Nominating. Awards, Program and Finance). Minor editorial changes were suggested after which regulations will be presented to Board for approval.

  4. Recognition: agreed to recognize Regis Duffy for his support of our work generally and the Excellence in Education Award specifically.

  5. Finance Committee: Agreed that the Executive Committee will act as a finance Committee until further notice.

  6. Incoming Executive: agreed to ask Katherine McQuaid to chair a nominating committee to propose a slate of Officers for the coming year

  7. Website: Bill recommended a contract with Crystal, a student at Holland College, to develop a website; he presented a draft agreement but is still consulting a couple of other Directors before awarding contract; contract will be with Crystal, a senior student at Holland College for $1,000.

  1. in the amount of $1,000; budget needs to reflect costs including name registration and web host. Thanks Tom for his contribution; Next meeting: at the call of the chair
