Don's critical analysis of contemporary education systems, characterizing them as age-based, bureaucratic, and inflexible with obscured accountability and teacher-centered delivery. He identifies persistent systemic gaps including insufficient outcomes definition, lack of learner centeredness, limited support for independent learners, and inadequate professional autonomy. This foundational document outlines problems he spent decades addressing through Holland College and his educational reform work.

Definition

  • all the schools and support services under the jurisdiction of a board of education

  • The system of formalized transmission of knowledge and values operating within a given society
