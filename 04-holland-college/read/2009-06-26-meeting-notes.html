Only 26 responses to date

Podium will  be placed between fireplaces north side of room

Don’t forget knife to cut cake

Need to cut a cheque for Glenn MacEachern; give it to Garth who will pass it to Glenn along with gift certificates

Have a guest registration book; encourage but not require registration, need a desk for book and pens
