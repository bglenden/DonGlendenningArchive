Credits for a slide-tape presentation introducing Holland College's STEP (Self Training and Evaluation Program) in September 1970. Don is listed as "Historian." The document credits include Sandy Carruthers (Graphics), Matt Rainnie (Narrator), Terrence McGaughey (AV Services), Sandi White (Coordinator), Henry Purdy (Consultant), and others. This artifact demonstrates Don's early involvement in shaping and documenting Holland College's identity and educational approach.
