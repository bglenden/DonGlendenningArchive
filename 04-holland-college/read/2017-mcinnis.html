May 4, 2017



To Whom It May Concern:



Kenneth F. McInnis


I’ve been asked to provide a letter of reference in support of Kenneth McInnis who is being nominated as a distinguished alumnus of Holland College.


I have known Ken for many years, first as a student in architectural technology and later as an instructor.  I have followed his career with interest and am certainly aware of his community involvement including his volunteer work at Holland College.  I had the pleasure of seeing him recently and was pleased with his comments about the role played by Holland College in the evolution of his life and career.


His resume is impressive; no doubt, he would represent the College well.


Sincerely




Donald Glendenning

President Emeritus

Holland College
