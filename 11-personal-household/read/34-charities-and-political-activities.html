EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Support Document: Meeting 34


Subject: Education 20/20 Objectives Prepared by: Don Glendenning

Number: SD: 1 Date: February 15, 2013

_____________________________________________________________________________________



APPROVED BY CRA IN 2016:

  1. Advance education by providing:

  • Leadership development programs and workshops to individuals who plan, administer and develop educational programs;

  • Supplemental educational support services such as study centres and educational programs for parents and children;

  • Awards to schools and early learning centres in Canada to encourage academic excellence; and


  1. To undertake activities ancillary and incidental to the attainment of the above charitable purposes.


SUBMISSION WITH REQUEST FOR CHARITABLE STATUS, MARCH, 2015


  1. To Advance education by organizing leadership development activities for people who plan, administer and deliver education programs.

  2. Advance education through increasing the amount and flow of information and ideas between and among education partners

  3. Advance education by providing supplemental educational support services

  4. Advance education by supporting research and innovation, and by promoting best practices in the field of education and training.



FROM ORIGINAL ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION:

  1. To promote education and educational opportunity for every Canadian

  2. To exercise leadership in the improvement in all facets of education

  3. To foster cooperation between and among all stakeholders

  4. Undertake and support research and innovation

  5. improve public understanding of the education system
