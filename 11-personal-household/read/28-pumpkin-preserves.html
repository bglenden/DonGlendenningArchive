Pumpkin Preserves


Ingredients:

Yield: 4 pints

Units: US | Metric

8 cups cubed peeled pumpkin (1/2 inch cubes)
4 cups granulated sugar
1 lemon
5 whole cloves

Directions:

1 Layer pumpkin with sugar in a large bowl.
2 Cover and let stand overnight, stirring once or twice.
3 Next day, drain juice off of pumpkin into saucepan.
4 Bring to a boil and boil for 10 minutes.
5 Add pumpkin pieces, grated rind and chopped pulp of the lemon.
6 Add cloves (tied in a cheesecloth bag).
7 Cook on a slow boil for 40 minutes, or until pumpkin is clear and the mixture 

   sheets from a spoon.
8 Remove the cloves and pour into hot sterile jars.
9 Seal.
