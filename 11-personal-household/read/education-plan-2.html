Glendenning compares education plan priorities across Canadian provinces. New Brunswick emphasizes belonging, competencies, literacy, arts/science/trades, Indigenous education, and leadership development. Nova Scotia focuses on renewal, refocusing, rebuilding, and teamwork. Newfoundland/Labrador's 2018 plan addresses inclusive education, student health and welfare, mathematics, reading, Indigenous education, early years, and teacher development. PEI's priorities are marked with a question mark, suggesting Glendenning viewed PEI as lacking a coherent provincial education plan. This comparative analysis likely informed his reform recommendations for PEI.
