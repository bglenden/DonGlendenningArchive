Letter to Donor to Memorial Fund 


Dear


On behalf of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island, I want to thank you for your contribution(s) to the Memorial Fund and to let you know that your contributions have made a difference to the Island community.  


Memorial contributions are used to support the work of the Living Trust, a fund which enhances  public spaces throughout Prince Edward Island.  In 2007, for example, we planted a cluster of Island oaks on each side of the entrance to the Confederation Trail at Wood Islands.  In 2008, Memorial Funds were used to plant trees along the Confederation Trail at Milton, and we expect to add to the Milton site again this year.  Also this year, we will be planting a Memorial Tree at the Confederation Park in Charlottetown.  As funds allow, we will develop other sites throughout the Island and expand activities to include to include planting of gardens and shrubs and the placement of benches for the enjoyment of Islanders and visitors alike.


Should you be interested, you can now arrange memorials through our website.  Simply log on to our website at   HYPERLINK "http://www.cfpei.ca" www.cfpei.ca and follow the instructions of the Canada Helps icon.  You will receive your receipt immediately and we will send a suitable memorial card as directed by you.


Again, we  thank you for using our Memorial Fund.  As you will see from the enclosed brochure, our Living Trust also provides for memorial trees and sites.  We also administer family funds should you, or your friends or family, be interested.  


Your contribution to the work of the Community Foundation is appreciated.


Sincerely,


John Robinson

President
