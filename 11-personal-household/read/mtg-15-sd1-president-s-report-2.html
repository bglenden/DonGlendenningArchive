Don Glendenning's president's report to Education 20/20 Inc. board (November 2013). Documents completed activities including a learning framework workshop with 15-20 participants; dialogue with PEI Deputy Minister on PISA results; resignation of board member Eileen Conway-Martin (transitioning to committee leadership); and ongoing conversations with professional engineers and other stakeholder groups. Outlines emerging work on governance structure, board composition, and leveraging the Community Foundation's Vital Signs report on PEI youth to inform education advocacy.


EDUCATION 20/20 INC.

Completed:
