A comprehensive comparative grid (dated June 2, 2015) mapping challenge for credit program availability across Canadian institutions including PEI Public Schools, UPEI, Holland College, Athabasca University, Memorial University, Ontario institutions, Manitoba, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, British Columbia schools, and private institutions like Citadel Halifax and Chignecto. The document notes differences between PLAR (Prior Learning Assessment and Recognition), exemptions, and challenge options, observing that Manitoba had particularly generous policies and that Colonel Gray's exam exemptions operated as a pilot for four years.
