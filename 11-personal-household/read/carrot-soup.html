Creamy carrot-dill soup recipe featuring fresh or dried dill, carrots, chicken broth, and cream. Preparation involves sautéing onions, cooking vegetables, blending mixture until smooth, then adding cream. Recipe yields four servings and includes note about substituting onion salt for individual salt and onion components, offering preparation flexibility.

CREAMY CARROT-DILL SOUP


1 Small onion, chopped

15ml (1 tbsp) butter or margarine

1 lb Carrots

2 cups Chicken broth

15ml (I tbsp) fresh dill or 1 tsp dried dill

Salt to taste

250ml (1 cup) half & half or milk


Note:  Substitute 1 tsp onion salt for onion and salt


In 3 l. saucepan, cook onion in butter for 5 minutes

Or until onion is tender

Add carrots, broth and dill

Cook over medium heat 15-18 minutes or 

until carrots are  very tnder

Put carrots, broth, dill and salt into blender container

Cover and process until smooth

Return mixture to sauce pan

Stir in half and half

Heat gently; do not boil


Makes four servings
