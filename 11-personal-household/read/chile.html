Recipe for chili (spelled "Chile") using red kidney beans, canned diced tomatoes, tomato soup, ground hamburger, onion salt, garlic powder, and vinegar. Simple preparation involves browning hamburger, adding all ingredients, and bringing to boil. Represents practical, economical family meal preparation using canned ingredients for convenience and consistency.

CHILE

INGREDIENTS

Red Kidney beans 2 cans

Diced tomatoe vcs  1 can

Tomato soup-1 can

Hamburger – 1- 1.5 lb

Onion salt -1 tsp

Garlic Powder – 1 tsp

Vinegar – 1 tsp

Oil – 1tbs


PROCESS

Table spoon oil in large saucepan

Add hamburger

Cook stirring occasionally

Drain if needed

Add other ingredients

Bring to a boil
