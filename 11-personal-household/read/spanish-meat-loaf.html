SPANISH MEAT LOAF

Meat loaf

1-11/2 lb.Lean hamburger

I egg

Can of tomatoes

1 cup oatmeal

Onion 

Salt

Pepper


Topping

½ cup Ketchup

1 tbs  Mustard

Brown sugar


Process

Mix initial ingredients

Makes one pan

Mix  ingredients for topping in separate bowl and spread on top

Put whole peeled onion in one end of pan

Cook at 250 degrees  for one hour
