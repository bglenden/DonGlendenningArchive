Subject: Georgetown


Contact: Mayor Peter Lewellyn

Residence: 652-2746; Town: 652-2924


Summer 2007: Stopped at the Mayor’s shop to introduce self and CFPEI; Mayor mentioned recent bequest to town and we talked about the e establishment of a fund in the donor’s name with earnings to be used in and by the town


Fall 2007 Maintained contact and sought opportunity to address Council; later Mayor told me that Council decided not to establish a fund 


Feb 12, 2008 John Robinson, Lou Ann Thomson, Joyce Gaudet and don Glendenning made a presentation in Georgetown library expecting the Mayor and perhaps other councilors to be present; there was another meeting that night.


April 21, 2008 Don Glendenning and Lou Ann Thomson made presentation to Town Council.  Mayor suggested that we put a link to CFPEI on Town website.

July 15, 2008 Discussed Georgetown Heritage Fund; he is supportive; not prepared to assume any role at present.  Also spoke with tonya at City Hall about website.

Proposal:

Georgetown Heritage Fund (Bea Mair fund); CFPEI establish a local committee –
