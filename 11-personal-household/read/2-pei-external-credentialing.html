This document catalogs external credentialing advisory committee programs in Prince Edward Island as of 2011-2014, including partnerships with organizations like Cadets Canada, Royal Conservatory of Music, Sport PEI, Duke of Edinburgh, Conservatory Canada Music, PEI 4-H Council, Dance Umbrella, and Royal Canadian Naval Reserve. Each organization provided credit equivalencies recognizing non-traditional learning achievements. The document reflects Don's involvement in educational innovation through recognition of diverse learning pathways and competency-based credentialing systems that accommodated student learning outside traditional classroom settings.
