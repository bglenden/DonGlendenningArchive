Working notes on writing assessment providing a 6-level rating scale based on creativity, organization, vocabulary precision, grammar, sentence structure, and focus. The scale ranges from disorganized/limited vocabulary to creative/well-organized work, reflecting Don's competency-based approach to defining clear performance standards for writing at any educational level.

Rating scale for writing


6 Creatively written while observing acceptable standards of grammar, sentence structure and organization. 

5 Very well organized showing precise and wide vocabulary, good sentence structure and appropriate grammar and some creativity. 

4 Organized with few errors in grammar and sentence structure. 

3 Organized with acceptable sentence structure, grammar and vocabulary. 

Generally organized but uses limited vocabulary and has significant errors in grammar and sentence structure. 

1 Disorganized and unfocused with many errors in
grammar and sentence structure accompanied by a
limited vocabulary.
