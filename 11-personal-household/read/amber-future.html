Don outlines an ambitious vision for PEI as an educational leader through competency-based teacher education, renewed federal partnership on skill training, and appointing a provincial Education Director equivalent to the Chief Medical Officer. He identifies immediate priorities (family education advisers, competency-based curriculum, performance-based assessment, area education councils) and government actions needed (clear statement of underlying principles, function-based educational organization, autonomy and accountability for schools). This document captures the essence of his comprehensive reform agenda emphasizing alignment of educational structure, teacher preparation, assessment, and governance around learner competency development.
