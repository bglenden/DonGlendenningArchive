Collection of three recipes: Hamburger Pie (hamburger, corn, tomato soup, mashed potatoes), Stew with Dumplings (stew meat, root vegetables, simple flour dumplings), and Spanish Meatloaf (ground hamburger, oatmeal, eggs, tomatoes, spices with brown sugar-ketchup topping). All recipes represent practical, family-friendly, economical meal preparation using basic ingredients available in mid-20th century Canadian households.
