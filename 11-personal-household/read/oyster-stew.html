Regional oyster stew recipe featuring fresh shucked oysters, milk, butter, and Worcestershire sauce, with salt and pepper to taste. Preparation involves heating oysters until curled, warming milk with seasonings separately, then combining without boiling. Yields three servings and served with crackers. Recipe reflects Atlantic Canadian coastal culinary traditions and seafood preparation methods.

OYSTER STEW



Ingredients

250 ml shucked  oysters

500 ml 10% milk

Salt – ½ tsp

Butter – size of an egg

Pepper – ½ tsp

Worcestershire sauce – ½ tsp


Procedure

Heat oysters in fry pan until oysters begin to curl

Add salt, butter, pepper to milk and heat in separate sauce pan

Combine, do not boil

add Worcestershire sauce to suit taste


Serve with crackers

Makes three servings
