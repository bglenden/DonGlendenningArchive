Souris Study

We are pleased to announce a survey of needs for continuing education in the Souris area of PEI.  The study will include a survey and consultations with Souris residents together with a strategy for the future.  The study is being funded by SkillsPEI.


Training survey announced for Souris Area

Education 20/20 Inc. Is pleased to announce a training needs survey for adults in the Souris Area of Prince Edward island.  The study will include review of current offerings and capacity, interests and needs for further training as viewed by residents, employers and the community at large.  Residents will be invited to make their views known through the distribution of a questionnaire.  The study is being funded by SkillsPEI.


The study comes at a crucial time in that PEI is in the midst of population growth and a strong economy; future employment will require a flexible and skilled workforce. The final report will be made public include recommendations for decisionmakers as to future directions in adult education supports.


Education 20/20 Inc is an independent, broad-based national, charitable organization located in Prince Edward Island with a focus on ensuring a quality education for every Canadian.
