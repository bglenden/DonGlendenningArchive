Panel recruitment worksheet for Math Map project identifying ideal characteristics (articulate, active in community, representative, balanced) and typical occupations represented (farmers, health workers, IT professionals, supervisors, youth workers, community organizers). Lists 11 specific potential candidates including Tanya Craig, Martina MacDonald, Regis Duffy, Margaret Ching, and others from various PEI sectors. Shows Don's deliberate network-building and community engagement in recruiting diverse panel members.
