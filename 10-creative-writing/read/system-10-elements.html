Shorter document synthesizing components of successful education systems from multiple sources. Identifies problems with current systems (examination-oriented, outdated teaching, lack of innovation). Lists Lay Kee Ching's inclusive system elements: rigorous learning models, diverse criteria, problem-solving mindset, personalized methods, technology use, faculty training, curriculum, adaptive courses. Presents Business Roundtable components (standards, assessments, accountability, professional development, autonomy, parent involvement, learning readiness, technology, safety/discipline) and Community Education Principles (lifelong learning, self-determination, help, leadership development, responsiveness, integrated delivery, localization, resource use, inclusiveness, information access).
