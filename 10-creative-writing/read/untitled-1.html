A short poem titled 'Untitled' (circa 1973-74) exploring themes of problem-solving, time's passage, and the need for finding balance. The poem reflects philosophical concerns about decisions, time's fleeting nature, and learning to value life's precious moments.

Untitled


Hyper

Perhaps

Problems are solved

In the Still of the night


Others

Less fortunate

Drowning their sorrows

Unable to cope

With a rising tomorrow



Minutes and hours

Relentlessly   passing

Decisions required

What will they be


No use delaying

Sand has all fallen

Answers are given

Time will tell


Time is in…cannot decipher, see original

Limited quantity

When will we learn

To Treasure its worth


Undated but lkely 1973-74
