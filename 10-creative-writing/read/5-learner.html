Framework document defining "Learner" as a person engaged in learning. Categorizes factors influencing learners into three areas: Prior Experiences (home, school, travel, culture, lifestyle, support) and Prior Conditions (physical, mental, emotional, social wellness), and Imagined Future (family, career, community, income, lifestyle). Emphasizes that learners bring diverse backgrounds and contexts to learning situations.
