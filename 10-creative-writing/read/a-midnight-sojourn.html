Final version of the camping poem (December 1973) with minor refinements from the draft. Same themes of stillness, natural beauty, and the ephemeral nature of moments creating lasting memories. The polished version corrects "overwhelms one" to "overwhelms me" and "reigh" to "reign," showing Don's attention to craft.
Creative Writing

A Midnight sojourn


Far from the traffic

Stillness o’erwhelmsd me

Frost in the hollow

Why am I here


Stars twinkle

Brooks trickle

Gentle sounds

As the campfire dwindles


Flickering shadows

Fade in the moonlight

Memories created

Long may they reign


Dec/73
