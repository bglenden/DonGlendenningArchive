A comprehensive education services framework showing how services meet the needs of "150,000 Islanders with differences" (those born with differences, who have learned, and who hold aspirations) seeking to change their attitudes, skills, and knowledge. The document presents education services across governance and leadership, instruction, quality assurance, information and counselling, human resources, learning products, and funding. This represents Don's systems-thinking approach to addressing diverse population needs.
