Children's story about Brian (Don's son) and his sister Janet visiting a Charlottetown TV studio where they meet Arthur, a commercial artist working on "The Krazy Keepers" show. Arthur explains different types of artistic work and careers, including set design, sign painting, and freelance work. The story demonstrates Don's educational approach to teaching children about careers and professions, incorporating his son's direct experience.
