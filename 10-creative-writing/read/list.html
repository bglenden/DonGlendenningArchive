This document presents a structured framework for educational decision-making, addressing key issues including vision, structure, school autonomy, accountability, philosophy, curriculum, and the need for self-correcting mechanisms. Don identifies essential starting points: overarching statements of beliefs, clear functions, defined services, and transparent allocation of government funding. The framework employs "Road of Life" as an organizing principle—described as simple, relatable, applicable across educational levels, and useful for addressing educational transitions. This approach bridges diverse perspectives and decision-making roles, providing practical guidance for educational policy and system design in PEI.
