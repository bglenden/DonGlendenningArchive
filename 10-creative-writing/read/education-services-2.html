Community Education Service-1

Souris/Eastern Kings, PEI

3-Souris/EduServe

Background:

Souris is an urban centre in eastern Prince Edward Island; it has a population in 2016 of 1,053; with additional 2745 people living in the immediate surrounding area. Together they make a community one of 3,798 people. The economy of the area is dominated by seasonal industries of fishing, aquaculture, agriculture and tourism. A recent study of the area revealed an interest and needs for additional continuing education services in the area. In response, Education20/20 proposes an education service agency to encourage and support educational services for the Souris area.


Purpose: increase training opportunities available in the Souris Area; especially training opportunities that lead to or support employment.


The Centre will:

  • Encourage and support education services for residents of the area

  • Alert residents, employers and community organizations of training opportunities especially those being offered locally

  • Encourage training agencies to offer courses and modules of programs in the Souris area

  • Promote and advertise training opportunities

  • Organize and host information sessions for residents and employers

  • Represent the community’s interests in matters of education

  • Liaise with other providers of educational services


Guiding principles:

  • Increase the local capacity to provide training capacity

  • Priority will be given to training activities that support employment

  • Be the “Go-To” organization in Souris for information about continuing education


Governance/administration

  • Three person local Committee/Board

  • Local chair

  • Represent community not education providers

  • May also have a program advisory/liaison committee of local education leaders

  • Project to be hosted by a not-for-profit organization
