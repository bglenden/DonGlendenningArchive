An ad hoc working document listing comprehensive reading competencies and skills. The document catalogs diverse reading-related abilities including critical reading, comprehension, vocabulary, interpretation of various text formats (prose, graphs, drawings, manuals, tables, symbols, labels), word attack skills, and specialized reading modes (skimming, scanning, extensive, intensive reading). The list addresses contemporary needs such as reading text messages and computer printouts alongside traditional literacy skills. This appears to be a working reference tool for curriculum development, organizing reading competencies by type and application to support evidence-based literacy instruction in educational systems.
