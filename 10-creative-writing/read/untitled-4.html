Untitled



Your note received

Considered

Reflected

Unanswered

But not forgotten


Thoughts jumbled

Tumbled

New Shapes

Change and uncertainty

Final transition

Just 10days away


Although role changing

Travels continue

Perhaps my next visit

Will see us share tea


June 21, 1987
