Document cataloging diverse assessment methods organized into five categories: Selected Response (true/false, matching, multiple choice), Essay Assessment, Performance Assessment (skills demonstration, product creation, role-playing, portfolio), and Personal Communication (questioning, discussion, interviews, parent/teacher conferences). Attributes approach to Richard Stiggins. Represents comprehensive, multi-modal approach to educational assessment reflecting contemporary best practices in educational measurement.
