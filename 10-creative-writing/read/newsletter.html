A comprehensive template defining the newsletter structure and content categories for Open Education. The document outlines a weekly publication model with diverse content elements including bilingual articles, member profiles, products, presidential columns, guest editorials, board meeting notes, event reports, student sections, photo galleries, regular columns, advertising opportunities, and reader letters. The template emphasizes management responsibilities (editor, committee roles, distribution, timelines), technical specifications (bilingual layout, PDF format, Arial or Times New Roman fonts, color elements), and editorial workflows including article preparation, selection, editing, and proofreading. This represents Don's comprehensive vision for a professional, member-focused educational publication.
