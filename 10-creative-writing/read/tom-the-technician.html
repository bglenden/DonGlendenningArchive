A children's story about young Brian visiting his Uncle Tom in Hamilton and learning about his work as an electronic technician at Westinghouse, testing color television sets. The story follows Brian's fascination with factory work and technical instruments, and his dream of becoming a technician himself. This reflects Don's family experience and interest in making technical work understandable to young people.
