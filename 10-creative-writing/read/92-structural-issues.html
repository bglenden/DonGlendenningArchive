Open Education

No. 92 February 20, 2020

When schools and ministries are managed well, learning happens-World Bank


TIME FOR A CONVERSATION

People want to talk about education, not just about schools, storm days or test results. People for Education, an Ontario group, has recommended that the Minister constitute a Task Force – with participation from all the education partners, including directors of education, teachers’ federations, principals’ councils, trustee associations, provincial student organizations, deans of education, Indigenous education associations etc. – to ensure that the plan for education during and post-COVID is both comprehensive and coherent.


I think PEI has a bigger agenda, it’s about Islanders – people of all ages, about their futures, and how education can help.











Don Glendenning, Editor
