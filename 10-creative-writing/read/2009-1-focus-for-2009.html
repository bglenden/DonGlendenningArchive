December 22, 2008 memo from Don to Development Committee outlining achievements and strategic focus for 2009. Details accomplishments in professional advisors outreach (Heather, John, Donnie), donor file updates (Jeniene), cemetery trust identification (David, Donnie), and persons of interest discussions (Norman). Emphasizes principles: "People give to people," past donors are best source of new donations, committee is forum for discussion, diverse outreach approaches (letters, presentations, brochures). Reflects Don's leadership philosophy.

Date: December 22, 2008


To: Development Committee


From: Don Glendenning


Subject:  Development focus for 2009


1 Achievements to date:


Professional Advisors – Heather (Chair), John and Donnie prepared a Power Point presentation  and met with (name organizations)  Meetings usually lasted xx minutes depending on the amount of discussion.  Additional meetings will be scheduled.  Proposing to expand beyond financial institutions and have target of three new presentations each month for 2009.

Existing Donors – Jeniene (Volunteer) has reviewed and updated donor files, worked with John in identifying priority individuals to contact, alerted Directors to progress in relation to target dates, 

Existing Trusts – David and Donnie have analyzed CRA lists, identified cemeteries as a place to commence and the particular cemeteries to contact first; prepared draft letter for short listed cemeteries, prepared brochure to enclose with letter, first  round of letters expected to be sent by mid-January ; are maintaining contact with Claude Matheson as to interest of Dundas Cemetery,

Persons of Interest – Norman (Chair) and Don discussed some options; very broad range including current and past directors, past donors, short listed names, new names, etc.; propose to initiate discussions with current and past Directors in 2009; 

Information/promotion – Marie (Chair) has had a number of family health issues, however, she and John have met to discuss the production of a brochure. 

Other Foundations/Charitable organizations – Tom is making contact with a number of the larger organizations with a view to keeping communication lines open but also to make them aware of the service that we provide
