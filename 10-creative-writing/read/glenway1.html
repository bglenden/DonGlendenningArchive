Earlier draft version of Glenway dated July 10, 1974, presenting a more austere perspective. Describes the property as windswept, rain-drenched, fog-shrouded, and barren of natural shelter, though humanized by erected structures. Emphasizes impermanence—inhabitants scatter, time marches on, memories linger. Contrasts sharply with the 1996 celebratory version, revealing how Don's emotional relationship to the place evolved over 22 years.

Glenway

Incomplete

July 10, 1974
