Untitled


In the US of A

On an earlier day

A son came to us

Without much fuss


Today he’s grown

Tall and lean

And on his way

He turned sixteen


It’s not hard to  guess

What he will find

But this little game

Will test his mind


The Beatkles record

He will tresure

It will give him

Hours of pleasure

The benedicton

Here’s money for

Your science fiction


Enjoy these things

And your birthday, too

Have a happy day

And a good year, too
