Creative Writing

Upon my desk, they sat

Opened, read and contents noted

But still unanswered

For ere so long

My conscience is much troubled


So with the spirit of the season

I sit this morn

To pen to you

A few rambling thoughts


First

Let me assure you that

My breach of etiquette

Has naught to do with you

Nor anything you penned

But simply 

My unorganized

And disorganized

And unfocussed

(Or too focused)

Style of living


Enough said

Let us move on

I note on re-reading

That the source of Learning

Or at least its instrument

Located on the Cam

Will expose your words to wider readership

My congratulations and good wishes


I am sure you will have noted 

my change of stationary

And how I still require

Some trappings

To provide

A sense of place

And also to provide

Some comfort and security

That those without

Find within

Perhaps soon

I will have the courage

To find out who I am

Without such things


In many ways 

I have revisited 

A much more basic cycle

Where work and home

Are less discrete

And more gestalt

Like yesteryear

But with the New Technology


It works

I work

Travel

And do the things I did before

But to a different end

And with less urgency


My note is short

But it will let you know, dear friend

That you still form 


part of my experience

And wish you well

In your existence

Whatever the season

And the New Year brings


Dec 13, 1987
