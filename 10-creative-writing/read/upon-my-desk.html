Upon my desk

A short poem dated June 10, 1986, reflecting on unanswered correspondence sitting on Don's desk. The poem explores his internal conflict about responding, noting uncertainty about the purpose and origin of the message, and his desire to respond with care and uncommon commitment. Brief but revealing of Don's conscientious personality and tendency to overthink correspondence.

Upon my desk


Upon my desk 

It sat 

INtruding 

INtriquing 

Occasionally Attracting. 


I read

AND thought

AND said 

I must respond

But HOW? 


Purposes, 

MESSages

Origin

UNcertain 


OBviously 

A MISSION personal 

Respond in Kind 

With CARE 

And UN-COMMITTED



June 10, 1986
