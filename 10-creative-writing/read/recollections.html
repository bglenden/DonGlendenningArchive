Short poetic reflection by Don (December 1973) on Christmas memories and lost loved ones, with meditation on stillness and the question of whether others will remember his Christmas with him. Shows his introspective and emotional nature, particularly around family and the passage of time.

Recollections

In the night

Stillness

Christmases

Of yesteryear



Images 

Of loved ones

No longer with us

Why so at Christmas

Why? Why? Why?


Memories flooding

Yet fully remembered

Will someone remember

This Christmas with me?


December 1973
