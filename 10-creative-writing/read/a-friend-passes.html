Short reflective poem dated June 1, 1974, meditating on the fragility of life and the impact of a friend's death. The piece captures the tension between everyday life and the sudden awareness of mortality that death brings, exploring themes of what truly matters in life.

A Friend Passes



Life

Fragile

Vibrant

Engrossed with living

We take it for granted


The lifeline snaps

Eternity

What is important


June 1, 1974
