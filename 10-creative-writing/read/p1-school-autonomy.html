School Autonomy

Poll No. 1


We spoke on occasion about conducting polls and surveys. I am wide open to any suggestion but propose school autonomy and accountability as a place to start. As background:


“PISA in Focus, an OECD report in October, 2011 reported that:

  1. In countries where schools have greater autonomy over what is taught and how students are assessed, students tend to perform better.

  2. In countries where schools account for their results by posting achievement data publicly, schools that enjoy greater autonomy in resource allocation tend to show better student performance than those with less autonomy. In countries where there are no such accountability arrangements, schools with greater autonomy in resource allocation tend to perform worse. “


Possible Questions:

  1. Do you favour greater autonomy over day to day operations for your school?

Yes, No, No opinion

  1. Do you favour posting of PISA results for your school

Yes, No, No opinion


Any thoughts?

Enjoy the weekend

Don
