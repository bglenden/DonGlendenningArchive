This 2015-2018 document outlines diverse funding-eligible project proposals including independent information surveys, assessment services, greenfield school design competitions, learning cafes, student advisors, community education workers, educational events, non-credit technical courses, and strategic "hook" campaigns. Don proposes diverse revenue models including fee-for-service, workshops, educational trade shows, self-instructional materials, and for-profit development ventures. Projects emphasize independent information provision, stakeholder engagement, innovation support, and community-driven solutions. The breadth of concepts demonstrates Don's entrepreneurial thinking about educational improvement funding and his conviction that market-based and community-based models complement traditional education systems.

PROJECT IDEAS

For which we may seek funds:


Surveys – become an independent  source of information in taking the pulse of people in relation to their education system

Independent Assessment Service – establishing levels of achievement is a regulatory function and not a teaching one; Education 20/20 could help, even lead, the establishiment of an independent assessment service.

Greenfield Project – sponsor an award to teams of people to develop the “best” school system for a hypothetical community

Learning Café- a space set aside for people who want to study and feel the need for occasional assistance – tutoring but not formal instruction

Student Advocat Student adciser, learning managers

Education development worker:  a community education facilitator to work with the community in resolving issues pertaining to the education of its citizens

Host – organize events and activities that bring educators together to encourage discussion on educational matters 

Non-credit Courses – coding, robotics, weekend courses, evening classes for any and all who are interested including children, employed people, people in prison, tourists, summer camps, religion, summer school

Hooks – public campaign(s) on subjects that are important but also high profile to attract attention to our cause – challenge for credit, home helper service, summer schools, call for proposals for study centres, 

There is a dearth of independent and current information aimed at stakeholders

Summer leadership workshops

For profit development company 

Self- instructional material/webinar, etc

Education products

Annual conference on aa current issue- mental health, for instance
