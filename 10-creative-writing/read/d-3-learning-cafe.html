A straightforward proposal for an informal, monthly discussion forum on PEI education improvements. The Learning Café would operate in accessible restaurant or lounge settings, open to anyone without membership requirements, using evening meetings for primarily informal discussion with optional speakers. Logistics are minimal: communication via email, no required minutes or meeting reminders, no fees. Governance emphasizes maximizing participation, maintaining non-partisan focus, and respecting diverse viewpoints while remaining issue-oriented. This modest initiative reveals Don's belief that grassroots community conversation is essential to educational reform and his talent for creating low-barrier opportunities for public engagement.
