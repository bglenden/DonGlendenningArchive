PAUL THE POLITICIAN 


Bruce 


Heard people talking. He listened 


And Listened 


But couldn't quite hear What was being said. 


After 


He went to bed 


The door bell rang And people 


Came to the house 


They sat in the living room And talked with his father. Mostly they talked 


In low voices 


But 


Once in a while He heard names 


Names he heard before And sometimes 


Read about in the paper. 


A few days later His daddy said 


He was going to run In the next election. 


Bruce didn't understand What his father meant So he asked his father Where he was going 


To Run.

His father said If he was elected He would work 


To find more jobs 


To make highways safer To help older people And 


Make main street Into

Mr. Coffin said That a mall

Almost every day The newspaper The radio 


The television Carried stories About the election. 


Some supported Bruce's Daddy And called on People

Finally election day arrived. Bruce was excited. 


He went with his Dad 


To the school 


Where people voted. He waited 


While his father voted. 


At Seven o'clock Channel 11 began Giving the results Of the election. 


His father was ahead. He was getting 


Most of the votes. Finally 


The television announcer Said that Dad had won. He had the most votes.

Bruce was happy. His mother let him Stay up late 


To help celebrate. 


After everyone went home Bruce went to bed. 


He fell asleep. 


And started dreaming About an election And he dreamed 


That he had become 


Prime Minister of Canada.
