This framework document presents "Typical Education Functions" addressing clientele with diverse needs (varying genes, prior learning, and goals) in multiple life roles: individual, family member, citizen, learner, producer, and consumer. The services needed to help islanders thrive include developing better attitudes, skills, and knowledge across information and counselling, human resources, learning products, and funding categories. The repetition of client descriptions emphasizes the comprehensive nature of education's role.
