Brief document advocating for establishing clear educational benchmarks based on practical community examples. Uses anecdotes (farmer's tractor plowing capacity, journeyman plumber standards) to illustrate the need for concrete performance standards. Proposes that by Grade 9, students should have developed essential skills for functioning in democratic society. Emphasizes aligning benchmarks with actual community needs and citizen role requirements.
