Perhaps you are right, that a note on the figure might have clarified the figure. The executive committee in our conversation confirmed that most folk agreed that 20' was sufficient, when we were all standing out at the corner. We used the phrase "approximate" to give us some negotiating space to work with. For example, Annette later proposed that one side be 20' and another 30' so that given the 16' distance in from the pin, we would actually have a more balanced corner. If the McInnis's are open to deeding the corner, I believe we can press the case for the 20-30 solution.

 

On another matter, you may recall that I offered to cut your grass while you were in Ottawa. The offer is still open if you want it don.

 

Have a good trip and enjoy the riches of the Capitol.
