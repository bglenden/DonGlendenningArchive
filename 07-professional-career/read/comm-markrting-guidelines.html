Guidelines for the Communications/Marketing Committee covering strategic planning for donor engagement, public relations, and communications tools (brochures, newsletters, annual reports, videos). Outlines three target publics: donors/potential donors, charitable organizations, and the community at large, reflecting institutional best practices for foundation communications.

Guidelines


The following are intended as guidelines for the development of a communications/marketing strategy:


• the plan should consider the purpose of the Foundation, the customers (donors),  the public (including grant recipients), the competition, and the operating environment.

• which will address three publics - donors and potential donors, charitable organizations and the community at large.

• ideally, the plan should include a general brochure, a gift/pledge form, periodic newsletters, an annual report, a short video for prospective donors.

• special materials for professional advisors.
