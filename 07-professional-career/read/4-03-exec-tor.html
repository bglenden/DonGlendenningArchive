Subject: TOR Executive Committee


The Executive Committee is comprised of the President, Past President, Vice President, Treasurer  and Secretary with the President as Chair.


The role of the Executive committee is to:


Provide leadership to the Foundation


Establish policies and procedures


Monitor and implement agreements


Provide for facilities and administrative support services


Recommend strategies for short and long term operational financing


Identify and manage partnership opportunities


Review and monitor revenue and expenditures


Establish committees


Ensure that the Foundation carries out its mandate
