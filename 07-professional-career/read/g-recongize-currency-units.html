RECOGNIZE CURRENCY UNITS

Competency description

1-8-Platform-

RATIONALE

The very first step in learning about money and finance is understanding what money looks like. We need to be able to recognize the parts of the currency of one’s country in order to participate in regular living.


ELABORATIONS

Upon development of this skill, you will be able to:

  1. Recall the units of currency in ascending order: various coins and bills in dollar amounts.

  2. Sort various units of currency into like groups and in order from smaller to larger.

  3. Identify the value of units of currency

  4. Differentiate between money and currency.

  5. Tender money and make change.


TYPICAL ACTIVITIES

The following activities are intended to assist you in the development of the above-mentioned skill. They are representative of the work you are expected to do in this guide.


PERFORMANCE ASSESSMENT

You can use performance assessments to assess what you can already do in the way of assessment and also as items showing progress. To demonstrate your performance you will have:

  1. Sort currency into ascending order

  2. Count money

  3. Explain the value of the money you have, including its purchasing power.
