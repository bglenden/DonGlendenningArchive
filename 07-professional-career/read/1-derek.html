Letter of reference from Don Glendenning (signed Don Glendenning, C.M.) for Derek McEwen applying for Curriculum Director position in reorganized PEI Department of Education. Don vouches for Derek's professionalism, educational knowledge, willingness to share expertise, and ability to stand his ground. Suggests Don's continued engagement in PEI educational circles and his influence in professional recommendations, likely 2016 or later.


Derek McEwen   


I am pleased to provide a letter of reference for Derek McEwen who is applying for the position of Curriculum Director in the re-organized Department of Education.


I’ve gotten to know Derek over the last couple of years and, during this time, found him to be professional in all our dealings, knowledgeable about matters of education, willing to  share his knowledge and expertise, and able to stand his ground on issues where we differ.   Although not familiar with his earlier work, I understand that he has a good record as a teacher and principal in the public schools of PEI.  Lacking information about his previous education roles and a position description for the Director of Curriculum, I am reluctant to comment more directly on his suitability for the emerging role.


Suffice it to say that, based on our working relationship, I have no hesitation in providing this recommendation. 


Sincerely



Don Glendenning, C.M.
