General Topics

Community Needs John

This item should be placed on a Board Agenda for their discussion.  Donnie to send an email with a list of issues that the Foundation may wish to consider in determining priorities affecting our communities


Sub-committee membership

The need for sub committees is not clear – needs further discussion in light of  terms for Directors so that the Foundation can have some continuity. Community members of committees may consider moving up to the Board.  Donnie raised the point that the Chair of a committee needs to be a Director.  The Board has 12 volunteers of which 5 are on the executive committee.  Out of the remaining 5 directors – location can be problematic for someone living in the Western or Eastern end of the Island.  Currently we have 3 directors living in Kings and Prince County.  Representation from these areas is likely a major consideration.  Due to location, these directors may well not be available to chair thus we are down to three people to chair committees who are not on the Executive.  Maybe the policy that the chair must be a Director should be looked at and maybe changed to each committee shall have a director on it.  Maybe even, assign an executive member as the executive liaison on committees.  Matter to be referred to the Nominating Committee.
