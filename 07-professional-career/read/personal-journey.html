About my education/insights:

Sandwich education: 18 year period of working and studying between high school graduation and my final degree, includes study in Canada, USA and England

Had many roles: student, teacher, principal, college president, consultant, columnist, teacher of teachers, curriculum developer, ran workshops on standard, conference speaker, represented Canada at OECD and UNESCO conferences

Consider myself a lateral thinker


Why Now 

It has been a lifelong passion

Most important issue of our time, more important than oil reserves or global warming

Kind of education we provide will determine to a large extent our future both as individuals and as a country

Seem to have built a system that is impervious to change. 

System too bureaucratic, monolithic, conservative, hierarchical, outdated industrial structure.

People, both inside and outside the system are calling for change

The climate for change has never been better

Incapable of changing itself

External intervention needed
