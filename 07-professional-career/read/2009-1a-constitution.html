Draft 1


Date: January 12, 2009


To: Glenway Cottage Owners Association


From: Don Glendenning


Subject: Constitution and by laws


Members and Directors:


Article 10:06 states that “Members shall be entitled to one vote for each lot of the Glenway Subdivision owned at any meeting of the membership of the Association but no lot shall be entitled to more than one vote, at any meeting of the membership, notwithstanding the number of registered owners of the lot.  …


Article 6.05 states that “Each lot in the Glenway Subdivision shall be entitled to appoint  one representative to the Board of Directors for each individual lot as set out in the Survey Plan but no subdivided lot shall be entitled to have more than one representative on the Board.  In the event that a member or members own more than one lot, then that member or members may have more than one representative on the Board provided that the representative is a member of the Association.”



Proposal:  To amend article 6.05; Remove sentence in red; an amendment requires 14 days notice and a 2/3 majority vote
