Minutes of Annual General Meeting 

of 

Glenway Estates Association 

August 4, 2020

Part 1

The Annual Meeting of the Association was held outdoors at the home of Don and Carol Glendenning.  Social distancing was practiced.  Meeting commenced at 4:00 pm. Don Glendenning as  president called the meeting to order.

Attendees were: Don Glendenning, Elizabeth Lang, Sergio Tolusso, Annette Galloway, Brenda Reid, and Carol McInnis and Paul Norris.

The Agenda was approved as circulated.. 

Brenda moved that the minutes of the last meeting (2019) be approved as written. Seconded by Liz Lang. The motion was carried

The treasurer’s report was presented by Liz and it was reported that the balance was  $1893.82 as of May 31, 2020.  A further $150.00 was received since making a balance of $2043.82.  Extensive road work (laying gravel) was done this spring amounting to $2218.00.  This left a shortfall of approximately $200.00 . The group thanked Paul Norris for organizing this work.  Liz moved that the treasurer’s report be accepted as presented.  Seconded by  Brenda;  Carried.  Sergio moved we assess dues of $100.00 per cottage owner for this year; Paul seconded the motion and carried. 

Projects to be considered for next year:

Beach access on east side: much discussion regarding need.  Carol McInnis suggested each cottage owner should be responsible for his or her stairs.  It was pointed out that the Tolussos cannot do that as at  high tide their stairs would always be under water.  Also renters and guests have used the east access to the sandier beach.  Brenda will obtain an estimate of the cost of these stairs.

Stump removal on east beach corner.  Don or Paul will put a reflector as a  temporary marker.

Gravel:- the common road to the west and south developed serious pothole problems this spring necessitating repairs. 

Brick Gates at entrance:  Carol McInnis brought to the group’s attention that these are in need of repair/refurbishment.  Carol McInnis and Annette G. will investigate costs and obtain estimates for consideration.

Executive for 2021:  The current executive will remain in office for the coming year.

  They are as follows:

President: Don Glendenning

Vice President: Rollie McInnis

Secretary: Brenda Reid

Treasurer: Elizabeth Lang.

The next meeting will be August 17th 2020 at 4:00 pm at Don Glendenning’s cottage.

There being no further business Brenda move the meeting be adjourned.



 Minutes of Annual General Meeting 

of 

Glenway Estates Association 

August 28, 2020

Part 2


The original date for the meeting (August 17) was postponed as estimates on proposed projects had not been obtained.

We again convened at Don Glendenning’s home at 4:00 pm. with attendees, Don; Sergio; Annette; Liz; Paul; Carol and Brenda present.

Updated Treasurer’s report:

From May 31 to July 31 some dues were prepaid for the current year and covered the shortfall of $218.00, leaving a balance of $514.89 as of July 31, 2020.


Follow-up on projects:

Access to East Beach: Pius estimated $700.00 for an 8-step set of stairs depending on where we located them.  Access to the West Beach will be discussed next year.

Survey: A survey is needed to mark the property line of the Association and the McInnis property to ensure new steps are located on Association property. 

Don has contacted Delta Survey and obtained a cost estimate of $200.00 to do this work.  The survey will be done this fall (2020). Moved by Liz, seconded by Brenda. Carried

Stump issue: Reflector is in place to clearly mark the location.  Cost of stump removal is estimated at $200-$300.

Gravel and road maintenance: gravel costs vary from $218-$500 per load.  Annette’s daughter-in-law will advise as to which gravel pits are best.  Gravel next year will be on the east road.

Brick Gates at Entrance: Carol McInnis had an estimate of $2500 for levelling and repairing the present gates;.  There was much discussion re planning ahead for this expenditure.  The aim is to be able to set aside some funds yearly depending on immediate demands for our funds.  After reviewing project estimates for next year and not wanting to be in a shortfall  situation again, Paul moved that we amend the dues from $100.00 to $150.00 per cottage owner to ensure adequate funds for present projects and start a fund for repairing the gates.  Sergio seconded; Carried.

New Business:

Liability Insurance:  Liz will look into that.

Don is to develop a set of principles as guidelines for future meetings.

There being no further business Annette moved we adjourn. 


Respectfully submitted: 

Brenda Reid, Secretary

Date: September 7, 2020
