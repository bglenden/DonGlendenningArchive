      1. Conclusions


For some time now, the writer has been exploring concerns about public eduation; below is a list of findings:

  1. There is low level but widespread concern about the state of public education

  2. Public concerns are mostly about essential skills with communications and math being mentioned most frequently.

  3. Structural problems arise from issues with clarity

  4. The system is unlikely to change itself

  5. PEI has a school system; it needs an education system .

  6. Important and useful changes can be made without incurring either great expense of disruption to the system. (Next right thing)

  7. Did not find a provincial education plan

  8. Schools need greater autnomy and accountability.

  9. Operating systems need better documentation

  10. There is little public conversation, engagement or collaboration in policy setting and direction

  11. System lacks a self-correcting mechanism
