A concise proposal to support school-level staff development through a competitive grant process. Don proposes soliciting proposals from schools with support from principals and school boards, requesting submissions of one-page applications with budgets for development initiatives grounded in sound educational principles. Funding would have a cap and require local contribution in cash or kind. This modest initiative demonstrates Don's belief that effective change depends on school-level practitioners and his commitment to enabling innovation through thoughtful resource allocation. The requirement for local contribution ensures ownership and sustainability.

E-4

STAFF DEVELOPMENT

Purpose: To foster and support staff development at the school level


Background: One of the keys to change is ongoing staff development


Proposal:

Solicit proposals from schools for proposals on staff development

  • At school level
  • Support of principal and local school board
  • Based on sound educational principles
  • Up to $
  • Some contribution from school inj cash or in kind
  • One page proposal including budget
