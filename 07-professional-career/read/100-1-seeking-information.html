RESEARCH

My research experience is very limited but I consider myself a user of research results. Even today, I am a regular scanner of the internet doing keyword searches on education and educators, seeking leads to recent research and authoritative opinion.

I am still seeking information:


Outcomes – I was an early convert to Competency Based Education and supported William Spadey and his work on Outcomes Based Education (OBE), I think in the 1960’s. PEI uses Curriculum Based Outcomes (CBO); I’d like to see some evidence as to which works best.


School management – What is the best way to organize a school to accommodate individual progress?nizational structure.


Curriculum Development - Have been searching without success forfor curriculum development handbooks. Found a number of organizations willing to sell copies but no guidelines as to how to develop curriculum materials


Learning guides – I am familiar with learning guides. Public school curriculum development, however, seems to focus on instructional material for teachers. I had a phone call from a friend recently who mentioned video descriptions of outcomes.


Student tracking – Several people have spoken about the Impact on education of Covid 19 and the need for better planning and tracking of student progress. The DACUM chart is a natural fit for individual profiles; Has anyone found alternatives?


Community learning pods - are learning pods an antidote for low literacy and numeracy scores? Learning pods are springing up all over; is it time to incorporate them into our education system. A focus on literacy and numeracy comes to mind but they may be suitable for other instructional roles as well.


As I reflect back on these paragraphs, I am reminded of the fact that our education system is focused on schools; expect another article.
