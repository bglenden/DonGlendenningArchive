Letter from Don, as Community Foundation Director, to Hon. M. Lorne Bonnell proposing administrative changes to the Lorne and Ruby Bonnell Scholarship Fund. Illustrates his leadership role in foundation governance and his efforts to streamline philanthropic processes for donor support.

Hon.  M. Lorne Bonnell


Lorne and Ruby Bonnell Scholarship Fund


We spoke during my recent visit about a change in the way the above noted Fund is administered.


While the Foundation publicizes the Scholarship, the current arrangement is for applications to be sent to the Registrar’s Office at the University of Prince Edward Island.  Our proposal is that applications come instead to the Community Foundation office.   Following the closing date, the Foundation will convene a selection committee taking into account the student’s academic record and recommendations.  The award will be publicized by the Foundation together with other scholarship winners and other awardees.


Again, we thank you for your support of the foundation and for making financial assistance to students for their advanced studies in the sciences.  Should you be interested, further contributions to the fund may be made at any time.


Sinjcerely


Don Glendenning

Director
