Subject: Committee - Development


The Development Committee is appointed by the Board and shall consist of at least five members two of whom shall be Directors with one Director acting as Chair.. The committee shall also include members drawn from the community at large.


The role of the Development committee is to:


  • Develop and recommend a development plan


  • Identify prospective donors and clients


  • Implement and monitor development activities
