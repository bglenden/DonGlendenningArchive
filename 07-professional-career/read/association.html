Critiques PEI Education Association structure and proposes reforms. Identifies critical gaps: functions inadequately identified, major services not represented at organizational top, operations organized by client rather than function, unclear roles and accountabilities. Cites OECD research showing better PISA results in autonomous schools. Don criticizes conflation of "getting an education" with "going to school." Recommends restructuring based on functions, writing role descriptions, and delegating authority downward.
