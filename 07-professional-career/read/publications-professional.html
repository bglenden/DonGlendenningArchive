Comprehensive bibliography of Don Glendenning's publications spanning 1966-2003, demonstrating a distinguished career in education, vocational training, and workforce development. Major works include "A Review of Federal Support for Technical and Vocational Education in Canada" (1991), DACUM methodology works (1997-1998), competency-based education materials for Mexico (1996), "Up To Standard" (2000), and "A Brief History of Presbyterians in Black River" (2003). Publications demonstrate expertise in vocational education, manpower development, secondary education policy, and educational systems. His output includes journal articles in Canadian Superintendent and other professional publications, conference papers, and books. The list reflects Don's influence across Canadian vocational and secondary education policy during the latter twentieth century.
