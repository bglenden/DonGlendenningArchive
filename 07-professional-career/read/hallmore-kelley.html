Email thread dated September 2008 between Dr. Arleon Kelley (now living in Elgin, Iowa) and Don Glendenning regarding ongoing search for the Hallmore tractor in Michigan City, Indiana. Kelley reports research efforts through local chambers and development offices. Earlier email from Don summarizes a July 30, 2008 committee meeting including Tom Hall and Jim Stavert, discussing strategies for securing the tractor and engaging public interest. Shows Don's collaborative leadership in the Agricultural Heritage Initiative.
