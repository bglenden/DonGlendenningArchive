COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND



Category : Policy No: 4.05

Approval Date : Effective date:

Last Amendment: ...................................................................................................................................................


Subject: Officers - President


The Officers of the Foundation are elected by the members at the first regular meeting following the annual meeting including a President who shall:


 If present, preside at all meetings of the directors and members;

 Sign all instructions which require his signature;

 Have general care, direction and supervision of the affairs of the company;

 Perform all duties incident to his office;

 Submit an annual report of the business and affairs of the company as soon as such a report may be conveniently prepared after the closing of each fiscal year, and to the members at the ensuing annual meeting;

 Represent the foundation in the Community

  • perform such other duties as the directors may required.
