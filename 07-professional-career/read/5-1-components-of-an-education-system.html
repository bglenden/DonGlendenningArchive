Defines education system as community-implemented services supporting citizen education, identifying six key components: governance (leadership, planning, research, coordination), information systems, financing, human resources, essential products and technologies (textbooks, internet access), and service delivery mechanisms (community agencies, counseling, instruction, qualifications).
