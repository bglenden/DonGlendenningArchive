A detailed curriculum framework for English language and communication arts based on Oregon's "Anentafel" methodology. Organizes learning around five life roles (Learner, Producer, Citizen, Consumer, Family Member) and maps two ultimate system outcomes to nine general exit competencies and 108 specific enabling skills across subjects including text interpretation, technology communication, and information evaluation. Demonstrates competency-based curriculum design integrating life-role preparation with language arts standards.
