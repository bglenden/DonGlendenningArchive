The DACUM (Developing A Curriculum) rating scale presents a six-level performance assessment framework ranging from 0 (cannot perform satisfactorily) to 6 (can perform with initiative and can lead others). Intermediate levels assess supervision and assistance requirements, speed, quality, and adaptability. This competency-based assessment tool was central to Holland College's outcomes-based education model and demonstrates Don's commitment to measurable performance standards in technical and vocational education.
