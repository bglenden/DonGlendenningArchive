Detailed system organization proposal designating all Islanders (estimated 40,000 learners annually) as clients. Identifies core functions: pedagogy, service delivery, learning devices/technology, counselling, quality improvement, independent learner support, facilities, volunteers, in-service training, self-management, administrative support, transportation, and family advising. Proposes establishing national standards, research capacity, and consultancy. Lists management functions: budgeting, personnel, pedagogy, maintenance, monitoring, evaluation, planning, leading, organizing, controlling, staffing. References Brookings Institution and Ontario Leadership Framework. Integrates UNESCO learning pillars (know, do, live together, be). This comprehensive organizational blueprint translates Don's theoretical framework into institutional structure.
