A visual systems diagram dated April 12, 2014, mapping the education delivery system's components and relationships. Organizes educational providers (professional organizations, early childhood, public schools, specialists, media, postsecondary, private schools) around a central "Learner" node, with auxiliary support structures (Home and School, CPF, Learning Disabilities programs, tutors) and education stores (media). Illustrates Don's systems-thinking approach to education as an integrated ecosystem rather than isolated institutions.
