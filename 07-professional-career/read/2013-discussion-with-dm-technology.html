NOTES FOR CONVERSATION 

DM, INNOVATION AND HIGHER LEARNING

January 24, 2013


Why am I here:

Education is most important issue of the day

Being retired, I am more free to call attention to things that need change

What the Premier, your Minister and Minister of Education say is important

What I say reflects  what I am hearing

Some of the solutions are my own


My comments:

Could say about any Province

Faults are in the system that we created

No self-correcting mechanism 

In education, decisions are bureaucratic


Institutions 

A school system and not an education system

Bureaucratic

Time based – more students could gain an A if given more time

Batch process

Reading special problem –levels of literacy; parents paying both taxes and tuition


Ideas

Challenge for credit

Qualification authority

Public advisory committee

Massive Open On-Line Course

Community based education advisor







General

Need external education body/association to raise issues


Priorities

Identify outcomes or competencies (Handout)

Define standards – Rating scale for writing

Establish external qualification authority 

Pass operational control to Units – let them be accountable

Choice as an agent of change


Closing comment:

Is the one I gave my successor

When making decisions, ask yourself how that will impact on individual learners that you know throughout the Island
