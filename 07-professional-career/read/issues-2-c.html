A Clarity


1 Terminology

  • confuse education and schooling. – World Bank
  • Education is an umbrella word that describes experiences designed to change what one knows, believes and is able to do, 
  • A school is a building or a room where learners and teachers meet to pursue selected components of the learning-teaching process
  • Schooling is an approach to helping groups of people who want or need to learn specified conent.
  • Credential- a statement by an  educational institution certifying performance of a student by course and level
  • Qualification - certification by an independent body of level of performance of a person in a field or occupation
