This document outlines a comprehensive workshop program for Education 20/20 in 2015-16, organized into school initiatives and new best practices sessions. Topics include grade 10 credits, secondary literacy assessment, career education, aboriginal education, PIAAC literacy competencies, technology skills (Handle With Care, Koding for Kids), DACUM methodology, assessment, teacher education, GED/ABE programs, apprenticeships, and reading instruction. Don led the DACUM session, demonstrating his active role in knowledge dissemination.

"Literacy for the 21st Century

The Programme for the International Assessment of Adult Competencies(PIAAC) looked at literacy, numeracy and problem solving in technology rich environments. The presentation will provide information on where PEI adults stand compared to the rest of Canada , and, in particular, to the other Atlantic provinces. Discussion will lead to is what available for adults in need of literacy training, what more needs to be done, and, generally, why these 3 areas are of importance to the future success of PEI.
