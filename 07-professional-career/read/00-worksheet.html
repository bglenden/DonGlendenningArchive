Project management planning worksheet structured around major project components: Management (establish committee, reference groups, secure funding, set timelines), Outcomes (conduct literature search, establish criteria, gather examples, analyze, draw conclusions), Math Levels (research and development), Rating Scales (literature search, criteria development, scale development, establish benchmarks), and Assessment (secure/develop instruments). Provides blank rows for task tracking and assignments. Demonstrates Glendenning's systematic project management approach and comprehensive planning methodology for complex education initiatives.
