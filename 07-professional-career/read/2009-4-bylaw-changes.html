Don's memo to Glenway members outlining additional commentary and suggested changes to association bylaws, emphasizing procedural thresholds for significant decisions and consultative governance principles.
Professional Career & Consulting

Date: August 1, 2009


To: Glenway Members


From: Don Glendenning


Subject: Glenway revisions


Thanks for the draft; it looks good.


Thanks to Wally for his analysis; here are some additional comments, suggestions, questions and proposed changes:


3.01 l (g) should that be “in the opinion of the members”?

4.05 should the statement be broadened to include “the power to conduct all such business as may benefit the Association”?

7.02 (1)  should that statement not be presented at annual meetings, special meetings or when requested by members?

7.02 (2) If we remove this, who will supervise contractors or employees?    Would think that this should be left in.

9.03 “shall hold office” or “may hold office”?

10.03 (1) confirm or approve the minutes?

10:03 (v) confirm or approve?

14.01 sets out a 2/3 majority for to borrow or mortgage and 15.01 requires 2/3 majority to amend the bylaws. It seems to me that there are other significant issues that should require a 2/3 vote such as the issue of access of last year.  Should we have also have a clause that allows/requires a 2/3 majority for any non-recurring or out-of- the- ordinary motions?

14.01 probably should read “necessary by membership” rather than “necessary by Board”
