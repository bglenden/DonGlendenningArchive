Don's letter to Wolf Marold regarding pension issues, book printing assistance, and establishment of the Marold Fund endowment for the PEI Humane Society through the Community Foundation. Demonstrates Don's expertise in community philanthropy and estate planning for charitable giving.

September 17, 2012

Wolf and Leilo:

Great to see you last week but sorry my visit was so rushed.  Here are some further thought on our conversation:

  • Formal notification about pension - I did not see mention of any penalty for not returning the form to Germany other than they would suspend payment of the pension.


  • Volume One of your book – I checked three places about having your book printed.  All recommended that it be retyped first.  Should be able to find a typist at $15.00 an hour but am not sure of the number of hours required to type the volume.  Although you have already typed Volume One, there are quite a few additional hand written notes to be inserted.  


After the typing, the relevant photos need to be scanned and inserted in the appropriate place on the relevant pages, then a copy made for your review.  My thinking is that we set a limit of, say, $500 and see what we can do with it both in terms of typing and editing.  


At that point, we will have a better feel for the final document and the costs involved. 


I’ll send a copy of one of my reports so that you can see how I handled photographs.


  • Proposed Marold Fund – we had spoken during an earlier visit about an endowment fund at the Community Foundation that would provide grants on an annual basis to the PEI Humane Society.  I left a copy of our standard agreement which provides for a contribution now and allows for additional contributions later.  


If, as you indicated, you want the residue of your estate to pass to the Community Foundation you need to consult your lawyer about an appropriate clause in each of your wills.  The following is an example of such a clause:

I, Wolfgang Marold, transfer and convey to the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island ________ % of the residue (or a specific amount) as a contribution to the Dr. Wolfgang and Leilo Marold Endowment Fund for the purpose of making annual grants to the PEI Humane Society.

I’ll be out of Province for a couple of weeks.  When I get back, I’ll be in touch. 

Regards

Don
