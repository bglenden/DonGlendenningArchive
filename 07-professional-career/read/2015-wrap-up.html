Comprehensive post-program analysis document authored by Don reflecting on the inaugural 2014 Education Award and proposing improvements for 2015. Don observes that engagement comes more naturally to early childhood centers than other educational levels and recommends considering this in future awards. He notes wide variation in documentation quality and proposes providing feedback to future applicants about the importance of engagement. Recommends expanding the program to include engagement with community organizations like 4H and Junior Achievement. Suggests media strategy improvements and potential expansion to neighboring provinces. Demonstrates Don's reflective, continuous improvement approach to leadership.
