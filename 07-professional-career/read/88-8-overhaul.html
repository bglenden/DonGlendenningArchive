Overhaul needed

Education on PEI has a vision problem; it needs an overhaul. What the system is trying to do is unclear. There is a gap, even a growing gap, between what Islanders want and what the system delivers. \


I do not, we cannot, blame the current government, the previous government or any single government that went before for the current status; neither should we blame our current leaders. The current government, however, has a responsibility however, for instituting corrective measures.
