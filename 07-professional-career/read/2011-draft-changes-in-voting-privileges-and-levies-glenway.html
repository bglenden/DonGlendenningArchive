Draft Changes in voting privileges and levies

(for discussion purposes only)


Current Reading of the BY-laws


10.06 Members shall be entitled to one vote for each lot of the Glenway Subdivision owned at any meeting of the membership of the Association but no lot shall be entitled to more than one vote, at any meeting of the membership, notwithstanding the number of registered owners of the lot. All votes at any meeting of the membership of the Association shall be determined by a majority vote unless approval of a greater proportion of the membership is required by law.


10.08. A quorum of the members shall consist of that number of members that represent one-half (1/2) of the total number of lots in the Glenway Subdivision, and, in any event, not less than two members_



11.01 The Association shall levy such charges upon the membership. per lot, as are necessary in order to fulfill the objects and purposes of the Association, in particular but without limiting the generality of the foregoing, to pay for any of its own administrative expenses and any expenses, construction, maintenance and improvements of roads, common areas, access stairs to the beach, drainage ditches and the like_ Such levies are to be set by and governed by the Association and arc non-refundable.









Draft Changes



10.06 At any meeting of the membership, members shall be entitled to one vote for each lot owned and on which a residential building is constructed. If one lot is owned but not occupied, that owner shall be considered a voting member. However, if a member owns more than one lot, that member will be entitled to only one vote per lot on which a building has been constructed. No lot shall be entitled to more than one vote, at any meeting of the membership, notwithstanding the number of registered owners of the lot. All votes at any meeting of the membership of the Association shall be determined by a majority vote unless approval of a greater proportion of the membership is required by law_


10.08 A quorum of the members shall consist of that number of members that represent one-half (1/2) of the total number of members as defined in 10.06 above, and, in any event, not less than two members.


11.01 The Association shall levy such charges upon the membership necessary in order to fulfill the objects and purposes of the Association. Such levies will be set based on actual cottage use and not on the number of lots owned, singularly or jointly, by a member. Levies are to be set by and governed by the Association and are non-refundable.
