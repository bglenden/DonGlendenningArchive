Conceptual proposal for "EDUSERV," an independent educational service provider. Identifies background issues (organized education unable to adapt, becoming politicized, lacks service orientation), proposes establishing an independent entity offering instruction, materials, information, counselling, quality control, and leadership services. Reflects Don's philosophy about education delivery limitations and need for independent, flexible service providers.

EDUSERV-1

Planning/ideas



Purpose: To supply quality educational products and services; to act as a middle man between those who teach/educate and those who employ;


Governance:

  • Independent Board of Directors


Background:

The world in which we operate has changed dramatically and will continue to change

Resulting in a change in the nature of education and how it is delivered

Organized education is unable or unwilling to adapt

Growing pressure to change

Has become entrenched

Politicized

Education is delivery not developmentally oriented

Expensive

Education is school centered

Not service oriented


Education services:

  • Instruction – Courses, Workshops

  • Instructional materials an technology-

  • Information and counselling

  • Quality control

  • Leadership

  • Human resources


Current perceived gap:

  • Information and counselling

  • Quality/Standards

  • Relevance


Proposal:

  • Establish an independent service provider
