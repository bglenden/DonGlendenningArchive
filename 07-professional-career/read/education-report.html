Glendenning proposes establishing a not-for-profit educational newsroom devoted to reporting on education matters, modeled on examples like CBC, the Hechinger Report (Columbia University), and AIMS think tank (Halifax). The proposal emphasizes keeping the public informed about education at all levels and for all citizens, distinguishing itself from lobbying or advocacy. The newsroom would publish reports, newsletters, and media items focused on education broadly conceived (not just schooling) with solution-oriented analysis. This initiative reflects Glendenning's belief that public understanding and engagement are essential to transforming education systems.

EDUCATION REPORT

About-4-1-11


BACKGROUND

  • Our individual and collective futures will depend to a large degree on the nature and level of our education



MODELS EXAMINED

  • Ideas – CBC
  • Hechinger Report operating out of Teacher’s College, Columbia University
  • Think Tank – AIMS,, Halifax 
  • North Carolina Forum, 

PROPOSAL

  • To establish a not-profit newsroom devoted to reporting on matters of education


NOTES:

  • Devoted to matters of education of all kinds, all levels and all citizens
  • Purpose – keep  the public informed about education matters
  • Focused on education not just schooling
  • Work in collaboration with media
  • Report on activities and Ideas; not a lobby for any level or kind of education
  • Publish regular reports and newsletters and items for inclusion in regular media
  • Report on but not undertake research or innovative projects
  • Solution oriented
