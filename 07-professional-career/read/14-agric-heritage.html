Agricultural Heritage



Had a phone call from Glenn Swallow today, Dundas has created a space for the hall thresher and it is about to b e moved.  They seeme pleased with the gift. I am not sure, Tony, if you will be around when the move occurs but, if so, would appreciate having some photos.

 

The Antique Tractor/MachinerCclub meets at the Dundas Fairgrounds on June 23 at 6:30 pm. and would be delighted to hear about the work undertaken by our committee but also some background information on Hall Manufacturing.

 

It is a great opportunity to further our initiative with private collectors.  Would any or all of you be able to attend?  I thought that it would provide some contacts and potential stories for the Newsletter, Charley.

 

I will be out of Province on that date; our granddaughter is being married in New Mexico and we are spending a few extra days while there.
