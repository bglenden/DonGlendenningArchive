A comparative analysis of departmental roles across Canadian provinces, compiled by Don. Shows PEI defines its role narrowly as delivering "high quality programs and curriculum" while other provinces articulate broader missions: New Brunswick (administration of public education system), Nova Scotia (children's education including early years), Newfoundland & Labrador (leadership on child development, care, K-12, libraries, postsecondary), Ontario (policy, funding, curriculum across all levels), Manitoba (equity and social justice), Alberta (enabling effective functioning in work and life), and British Columbia (enabling individual potential and healthy society). Don's compilation demonstrates that PEI's narrow definition is outlier.
