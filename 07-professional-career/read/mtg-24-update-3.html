Activity Update-3

April 15, 2015

_____________________________________________________________________________________


I know that all are busy, some have been travelling, and hope all are surviving the winter weather; the following notes are intended to keep you up-to-date.

Completed:

  1. Virtual tour of PEI education: convened on March 3 as planned; only a half dozen participants so session converted to an open discussion.

  2. Discussion on Education- We agreed at our January 14 meeting to convene a discussion of Directors to exchange views on education.

  3. Presentation – January 21 from four to 6:00 at Haviland Club with Dr. Tamara Hubley-as guest speaker.

  4. DACUM/HC workshop- at invitation of Peter Rukavina, I made a presentation to PEIHSF and some Directors about DACUM model developed in Nova Scotia and used at Holland College.

  5. Workplace Learning PEI – arranged for a presentation by Lori Johnston and team at Workplace Learning PEI on training, assessment and support services provided to employers on PEI.

  6. Information –

  • Published Newsletter No. 27;

  • Recirculated Occasional Paper No. 1, Preparedness;

  • Released Occasional Paper No. 2 The Eight Year Study;

  • Blog site: thanks to Tom we now have a blog site

  • Released A Call for Action: given the announcement by the PEIHSF of a leaders debate, in consultation with VP and Sec-Treasurer, decided to release the Call for Action.

  1. PLAR and Challenge for Credit – have asked Minister for opportunity for Bill and I to make presentation; request has not been acknowledged yet

  2. CRA – mailed application seeking charitable status

  3. School Award: Nomination papers distributed throughout the school system thanks to Imelda Arsenault, a number of hits on our website but we do not know the number of nominations copied, nominations close on April 25; our award committee includes Donald DesRoches, Peter Russell, Dave MacCabe and Don Glendenning, Ex officio.
