What is The Living Memorial Trust?


The Living Memorial Trust is a permanent endowment administered by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  The purpose of the Living Memorial Trust is to make grants to enhance outdoor public spaces on Prince Edward Island.


Fund was established by Mr. MacKay’s family in recognition of his many accomplishments, his larger than life character, his love for his family, and his love for Prince Edward Island.  


Who was David A MacKay?




David Arthur MacKay, (1932-2002) was born and raised in Albany, Prince Edward Island. Dave was a successful businessman and an excellent judge of character who took great pride in being a “builder’ and not a “knocker”. He valued friendship and loyalty. He looked for the entrepreneurial spirit and encouraged and fostered it when  he encountered it, especially in young people. He was a sportsman who loved golf and the camaraderie of the game. His faith was solid and his support and assistance to many charities and organizations reflected the help he was always willing to give on a personal basis. Although known for his listening skills, he was especially loved for his story telling and joy of life.

 

Dave had a full and satisfying career which included numerous business partnerships and investments throughout Canada and the USA.  His greatest passion was building   MacKay and Hughes Ltd. of Toronto into the largest fresh potato distributor in North America.


What will the fund do?


The Living Memorial Trust is one of a number of funds within the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island, a non-profit organization administered by a volunteer board of Directors, which oversees permanent endowments set up by members of the community.  The Foundation is a registered charity and all donations are tax deductible in Canada and the USA


Income from the Fund will be used to make grants for the enhancement and beautification of public spaces such as parks, Trails PEI .  Enhancements include, but are not limited to, planting of shrubs and trees, provision of comfortable benches and erection of shelters.  



How can I help?


People wishing to celebrate the memory of a friend or relative are encouraged to make a direct donation to the Living Memorial Trust fund at the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  Donations are tax deductible in both Canada and the USA, and appropriate receipts will be issued by the Foundation.











How Does It Work?


Each year the Foundation will determine the amount of money available for the purpose of making grants, following which projects  designed to enhance outdoor public spaces will be solicited.


To assist in the decision making, advice will be sought from an advisory committee drawn from the community.
