Writing proficiency rating scale spanning six performance levels assessing organization, grammar, sentence structure, vocabulary, and creativity. Level 6 represents creative writing with proper grammar and standards adherence; Level 5 shows precision and vocabulary richness with good structure; Levels 4-3 demonstrate acceptable to organized writing with varying levels of error; Levels 2-1 show disorganization and significant errors. This comprehensive scale acknowledges writing as complex skill requiring simultaneous attention to mechanical competence and creative expression. The framework reflects belief that quality writing instruction must balance technical proficiency with meaningful communication and creative voice.
