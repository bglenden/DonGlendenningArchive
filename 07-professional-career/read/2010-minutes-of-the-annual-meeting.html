Minutes of the Annual Meeting 

                                    Glenway Association Estates


The Annual meeting of the Glenway Estates Inc. Was held on Wednesday, August 4th,2010 at the cottage of Paul Norris, a quorum was present.


Paul Norris, President called the meeting to order and outlined the agenda.  The minutes of the last general meeting held on July 30th, 2009 were read.  Ian Gallaway moved the approval of these minutes, seconded by Thoran Brule.  Motion carried


Liz Lang handed out the Treasurer’s report showing a bank balance of 1649.96.  Moved by Wally, seconded by Don Glendenning that this report be accepted.  Motion carried.


Under New Business:

 It was agreed that Pius McInnis scrape the upper road again in early spring.  Moved by Liz and seconded by Karen.  Motion carried

There was discussion regarding the cutting of an interior swath around the road on the interior side for better visibility as well as trimming the bushes in the bog area.  To be discussed again next year.

The matter of 1 vote per lot or 1 vote per cottage owner was brought up again.  Wally pointed out that the By-Laws were suspended last year for the purpose of the road assessment costs.  They were applied to cottage owners only.  Wally made the motion, seconded by Don G.:     That the present Executive review the existing by-laws and propose changes to be voted on at the next general meeting.  Changes will include the voting procedure(1 vote per lot or 1 vote per cottage owner) The Past President was noted as being a member of the Executive.  The term of office for Executive to be 3 years instead of 2.  This proposal is to be in the hands of the Association members by June 1st, 2111.  Motion carried.


The assessment of 25.00 per lot for the year for the road  was proposed and moved by Wally, seconded by Karen..Motion carried


There being no further business, Wally adjourned the meeting.


Respectfully submitted

Brenda  Reid(Acting Secretary)

for Carol McInnis
