Draft newsletter (2007) inviting PEI summer residents to lecture series and social gathering. Lists presentations on higher education, heritage, environmental protection, and coastal landscape. Emphasizes building community among seasonal residents who care deeply about Island preservation. Includes social event at UPEI student center. Reflects Glendenning's recognition that seasonal residents represent significant philanthropic potential and community engagement opportunity.

Community Foundation News

No. , 2007


Attention: PEI Summer Islanders


On behalf of the Summer Islanders Committee of the Community Foundation of PEI, we would like to invite you, your friends and family to join us for:


1 A series of lectures of interest to summer residents


Where: The Victoria Playhouse

Victoria, Prince Edward Island


When: 3:00 – 4:00 PM - Tuesdays,


What: Lecture, Discussion, Lots of socializing, Refreshments


Program:

  • July 17 - Higher Education in PEI - Wade McLaughlin, President, UPEI


  • July 24 - Heritage in PEI, David Keenlyside, PhD., Exec. Director

PEI Museum and Heritage

Foundation


  • July 31 - Protecting the Island's natural places so PEI stays

special - Dianne Griffin, Chair, Atlantic Board, The Nature Conservancy of Canada


  • Aug.14 - Whose shore is it? The uncertain future of the PEI

coastal landscape - David Sisam,

Founding partner,

Montgomery Sisam Architects,

Toronto, and PEI Summer

resident.







2 A social for Summer

Islanders


Where: The W A Murphy Student Center

University of Prince Edward Island

Charlottetown, PEI


When: 1:00 – 3:30 PM – Mon. Aug. 6, 2007


What: Very short program with lots of socializing, background music and a cash bar. BYO Munchies


Summer Islanders are all those people who spend time on the Island on a regular basis and who think that the Island is truly a special part of the world. Let’s get together and socialize in the hope that new relationships might lead to the preservation of PEI as the unique little place it is.




The Summer Islander Committee
