Subject: Committee - Nominating


The Nominating Committee is elected annually by the Board of Directors and shall consist of The Past-President who will act as Chair, The President and a member from the community not a Director.


The role of the Nominating Committee is to:


  • Prior to the Annual Meeting each year, to receive from the Board a list of actual and expected vacancies and thhe expertise of most benefit to the Board

  • Seek candidates who, collectively, are representative of the Island community

  • Interview prospective candidates with a view to determining their level of interest and their suitability

  • To present a list of candidates, including a brief biography, for election to the Board of Directors at its annual meeting.

  • To assist the Board in filling vacancies which may occur between annual meetings
