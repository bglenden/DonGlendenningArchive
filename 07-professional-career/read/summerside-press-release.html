Draft Press Release

Tuesday, December 5, 2006



Farm Machinery Collectors Meet


A meeting was held in the St. Eleanor’s’ Community Centre for residents of Prince County interested in old farm equipment - especially equipment manufactured in PEI.


Many of those present already have pieces of farm equipment that have been restored while others have equipment on which they are still working.  The gathering was co-chaired by Tom Hall and Don Glendenning, both of whom have an interest in the subject and want to see more done to identify and save this part of the Island’s heritage.  Meetings are also planned for Kings and Queens Counties.


Those present decided to start by making a list of pieces of old equipment in their possession and will also ask others in Prince County to do the same.  Willy Dunbar of Elmsdale agreed to coordinate the gathering of information.  The group plans to meet again early in the new year.
