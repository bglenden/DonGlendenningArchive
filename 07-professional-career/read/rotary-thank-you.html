Grateful letter from Don acknowledging receipt of the Rotary Mentor Award and expressing his appreciation for the recognition and fellowship of the Rotary Club. Reflects his participation in Charlottetown civic organizations and the community's regard for his mentorship.

62 Queen Elizabeth Drive

Charlottetown

 PEI,   C1A 3A9

December 2, 2012



John McQuaid, President, 

Rotary Club of Charlottetown-Royalty

PO Box 3340,

Charlottetown, PEI C1A 8M5



Dear Sir:

Mentor Award


I am honoured, indeed, to join the ranks of recipients of your Rotary Mentor Awardees.  I am humbled by the award, by the generous words of my proposer, Norman Carruthers, and the warmth and camaraderie of the evening.  To you, and through you to your fellow Rotarians, thank you, thank you, thank you.  


I was a Rotarian for a few years, although not a very active one, and recall with great pleasure the fellowship extended to me as a newcomer to PEI.  I am also much in awe of your good works both on PEI and abroad.  Our community is the richer for your efforts and energies.


 Wishing you the compliments of the season and continued success in your work


Sincerely



Don Glendenning


Cc:  Norman Carruthers
