Outlines the "Glendenning Model" of education, grounded in his experience at Holland College and informed by OECD research and police training models. Articulates core principles (learner-centered, individualized, outcome/competency-based, community-based, client-driven, fee-for-service) and key components (diagnosis, learning plans, instruction, monitoring, credentials). Represents the crystallization of Don's educational philosophy and practical innovations, particularly relevant to his founding presidency at Holland College and subsequent consulting work.
