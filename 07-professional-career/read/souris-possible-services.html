This document outlines priority education services for Souris under consideration by Don's team. Priority services include identifying educational assets, surveying young people's employment needs, and promoting technology awareness. Proposed offerings include job-specific courses, personal interest programs, career exploration modules, and a community showcase connecting educational resources with residents. Additional initiatives include tutoring services, community information centers, and various consultation and capacity-building activities. The document represents a comprehensive vision for addressing educational gaps identified in earlier community research.
