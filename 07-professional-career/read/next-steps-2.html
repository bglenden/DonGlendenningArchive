PEI NEXT STEPS - 2

For some time now, I have exploring public education; here are some recommendations based on these explorations:

  1. Develop and disseminate a PEI taxonomy of common educational terms: education, school, learner, student, grade, etc.

  2. Engage both staff and community in discussions about education matters

  3. Provide community with regular information about education matters

  4. Document services, learning models, curriculum development model, learning management model, standards, accountabilities, etc.

  5. Develop and post a clear and mesurable statement of purpose

  6. Review and upgrade role of Department from that of “producing and delivering curriculum” to one of providing oversight.

  7. Switch focus of Department from schools to education.

  8. Educating is a collaborative venture; schools operate as silos

  9. Clarify accountabilities

  10. Increase school autonomy and accountability

  11. Develop education system

  12. Separate oversight from operations

  13. Establish an education board and/or education association

  14. Consider area directors

  15. Increase learner choice

  16. Replace lesson plans with learning maps and guides
