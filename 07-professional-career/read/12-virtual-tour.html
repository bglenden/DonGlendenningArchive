Virtual tour of education planned


Islanders will have an opportunity next week to take part in a virtual tour of education. The tour, the first of its kind, will allow participants to visit a variety of education issues on their own terms, no boring speakers, no scheduled meetings and no tense drives on icy roads. Instead, participants will gather with at the Stratford Town Hall and, through their iPads, iPods and iPhones, explore some of the pressing issues of education.


The first stop on our tour will be a visit to PEI’s philosophy of education, following which participants will sample some actual education outcomes, drop in unannounced on some actual school websites, and find out what principals actually do. There will also be time for you to explore on your own.


The tour will commence at 7:00 pm in the Southport Room of the Stratford Town Hall on Tuesday, March 3 with members of the 20/20 team acting as your tour guides. The tour ends at 9:00 pm.
