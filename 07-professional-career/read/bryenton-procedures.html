Draft


Date: February 16 2006


To; John R


From: Don


Subject: Bryenton Fund


The Bryenton Fund has a unique feature in that there is both a flow through component and an endowment; you will see by the agreement that we have the authority to change the arrangement after 5 years.  It works like this.


Marlene wants to retain an operating account of about $1,500.  this will give her access to money each year for the purchase of flowers and costs incurred for their planting and other maintenance.  As funds are withdrawn, contributions will be allocated to bring the total up to the $1,500 mark.


Contributions received when the fund is at $1,500 will be allocated to the endowment; they are not to be withdrawn for operating purposes. 


I expect that most funds will be carried in to our office by Marlene.  Receipts will be written to the various donors and I think that she will deliver them.  One of the Ghiz family told me that she intends to use the Bryenton fund as a memorial fund.
