Critical analysis of PEI education Department's naming, definitions, purpose, role, and organizational structure. Criticizes department name "Education and Early Years" (formerly "Lifelong Learning") as unnecessarily limiting when education encompasses all ages. Notes Department lacks formal education definition; School Act revision replaced "education" definition with "school" definition. Questions whether education should serve all Islanders versus only children. Reviews official purposes from multiple sources (School Administrative Handbook, Department Website, Philosophy documents) and notes inconsistencies and insufficient specificity for program planning/progress measurement. Lists comprehensive departmental goals (2015-16) spanning citizenship, critical thinking, knowledge acquisition, creativity, workplace skills, mental/physical health, bilingualism, gender equality, human rights. Dated June 13, 2024. Argues for clarity, transparency, and comprehensive documentation.
