Reframe the issues


As background:


  • Educators confuse

  • Education and schooling not the same

  • Using them interchangeably confuses the issue

  • Education is all the skills that you have learned

  • Schooling refers to that part of one’s education arranged by schools

  • Important but not the same

  • Education helps us to function in our life roles

  • Family member, individual, consumer, citizen, producer, learner

  • That’s the basis on which we measure education

  • That’s the basis on which we measure schools

  • We have a school system not an education system

  • Education is the issue of the day and will continue to be the determining factor in our future both as individuals and as a country

  • There is a widespread concern about the quality of education

  • Large measure of support for change, both within the system and in the public at large.

  • System is making some changes but they tend to be short term

  • Some key issues with long-term impact not being addressed

  • The changes that are needed will require external interventions

  • My comments come as a person with some knowledge of the system but external to it.

  • Reframe the issue

  • Place it in the public domain
