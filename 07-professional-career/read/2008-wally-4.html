Brief response from Don and Carol closing the McInnis access matter in 2008, stating the Association's minimum position was presented and McInnis response made clear their broader intentions, leading Don to consider the matter concluded.

Reply to McInnis Response


Thanks for note.


The Association spent considerable time discussing ideas and options and presented a minimum position.  


From the response by Gerald and Frances, it is now clear that the intention was to use our road for access to more than the one lot that they had requested. 


Given the response, we consider the matter closed.


Don and Carol
