Framework identifying eight key components of effective learner assistance based on Business Roundtable 1990 research: standards, assessments, accountability, professional development, school autonomy, parent involvement, learning readiness, technology, and safety/discipline. This document reflects Don's engagement with business-education partnerships and his belief that multiple stakeholders (educators, parents, businesses) must collaborate on systemic improvement. Demonstrates his contemporary awareness of educational reform movements.
