School Change-Reluctance

  • Politicization

  • Focus on knowing not doing – pay, higher salaries, orientation of studies
  • Education measured by time spent
  • Degree people earn more money
  • Silos
  • Little accountability for work
  • /little teamwork
  • Lack of autonomy
  • Bureaucracy emphasis on delivery not development

  • Strong union
  • Lacks independent professionals
