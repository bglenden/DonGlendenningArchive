Memorandum from Don to Norman Carruthers, John Robinson, and Marla (September 13, 2007) regarding the Robert C. Schurman Memorial Trust. Reflects Don's coordination of trust administration and donor relations with board leadership.

Date: September 13, 2007


To: Norman

John

Marla


From: Don


Subject: Schurman Trust


I exchanged email earlier this month with John Morrison about the Schurman Trust; we were waiting to hear from John Morrison and he was waiting to hear from us.  


I’ve reviewed my notes, draft agreements and recollections, although I am not sure that they are complete, and my understanding is that all parties agree that the Trust be transferred to CFPEI and become a CFPEI Fund – not a managed fund.  I believe that differences have been resolved except for a “save harmless clause” for the existing Trustees.


Attached is a revised agreement.  I’ve put in a couple of options for clause 15.  You folk know more about this than I so please change accordingly.


After that, we should forward a copy to John Morrison and seek a meeting with both he and Janice MacCallum, their legal advisor.  Any differences should be able to be ironed out on the spot.  Since both parties, support the transfer, I see no need to discuss further with Jim Travers.


I will be glad to forward this to John on our behalf after I hear from you


I will take my file to the office the next time I go in
