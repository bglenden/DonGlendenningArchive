EDUCATION

Education is important

Definition ASK


Have an education system for two reasons:

  • Help citizens develop their personal capabilities

  • Ensure that society has people needed to sustain itsellf


Evidence of need for change:

  • PISA results

  • Youth surveys show lack of preparedness

  • Increasing letters, editorials and coverage,



Conclusions:

  • Standards have declined/are less predictable

  • Sector has become politicized

  • There are gaps our education system

  • Conflict in roles – sorting vs grading

  • Limited our thinking to what schools can do - knowledge building


Components of an education system

  • Learning/teaching Institutions

  • Information and counselling

  • Qualification mechanism

  • Home service

  • Community education services

  • Funding mechanism



Action:

  • Government shed operational responsibility for schools

  • Change to stage based grading in math, Communications and science

  • Provide independent Information and advice

  • Transfer responsibility and accountability to the professionals
