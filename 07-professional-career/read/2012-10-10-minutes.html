Minutes from October 10, 2012 meeting with Dr. Glendenning, Sherri Russell, Carolyn Taylor, and Alice Taylor. Discussed newsletter necessity and format, community engagement approaches, and Dr. G's personal column writing. Agreed that Dr. G would write on behalf of Education First while maintaining ability to publish personal opinion pieces with disclaimers. Established priorities including information sharing, community engagement, excellence awards, teacher involvement, and organizational development.

Education First Inc

Meeting, October 10th, 2012

Sobey's, Stratford, Community Room

Time: 11:30 to 1:00


Minutes


Participants: Dr. Glendenning, Sherri Russell; Carolyn Taylor, Alice Taylor

Regrets: Eileen Conway- Martin; Jennifer Cairns-Burke, Sherri Barrett and  

Bernadette Allen.


1. Meeting called to order by Dr. G. By reviewing following points:

  • he is convinced we need to get engagement/ time to get debate in open/ not necessarily looking for agreement
  • need to probe more what people are interested in “info is fuel for change”

2. Newsletter: Do we need one? 

Discussion around who is it for? How do we circulate? Copying? How to make more visible?

Electronic version? Paper version? Content?

Conclusions

  • Yes, we need a newsletter.
  • Focus needs to be on improving Quality of Education for everyone.
  • Not too wordy. include quick facts, and/ or tips for parents and teachers/bits and pieces of significant information
  • can advertise community engagement meetings
  • local, national, international info on education.
  • Site examples of best practice
  • Advertise award to school or person ie Exceptional teacher/ school
  • need to look at mechanism for distribution
  • Invite schools to submit a logo for the organization (Education First Inc.)


3. Discussion on Community engagement:

Questions arising and discussed.

What would we achieve?

What would we do with info gathered?

Some discussion on inviting articles to be published in our newsletter, some concerns raised included 

How to judge what to publish? What about copyright? What about quality of submissions?

Conclusions:

Yes, a good thing to do if we can encourage people to do things differently

  • Must be a focused discussion
  • Community participants will want to hear new things for example we must legitimize we are speaking for a broader community
  • Possibility of developing a discussion guide for community engagement.
  • Re articles: suggestions was made that perhaps could engage education students at UPEI by calling for submissions to get their perspectives on various related topics to stimulate wider discussion.

This was thought to be a good idea but that we could defer this until we get website activated down a little further down the road and so was put on back burner for further discussion.


4. Dr. G asked question of group regarding his writing of articles for publication in local paper etc.


Did we think it could be perceived as a conflict?

Question was discussed and there was agreement that it could be confusing since he is well known as an advocate for change and so is the organization Education First Inc.


Conclusion:

Dr. G would write on behalf of Education First Inc. expressing views of the organization.


He could/would however do occasional papers based on his personal opinions and values, which may or may not be those of the organization. 


Note:

(Perhaps to be clear in distinguishing Dr. G could cite “the opinions/etc. expressed in this paper are his own and do not necessarily reflect the views of Education First Inc.) 



Meeting Concluded with the following listed as our immediate priorities:


  • Provide /Share information through newsletter, occasional papers,website and media 
  • Encourage community/public engagement.
  • Develop excellence/leadership award for teacher or school in a community 
  • Involve teachers -in- training in some way
  • Develop a plan of action for next twelve months which will include a stable, secure and vibrant organization with broad representation and involvement.
