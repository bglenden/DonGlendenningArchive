Planning sheet for a 90-minute virtual discovery tour comparing public education across the three Maritime provinces (January 21, 2015, 7:30-8:30 PM at Stratford Town Hall). Limited to 20 participants, the session would examine differences in philosophy, organization, and teaching/learning models across PEI, Nova Scotia, and New Brunswick. Participants would work in small groups to research education systems and draw comparative conclusions. Co-hosted by Don Glendenning, the event continues his commitment to regional educational analysis and cross-jurisdictional learning.
