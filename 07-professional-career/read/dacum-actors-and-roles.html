Recognized the value of the approach and contracted with Dr. Rice to develop a cooking course outline based on the model.

Adopted the model, both the DACUM chart and learning model, as a learning model for a newly established community college of which he was the founding President.



Continued development of the single page format curriculums gradually changing the emphasis from curriculum to analysis
