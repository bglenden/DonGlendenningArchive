Don's strategic framework for implementing education system reform through four mechanisms: appointing community education counselors to advise individual learners; establishing independent qualification authorities to resolve credential/qualification confusion; restructuring delivery systems to clarify ministerial, superintendent, principal, and teacher roles; and reaffirming mission and culture by moving toward outcome-based education, flexible time structures, and recognition of prior learning.
