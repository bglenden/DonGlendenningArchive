July 16, 2007 governance policy establishing financial signing authority for Board positions (President, Vice-President, Secretary, Treasurer, Finance & Investment Committee Chair). Requires two signatures on all cheques, typically including Treasurer. Endowment agreements require Board or Executive approval and execution by two authorized Directors. Standard financial controls documentation.


July 16, 2007

THE COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND



Category : General Policy No: 3-01

Approval Date : Effective date:

Last Amendment:

Board Approval:

______________________________________________________________________________


Subject: Subject: Signing Authority


Signing authority is delegated to Directors occupying the following positions:


  • President

  • Vice-President

  • Secretary

  • Treasurer

  • Chair of the Finance & Investment Committee


      1. Two signatures are required on all cheques, normally one signature will be that of the Treasurer;


      1. Agreements to establish Endowments require the Board or Executive approval and will be executed by two Directors with signing authority.
