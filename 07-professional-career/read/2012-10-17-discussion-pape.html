EDUCATION FIRST CANADA INC.

Discussion Paper

September 17, 2012



Education:

  • Is a very personal matter
  • Is an important factor in determining one’s quality of life
  • Will determine to a significant degree the future of our community, country and ourselves
  • Is a life-long process of engaging in experiences that have a formative effect on the way we feel, think and act.  
  • As  a system is focused on schooling
  • Benefits from parent and public engagement, politicians and? want meaningful engagements in bringing about the transition.  
  • Needs mechanisms that encourage continuous, but planned change.  
  • Depends upon quality input from all providers but especially the home, the school and community at large.

Credentials and qualifications are not the same. (Not sure where this fits but is very important, can it be said another way?)

Vision:   An education system that is

  • Learner centered
  • Flexible
  • Transparent
  • User friendly
  • Responsive  
  • Accountable


Strategies for change:

  • Providing relevant information.  
  • Engaging the Community in Conversation.   
  • Encouraging and supporting innovation and best practices   



  • Recognizing leadership of people, organizations and institutions.  
  • Encouraging and supporting research.


Education First Canada Inc. is:

  • An independent not-for-profit national organization
  • Committed to a quality education for every Canadian
  • Headquartered in Prince Edward Island
  • Operating under the aegis of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island


Near-term objectives:

  • Provide information via:
  • A printable electronic newsletter
  • Media  
  • Occasional papers
  • Website
  • Encourage public engagement
  • Conduct a public participation activity using community engagement software.
  • Support local discussion groups
  • Engage educators in conversation


  • Recognize leadership through:
  • Awards to a school or schools that best demonstrate a high level of public engagement
  • Profiling educational leaders
  • Providing support for best practices


  • Develop a supporting organization by:
  • Recruiting members
  • Securing charitable status
  • Securing both operating and project funds
