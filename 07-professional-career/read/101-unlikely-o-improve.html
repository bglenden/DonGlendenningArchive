An incomplete working draft noting that quality improvement is not being discussed or prioritized. Don observes that new technology, poverty support, and increased TAs are not explicitly linked to quality outcomes. Lists barriers: narrow official education definition, failure to distinguish education from schooling, politicization, fear factor, and lack of leadership. Argues that meaningful change must come from provincial government granting organizational freedom and demanding accountability.
