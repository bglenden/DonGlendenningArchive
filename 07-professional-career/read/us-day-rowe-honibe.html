This letter to John Rowe at Island Abby Foods (87 Watts Ave) invites $250 sponsorship of the July 4th US Day Reception. Addressed to "Island Abby Foods Limited," the letter replicates the standard US Day solicitation language. This appears to be another variant in Don's targeted corporate sponsorship campaign, reaching out to multiple island businesses with similar event descriptions.





2011 04 04


Mr. John Rowe

Island Abby Foods Limited  

87 Watts Ave

Charlottetown, PE C1E 2B7


Dear ,


The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island (CFPEI) is a volunteer driven, registered, non-profit, charitable organization.  Our mission is to improve the quality of life on PEI through the creation and building of endowment trust funds that will enhance life for all residents.  


The Foundation provides an established means for individuals, families, community groups, corporations, and private foundations to support philanthropic activities which invest in sustainable solutions to community needs.  We connect caring people and worthy causes through endowment building. 


For the past five years, CFPEI has organized a reception on July 4th for “Summer Islanders” who are US citizens.  The purpose is to give US citizens a venue to meet and celebrate their national day, to thank them for their contributions to our community and to keep them informed about the work of the Community Foundation.


The gathering is informal, with a cash bar, light food, a speaker, our national anthems, US flags and background music.  On three occasions, the US consul General from Halifax has joined us for the occasion.  Attendees are mostly US citizens with summer homes or who live here year round and contribute in so many ways to our Island life and Island economy.  Numbers usually range from 75 to 125 people.   


By means of this letter I am inviting Island Abby Foods  Limited  to become a sponsor at the $250 level.


The enclosed information will provide background on both the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island and the annual US citizen’s Day reception.  I’ll phone you next week to see if you have any questions about the Foundation or the Reception.


We thank you for your consideration.


Sincerely,



Don Glendenning, C.M.

Director
