New Site Designated


The North Yorke River Road entrance to the Confederation Trail has been designated by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island (CFPEI) as a Living Trust site.  As a result, individuals and organizations who want to support the planting of trees, remember friends and colleague, mark a milestone in the life of a person or an organization or add to the beauty of a public space may do so and later check on growth while strolling the Trail.  


Last year, CFPEI planted a cluster of Island oaks on each side of the Confederation Trail entrance in Wood Islands in memory of Dr. Charles Keuper; this year the Foundation plans to plant trees at the Upper Yorke River road site; eventually, the Foundation will designate sites across the Island..


Donations may be made in any amount; in return the donor receives a charitable receipt.  If the donation is to recognize a person, a suitable card will be sent.to the person or the family.  For further information, or to make a donation, phone the Foundation (892-3440) or log on to  HYPERLINK "http://www.cfpei.ca" www.cfpei.ca, click Canada Helps and donate to the Living Trust
