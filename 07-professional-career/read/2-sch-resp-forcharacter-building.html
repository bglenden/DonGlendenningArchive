SCHOOLS AND TROUBLESOME STUDENTS



Again, we have some episodes of troublesome students harassing local businesses while on noon break.  Schools are blamed but can schools solve the problem?  I think not - but they do have a role to play in solving the problem.  Is it a leadership role?  The answer to that question is less clear.


What is the attribute we want to encourage?  Is it taking responsibility for one’s actions?  Showing respect for others? 


Schools should askthemselves:


What responsibility is assumed, implied or taken on by schools in this aspect of a learner’s education ?

Is it a sole responsibility or is it shared?

Are schools responsible when students are off campus?

Is it a school’s responsibility to lead, participate, support or simply to report?

If not the school, who should lead in resolving the issue?

If a school is responsible, shouldn’t it be part of a student’s record? 



And the answers should be written and made known to all stakeholders.
