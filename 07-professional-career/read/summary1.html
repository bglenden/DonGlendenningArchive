Foundational summary of education concepts. Don defines education (learning results demonstrated through capability), person (born knowing little, requiring education to survive/thrive), learning (personal, behavior-changing process where only learners learn), teaching (arranging learning circumstances), and educated (ability to handle daily activities). This concise synthesis establishes philosophical foundation for his education system advocacy.
