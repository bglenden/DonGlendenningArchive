A brief proposal to establish an Innovators Network supporting educational improvement through fostering innovation climate, identifying innovators, linking those with similar interests, recognizing contributions, and organizing annual front-line innovator conferences. Don's vision recognizes that innovation often happens at the grassroots level among practitioners rather than only in policy circles. By creating networking opportunities and recognition mechanisms, he aimed to amplify practitioner innovations and create communities of practice. This initiative reflects his understanding of organizational change dynamics and his belief in building from existing energy and expertise rather than imposing top-down reform.
