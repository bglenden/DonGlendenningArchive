Don's planning notes for establishing a Living Memorial Trust endowment fund at the Community Foundation of PEI. Documents strategic thinking on fundraising, donor engagement, commemorative tree planting, and creation of a Book of Remembrance. Shows Don's leadership in community philanthropic initiatives.

Living Memorial Trust


Contributions of any amount will be accepted

All contributions will be placed into an endowment

Persons wanting a named tree in a specific location will pay an administrative fee to cover the cost.  Details need to be worked out but I would expect it to be in the $500 dollar range.  Making this an administrative fee gets around the matter of self delivery for Revenue Canada purposes.

Once a year, we would make a grant from the general fund for planting of trees

Customized planting may be made at other times.

Generally, we will work through the Province or Municipality of some other organization

Perhaps we need a committee?

I see us promoting this fund fairly widely including off Islanders.

We want a nice Book of Remembrance but one where the pages can be removed and list of donors typed on pages.

I do not know the proposed amount of the donation but will suggest that it include a contribution to the endowment plus money to cover the purchase of a book of Remembrance, printing of brochures, and an early planting to gain publicity.
