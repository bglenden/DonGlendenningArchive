A research compilation documenting sources and references related to DACUM (Development of Curriculum Analysis and Utilization Models), Canada's competency-based education framework. Don compiled web resources from universities, organizations, and researchers working with DACUM and competency-based education, including references from UBC, University of Manitoba, Dalhousie, Ohio State, and others. The list includes academic papers, facilitator training programs, and work by key figures in the field.
