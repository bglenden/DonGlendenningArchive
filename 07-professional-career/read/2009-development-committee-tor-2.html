November 22, 2008

Development Committee Structure


Board:


A major function of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island is to build endowments for the purposes of making grants in support of worthy causes throughout Prince Edward Island


Directors:


Directors are expected to seek out and engage people with financial resources in conversations about the Foundation and causes that matter with a view to them becoming friends 


The Development Committee: 


The Development Committee reports to the Board and is tasked to develop and implement a strategy and initiatives to increase Foundation endowment with a particular emphasis on endowment owned by the Foundation.   Based on Foundation experience to date, The Board recommends that priority be given to the following:


1          Friends - to convert to donors


2 Donors - to develop and implement a strategy that encourages donors to continue to use CFPEI services to meet their charitable objectives.  


3 Trusts - to seek out not for profit organizations, that may of may not be registered charities, with an investment portfolio or a desire to establish an investment portfolio with a view to effecting a transfer of investment funds to CFPEI for administration.  Foundation interest is first of all, for unrestricted or restricted funds followed by agency or managed fund.


4 Communities – to encourage the establishment and growth of community funds.


5 Special initiatives – to seek out opportunities and exercise leadership in tapping funds for the benefit of the not-for-profit sector.


6 Information – to increase the awareness of relevant populations as to the role of CFPEI


Administrative/volunteer Support


8 Promotion – to provide customized support to initiatives described above
