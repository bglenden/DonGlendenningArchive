DACUM (Develop A Curriculum) rating scale with performance levels from 0-6, emphasizing competency-based assessment aligned with workplace participation. Level 6 represents exceptional performance with initiative and leadership capability; descending levels show declining ability to work independently; Level 0 indicates inability to perform satisfactorily in work environments. This framework bridges education and employment, focusing on practical skill demonstration. DACUM methodology originated in vocational and technical education, aligning curriculum development with actual job requirements. The resource reflects Don Glendenning's interest in connecting educational outcomes to practical workforce needs and community employment.
