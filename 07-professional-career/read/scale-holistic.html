Academic rating scale emphasizing holistic assessment of learner understanding from Levels 0-5. Level 5 indicates complete understanding with all task requirements met; Level 4 shows considerable understanding; Level 3 demonstrates partial understanding with most requirements included; Levels 2-1 represent diminishing levels of comprehension; Level 0 indicates no response or task not attempted. This holistic approach evaluates overall grasp of material rather than component-by-component analysis, reflecting progressive educational assessment philosophy. The scale acknowledges partial progress and incomplete attempts, supporting constructivist learning frameworks that value student effort and development trajectories.
