This is an offer acceptance agreement (March-July 2018) nearly identical to FILE 0, establishing the consulting relationship for the Souris area continuing education assessment. It reiterates the $32,051 budget with 30% payment upon commencement, 30% upon preliminary findings, 20% upon draft report submission, and 20% upon final report completion. The agreement specifies payment structure, confidentiality, liability provisions, and confirms Don Glendenning as the Company representative and signatory for Education 20/20.
