A third version of the consulting agreement (March-July 2018) with refined statement of work details. It specifies detailed objectives including developing an inventory of continuing education organizations, assessing adult education needs, identifying gaps between available services and workforce requirements, and determining optimal service delivery strategies. The statement of work (Section 9) outlines specific deliverables including recommendations to stakeholders on addressing service gaps and establishing monitoring strategies. The $32,051 budget and payment terms remain consistent.

9 STATEMENT OF WORK

  1. Develop an inventory of existing continuing education organizations and their services (assets)

  2. Assess the continuing education needs and interests of adults who have completed or left public school relevant to community, workforce, and economic development (all ages 18 and above)

  3. Determine the degree of match and identify gaps between available assets and continuing education needs

  4. Identify the capacity and willingness of educational organizations to meet the identified continuing education needs

  5. Determine the best strategy for service organization and delivery

  6. Develop a strategy to monitor the delivery and outcomes of continuing education in terms of community, workforce, and economic development

  7. Make recommendations to stakeholders (e.g., literacy alliance, continuing education, provincial government, etc.)
