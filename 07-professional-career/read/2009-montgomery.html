Formal thank you letter dated after May 9, 2009, from Don to Dr. Roy Montgomery for hosting heritage equipment collectors. Don expresses appreciation for Montgomery's detailed knowledge of equipment origins, specifications, and model variations. Letter reflects Don's respect for collectors' expertise and his own background in farming in Northeastern New Brunswick. Shows Don's gracious leadership style and genuine appreciation for community collaboration in heritage preservation.


Dr. Roy Montgomery

176 Willy Birch Road

Birch Hill, PEI

C0B 2C0



Dear Roy:


On behalf of the many interested heritage farm equipment collectors, I want to thank you and Diane for your hospitality on Saturday, May 9, 2009.


Although not a collector myself, I am of the view that not enough is done to preserve our agricultural heritage; seeing your collection and knowing of others who also squirrel away antique equipment and implements, makes me feel better.  Yours is a significant collection and I was impressed by your detailed knowledge of the original owner and year together with detailed specifications and differences by model and year.


I know that I speak for all when I say thanks to you and Diane.  As a practicing medical doctor, I know that your personal free time is scarce but you gave generously of the little available to you.


It was a great day and brought back many memories of my early years on a mixed farm in Northeastern New Brunswick.  I enjoyed the day.



Sincerely



Don Glendenning

Director
