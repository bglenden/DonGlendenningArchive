COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND

Operations Handbook




INTRODUCTION


The Operations Handbook brings together a number of key documents that determine the nature and operation of the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island. Documents include key decisions made and by the Board from time to time.


This volume is very much a work in progress. Although all documents have had board approval, some may have been amended over the years. The plan is update all and seek new approvals. In the meantime, changes to documents in this book will be made by the Secretary.


In determining what the Foundation can and cannot do, the following documents take precedence:


  • Letters Patent dated January 3, 1993 and amended May 19, 1994, and

  • By-Laws dated January 5, 1993.


Both are included as appendices..


The Operations Manual is intended as a ready reference for:

  • Directors

  • Officers

  • Committees members

  • Staff, and

  • Volunteers.
