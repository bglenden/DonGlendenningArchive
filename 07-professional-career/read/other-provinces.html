Comparative reference documenting education mission and mandate statements from British Columbia, Alberta, Ontario, New Brunswick, and PEI, plus Glendenning's own synthesis proposal: "encourage, support and regulate education programs and services." This document provides the empirical foundation for his argument that PEI's education mandate is anomalously narrow, unfocused, and unambitious compared to other Canadian jurisdictions. The comparison establishes that PEI's emphasis on curriculum delivery and youth education excludes essential functions other provinces consider central.
