A brief statement of Don's educational credo, asserting that education is fundamentally about changing what people know, believe, and can do. He emphasizes that a community's future depends on its citizens' education and that good education enables people to cope with everyday life. This concise philosophy encapsulates Don's lifetime of work in education.

Glendenning Notes

CREDO

  • Education is a process  of  changing what a person knows, believes and is able to do
  • Future of community and individuals determined largely by nature and level of education gained by citizens
  • Person’s level of education is demonstrated by what a person does
  • Good education reflects the ability to cope with everyday activities
