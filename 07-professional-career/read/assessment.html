Concise assessment service proposal addressing practical employment gaps. Documents conversations with local employers revealing that while new hires are expected to have high school education, they frequently lack basic skills in mathematics (making change, mental arithmetic) and communications (written messages, giving directions). Notes that high school graduation requires 20 credits including 4 language (English/French), 2 math, 2 science, 2 social studies, with courses at 110 hours and 50% pass mark. Proposes establishing an independent assessment service to certify basic skill levels for individuals wanting or needing proof of competency—particularly for entry-level employment access. Specifies service characteristics: fee-based, open to individuals/companies/schools. Represents practical application of Don's assessment philosophy to immediate labor market needs.

Assessment Service-1



BACKGROUND

Based on conversations with local employers, most seem to want new hires to have at least a high school education (likely to mean high school graduation). Many, however, report, however, that new hires frequently lack basic skills; mentioned most often are mathematics including making change, mental arithmetic, basic communications including leaving written messages for work colleagues or supervisors and giving directions. Media reports about a basic skills gap, it would appear, is an extension of the same issue


Suffice it to say thatm while grsduation requirements appear to be straightforward, As further background, I should point out that students need 20 credits to graduate including 4 language credits (English/French), (b) 2 math credits, (c) 2 science credits; and   (d) 2 social studies credits. Courses are 110 hours in length and require a pass mark of 50 %. Course decisions may also reflect requirements of institutions of choice and other soecial requirements

PROPOSAL - to establish an independent assessment service assess for people desiring or needing to establish basic skill levels that employers may use as an alternative to high school for any reason but especially to access entry level employment.


CHARACTERISTICS:

  • Fee for service basis

  • Open to any individual, company or school
