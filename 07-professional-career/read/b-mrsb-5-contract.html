APPENDIX A


SERVICES


  1. The Consultant shall perform the following Services:


  1. identify the target audience, including students and workers in the Souris Area;

  2. design a survey to identify the continuing education needs and workforce development needed among adults in the Souris area as well as the available supports and recommended strategy to address gaps and better meet needs;

  3. ensure the survey questionnaire is a minimum of five pages as per Skills PEI requirements;

  4. conduct surveys from time of signing to June 1, 2018;

  5. ensure a minimum sample size of 500 citizens;

  6. review with the Company the data collected;

  7. write a detailed report of the findings from the study;

  8. facilitate the dissemination of the findings from the study with key stakeholders and community members during the month of June.

  9. recommend a strategy to address and enhance accessibility /delivery of needed services.

  1. The Consultant agrees to complete the project no later than June 30, 2018.



  1. The Consultant shall be responsible for the completion of the work and deliverables as described above and shall report to: Dr. Don Glendenning.

Fees and Payment Schedule:

  2. The Company may, at their discretion, may make interim payments based on milestones reached.


HST Information:

  1. To receive payment, a valid business invoice must be issued to the Company containing a HST registration number or information indicating that the business is not required to be registered for HST purposes.


  1. Any HST charged will be calculated at the PEI HST rate.
