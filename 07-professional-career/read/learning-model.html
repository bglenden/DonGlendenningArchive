Teaching-Learning Model

Characteristics of a dynamic learning model:

  • Clear purpose for learning
  • Relevant to learner objective
  • Performance levels are separate from school grades. 
  • Competency based
  • Advisory committee includes community members
  • Holistic rating scale 
  • Student progress reported on a competency-by-competency basis.
  • Learners has copy of master list of competencies.
  • Curriculum includes a descriptor of each competency
  • Students self-evaluate 
  • Substantial emphasis on application/practice
  • Instruction includes written and spoken activities
  • Includes self selected learning products
  • Standard  learning platform for all ages and applications
  • Externally assessment available
  • Explain context
  • Tutoring available Poster-Learning model
