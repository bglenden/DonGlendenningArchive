This document outlines basic skills in DACUM (Developing a Curriculum) format, a systematic approach to skills-based training. Identifies categories: literacy, numeracy (fractions, decimals, percentages, geometry, equations, trigonometry), science, computer literacy/technology, self-care, interpersonal relations, personal service, construction, and personal competencies. The DACUM framework provides structured skill identification for vocational and skills-based training programs, enabling clear specification of competencies needed for particular occupations.
