Competency description

Mathematics Code 2-3 1--8-Num/example

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

RATIONALE

Canada; system of weights and measures is most;y metric (SI) ; the US is largely based on Imperial measures. For most of us conversion from one to the other is necessary. This competency helps people understand and practive conversion.


ELABORATIONS

Upon development of this skill, you will be able to:

  1. Identify commonly used metric and Imperial units of measurement

  2. Perform conversions from one system to the other

  3. Operate on international time units


TYPICAL ACTIVITIES

The following representative of activities are intended to assist you in the development of the above-mentioned skill. They are representative of the work you are expected to do this guide.

  1. Aggregate pay slips

  2. Interpret company policies

  3. Report errors

  4. Record transaction


PERFORMANCE ASSESSMENT

You can use performance assessments to report what you can already do or to show progress. To demonstrate your performance you will have:

  1. Familiarized yourself with both systems

  2. Practiced conversions

  3. Explain system differences to customers or others you work

  4. Know where to find information when in doubt
