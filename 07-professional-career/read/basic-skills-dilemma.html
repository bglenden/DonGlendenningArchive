Basic skills dilemma


It is time to address the issue of basic education

  • Ongoing issue
  • Consuming a great deal of attention
  • Impacting our economy and quality of life
  • Question about quality of our education
  • Most often raised in the context of citizenship and employment


Issue 1:  We do not have a common understanding of the term nor a recognized list


Definition: Range and  level of skills thought to be needed by every citizen to function in our community


In my discussions with Islanders, math and communication skills are the first to come up in any conversation; Remainder of these notes, I will refer to math skills


Are learnable by practically all people; they are teachable and most likely most are now being taught in PEI schools


Recommendations:

  • Role of schools – assist people to learn skills of citizenship
  • Define graduation in terms of skills learned not time spent in school
  • Priority to basic skills
  • Clearer definition of achievement levels
  • Benchmarks
  • Reporting
