Crystal Bysterveldt

346 Bedford Road

Mount Stewart, 

PEI C0A 1To


Dear Crystal:


It is my pleasure to welcome you to the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island for a work placement.  We are delighted to have you as a volunteer and know that you will make a contribution to our work.  


Since I coordinate the activities of volunteers, I will be assigning tasks and priorities, however, you will also be working with Marla MacDonald our Office Administrator.  It is my understanding that your work placement is for a period of five weeks with the first part being on a part time basis.


As well as helping the Foundation, we want your experience be helpful to you.  Do not hesitate, therefore, to let us know other experiences we may be able to provide.


Again, welcome and we look forward to working with you.


Sincerely


Don Glendenning
