Development Committee activity report from February 11, 2010 following successful January cemetery workshop (17 participants plus 4 resource people including Katharine Tummon and John Robinson). Reports ongoing cultivation of donors (Auld, Hesta MacDonald funds), existing trust contacts, agreement with North Tryon Women's Institute and pending arts agreements (Victoria Playhouse, Carrefour, Jubilee Theatre). References growing endowment partnerships including Indian River Festival matching grant arrangement with DOCH. Shows sustained momentum in development activities across multiple streams.
