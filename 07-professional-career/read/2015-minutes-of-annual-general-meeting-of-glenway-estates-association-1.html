Minutes of Annual General Meeting of Glenway Estates Association

The Annual Meeting of Glenway Estates Assoc. was held at Paul Norris’ cottage on Sept. 2nd 2015 with a quorum present consisting of Don Glendenning, Elizabeth Lang, Donna Tolusso, Arlie Kelley, Donna Meinhard, Ian Galloway, Brenda Reid, and Rollie McInnis.  Donna T. had a proxy vote for the Brulé’s.  David Cairns sent his regrets.

Meeting called to order at 9:30 am by Paul.  The minutes of the last meeting (2012) were approved as written. Moved by Paul, Seconded by Brenda. Carried

President’s Report:  A few informal meetings have been held throughout the year.  Concern has been express over pot holes on the road and ruts on the back road.  Don priced cost of filling them in at $250.00 per load.  Pius’ cost is $235/load.  Consensus is to investigate other companies with a view to putting two (2) layers of fill for pot holes and ruts.  Priority is to fill pot holes on east side with a BobCat (bladed tractor).  Donna M. moved and Rollie seconded to have this work done this Fall.  Motion carried.

Estimate to be obtained for north part of road (ruts) to be done in Spring of 2016.  A friend of Paul is to be approached re the above.

Don Glendenning will contact Dept. of Forestry about planting trees across the back north line 

Treasurer’s Report:

Liz handed out her report indicating a balance of $1037.98.  After a discussion re dues, she moved acceptance of the report and that $50.00 be the dues for 2015.  This was seconded by Iain. Carried.

Cottagers are encouraged to pay dues in a timely fashion.  Make cheques out to Glenway Estates Assoc.  Thank you.

Other business:

A memorial tree for Karen Brulé was discussed.  Donna will pursue the matter with Owen, Madeleine and Mark regarding details.

Erosion on the corner where the entrance road swings towards cottage properties is a concern.  We need to speak to Pius as to how it would impact him if trees were planted.  Donna Meinhard will follow up on this.  The Association would pay for any costs.

New Executive”  Discussion re possible 3 year term instead of 2.

Paul -  president ; Rollie  -Vice President; Brenda  - Secretary: Liz  - Treasurer.

There being no further business Don G. moved the meeting be closed.


Respectfully submitted: Brenda Reid.
