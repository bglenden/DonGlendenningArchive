Don's 2006 letter to Dr. Hesta MacDonald establishing the Hesta MacDonald Legacy Fund through the Community Foundation to support school library book purchases. Reflects Don's work in philanthropy and educational resource development.








July 27, 2006


Dr. Hesta MacDonald

206 Spring Park Road

Charlottetown, PE

C1A 3Y9


Dear Dr. MacDonald:


Arising from our discussion of July 26, I am enclosing an agreement for the establishment of the Hesta MacDonald Legacy Fund to provide grants to schools for the purchase of library books.  I believe it reflects our conversation, however, if not I will be happy to make changes.  Please sign and return both copies to the Foundation Office and we will sign both copies and return one to you.


We also discussed the option of making grants even while the endowment is being built.  An additional contribution of $200 per year for the next five years would allow the granting process to begin immediately and also allow the endowment to build so that a similar grant can be made available in perpetuity.  


Since our discussion, I talked with both Dr. Sandy MacDonald and Mr. Tony Gould of the Eastern School District Office about procedures for making grants.  Both were pleased to learn of the potential grant and are comfortable with the Foundation working directly with the designated schools.  I will provide them with more detail once the agreement is concluded.


I commend you, Hesta, for your continuing interest and support for education in Prince Edward Island and thank you for choosing the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island to administer the Hesta MacDonald Legacy Fund.


If this letter reflects your interest, please sing one copy and return it to the Foundation office.


Warmest personal regards.



Don Glendenning

Director
