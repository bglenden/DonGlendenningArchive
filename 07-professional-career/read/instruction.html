(130 words):** This document outlines teaching competencies and instructional steps necessary for educator development. Don identifies nine instructional competency areas: developing personal and professional competencies, instructional techniques, learning materials, learning environments, assisting self-directed and occupational learning, evaluating learner progress, administrative functions, and community interaction. The document compares Alberta's teaching competencies (communication, organization, classroom management, facilitation, assessment, collaboration, caring, flexibility) with broader instructional frameworks. The emphasis on community interaction and broad competency development suggests Don's view that teaching extends beyond classroom delivery to include community engagement and systemic responsibility.
