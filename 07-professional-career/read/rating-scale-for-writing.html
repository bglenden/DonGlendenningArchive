A 6-point rating scale for evaluating student writing quality. Level 6 indicates creative writing with acceptable grammar and organization; descending levels reflect declining organization, vocabulary precision, and technical accuracy through level 1 which is disorganized with many errors. This scale evaluates both content creativity and technical writing proficiency, appearing twice in the document possibly indicating revision or emphasis.

RATING SCALE FOR WRITING



6 Creatively written while observing acceptable standards of grammar, sentence structure and organization

5 Very well organized showing precise and

wide vocabulary, good sentence

structure and appropriate grammar and

some creativity.

4 Organized with few errors in grammar and

sentence structure.

3 Organized with acceptable sentence structure, grammar and vocabulary.

2 Generally organized but uses limited vocabulary and has significant errors in grammar and sentence structure.

1 Disorganized and unfocused with many errors in grammar and sentence structure accompanied by a limited vocabulary.
