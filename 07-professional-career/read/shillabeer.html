Letter from Don Glendenning to Audrey Shillabeer supporting the Belfast Historical Society's summer lecture series. He endorses the initiative as valuable for summer visitors and island cultural connection, offering to participate.
Professional Career & Consulting




Ms. Audrey Shillabeer, Secretary, 

Belfast Historical Society


Dear Ms. Shillabeer:


I am delighted to learn of your plans to offer a series of lectures for summer visitors.


I am aware of you custom of having guest speakers at your meetings and am impressed with the range and nature of the work of your Society over the years; the rich history of the Belfast area in the life of our Province, makes it  a further attraction and a natural setting for such a series.  I am pleased to note also that you plan to offer the lectures over an extended summer period.


Our summer visitors want more than simply  a warm summer holiday filled with fun; they want to know about their summer home,  its history, its traditions and its people.  The summer lecture series will not only help make that happen, it will also expand their linkages with other visitors and Islanders alike.

.

It is a great project; I commend you for the initiative, and look forward to participating in at least some of the events.


Let us know if we can help

Sincerely


Don Glendenning
