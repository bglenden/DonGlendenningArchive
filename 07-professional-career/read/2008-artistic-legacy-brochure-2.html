Leave an Artistic Legacy



Part of the vibrancy and attraction of Prince Edward Island is the sheer range and nature of the arts scene, especially during the summer.


In addition to some core funding from the provincial government, arts organizations depend on support from patrons and donors; without private support, the arts scene on PEI would be severely limited.


Recently a number of arts organizations in Prince Edward Island participated in an organizational strengthening activity organized by the Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island.  This brochure is a follow-up promotion for the six major participants.
