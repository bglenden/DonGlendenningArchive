COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


SD- 2008- 31

Date: December 5, 2008

Prepared by: Don Glendenning

To: Board


Subject:  Information Officer


This is a volunteer position* reporting to (Volunteer coordinator) but working directly with individual Directors and Executive Director depending on the story.


The person we are seeking will be able to:


Personal Qualities

Work as part of a team

Work well with people in different roles

Assume responsibility for own work

Maintain confidentiality

Appreciate importance of role

Dress appropriate to occasion


Desired skills

Demonstrate a high level of verbal and written communication skills 

Take photos

Undertake some research 

Use technology to locate, store and disseminate information


Duties


Prepare an communications strategy for the Foundation

Organize publicity activities

Develop and maintain relationships with media representatives

Prepare media releases 

Interview sources

Check facts

Cover CFPEI events

Respond to enquiries

Make presentations


Typical tasks: 

Identify publicity opportunities

Organize media events for new Funds

Cover annual meeting

Cover photo ops

Cover activities such as new endowments, summer Islanders activities, annual reception, awards. etc.

Draft brochures and print materials


* Volunteers are special people who, I believe, come to the organization at least in part, because of the person who issues the invitation.  It would be useful to discuss how a volunteer’s work load is to be assigned, how they are recognized and how they maintain contact with their original “inviter”.
