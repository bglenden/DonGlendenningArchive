Meeting about McInnis Requests


Glenway is not a charity. It’s a property holding owned by its cottage lot owners

And should be regarded and treated like private property, just as your house and driveway in town are.


We have owned the glenway property since 1971 and legal access for the McInnis’s

to use our road had never come up until the winter of 2005, 34 years!


Giving someone who is not a property owner access to our road compromises our rights

as property owners. It increases our liability, road erosion, etc and  loss of control without any apparent compensation.

This was certainly not part of our plan when glenway was purchased. The full plan of glenway was apparent from the beginning to everyone who wished to purchase a lot;

Roadways, accesses .covenants, middle lots , common grounds.


Last year glenway roads, accesses, common ground was turned over to the association of lot owners in good faith that care would be taken for the benefit of the owners.


My take on Linda having access on our road  is this.

By now Linda would already have the 14 foot  road access on the McInnis’s own property

And her property lot if it were important  to any of them. They would not wait for

For us to change our mind. Therefore I think this request is a red herring and an opening for the McInnis’s to make further requests.
