Agenda for Education First (later renamed Education 20/20) founding meeting on April 25, 2012, at 5:00-7:00 pm at CFPEI Office in Charlottetown. Don Glendenning chaired the meeting and led discussions on incorporation, launching the "Imagine Education" initiative with public discussion groups, establishing an awards program, creating an information initiative with monthly articles and newsletter, and securing federal incorporation and charitable status. This document marks the formal organizational launch that Don spearheaded.
