Practical recommendations for increasing educational value in schools, emphasizing process over product, school autonomy, appropriate scheduling, and trust. Don advocates for moving away from grade worship and ranking systems toward self-rating. Key recommendations include: encouraging schools to self-advocate, striking balance between individual and organizational needs, careful teacher selection, and developing human capital strategies. Draws on Susan Moore Johnson's research about balancing individual and organizational reform.
