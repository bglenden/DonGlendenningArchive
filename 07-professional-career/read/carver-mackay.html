Gordon Mackay


Further to our discussion of January 16, this is to confirm that we are putting forward proposals to the Government of Prince Edward Island for changes in the Agreement covering the Orin Carver Scholarship Trust.  The proposed changes do not infringe upon the operation of the Orin Carver Scholarship of which you are the Chair.


As a result of our meeting, however, I have since discussed the Carver Scholarship with the Registrar of the University of Prince Edward Island and have copies of the brochure, application and poster.  It appears  that we all agree that it is appropriate for the applications to come directly to the foundation rather than to UPEI.


I will write a follow-up letter shortly outlining the planned changes to all parties involved.



Sincerely



Don Glendenning
