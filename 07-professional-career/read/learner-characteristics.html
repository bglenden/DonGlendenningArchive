Concise framework describing learners through three dimensions: conditions (physical, mental, emotional, social wellness), experiences (home, school, travel, culture, lifestyle, support), and aspirations (family, career, community, income, lifestyle). This taxonomy reflects Don's holistic understanding of learners as multidimensional beings whose education must account for their complete context and forward-looking aspirations.
