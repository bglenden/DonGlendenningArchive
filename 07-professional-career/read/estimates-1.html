This budget document (EdServePEI-Souris Budget 1) provides estimated operational costs for the proposed education service agency. Director wages/benefits comprise largest cost category (amounts not filled in), with office ($3,000), phone ($1,000), equipment/computers ($2,000), materials/copying ($500), insurance ($750), travel ($550), advertising ($5,000), marketing ($1,000), and course support contingency. Project notes emphasize individual course costing and blended funding (cash and in-kind contributions, space, technology, communications, employer grants, tuition, volunteers).
