Don provides a critical analysis of current education system characteristics: bureaucratic/top-down, monopolistic, institution-centered, with obscured accountability, time-based organization, lacking change mechanisms, using batch processes, fragmented curriculum, and credential-driven focus. He criticizes the prevalence of "educationese," the "tyranny of time," inadequately defined outcomes, credential-based licensing, insufficient innovation funding, and lack of support for independent learners. The analysis targets what he sees as fundamental structural problems hindering effective education.
