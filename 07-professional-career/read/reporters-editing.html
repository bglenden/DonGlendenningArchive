Replace message from the President with “The Community Foundation of Prince Edward Island will celebrate its fifteenth Anniversary in 2008.  Some people may not consider this very significant but a lot of great things have happened to the Community Foundation lately which will ensure a significant anniversary celebration.  The Foundation now has over $1,000,000.00 million dollars under its management and control with a total of 35 separate funds.


The success achieved to date has been made possible by the efforts of a great many people including the Directors, Volunteers, our Office Administrator, Committee Members, Donors and Friends.  A very special word of thanks is extended to each one of you who has assisted the Foundation in any way.  I encourage everyone to continue the good works in the years to come.


The July 4, 2007 Reception for the American Summer Islanders will soon be taking place and plans are nearing completion for the Summer Islanders Reception in August 2007.


The Foundation endeavours to “Link those who can with those who need” by challenging those who can to take action which will result in enhancing the quality of life in the communities of Prince Edward Island.


Again, I say thank you to everyone who has helped the Foundation achieve its present status.



Norman Carruthers, President
