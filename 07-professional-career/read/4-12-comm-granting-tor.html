Subject: Grants Committee


The Grants Committee is appointed by the Board and shall consist of at least five members two of whom shall be Directors with one Director acting as Chair.


The role of the Grants Committee is to:


 Develop and recommend policies and procedures for the awarding grants

 Design grant application forms as required

 Publicize and otherwise solicit applications and nominations for grants and awards

 Review applications and recommend worthy candidates

 Receive and review reports from grantee(s)

 Monitor compliance with Donor wishes.

  • Publicize grants

  • Provide the Bard with regular reports on the status of grants

  • Maintain records
