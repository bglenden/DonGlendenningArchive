Preliminary workshop schedule for Education 20/20 2015-2016 season, approved by Directors on June 3, 2015. Features scheduled sessions including Tamara Hubley-Little on formative assessment, Alice Taylor on "Handle With Care," and Don Glendenning on "DACUM: A Canadian Achievement." Reflects Don's expertise in competency-based educational methodologies and his role as both organizer and presenter of educational programming.
