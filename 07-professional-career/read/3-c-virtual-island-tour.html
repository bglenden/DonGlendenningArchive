Planning sheet for an Education 20/20 virtual tour workshop examining Island education resources electronically. Participants search information on PEI education systems, programs, and standards while comparing with other provinces. Reflects Don's education advocacy work and community engagement in education reform.


PLANNING SHEET -3

Emerging

TITLE: A virtual tour through Island education

PURPOSE: To familiarize participants with sources of information about education available electronically and to engage then in discussions related to some of the issues.

FORMAT: Following an initial overview, participants will break into teams to search out and report findings on issues, programs, organization, standards, curriculum, and other matters of interest or concern.


DATE: March 3 with March 10 as a storm date

TIME: 7-9:00 pm

RESOURCES: Education 20/20 team

CLASS: Open to public; willing to repeat if numbers warrant

LOCATION: Southport Room, Stratford Town Hall

COST: None

NOTES: WiFi available; participants will need an IPad or smartphone


Typical activities:

  1. Find and review “PEI philosophy of education” on Department of Education website; check definition of education, is it clear? Where does it rank “preparation for work”? Does it address social and emotional wellness of preschool children? Compare it with statements from NB and NS

  2. Look at the Grade 9 outcomes? Could you meet them? Could you help your child with them? How do math outcomes on PEI compare with those in Quebec?

  3. Compare the new Souris school website with that of Westisle? Which is most educational? Compare with one other school

  4. Principals are designated as instructional leaders in their schools. What is an instructional leader? Find a job description of an instructional leader in PEI.

  5. What is the purpose of Centre Goeland? How does it contribute to education in PEI

  6. Find an article which places social and emotional development/learning as a priority for children and youth; what can we do to ensure that this happens on PEI

  7. Check articles on education in today’s newspapers in Vancouver, Winnipeg and St. Johns, and compare with issues in PEI

  8. Pick a foreign newspaper and check the education issue of the day

  9. ???
