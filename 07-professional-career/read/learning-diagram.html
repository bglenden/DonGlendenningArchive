A visual flowchart organizing learning into three phases: planning, engaging, and documenting. The planning phase includes attempting tasks, gathering information, seeking advice, observing others, undertaking assessment, and developing learning plans. The engaging phase encompasses attempting tasks, receiving instruction, gathering information, seeking advice, receiving feedback, observing others, using resources, and applying new learning. The documenting phase involves transcripts, credentials, and qualifications. This diagram represents Don's effort to create a structured model showing learning as a dynamic, iterative process with clear entry/exit points and documentation procedures, reflecting his systems-design approach to educational reform.
