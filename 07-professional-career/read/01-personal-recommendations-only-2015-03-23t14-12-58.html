A Call for Change



1) Develop a “clear and compelling” vision for education on PEI.



2) Develop a learner centered service model that provides support

in all domains to learners as, when and where needed.



3) Government assume a leadership role in creating a learning culture an

providing a quality education for every Islander.


4) Delegate the role of providing instruction



5) Establish a qualification body for the purpose certifying individual levels

of achievement to persons wanting or needing such documentation.




6) Establish a professional model of service delivery where professionals are

directly accountable to clients for the nature, extent and quality of

service provided.



7) Establish an Innovation Fund
