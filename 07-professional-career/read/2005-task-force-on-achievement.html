Speaking notes from Don's June 2005 presentation to the Task Force on Achievement, discussing competency-based education. Notes his dual academic and vocational teaching credentials and his belief in educational standards as key to improvement, setting up discussion of the May 2005 commission document.

TASK FORCE ON ACHIEVEMENT


Monday, June 6 at 7:45 pm

Queen Charlotte School


Opening comments


Thanks for opportunity to present.


While many of you know me for my work at the college level and in technical and vocational education, I am one of a very small number who hold both an academic and vocational teachers license 


Although I have been out of the formal field of education for a number of years, I have maintained an interest in the field generally.


Am of the view that educational standard is at the cutting edge of educational improvement


Tour work, therefore, I believe is both timely and important.



My focus/comments


Commission members who know me, will expect me to talk about competency based education.


Instead, however, I’d like to focus my comments on the discussion document dated May 2005.
