A concise list identifying gaps in PEI's educational service delivery: support for independent learners, limited public engagement, inadequate forums for user-provider dialogue, absence of learner advocates or family educators, insufficient help for parents with infants, limited certification accessibility for prior/self-directed learning, poor information dissemination, and inadequate skills acquisition support. This document likely formed part of Don's analysis for advocating educational system improvements, pinpointing where existing institutions fail to serve learner needs.

Gaps


Gaps: 

  • support for independent leaners, 
  • Limited engagement
  • public information,
  • Forum where user and provider meet (CVA)
  • Advice/counsel - Learner Advocates/family educator/navigator
  • Help for parents and their infants 
  • Easy access to certification/credit for prior and self-directed learning
  • Information and advice for learners, especially for learners who are not students
  • Limited support for the acquisition of skills
