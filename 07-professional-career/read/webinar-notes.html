Notes about Webinar


  • General
  • This is the framework for those who plan  education programs
  • Key components – (1) understanding the complexities of the learner, (2) a clear purpose (providing help for people on the road of life), (3) identifying the help needed and (4) organizing support servicesto make help available when needed
  • The “Road of life” and ”life roles” are relatively new concepts to most but are crucial to future webinars on assessment, standards, qualifications
  • I find the line showing the road or path give it a sense of motion
  • Learner should always see openings/opportunities; some of the characters could actually be motioning the learner forward (encouraging)
  • May not use all visuals in all presentations
  • Learner
  • Stepping stones
  • We are showing this as help needed but I am thinking that we could show barriers, roadblocks, problems likely to be encountered such 
  • Health concerns
  • Security
  • Access
  • Relationships
  • Social support
  • Poverty
  • Stress
  • Addictions
  • Fear
  • Rigidity of system


  • Conditions, learned, aspirations
  • Appear to be blocking the way; perhaps we could have them sticking out of the bags the learner is carrying or  sitting at the side of the road


  • Hats - We talked about an archway or series of archways 
  • Learning community/culture/resources
  • Remove access building
  • Add building for monitoring and feedback
  • Add self study and coaching – could be like a picnic area on the centre
