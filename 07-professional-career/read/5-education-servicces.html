EDUCATION SERVICES


Education is a service with a focus on creating circumstances or experiences that will help people learn; surprisingly, the literature seldom mentions them. The literature suggests the following typical forms of help:

We talk often about teaching but we seldom describe what teachers actually do; here’s how I see it.

Teaching is a service; it is a matter of creating experiences that allow or cause others to learn. Typical education services as identified by the Business Roundtable:

• Information 

• Counselling. 

• Instruction  

• Quality assurance 

• Learning products and Technology 

• Funding 

• Human resources

The above services should be available to all learners


Learning and teaching can take place most anywhere; schools are rooms or facilities designed and equipped to support learning and teaching. Teachers in those schools provide the services listed above.
