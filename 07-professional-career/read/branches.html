This document organizes educational concepts into multiple frameworks: areas of learning (communication, physical development, personal development, literacy, mathematics, etc.); types of education (formal, informal, non-formal); branches (sciences, social sciences, arts/humanities); life roles (individual, family member, producer, consumer, student, citizen); and learning levels. Don also outlines classification systems for educational objectives and proposes specific reforms including competency-based approaches, community education centers, area directors, and eliminating attendance enforcement requirements.
