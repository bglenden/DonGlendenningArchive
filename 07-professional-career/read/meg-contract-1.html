Schedule A: Activities, Roles and Responsibilities


Purpose of this Proposed Analysis: 


The CLIENT would like to create a detailed job description for the “citizen’s’ or “everyday” mathematics. 



Scope of the Analysis: 

The analysis will include all of the mathematics tasks a citizen undertakes as part of their everyday life. It includes math utilized in entry level, summer or student work that does not require training beyond high school. 


Depth of Analysis:

This proposal is to complete the DACUM Analysis to the task level. The output will be a DACUM chart which includes all of General Areas of Competence, the tasks in each of these, and the Transversal competencies – those underlying skills, abilities and attributes which contribute to the success of a person performing their tasks. 

Optional: It is possible to include Levels of Proficiency in the analysis if there is time. This means identifying, for a specific moment in time or in a learning process, what level of proficiency a practitioner is expected to execute each task at. 


Activities:

Kathleen (Meg) Savory (the Consultant) proposes to facilitate and prepare the DACUM Analysis. This process will take place September 23 and 24th, 2019.
