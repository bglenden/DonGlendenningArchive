Minutes of Annual General Meeting


Glenway Estates Association


The Annual Meeting of Glenway Estates Association Inc. was held at the Norris’ cottage on July 30, 2009, with a quorum present.


Wally Ford, president of the Association, presided and brought forth the agenda for the evening’s business.


Arleon Kelly moved adoption of the report.  Don Glendenning seconded the motion.


It was moved by Don Glendenning and seconded by Ian Galloway that the minutes of the 2008 meeting be approved.  Motion carried.


It was moved by Ian Galloway and seconded by Arleon Kelly that the minutes of the continued 2008 meeting be approved.  Motion carried.


There was sufficient approval received for the setting aside of the By-Laws, concerning the notification for this meeting and other items on the agenda.


Concering the presentation made to the McInnis re the road access, the proposal made at the last meeting of the Association was not accepted.


Paul Norris presented the treasurer’s report indicating that we have a bank balance of  $770.69.  Don Glendenning, Carole Glendenning and Ian Galloway, asked about the bill from Stewart McKelvey; there was some discussion concerning this. The Treasurer’s report was received.


Ian Galloway talked about road assessment per lot and the possibility of having two budgeted items: one for road maintenance and one for other items.   Ian also suggested that one item to be looked at when reviewing the By-Laws is the posssibility of changing the By-Laws from one vote assigned per lot to one vote per cottage owner.  This is to be passed on to the executive or other committee looking into the By-Laws.


The nominating committee report was presented by Karen Brule and Arleon Kelley as follows – 

President – Paul Norris

Vice-President – Don Glendenning

Secretary – Carol McInnis

Treasurer – Elizabeth Lang


Ian Galloway moved nominations cease.  Moved by Karen Brule, seconded by Brenda Reid.  Motion carried.


Wally Ford gave a report on road repair with different scenarios presented.  Arleon Kelly moved that we authorize the new officers to spend up to $1000 re:  road work where required and that the assessment for next year to be $50.00 per cottage. Seconded by Elizabeth Lang.  Motion carried.


Re the repairs needed to the gates, new offiers can ponder this item.


There followed some discussion re by-law changes.  Wally Ford presented a draft for discussion purposes.  The By-Laws stipulate that changes in the By-Laws require 14 days prior to a meeting for presentation to the members.  Arleon Kelly moved that we refer this matter to the officers to work thru and to bring any changes to the assosication at least 14 days prior to the annual meeting.  Brenda Reid seconded the motion.  Motion carried.


Donna Meinhard moved that we give thanks to the previous officers for their work, this motion was seconded by all.


Don Glendenning moved that the meeting be adjourned.
