A memorial book plate commemorating Robert Edwin Adams (1932-2016), designated for Holland College Library. Adams is recognized as a teacher, curriculum analyst, and innovator who originated the DACUM model and led developments in competency-based education in Canada. The plate indicates the book was donated in his memory to Holland College, the institution where Don first implemented Adams' methodology. This memorial reflects Don's personal connection to Adams' legacy and his appreciation for DACUM's transformative impact on post-secondary education.
