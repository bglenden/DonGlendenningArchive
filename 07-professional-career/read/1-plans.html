COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND


Office operation in Marla’s absence




I offered to cover the office during Marla’s absence and propose the following:


I have cancelled a number of appointments, delayed others for the period

I expect to be at the office most days, not all day or each day at the same time.

I suggest the phone message be changed to say that “office will be open on irregular hours for the next month. Mail or packages can be left across the hall, leave a message and we will get back to you

A similar notice to be posted on the door saying that packages can be left across the hall

Incoming mail to be logged, answered or referred as appropriate, to the extent possible I will draft a response for the appropriate signature.  Unless advised otherwise, a Directors mail will be placed in his/her file on the bookcase.  I would appreciate having you note action taken so that the loop can be closed

If you have letters you want typed for signature, please leave them on Marla’s desk

I will need help from the Treasurer on the handling of money both in and out

I am assuming that Lois will file as needed
