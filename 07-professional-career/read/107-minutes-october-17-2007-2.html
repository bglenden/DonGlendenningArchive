COMMUNITY FOUNDATION OF PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND

Board Meeting No. 107

Minutes


Meeting No: 107

Date and time: 10:00 a.m., October 17, 2007

Location: Century 21, 2007


In attendance: Norman Carruthers, David Campbell, Myron MacKay, 

Tom Davies, Joyce Gaudet, Don Glendenning, Marie Salamoun-Dunne

John Robinson, David Anderson, Heather MacDonald

Regrets: JoAnn Chisholm, John Ives

Guest: Chair welcomed Tommy Kickham, Souris, to the meeting representing JoAnn Chisholm


Call to Order and Welcome

Approval of Agenda

Adoption of Minutes of meeting #106  (Campbell/Anderson); 

Heather MacDonald attended meeting #106 but her name was omitted in 

the minutes.  (Sorry Heather); there was also discussion about meeting times

and Norman offered to contact JoAnn about her schedule.

Business Arising

Office space – John R reported that details are still under discussion

Treasurer’s Report – Myron presented financial reports which resulted in some discussion and questions but no action required.  It was noted that many foundations draw down some or all of their administrative fee earlier in the year thus giving a small financial boost to operating funds.

Priorities for 2008 – a support document was not available, however, general discussion supported the need for such a discussion.  President Norman offered to contact Carol Gaban, who led us through a planning activity earlier in the year, asking for a summary of our discussions.

Cadogan Fund – still under discussion - Don

Confidentiality form presented and Norman offered to review and revise for next meeting; also a discussion of liability insurance and a request for information about our coverage. Don

Meeting with Minister – John reported that he, David C and Joyce had a very good meeting with Minister Sheridan; later the Minister contacted John for additional information on the proposed role.







Committee reports

Granting – Tom reported that a reception and announcement of awards will take place at the Arts Guild on November 14 at 4:00 pm; committee membership was confirmed and a copy is attached - Don

Investment – considerable discussion followed David C’s report on investments; discussion  centered around the percentage allocated to US funds and the impact of the rise of the Canadian dollar.; David agreed to call a meeting of the investment at an early date to review investment results; David also reported that $400 was being forwarded to the PEI Sports Council from the Beach Tennis Fund

Nominating – John Reported that our current vacancy has not been filled yet.

Information/Publicity – Don reported that, because of travel, a meeting of the committee had not taken place; Directors agreed that community conversations should be organized; Don agreed to circulate a proposal to committee members about community conversations to be held soon and to schedule a meeting of the committee to look at the whole question of  publicity

Other reports – Don provided a status report on the Living Memorial Trust

New Business

Priorities- dealt with under earlier agenda item

Summerside challenge – Paul Schurman has agreed to convene a meeting

A letter from the Carding Heritage Centre Board will be deferred to the summer Islanders Committee - John

President’s Report – covered earlier


Next meeting – November 21, 2007 at 10:00 am at Century 21 Board Room


Adjournment
