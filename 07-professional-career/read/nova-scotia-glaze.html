This document outlines recommendations for reform of Nova Scotia's education system, focusing on reorganizing from "nine silos" to an integrated system. It includes catalysts for change emphasizing student learning, resource allocation to classrooms, improved teacher support, and increased transparency. Key recommendations include establishing a College of Educators, removing principals from the teachers' union, and creating new administrative structures. The document includes comments reflecting critical thinking about whether improvements can focus only on public schools.
