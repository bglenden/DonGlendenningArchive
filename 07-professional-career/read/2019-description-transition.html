Award description for 2019 competition focusing on student transition from secondary school to post-secondary education and labour market. Quotes CMEC (Council of Ministers of Education, Canada) July 2016 statement that "A student's ability to transition from secondary school to post-secondary education and the labour market is a key measure of the education system's success for students." Establishes December 15, 2018 application deadline and June 15, 2019 project completion with June 30, 2019 final report due date. Demonstrates Don's alignment with national education priorities.



EDUCATION AWARD (2019)

$2,500


BACKGROUND

“A student’s ability to transition from secondary school to post-secondary education and the labour market is a key measure of the education system’s success for students” –

CMEC, July 2016


Education 20/20 Inc. is pleased to offer an Award in the amount of $2,500 to an Island school for a project designed to support the above conclusions.


SUBMISSIONS

Submissions should address the above issue by providing project details as to purpose, clientele, staff involvement, activities, participants, budget, start and finish dates and time span, number of people involved and any other information supportive of the proposal. Also tell us how the money will be used and whether or not the activity is likely to continue.


APPLICANTS

Applicants should note that the competition is limited to Prince Edward Island schools; costs of the proposal. if any, are to be borne by the school; decisions will be based on documentation received; Education20/20 Inc reserves the right not to make an award in any year; final decisions will rest with Education 20/20 Inc.; proposers may be invited to present their proposals publicly, submissions shall include but not be limited to a written format not exceeding 3 pages, proposals shall arise from the group and not be the result of a contract with professionals; and selection will be based on documentation received. The successful applicant is expected to file a report with Education 20/20 at the close of the project.
