Six-level writing assessment rubric establishing performance criteria from disorganized/many errors (level 1) through creatively written with acceptable standards (level 6). Each level describes sentence structure, grammar, vocabulary appropriateness, and organization. Level 6: creative writing with acceptable grammar, sentence structure, organization. Level 5: organized with precise vocabulary, good sentence structure, appropriate grammar, some creativity. Level 4: organized with few errors. Level 3: acceptable sentence structure, grammar, vocabulary. Level 2: limited vocabulary, significant grammar/structure errors. Level 1: disorganized, many errors, limited vocabulary. Document cites Information House 1995 source. Reflects competency-based assessment framework consistent with Don's educational philosophy of defining observable performance levels.
